✨ Vibe

A hidden gem of a hotel gastropub that combines a cozy, welcoming atmosphere with a sophisticated culinary approach that blends American comfort and global flavors.

🍸 Best For

Post-museum lunches, wood-fired flavors, group gatherings, and elevated hotel dining.

🔥 Highlights

  • Global Gastropub Fare: The menu features creative standouts like the lamb meatball sandwich on naan with smoked yogurt and sriracha chicken pasta, showcasing "old world" culinary expertise.
  • Wood-Smoked Classics: Guests rave about the signature burgers and grilled chicken, which deliver an authentic, wood-smoke barbecue flavor right in the heart of the city.
  • Warm, Professional Service: Known for an attentive and diverse staff that excels at making every guest feel at home, whether you are dining solo on business or visiting with a large group.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Conveniently located within walking distance of the Museum of the Bible and the National Mall, offering a peaceful and high-quality alternative to more crowded tourist hubs.
  • Parking is accessible directly within the building, providing a stress-free arrival for those driving into the downtown area.
