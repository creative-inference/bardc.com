St. Arnold's Mussel Bar

St. Arnold's Mussel Bar

Category: Belgian, Gastropubs

Neighborhood: Dupont Circle

Price:

Address: 1827 Jefferson Pl NW, Washington, DC 20036, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A charming subterranean gastropub that perfectly balances the cozy intimacy of a Belgian beer cellar with the warm, welcoming energy of a cherished neighborhood local.

🍸 Best For

Belgian beer enthusiasts, seafood lovers, casual date nights, family dinners, and lively happy hour gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Signature Mussel Kettles: Renowned for succulent Prince Edward Island mussels shipped in daily and served in cast iron kettles, with the "St. Arnold’s" house broth featuring Ommegang Abbey Dubbel beer being a standout favorite.
  • Authentic Belgian Pairings: An impressive selection of cold, flavorful Belgian beers and refreshing drafts curated to complement their perfectly crispy, double-fried frites and hearty beef stew.
  • Exceptional Hospitality: Known for a "community-first" atmosphere where owners and friendly staff provide personable service that makes both first-time tourists and long-time locals feel right at home.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located on Jefferson Place, this hidden gem is a true "underground" destination where you walk down the steps to enter a unique, cellar-style pub environment.
  • Look out for their popular weekday happy hour featuring discounted mussel and beer pairings, perfect for an early evening treat in Dupont Circle.

📰 In the News

Erie Times-News

Erie Insurance, other companies face mounting litigation

St. Arnold's Mussel Bar, a Belgian beer and mussel restaurant in Washington, D.C., closed its doors in 2020 following pandemic-related operating restrictions. The venue's owners, Hello Hospitality LLC, filed a class-action lawsuit against Erie Insurance for denying business interruption coverage claims.
