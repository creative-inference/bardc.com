✨ Vibe

Easy Company brings the sophisticated charm of a European piazza to the DC waterfront, offering a breezy, social atmosphere that blends Italian-inspired elegance with a relaxed, sun-drenched energy.

🍸 Best For

Waterfront wine carafes, golden hour date nights, large group celebrations, and breezy weekend lunches.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Rosé Garden: A stunning, year-round outdoor oasis featuring retractable glass walls and heaters, allowing guests to enjoy incredible river views in any season.
  • Exceptional Group Hosting: Recently debuted as a premier event destination, the venue excels at hosting seamless private parties and engagement celebrations with dedicated, high-touch service.
  • Bistro-Style Excellence: The menu features a delicious array of approachable fare, from their signature creamy spicy hummus and house-made pastas to a highly-praised Atlantic salmon.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy a daily happy hour featuring accessible wines and refreshing frosé at the impressive 34-foot central marquee bar.
  • The venue is highly accommodating for large parties and private events, offering flexible patio configurations and personalized staff attention.
