Casta's Rum Bar

Casta's Rum Bar

Category: Rum Bars, Salsa, Latin

Neighborhood: Foggy Bottom

Price: $$

Address: 1121 New Hampshire Ave NW, Washington, DC 20037

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A vibrant and tropical hidden gem that transports guests to Havana with its speakeasy-style entrance, lush "indoor" courtyard, and a high-energy atmosphere fueled by pulsating Latin rhythms.

🍸 Best For

Social dancing, late-night cocktails, celebratory group outings, authentic Cuban street food, and lively happy hours.

🔥 Highlights

  • Wednesday Salsa Nights: Experience the ultimate midweek escape with free Cuban-inspired salsa lessons for all skill levels led by expert instructors, perfectly paired with all-day happy hour deals.
  • Authentic Cuban Fare: Savor highly-praised classics like their signature Cuban sandwich, savory beef and chicken empanadas, and a curated cigar menu for a truly immersive island experience.
  • Rum & Mojito Oasis: Explore an extensive library of premium rums and expertly crafted mojitos that have earned the bar a reputation as a top destination for Latin spirits in the city.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Visit during daily happy hour from 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM for excellent value on signature drinks and appetizers.
  • While the venue features a spacious dining room and patio, reservations are recommended for weekend nights and special themed events like their "Año Nuevo en Havana" celebrations.

Dish of the Week: Casta’s Rum Bar’s maduros envueltos en tocino

Casta’s Rum Bar offers an immersive Cuban experience in D.C.’s West End, featuring a tropical atmosphere complete with a cigar menu and specialty rum cocktails. The menu highlights include the maduros envueltos en tocino—sweet plantains wrapped in charred bacon with guava sauce—alongside traditional favorites like black bean hummus and classic Cuban sandwiches.
