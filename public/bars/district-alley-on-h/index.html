✨ Vibe

A vibrant, multi-faceted destination that blends the eclectic energy of a curated food hall with the high-octane spirit of a premier H Street nightlife hub.

🍸 Best For

Late-night dancing, group celebrations, exploring diverse food stalls, live music, and high-energy weekend outings.

🔥 Highlights

  • A "Union Market Lite" Experience: This ambitious new event space features 14 curated stalls offering a dynamic mix of gourmet food, craft drinks, and retail, creating a "city within a city" feel.
  • Pulsing Nightlife: Celebrated for its great music and atmospheric lounge setting, it serves as a top-tier destination for those looking to transition from dinner to the dance floor.
  • Community-Centric Space: Owned and operated with a passion for the H Street corridor, the venue offers a welcoming and spirited environment perfect for local socialites.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • RSVPs are available and encouraged for special events and weekend entry to ensure your spot in the crowd.
  • Keep an eye on their rotating schedule for unique pop-ups ranging from fitness sessions to exclusive live music performances.

Happy Hour

District Alley offers Happy Hour options every day, designed to encourage a networking atmosphere for young professionals to collaborate and build with others.
