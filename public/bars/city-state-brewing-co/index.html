City-State Brewing Co.

City-State Brewing Co.

Category: Breweries, Game Bars, Kid Friendly

Neighborhood: Ivy City

Price: $$

Address: 705 Edgewood St NE, Washington, DC 20017

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

City-State Brewing Co. offers a welcoming and laid-back atmosphere that celebrates the history and culture of the District through exceptional craft beer and a spacious, community-driven setting.

🍸 Best For

Family outings, retro gaming enthusiasts, large group gatherings, craft beer discovery, and casual weekend afternoons.

🔥 Highlights

  • DC-Inspired Brews: An impressive tap list featuring standouts like the "Kingman" chicory stout, "Lost Laws" pilsner, and the tropical "202 Juice" hazy IPA.
  • Arcade & Entertainment: A vibrant gaming scene packed with pinball, skee ball, and classic video games, plus a dedicated selection of toys to keep younger kids entertained.
  • Spacious Social Areas: Ample seating at indoor and outdoor picnic tables, including a large covered patio that makes it a perfect destination rain or shine.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue is highly event-friendly, offering a dedicated back room that is ideal for hosting private parties and celebrations for up to 45 guests.
  • Enjoy a rotating selection of local food trucks parked on-site, such as the popular La Michoacana, which serves authentic Mexican cuisine that pairs perfectly with the brewery’s craft selections.

D.C.'s City-State Brewing ceases beer production

City-State Brewing Co. has ceased in-house beer production due to rising costs and market shifts, though its Edgewood pub remains open as a community-focused taproom. The venue continues to serve a selection of unique beers and collaborations, maintaining its commitment to D.C. history and culture despite the end of its wholesale distribution.
