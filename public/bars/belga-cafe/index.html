Belga Cafe

Belga Cafe

Category: Brunch Spots, Belgian, Neighborhood Bars

Neighborhood: Barracks Row

Price: $$$

Address: 514 8th St SE, Washington, DC 20003

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A charming and cozy Belgian brasserie led by Top Chef alum Bart Vandaele, offering an authentic European sidewalk cafe experience in the heart of Barracks Row.

🍸 Best For

Weekend brunch, authentic Belgian cuisine, al fresco dining, and neighborhood happy hours.

🔥 Highlights

  • Renowned for signature Belgian staples including legendary moules frites, lobster waffles, and Flemish beef stew.
  • An extensive waffle menu featuring both sweet and savory authentic creations, such as the herb-pesto "Beruchte" Groene Eieren and fruit-loaded Belgian waffles.
  • Exceptional outdoor seating options, highlighted by a vibrant sidewalk patio and cozy alfresco greenhouses perfect for year-round dining.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended for their popular weekend brunch and exclusive wine dinner events.
  • Sign up for their loyalty program to earn rewards and stay updated on seasonal specials and happy hour offerings.
