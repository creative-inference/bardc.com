✨ Vibe

A quaint and intimate neighborhood sanctuary where passion for domestic artisanal cheese meets a warm, welcoming atmosphere perfect for deep conversation.

🍸 Best For

Romantic first dates, wine and cheese aficionados, cozy happy hours, and bespoke event catering.

🔥 Highlights

  • Expert Pairings: Enjoy a rotating menu of seasonal domestic cheeses paired with curated wines and beers, including standout bites like Appalachian cheese with shallot confit.
  • Unique Celebration Pieces: The shop is a go-to for creative event solutions, offering everything from stunning multi-tier "cheese wedding cakes" to convenient, individual "cheese cones."
  • Artisanal Leadership: Founded by industry expert Kara McGrath, the venue is a champion for American makers and has recently expanded its presence with a dedicated cheese counter at the historic Union Market.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Join the "Trolley Club" to receive a monthly curated selection of hand-picked artisan cheeses and expert-led tasting notes delivered to your door.
  • The space doubles as a morning destination, hosting the "Amazonica" coffee pop-up Tuesday through Friday starting at 7 a.m.

📰 In the News

PoPville Dec 3, 2025

Paste & Rind is closing on H Street, NE early 2026, bar service ending Sunday 12/28

Paste & Rind is closing its H Street, NE location in early 2026 after five years of operating as a champion for domestic artisanal cheese producers. While bar service concluded on December 28, 2025, the business will continue to sell through inventory at the Trinidad Market and fulfill its final cheese club deliveries through the first week of January.
PoPville Jul 2, 2025

“There’s a coffee pop up on H Street!”

Paste & Rind hosts a morning coffee pop-up called Amazonica at its 904 H Street NE location. The pop-up operates Tuesday through Friday from 7 a.m. to 12 p.m., providing additional coffee service to the H Street and Trinidad area.
Tasting Table Oct 22, 2024

A Beginner's Guide To Artisanal Cheese

Paste & Rind owner Kara McGrath provides expert insight into the complex flavor profiles and sustainable benefits of artisanal cheese. The Washington, D.C. cheese shop emphasizes the importance of supporting local economies and offers tips for beginners on how to identify authentic, handcrafted products.
PoPville Feb 12, 2024

“Paste & Rind Cheese Co. is opening a cheese counter in the historic Union Market”

Paste & Rind Cheese Co. has expanded its footprint in Washington D.C. with a dedicated cheese counter inside the historic Union Market. The stall offers an extensive selection of artisan cheese, charcuterie, and accompaniments available for retail purchase or to be enjoyed on-site at the market's picnic tables and rooftop bar. This location complements the company's existing presence in the district, which includes a cheese-focused wine bar on the H Street corridor.
