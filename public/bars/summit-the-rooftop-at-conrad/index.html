Summit the Rooftop at Conrad

Summit the Rooftop at Conrad

Category: Rooftop Bars, Cocktail Bars, Lounges

Neighborhood: Downtown

Price: $$$$

Address: 950 New York Ave NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated garden sanctuary in the sky, Summit offers a stylish, eco-conscious atmosphere with breathtaking panoramic views of the Washington Monument and the U.S. Capitol.

🍸 Best For

Sunset cocktails, luxury date nights, panoramic sightseeing, sustainable dining, upscale group outings

🔥 Highlights

  • Culinary Excellence: Under the leadership of Executive Chef Andrew Whiteside, the kitchen serves elevated regional fare like Lobster Mac & Cheese and artisanal flatbreads that highlight Chesapeake Bay ingredients and global flavors.
  • Eco-Conscious Innovation: The venue features a unique onsite rooftop garden and an innovative solar oven, powering a luxury zero-waste dining experience that includes house-grown garnishes and sustainable techniques.
  • Crafted Spirits: The bar program focuses on craftsmanship, offering a curated selection of spirits from local distillers, sustainably produced wines, and expertly balanced cocktails like the signature Beijing drink.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Time your visit for sunset to experience the most stunning 360-degree views of the D.C. skyline as the city lights up.
  • Located atop the Conrad Washington DC, this upscale lounge is a premier destination for travelers and locals seeking a high-end, inclusive, and stylish nightlife experience.
