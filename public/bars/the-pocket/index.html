The Pocket

The Pocket

Category: Live Music Venues, Neighborhood Bars

Neighborhood: NoMa

Price: $$

Address: 1508 North Capitol St NW, Washington, DC 20002

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A funky and intimate "music scene watering hole" that combines a cozy neighborhood bar feel with world-class sound and professional lighting.

🍸 Best For

Intimate live shows, discovering local talent, Tuesday night outings, all-ages concerts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Pristine Production: Highly praised by both performers and photographers, the venue features top-tier acoustics and a professional lighting rig that creates a high-impact concert experience in a 75-capacity space.
  • The Tuesday Open Mic: A high-energy weekly tradition where you can catch over a dozen performances in one night, ranging from rising DMV gems to surprise professional appearances.
  • Diverse Genre Hub: As part of the 7DrumCity community, the programming is incredibly inclusive, spanning everything from indie pop and R&B to metal and electronic music.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The Pocket is an all-ages venue located inside 7DrumCity, making it a welcoming spot for music lovers of all generations.
  • Given its cozy 75-person capacity, it is a great idea to check the online schedule and grab tickets in advance for popular local acts.
