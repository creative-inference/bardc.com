✨ Vibe

Lola's is a stylish and "sexy" neighborhood gem on Barracks Row, boasting killer decor and a welcoming atmosphere that makes it a premier destination for locals and industry professionals alike.

🍸 Best For

Late-night industry hangs, competitive pool games, expert craft cocktails, and sharing flavorful small plates with friends.

🔥 Highlights

  • Stunning Ambiance: Renowned for its unique and captivating interior design, the bar offers one of the most visually impressive and inviting settings in the District.
  • Expertly Crafted Drinks: The talented bar team is known for their mixology skills, specializing in custom cocktails and elevated spirits tailored to your specific taste.
  • Elevated Pub Fare: A diverse menu of small plates features standout items like tandoori chicken kebabs, mezza plates, flatbreads, and savory sliders.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • As a popular "industry hangout," it offers an authentic DC energy and a great spot to mingle with the local crowd.
  • The venue features pool tables, making it an ideal choice for those looking for some friendly competition alongside their drinks.
