Capo Speakeasy

Capo Speakeasy

Category: Speakeasies, Cocktail Bars, Late Night

Neighborhood: U Street

Price: $$$

Address: 715 Florida Ave NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Hidden behind a nondescript industrial freezer door in a bustling Italian deli, this U Street gem offers an "in the know" experience that blends 1920s Prohibition glamour with a high-energy modern lounge atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Late-night dancing, clandestine cocktails, group celebrations, "in the know" nightlife seekers, post-bar cravings.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Secret Reveal: The entrance is cleverly disguised as a functioning freezer door, providing a thrilling transition from a classic deli to a sleek, low-lit lounge with a spacious dance floor.
  • Expert Mixology: Recognized as one of D.C.’s must-try speakeasies, the venue features artisanal cocktails like the strawberry gin-infused Strawberry Fields and acclaimed Espresso Martinis, often served with a theatrical fire show.
  • Premier Late-Night Dining: Recently honored as one of the city’s top late-night dining destinations for 2025, patrons can enjoy world-class Italian sandwiches from the deli side well into the early morning hours.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Visit Thursday through Saturday to catch live DJ sets spinning a high-energy mix of hip-hop and Top 40 hits.
  • The venue is a prime destination for bottle service and lively group outings in the heart of the U Street corridor.
