✨ Vibe

An intimate and sophisticated neighborhood gem that perfectly balances candlelit elegance with a playful, unpretentious energy, recently recognized as Newsweek’s #1 Best Wine Bar for 2026.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, wine enthusiasts, refined girls' nights out, and adventurous foodies seeking unique pairings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Wine Program: A 2025 RAMMYS finalist and top-ranked national wine bar, featuring a curated list of over 100 organic and biodynamic bottles with a specialized focus on labels from Lebanon, Israel, Greece, and Morocco.
  • Playful Pairings: The venue offers a whimsical approach to fine wine, featuring expert-led tastings that pair high-end pours with unexpected snacks like Cheetos, Reese's, or seasonal Girl Scout cookies.
  • Hearth-Fired Mediterranean Cuisine: Chef Ryan Moore’s menu focuses on soulful, stone-oven-fired dishes including hearth-roasted branzino, traditional tart flambée, and seasonal gnocchi with parmesan cream.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • To ensure a tranquil atmosphere, the restaurant welcomes children ages 8 and older; however, guests with younger children can enjoy the full Little Blackbird menu in the adjacent Sababa lounge.
  • Reservations are highly encouraged as the dining room fills up quickly, though the hospitable staff is known for going above and beyond to accommodate walk-ins when possible.
