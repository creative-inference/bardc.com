Ben's Upstairs

Ben's Upstairs

Category: Rooftop Bars, Live Music Venues, Comedy Clubs

Neighborhood: H Street NE

Price:

Address: 1001 H St NE, Washington, DC 20002, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A stylish and artistic rooftop oasis that blends the historic legacy of the Ali family with a vibrant, contemporary H Street energy.

🍸 Best For

Rooftop cocktails, weekend brunch, live comedy, romantic date nights, local art appreciation.

🔥 Highlights

  • Enjoy an elevated dining experience featuring a contemporary menu with standouts like steak frites, fresh crab cakes, and signature avocado toast.
  • Soak in the atmosphere of the open-air rooftop bar, which serves as a premier destination for outdoor drinks and scenic H Street views.
  • Experience a rotating schedule of top-tier entertainment, including live music performances and a popular weekly comedy showcase every Thursday night.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • As a proud expansion of the iconic Ben’s Chili Bowl legacy, this Black-owned venue is a cornerstone of DC’s culinary and entrepreneurial history.
  • The space features beautiful displays of local art and offers a celebrated happy hour perfect for a post-work wind-down.
