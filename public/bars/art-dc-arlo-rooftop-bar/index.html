ART DC - Arlo Rooftop Bar

ART DC - Arlo Rooftop Bar

Category: Rooftop Bars, Cocktail Bars, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: Gallery Place / Chinatown

Price:

Address: 333 G St NW, Northwest Washington, DC 20001, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated indoor-outdoor lounge where art-forward aesthetics meet panoramic city skyline views, creating a high-energy atmosphere perfect for social nights out and scenic sipping.

🍸 Best For

Sunset cocktails, late-night happy hour, impressive date nights, and lovers of Japanese-inspired small bites.

🔥 Highlights

  • Expertly Crafted Japanese Menu: Indulge in a sophisticated menu from Chef Pepe Moncayo featuring yakitori skewers, hand rolls, and a standout Wagyu tasting menu that includes creative delicacies like Wagyu ice cream.
  • Breathtaking Skyline Vistas: The expansive rooftop offers stunning panoramic views that span from the U.S. Capitol building to the Washington Monument.
  • Immersive Art & Events: True to its name, the venue serves as a hub for local art activations, rotating installations, and a vibrant nightlife scene complete with regular DJ sets.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of the "reverse happy hour" starting at 8:30 p.m. for late-night deals on sake-infused cocktails and snacks.
  • While the rooftop specializes in small bites and drinks, the lobby level features Arrels, a full-service restaurant perfect for a complete dining experience.
