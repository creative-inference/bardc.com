Jackie Lee's

Jackie Lee's

Category: Neighborhood Bars, Dive Bars, Game Bars

Neighborhood: Manor Park

Price: $$

Address: 116 Kennedy St NW, Washington, DC 20011

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Jackie Lee’s is a chill, unpretentious neighborhood gem that perfectly blends a welcoming dive-bar soul with a nostalgic, high-energy community spirit.

🍸 Best For

Competitive trivia nights, late-night comfort food, retro gaming with friends, and hosting private celebrations in the hidden upstairs event space.

🔥 Highlights

  • Retro Nostalgia: Lean into the "sick atmosphere" with a Nintendo 64, a 24/7 movie projector, and a unique vending machine stocked with local essentials.
  • Elevated Bar Fare: The kitchen serves up standout comfort food, including a raved-about brisket sandwich, smoked baby back ribs, and their famous BBQ seasoned fries.
  • The Dice Roll Combo: For just $11, you can score a surprise beer-and-shot pairing that stands as one of the best value deals in the District.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Check their Instagram for the weekly trivia schedule and updates on live DJ sets hosted on the second floor.
  • Parking is convenient with plenty of street spots available directly in front and throughout the surrounding Manor Park neighborhood.

Jackie Lee’s is a historic neighborhood venue on Kennedy Street NW that originally gained fame in the 1970s and ’80s as a hub for R&B, funk, and soul music. The establishment has since reopened, offering a modern space for patrons to gather while maintaining its legacy as a significant piece of Washington's nightlife history.
