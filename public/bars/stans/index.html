Stan's

Stan's

Category: Dive Bars, Sports Bars, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: Downtown

Price: $

Address: 1028 Vermont Ave NW, Washington, DC 20005

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A legendary and high-energy basement cornerstone of the DC scene, Stan’s offers a warm, multi-generational atmosphere where classic dive bar charm meets a bustling downtown spirit.

🍸 Best For

Strong cocktails, lively happy hours, game day viewing, and signature comfort food.

🔥 Highlights

  • The "strongest drinks in D.C." guarantee, featuring legendary pours and the crowd-favorite Ciroc Obama cocktail.
  • Famous fried and baked wings that have been a District staple for over 30 years, perfectly paired with their original Mambo sauce.
  • Generous, shareable portions of classic American fare like the Fisherman’s Platter, Chicken Alfredo, and indulgent Red Velvet Cake.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of one of the city's best happy hours Monday through Thursday from 2 PM to 5 PM, featuring $10 rail cocktails and discounted appetizers.
  • Look for the special late-night fried food menu available until 1:00 AM on Thursday, Friday, and Saturday nights.
