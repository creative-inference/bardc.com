✨ Vibe

Boqueria Dupont Circle blends local charm with downtown energy, offering a high-energy, Barcelona-inspired atmosphere that captures the spirit of a bustling Spanish tapas bar.

🍸 Best For

Lively group celebrations, award-winning weekend brunches, high-stakes business lunches, and festive happy hours on the patio.

🔥 Highlights

  • Acclaimed Spanish Cuisine: Recently named one of the "Best Spanish Restaurants Around D.C." by Eater editors, the menu features authentic gems like the 48-month aged Jamón Ibérico de Bellota, Gambas al Ajillo, and fresh seafood paella.
  • Legendary Weekend Brunch: A neighborhood favorite known for its "Brunch Feast" and spirited all-you-can-eat bottomless options served every Saturday and Sunday.
  • Interactive Drink Program: The social experience is elevated by signature sangría pitchers, curated Spanish wines, and the traditional porrón for communal sharing.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended as the dining room and expansive bar stay packed with energy, even on weeknights.
  • For a curated culinary journey, the "Boqueria Experience" chef's menu offers a pre-set selection of guest favorites and seasonal specialties perfect for groups.

🔄 Weekly Events

🥂 Award-Winning Brunch Feast

Sat, Sun

A family-style brunch featuring a selection of tapas, brunch mains like Boqueria B.E.C. and Torrija, and churros. Includes an option for 90 minutes of unlimited Sangría, Mimosas, Estrella, or Bloody Marys.

🥘 Tapas Lunch

Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri

A curated selection of Boqueria's best and quickest tapas served to share among the table, including dessert for $24 per person.

Tapas Lunch Special 🥘

Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri

A selection of Boqueria's best and quickest tapas, served to share amongst the table. Includes 2 tapas per person and Churros con Chocolate for dessert for $24 per person.

Happy Hour

The neighborhood gathers around the expansive bar and prominent patio for legendary, Barcelona-inspired happy hours featuring a curated lineup of Spanish wines, sangrías, and expertly crafted cocktails.
