✨ Vibe

Modern and industrial-chic, this sun-drenched Logan Circle gem seamlessly transitions from a sophisticated specialty coffee house to a lively evening bar within its newly updated home at the Studio Theatre.

🍸 Best For

Single-origin coffee flights, all-day brunch, prosecco pairings, post-theater cocktails, and cozy work sessions.

🔥 Highlights

  • Curated Coffee Experience: Choose your perfect brew using their unique flavor profile system (Floral, Fruit, Bright, or Comfort) or enjoy specialty creations like the Tahitian Vanilla Latte and Venetian coffee.
  • Elevated All-Day Dining: Savor highly-rated seasonal bites, including the famous bacon jam egg and cheese sandwich, fresh "Jimmy" sandwiches, and the popular carrot cake bread.
  • The Giggle Water Partnership: In the evenings, the venue shines as a social hub featuring a specialized menu of prosecco flights, craft beers, and creative cocktails.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Recently relocated to a modernized, two-story space within the Studio Theatre, offering excellent natural lighting and a "hidden gem" feel right on the corner.
  • A prime destination for organized events, offering customizable catering and service for everything from company happy hours to personal celebrations.
