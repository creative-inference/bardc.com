Her Diner

Category: LGBTQ+ Bars, Cocktail Bars

Neighborhood: Adams Morgan

Price:

Address: 2004 18th St NW, Washington, DC 20009, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A vibrant, queer-owned sanctuary in Adams Morgan that reimagines the "gay Cheers" atmosphere with neon-hued nostalgia, inclusive energy, and a warm, home-away-from-home feel.

🍸 Best For

Drag brunch, late-night comfort food, craft cocktails, community events, and lively happy hours with friends.

🔥 Highlights

  • Immersive Themed Restrooms: Experience the ultimate tribute to pop royalty with dedicated Beyoncé and Madonna bathrooms, featuring curated decor and their iconic hits playing on loop.
  • Elevated Comfort Classics: The menu shines with nostalgic favorites like meatloaf, chicken pot pie, and perfectly seasoned wings, alongside signature drinks like the "Bi Felicia" and the "Betty White Cosmo."
  • Community-Driven Spirit: Recently opened as a safe haven in the District, this venue hosts high-energy drag shows, themed trivia nights, and special sessions supporting local civil servants and federal workers.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Visit early to catch the popular daily Happy Hour, which runs until 7 PM.
  • Street parking is tight in Adams Morgan, so taking a cab or rideshare is recommended to fully enjoy the expert mixology.
