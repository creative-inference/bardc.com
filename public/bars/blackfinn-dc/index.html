✨ Vibe

A high-energy, personality-filled American pub that blends the excitement of a premier sports bar with a welcoming, "Meet Me at the Finn" atmosphere just steps from the White House.

🍸 Best For

Game days, post-work happy hours, budget-conscious professionals, casual business lunches, group gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • An expansive menu of craveable American comfort food, featuring fan favorites like BBQ mac n' cheese, bacon-wrapped meatloaf, and signature fish tacos.
  • A premier destination for sports enthusiasts, offering a lively environment to catch the big game alongside specialized game-day snacks like Nashville hot chicken sliders.
  • A long-standing downtown staple for over a decade, celebrated for its extensive craft beer list and vibrant energy that stands out from the surrounding area.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy their popular happy hour every Monday through Friday from 3 p.m. to 7 p.m., featuring $2 off all draft beers and pub snacks.
  • Conveniently located in the heart of downtown, it is a reliable and affordable destination for locals and visitors exploring the city's historic center.
