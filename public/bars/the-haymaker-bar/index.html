✨ Vibe

A quintessential H Street "hidden gem" that transitions from a relaxed, intimate neighborhood haunt early in the evening to a high-energy, bustling hotspot as the night progresses.

🍸 Best For

Craft cocktail enthusiasts, late-night social scenes, neighborhood happy hours, frozen drink fans

🔥 Highlights

  • Expertly Crafted Cocktails: Renowned for serving some of the best drinks on the corridor, including a "spot-on" Pisco Sour and a vacation-inspired frozen Painkiller.
  • Must-Try Bar Bites: The menu features standout comfort food, most notably the loaded tater tots with bacon and cheese and their popular chicken wings.
  • Exceptional Hospitality: Guests are treated to attentive, friendly service from a bar staff known for their warm welcome and thoughtful touches, like complimentary snacks.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Arrive before 9:00 PM for a quieter, more conversational atmosphere before the venue fills up with its signature high-energy crowd.
  • Keep an eye out for the "hidden gem" entrance along H Street NE, making it a perfect spot for those who want to escape the more touristy areas.

The Haymaker Bar has landed on H Street NE

The Haymaker Bar is a bar-focused concept on H Street NE that offers affordable drinks and comfort food in the space formerly occupied by the Atlas Room.
