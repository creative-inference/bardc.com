Nero

Nero

Website

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated three-story Dupont Circle destination that seamlessly blends a modern self-serve wine lounge with a moody, candle-lit subterranean speakeasy featuring exposed brick and an intimate atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Speakeasy explorers, wine enthusiasts, adventurous foodies, and romantic date nights.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Sabina Experience: Recently reopened in late 2024, this hidden basement gem is accessible through a secret brick-wall door and features dramatic cocktail presentations where bartenders incorporate fire into the drink-making process.
  • Interactive Wine Tasting: The upper floors feature state-of-the-art WineEmotion self-dispensing machines, allowing guests to sample an extensive selection of curated wines at their own pace.
  • Italian-Indian Fusion: The menu offers a unique culinary crossover with standout small plates like their signature Brussels sprouts, artisan pizzas, and flavorful shrimp dishes.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Be sure to check out the happy hour menu for highly-rated deals on both food and craft cocktails.
  • The venue spans three levels: head upstairs for the self-serve wine bar or downstairs for the intimate, speakeasy-style cocktail lounge.
