Kitchen + Kocktails

Category: Supper Clubs, Cocktail Bars, Soul Food

Neighborhood: Downtown

Price:

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

This high-energy downtown destination blends upscale Southern elegance with a vibrant, "Instagrammable" social scene featuring floral walls and live musical performances.

🍸 Best For

Celebratory group dinners, birthday bashes, sophisticated date nights, and enjoying showstopping cocktails with a soul food twist.

🔥 Highlights

  • Decadent Southern Fare: The menu features elevated comfort classics like Mississippi Catfish + Grits, Kajun Stuffed Salmon, and lobster-topped shrimp and grits, all served in generous, flavor-packed portions.
  • Showstopping Cocktails: Sip on legendary drinks like the Peach D’usse Frose served in full bottles or the tableside-lit TRUE Flame, with an extensive list highlighting premium Black-owned spirit brands.
  • The Supper Club Experience: Beyond the stunning main dining room, guests can head downstairs to enjoy a live band, creating a seamless transition from a world-class meal to a lively night out.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended for this popular downtown hotspot, as it frequently hits capacity during weekends and special city events.
  • Expect professional, attentive service from a knowledgeable staff dedicated to making every first-time visit feel like a special occasion.
