✨ Vibe

Whitlow's U Street is a high-energy, multi-level destination that blends the classic warmth of a neighborhood tavern with a vibrant rooftop scene and an unbeatable gameday spirit.

🍸 Best For

Football watch parties, Trivia nights, Rooftop cocktails, Group celebrations, Weekend nightlife

🔥 Highlights

  • Premier Sports Destination: Known for its "tailgating energy," this is a top-tier spot for football fans, serving as an official watch bar with a packed house for everything from college games to the Super Bowl.
  • Scenic Rooftop Bar: The rooftop terrace offers fantastic views of the iconic U Street corridor, providing a perfect backdrop for a relaxed afternoon happy hour or an evening under the stars.
  • Fan-Favorite Fare: The menu features elevated bar classics like smoked wings, jumbo jalapeño poppers, and unique draft options such as their signature boozy peach iced tea and festive Jell-O shots.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Participate in a DC tradition by joining their popular mid-week Trivia Nights, which draw a lively and competitive local crowd.
  • The venue features multiple levels and different paces, shifting from a chill afternoon hangout to a high-energy party scene on Friday and Saturday nights.

Whitlow’s Opens a Bar at the Wharf—Alongside Its Tiki Boat

Whitlow’s U Street serves as the flagship for the historic D.C. brand, which has expanded its presence with a new multi-level bar and entertainment venue at The Wharf. The waterfront location honors the bar's nearly 80-year legacy with vintage decor, classic menu items like smash-burgers and smoked wings, and a reservable tiki boat.
PoPville Mar 20, 2025

“Whitlow’s at The Wharf opens FRIDAY!” in the former Brighton space.

Whitlow's U Street is a three-level bar and live entertainment space located at the corner of 9th and U Streets NW. As the sibling location to the newer Wharf venue, it continues a D.C. hospitality tradition dating back to 1946 and features vintage neon signage and memorabilia from the brand's original downtown tavern.
The Business Journals Jan 8, 2025

A longtime Greater Washington favorite is coming to The Wharf

Whitlow's is expanding to The Wharf through a partnership between proprietor Jon Williams and the Hilton brothers' H2 Collective. The venture involves rebranding the existing Brighton SW1 space as the latest iteration of the longtime local favorite.
Washingtonian Jul 26, 2023

Whitlow’s Is Moving (Again), Taking Over the Brixton on U Street

Whitlow's U Street has relocated to the former Brixton space at 901 U St. NW, reviving its classic tavern atmosphere with a multi-level venue and a spacious rooftop bar. The establishment features an approachable menu of burgers, chicken clubs, and jambalaya pasta, complemented by a selection of beers, whiskeys, and signature rooftop drinks like rum buckets.
