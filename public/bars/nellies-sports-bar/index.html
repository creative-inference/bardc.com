✨ Vibe

Nellie’s is a high-energy, inclusive U-Street staple that perfectly blends the spirit of a classic sports bar with the electric, welcoming flair of a top-tier drag and dance venue.

🍸 Best For

Drag brunch, late-night dancing, karaoke enthusiasts, strong cocktails, and Sunday Funday sessions.

🔥 Highlights

  • Legendary Drag Brunch: A high-energy weekend staple featuring a delicious buffet and charismatic performers who keep the crowd laughing and the drinks flowing.
  • Dynamic Weekly Events: The calendar is packed with interactive fun, including Tuesday night Karaoke, Trivia, and "Xavier Thursdays" featuring live DJs spinning the best in Hip-Hop and R&B.
  • Exceptional Hospitality: Guests consistently rave about the friendly, attentive staff and professional security team that ensure every visitor feels safe and welcome.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Visit for "Sunday Funday" from 6–10 PM to enjoy the "Sunday & Chill" menu with exclusive deals on Casamigos and Hennessy Margaritas.
  • Street parking is limited in the busy U Street corridor, so arriving via rideshare is highly recommended for a stress-free experience.
