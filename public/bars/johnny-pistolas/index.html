Johnny Pistolas

Johnny Pistolas

Category: Taco Bars, Mexican, Brunch Spots

Neighborhood: Adams Morgan

Price: $$

Address: 2333 18th St NW, Washington, DC 20009

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A vibrant, multi-level Adams Morgan staple that seamlessly blends a sun-drenched taqueria with high-energy nightlife and a sophisticated new cocktail lounge.

🍸 Best For

Bottomless brunch, large group celebrations, sustainable cocktails, private events with no rental fees, and late-night dancing.

🔥 Highlights

  • Multi-Concept Experience: The venue recently expanded to include Mi Pana, a Latin-fusion gastro pub on the main floor, and the Riff Raff Room, a hidden, zero-waste cocktail bar led by industry veterans.
  • Fan-Favorite Mexican Fare: Indulge in Los Angeles-style street food, including the legendary "Nachos Libre," fresh house-made guacamole, and the signature "A-Team Travels" style fajita rice bowls.
  • Ultimate Party Destination: With multiple levels of seating and private event spaces equipped with independent bars and AV, it is the perfect spot for graduation or birthday celebrations—especially with their "no rental fee" booking policy.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • For the ultimate weekend experience, book a reservation for their popular bottomless mimosa brunch, served Saturday and Sunday from 11:30 AM to 3:00 PM.
  • If you're looking for late-night energy, the venue stays open until 3:00 AM on Fridays and Saturdays, featuring a full tequila bar and a dedicated nightlife floor.
