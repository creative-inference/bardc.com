✨ Vibe

A legendary Adams Morgan landmark since 1992, Bukom Restaurant & Lounge offers a soulful blend of authentic West African culture, vibrant local art, and a homey, multi-level space perfect for immersive nightlife.

🍸 Best For

Live reggae fans, authentic West African cuisine, cultural date nights, and potent house cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • Authentic West African Staples: Savor a menu rich with traditional flavors, featuring standout dishes like spicy Beef Suya, Jollof rice, savory Egusi soup, and tender Chicken Yassa.
  • Rhythmic Weekend Nights: As a premier live music venue, Bukom is famous for its weekend reggae performances that bring a high-energy, rhythmic pulse to the Adams Morgan strip.
  • Signature Drinks with a Punch: Don't miss the "Agege Slammer," a house-favorite cocktail known for its strength, or choose from their unique selection of Kenyan beers.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Visit on weekend nights to experience the full live music lineup and the restaurant's most vibrant cultural atmosphere.
  • Since the venue is located on a bustling main road where parking is limited, taking a rideshare is the best way to ensure a smooth arrival.
