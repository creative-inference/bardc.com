District Bites

District Bites

Category: Happy Hour Spots, Cocktail Bars, American

Neighborhood: Gallery Place / Chinatown

Price:

Address: 701 7th St NW, Washington, DC 20001, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

Located in the heart of Gallery Place-Chinatown within the District E complex, District Bites is a high-energy dining and entertainment hub that serves as the ultimate gameday and esports destination. Recently refreshed for the 2025-26 season, the venue features an upgraded culinary program and exclusive fan activations designed to elevate the pre-game experience for Wizards and Capitals fans alike.

✨ Vibe

An electric, high-octane atmosphere where industrial-chic design meets the cutting edge of esports and live entertainment.

🍸 Best For

Pre-game gameday drinks, esports enthusiasts, lively happy hours, Detroit-style pizza fans, high-energy group outings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Refreshed Culinary Lineup: Indulge in a newly debuted menu featuring signature Detroit-style pizzas, hand-made desserts, and the fan-favorite "Chix N Stix" wings.
  • Immersive Entertainment: As the dining heart of District E, guests can enjoy live DJ sets, professional esports tournaments, and massive screens broadcasting the biggest games in the DMV.
  • Expert Mixology: The bar is known for its high-impact specialty cocktails and "get-drunk-intentions" drinks, paired with a popular happy hour that offers incredible value on burgers and wings.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Check the schedule for special "Fan Activations" during Washington Wizards home games for exclusive gameday perks and experiences.
  • Take advantage of early happy hour pricing to snag high-top seating before the arena crowds arrive.
