Cafe Milano

Cafe Milano

Category: Italian, Romantic, Upscale

Neighborhood: Georgetown

Price: $$$$

Address: 3251 Prospect St NW, Washington, DC 20007

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A legendary and high-energy Georgetown institution that perfectly captures the "see-and-be-seen" DC power scene within a beautiful, intimate, and star-studded setting.

🍸 Best For

Power lunches, romantic dates, celebrity spotting, upscale celebrations, wine connoisseurs.

🔥 Highlights

  • A Magnet for Icons: Renowned as a premier hotspot for world leaders, diplomats, and celebrities, offering the most elite people-watching in the nation's capital.
  • Exquisite Italian Artistry: Features elevated classics such as stuffed zucchini flowers with cheese, signature veal ravioli, and beautifully presented seafood pastas.
  • Impeccable Service: Boasts a world-class service team known for providing attentive, friendly, and professional hospitality that ensures a memorable experience.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • As one of DC's most popular dining destinations, making a reservation in advance is highly recommended to secure your table.
  • The atmosphere is sophisticated and upscale, making it the perfect venue to dress in your finest attire.
