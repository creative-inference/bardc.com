Copycat Co.

Copycat Co.

Category: Cocktail Bars, Tiki Bars, Late Night

Neighborhood: H Street NE

Price: $$

Address: 1110 H St NE, Washington, DC 20002

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An intimate and high-energy H Street staple where master-craft mixology meets the bold, spicy flavors of authentic Chinese street food in a stylish, music-forward setting.

🍸 Best For

Late-night cocktails, Szechuan street food cravings, tiki drink enthusiasts, intimate date nights, craft beverage education

🔥 Highlights

  • World-Class Mixology: Renowned for an inventive, rotating beverage program that features a unique pie-chart menu and expertly crafted tiki classics like the Shark’s Tooth and Tahitian Rum Punch.
  • Authentic Szechuan Snacks: The kitchen specializes in flavor-packed Chinese street food, including signature chili wontons, triple delight potstickers, and savory lamb noodles that perfectly complement the drink menu.
  • Expert Curation: The highly skilled bartenders are known for their craft, offering personalized recommendations and a curated experience that has made this venue a cornerstone of the H Street corridor.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • To guarantee a seat at this popular and cozy hotspot, reservations are now conveniently available through Resy.
  • Expect a lively, late-night atmosphere that is perfect for those seeking high-quality craft cocktails and substantial small plates well into the evening.
