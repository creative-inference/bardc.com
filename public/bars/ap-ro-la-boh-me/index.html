La Bohème

Category: Supper Clubs, Cocktail Bars, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: Georgetown

Price: EXPENSIVE

Address: 2622 P St NW, Washington, DC 20007, USA

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A chic and intimate "perch up top" that blends sophisticated, refined elegance with a warm, creative energy, perfect for an evolving and inspired culinary journey.

🍸 Best For

Elegant date nights, milestone birthday celebrations, caviar enthusiasts, and adventurous foodies seeking world-class cocktail pairings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Evolving Themed Menus: Recognized as one of the best wine bars in D.C., this venue features a rotating 6-course prix fixe experience with inspired themes ranging from Tim Burton-inspired concepts to deeply flavorful Miyazaki-themed menus.
  • Artisanal Cocktail Pairings: While the wine list is exceptional, the bar team specializes in unique cocktail pairings that offer bold, creative flavors specifically designed to enhance the layers of each dish.
  • Premier Caviar Service: Enjoy a sophisticated happy hour featuring beautiful caviar presentations, fresh oysters, and a premier selection of Champagne in a stylish Georgetown setting.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located on the upper level of the historic row house shared with its award-winning sister restaurant, Apéro; reservations are highly recommended for the tasting menu.
  • Look out for exclusive seasonal events like "Champagne & Stardust" for a festive and upscale nightlife experience.
