The Admiral

The Admiral

Category: Sports Bars, Happy Hour Spots, American

Neighborhood: Dupont Circle

Price: $$

Address: 1 Dupont Circle NW, Washington, DC 20036

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sprawling, nautical-themed retreat in the heart of Dupont Circle that seamlessly blends an elevated sports bar energy with a cozy, communal atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Large group gatherings, post-work happy hours, alumni game watches, breezy outdoor drinks, and corporate receptions.

🔥 Highlights

  • Premier Outdoor Patio: Boasting a massive 200-seat capacity and its own dedicated bar, this sun-drenched space is one of the neighborhood’s top destinations for open-air socializing and people-watching.
  • Tailored Event Spaces: From the private, 18-foot ceiling Bancroft Bar to the vibrant Front Bar overlooking the circle, the venue offers multiple unique settings equipped with high-definition TVs for any occasion.
  • Coastal-Inspired Pub Fare: The menu features elevated East Coast favorites like fresh lobster rolls, crab dip, and highly-rated guacamole that pair perfectly with their extensive craft beer and cocktail selections.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The Admiral is renowned for its stress-free event planning, offering easy group reservations that often come without rental fees or minimum spends.
  • Arrive early to snag a spot during their popular happy hour, which features excellent drink deals and a lively atmosphere for local professionals.

🔄 Weekly Events

Bottomless Brunch 🥂

Sat, Sun

Enjoy ninety minutes of bottomless mimosas, bloodies, Miller Lite, and orange crushes. Guests must purchase a brunch or lunch entree to participate.

Late Night Football 🏈

Mon, Thu, Sun

The Admiral is a go-to spot for game watches and football viewing with multiple HD televisions.

Late Night Vibes 🎶

Fri, Sat

Great music and a fun laid-back atmosphere after dinner and game watch parties wind down.

Happy Hour

Happy hour is available throughout the entire restaurant and the patio.
