Sly Rooftop Bar

Sly Rooftop Bar

Category: Rooftop Bars, Cocktail Bars, Hotel Bars

Neighborhood: NoMa

Price:

Address: 222 M St NE, Washington, DC 20002, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Located atop the Morrow Hotel in NoMa, this sultry rooftop sanctuary blends panoramic U.S. Capitol views with vibrant jewel-toned decor and a high-energy Afrobeat soundtrack.

🍸 Best For

Sunset cocktails, upscale group celebrations, stylish date nights, and global fusion foodies.

🔥 Highlights

  • Signature Samuelsson Menu: Helmed by celebrity chef Marcus Samuelsson, the kitchen serves inventive "African-twist" plates like doro wat empanadas, fresh ceviche with plantain chips, and some of the most celebrated crispy fries in the District.
  • 360-Degree Vistas: The expansive outdoor patio features plush lounge seating and cozy fire pits, offering an unpretentious yet sophisticated setting to take in unobstructed views of the Washington skyline.
  • Bold Mixology: The bar program shines with creative cocktails like the "Electric Sly"—a spicy passion fruit and mezcal margarita—alongside berbere-spiced specialties and a curated sparkling wine selection.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Secure a reservation in advance to snag a coveted patio table for the sunset, and take advantage of the convenient valet parking for a seamless arrival.
  • Visit during the popular weekday happy hour to enjoy discounted fresh oysters and sparkling wine in the heart of the Union Market District.
