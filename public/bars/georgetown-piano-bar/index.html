Georgetown Piano Bar

Georgetown Piano Bar

Category: Piano Bars, Live Music Venues, Cocktail Bars

Neighborhood: Georgetown

Price: $$

Address: 3287 M St NW, Washington, DC 20007

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An energetic, underground singalong lounge featuring a signature red piano and a warm, communal atmosphere perfect for late-night Georgetown revelry.

🍸 Best For

Dueling piano performances, high-octane group singalongs, "Jersey Night" Wednesdays, and celebrating with a lively local crowd.

🔥 Highlights

  • Weekend Dueling Pianos: Every Friday and Saturday night at 9pm, the bar transforms into a high-energy stage for talented dueling pianists who keep the crowd singing until closing.
  • Signature Red Piano: The venue's centerpiece fosters a unique, interactive environment where guests are encouraged to participate in classic singalongs and request their favorite hits.
  • Flexible Dining Policy: While the bar focuses on themed cocktails named after lounge legends, guests enjoy the rare perk of being able to bring in their own food or order delivery from nearby Georgetown favorites.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of Happy Hour specials from 5pm to 7pm, Wednesday through Sunday, before the nightly live music kicks off.
  • Recently highlighted as one of the "Best Things to do in Georgetown," this M Street staple is a popular destination for both local students and live music enthusiasts.
