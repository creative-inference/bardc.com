✨ Vibe

An upscale waterfront sanctuary at The Wharf featuring chic, mid-century modern decor and breathtaking views of the Potomac River.

🍸 Best For

Waterfront Dining, Special Occasions, Date Nights, Steak Lovers, Outdoor Cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Ribeye Tasting Menu: A curated multi-course experience that offers a spectacular value for those looking to sample premium cuts and signature appetizers.
  • Signature Mussels: A must-order dish featuring a mouth-watering white wine, blue cheese, and chipotle cream sauce that keeps locals coming back.
  • Stunning Outdoor Patio: The perfect spot to enjoy the river breeze and vibrant energy of the District Wharf while sipping craft cocktails.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Request a table on the patio for the best water views, especially during sunset.
  • This is a premier destination for celebrations, known for a welcoming staff that goes the extra mile for birthdays and special events.
