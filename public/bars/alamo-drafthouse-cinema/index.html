Alamo Drafthouse Cinema

Alamo Drafthouse Cinema

Category: Themed, Burger Bars, Kid Friendly

Neighborhood: Ivy City

Price: $$

Address: 630 Rhode Island Ave NE, Washington, DC 20002

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A premium, boutique cinematic experience featuring state-of-the-art technology and curated decor that makes every screening feel like a special event.

🍸 Best For

Immersive date nights, themed movie screenings, family outings, and high-quality "dinner and a movie" experiences.

🔥 Highlights

  • State-of-the-art audiovisual technology featuring top-tier projection, crystal-clear sound systems, and luxury reclining seats.
  • Full-service dining delivered directly to your seat, including a diverse menu of craft burgers and their signature bottomless popcorn served with real butter.
  • Unique pre-show entertainment and immersive corridor decorations that are custom-tailored to the specific films being screened.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Patrons can take advantage of four hours of free parking in the adjacent garage or easily access the venue via the nearby Rhode Island Ave Metro station.
  • The theater offers a Season Pass program for frequent moviegoers looking to enjoy regular screenings and special event programming.

Washingtonian Dec 10, 2021

Alamo Drafthouse Cinema Has Finally Come to DC

Alamo Drafthouse Cinema has opened its first District location in Edgewood, featuring nine screens equipped with reclining seats and seat-side dining service. The theater boasts a large-format screen called "The Big Show," a presidential-themed lobby, and a spy-themed bar named Highbinder that serves local beers and boozy milkshakes.
WTOP Dec 10, 2021

DC gets its first Alamo Drafthouse Cinema

Alamo Drafthouse Cinema opened its first District location in the Edgewood neighborhood's Bryant Street development, featuring nine screens and a full-service bar and restaurant. The venue includes 'The Big Show,' a premium large-format auditorium with a 66-foot wide screen, and features a unique DC-themed 'Hall of Presidents' dedicated to actors who have played U.S. presidents.
Boxoffice Pro Dec 9, 2021

Alamo Drafthouse to Open First-Ever Washington D.C. Location on Dec. 10

Alamo Drafthouse Cinema opened its first Washington, D.C. location at Bryant Street, featuring a "Washington, D.C. on the big screen" theme and a premium large format auditorium called The Big Show. The venue includes the spy-themed Highbinder bar and offers signature programming like movie parties, brunch screenings, and genre-specific film series.
The Washington Post Dec 9, 2021

Alamo Drafthouse Cinema comes to Washington, D.C. — at long last

Alamo Drafthouse Cinema opened its first location within the District in December 2021 at the Bryant Street NE development. This hybrid tavern and theater chain serves as a dedicated destination for film enthusiasts, offering full-service food and drinks during movie screenings.
