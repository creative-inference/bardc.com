✨ Vibe

Ama is an intimate and authentic Northern Italian sanctuary in Navy Yard that seamlessly transitions from a sun-drenched café by day to a lively, romantic dining destination by night.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, special occasions, solo dining at the bar, and upscale weekend brunch.

🔥 Highlights

  • Authentic Northern Italian Cuisine: The menu celebrates tradition with standout dishes like the signature cheese-filled focaccia di formaggio, wild mushroom lasagnetta, and fresh pasta imported directly from Italy.
  • Sustainable & Organic Focus: Every dish is crafted with a commitment to integrity, utilizing nourishing, sustainable, and organic ingredients to create bold and balanced flavors.
  • Exceptional Service & Atmosphere: Guests are treated to a transportive experience thanks to a warm, knowledgeable service team and a sophisticated setting that has quickly made it a social media favorite.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Recently honored as one of the best restaurants in D.C. for date nights and special occasions.
  • Features a charming breakfast window offering premium lattes and artisan pastries for those looking for a high-end morning start.

Washington.org

January at Ama with Dr. William Li

Ama is offering a special January menu in collaboration with Dr. William Li, focusing on restorative Northern Italian cuisine and the "food as medicine" movement. The experience, developed alongside Chef Johanna Hellrigl and herbalist Micah Wilder, features nutrient-dense dishes and functional beverages designed to promote wellness and balance.
FOX 5 DC

Ama Italian Recognized for Sustainability and Deliciousness

Ama Italian has earned significant acclaim within its first year, including a nomination for Best New Restaurant at the RAMMYS and winning DC’s 2025 Sustainability Award. The venue was also honored with a Gambero Rosso Award, distinguishing it as one of the top Italian restaurants in the United States.
