✨ Vibe

Known as DC’s oldest sports bar, The Exchange Saloon offers a warm, "Cheers"-style neighborhood atmosphere where historic charm meets a lively, welcoming energy just steps from the White House.

🍸 Best For

Game days, post-sightseeing meals, weekday happy hours, and casual gatherings with a "local favorite" feel.

🔥 Highlights

  • Elite Service & Hospitality: Frequently praised for its "personal touch," the staff and owners go above and beyond to make tourists and regulars alike feel like family.
  • Superior Pub Fare: From "to-die-for" homemade beef chili and mouthwatering Reubens to crispy fish n' chips, the kitchen serves up high-quality comfort food that exceeds typical sports bar expectations.
  • Incredible Rooftop Views: This downtown gem features a rooftop seating area that provides breathtaking, unobstructed views of the Washington Monument and the National Mall.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of the weekday happy hour featuring discounted rail drinks, house wines, and select bottled beers.
  • The venue is famous for its attention to detail, including serving every beer in a fresh, ice-cold chilled glass.

📰 In the News

Washingtonian Apr 19, 2019

The DC Spy Sites You Walk by Every Day

The Exchange Saloon is featured as a historic D.C. espionage site where Czech spies Karl and Hana Koecher once operated. During the 1970s, the couple used the venue's social clubs to establish relationships with CIA officers and government officials for the purpose of gathering intelligence.
