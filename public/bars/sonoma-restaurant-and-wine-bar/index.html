✨ Vibe

Sonoma is a sophisticated yet welcoming Capitol Hill staple that seamlessly blends the elegance of a premier wine bar with the high-energy atmosphere of a political power-broker hub.

🍸 Best For

Political networking, wine flights, artisanal Italian-inspired dinners, after-work drinks, and upscale private events.

🔥 Highlights

  • Washington Insider Hub: Recognized as a premier destination for political networking, this venue is a favorite among lawmakers and frequently hosts high-profile panel discussions and receptions.
  • Chic Event Spaces: The stunning second-floor Avenue Lounge features a two-story fireplace and an airy, elegant design, making it one of the neighborhood's most sought-after spots for fundraisers and celebrations.
  • Sustainable Local Flavors: The menu highlights the best of the Mid-Atlantic with standout dishes like wood-grilled specialties, house-made gnocchi, and signature duck á l’orange, all paired with a dynamic, world-class wine list.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Visit for Happy Hour Monday through Friday from 2:30 PM to 5:30 PM for a curated selection of seasonal beverages.
  • The restaurant is located just steps from the U.S. Capitol and the Library of Congress; reservations are recommended for indoor dining.
