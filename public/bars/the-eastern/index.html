✨ Vibe

The Eastern offers a sophisticated yet relaxed neighborhood atmosphere that makes it a perfect, calming escape for unwinding with a glass of wine.

🍸 Best For

Wine connoisseurs, casual date nights, gourmet burger enthusiasts, and lively group wine tastings.

🔥 Highlights

  • An expertly curated wine program that is playful and approachable for novices while remaining deep and engaging for seasoned experts.
  • The acclaimed "Eastern Burger," frequently cited as one of the best in the city, alongside high-end shareables like the Beastern Board and fresh crudo.
  • A warm and knowledgeable staff that excels at hosting specialized tasting events and providing personalized drink recommendations.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • A top-tier choice for a refined Happy Hour experience in the heart of Barracks Row.
  • Look out for their special themed events, such as their popular wine tasting series.
