✨ Vibe

A sultry and energetic "velvet-smirk" sanctuary where French cocktail chic meets a high-octane modern Chinese dim sum parlor.

🍸 Best For

Daily Happy Hour, Award-Winning Mixology, Late-Night Eats, Trendy Weekend Brunch, Post-Workout Refueling

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Excellence: Recognized as the 2024 RAMMY Award Winner for Best Bar in DC and featured in the prestigious Michelin Dining Guide USA.
  • Culinary Fusion: An evolving menu featuring fan favorites like the $1 happy hour dumplings, "1970s Garlic Noodles," Gyoza de Boeuf, and a decadent Matcha Brûlée.
  • World-Class Bar Program: Expertly crafted French-inspired cocktails and smooth Espresso Martinis served by a staff frequently praised for their "cream of the crop" service.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Happy Hour is hosted daily from 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM, offering a legendary dollar-dumpling deal and 50% off select cocktails.
  • Open late until 1:00 AM on weekdays and 2:00 AM on Saturdays, with a popular weekend brunch served from 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

Bar Chinois remains a popular fixture in Mt. Vernon Triangle, continuing to serve its signature French-inspired cocktails and dumplings from happy hour until late. The team behind the venue recently expanded their D.C. footprint by opening Katsumi, a focused Japanese sushi and hot plate concept in Logan Circle.
PoPville Oct 30, 2025

More Dim-Sum News – Bottomless Dim Sum Brunch at Bar Chinois!!

Bar Chinois offers a bottomless dim sum brunch every Saturday and Sunday from 11 a.m. to 5 p.m. featuring a variety of plates like red chili wontons and French onion beef gyoza. Guests can choose from unlimited dim sum for $37, select bottomless drinks for $23, or a combined package for $57.

DC's Bar Chinois expands to National Landing, offering popular menu and happy hour deals

Bar Chinois has expanded from its original Washington, D.C. location to a second spot in National Landing, Northern Virginia. The new venue features the same popular menu items and happy hour deals that established the brand's reputation in the District.

The Biggest D.C.-Area Restaurant Openings in May

Bar Chinois expanded its D.C.-area presence with a second location in National Landing, Arlington, which opened in May 2025. The 90-seat venue features a turquoise-toned interior and a large outdoor patio, serving the brand's signature French-influenced cocktails and dim sum favorites like black pepper duck, crab Rangoon, and bao buns.
