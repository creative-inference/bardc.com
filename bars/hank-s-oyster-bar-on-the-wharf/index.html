Hank’s Oyster Bar on the Wharf

Hank’s Oyster Bar on the Wharf

Category: Oyster Bars, Seafood Bars, Waterfront

Neighborhood: The Wharf

Price:

Address: 701 Wharf St SW, Washington, DC 20024, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A vibrant New England-style seafood destination offering a relaxed, coastal energy and stunning views from its expansive waterfront patio at The Wharf.

🍸 Best For

Waterfront dates, sunset happy hours, fresh raw bar towers, and late-night seafood cravings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Signature Seafood: From the massive "Grand Plateau" towers to their famous lobster rolls and unique Hog Island-style BBQ oysters, the menu celebrates premium coastal flavors.
  • Scenic Waterfront Seating: Enjoy the Potomac River breeze on a 70-seat outdoor patio or in the cozy enclosed patio, making it a premier spot for people-watching at The Wharf.
  • Celebrity-Approved Excellence: Recognized as a top women-owned destination by chef Jamie Leeds, this local staple has famously hosted high-profile guests including Barack and Michelle Obama.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Oyster Happy Hour features discounted drinks and oysters Monday through Friday from 4pm to 6pm at the bar.
  • For a quick bite, check out the walk-up carry-out window serving boardwalk-inspired snacks like lobster rolls and frozen slushies.
