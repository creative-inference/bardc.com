✨ Vibe

Spark Social House is a vibrant and inclusive LGBTQ+ "third place" that seamlessly transitions from a sun-drenched, work-friendly cafe by day to a spirited, community-focused social hub by night.

🍸 Best For

Remote work sessions, inclusive LGBTQ+ gatherings, creative mocktails, low-sensory nightlife, and relaxing on a hidden garden patio.

🔥 Highlights

  • Innovative "1-for-1" Drink Program: Following its exciting transition to a full liquor license, the venue features a unique menu where every signature cocktail is available as a high-quality non-alcoholic version.
  • Versatile Multi-Level Space: Explore a cozy coffeehouse with a record-listening lounge upstairs, a bustling bar downstairs, and a gorgeous, peaceful outdoor patio.
  • Community-Driven Programming: The venue hosts a diverse array of events including drag trivia, live DJs, art shows, and interactive community projects like the "Heart Strings" storytelling installation.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The space is exceptionally remote-work friendly during the day, offering reliable Wi-Fi, numerous power outlets, and a welcoming, low-judgment atmosphere.
  • Designed for inclusivity, the venue offers an 18+ social environment and sensory-friendly options, making it a rare nightlife destination that caters to the sober-curious and LGBTQ+ families alike.
