✨ Vibe

ilili DC offers a breathtaking "enchanted courtyard" experience, blending lush citrus trees, hanging greenery, and a trickling stone fountain within a sun-drenched glass space that transports guests to the heart of old Beirut.

🍸 Best For

Romantic dates, milestone celebrations, sophisticated group brunches, pre-show dinners near The Anthem, and upscale waterfront dining.

🔥 Highlights

  • Recently recognized as one of the city's best destinations for special occasions, the interior features stunning artisanal details like white dove sculptures and hanging steel bird cages.
  • The refined Lebanese menu excels in flavor and presentation, featuring must-try signature dishes such as Brussels sprouts with fig jam, duck shawarma, and the decadent "candy bar" dessert.
  • An inventive cocktail program highlights unique Arak-based creations and a curated selection of premium wines sourced directly from Lebanon’s renowned Bekaa Valley.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly encouraged for this high-demand Wharf hotspot, which is conveniently located directly above the District Square underground parking.
  • The venue offers versatile private event spaces and full-service catering for everything from intimate dinners to large-scale corporate gatherings.
