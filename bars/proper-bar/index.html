✨ Vibe

Recently opened in Mount Vernon Triangle, Proper Bar is a sophisticated, mid-century-inspired supper club that blends "grown and sexy" Miami energy with an elegant, gold-and-green-accented aesthetic featuring a stunning 50-foot marble bar and live piano music.

🍸 Best For

Celebratory birthday dinners, upscale girls' nights out, romantic date nights, and anyone seeking a polished cocktail lounge experience with a live soundtrack.

🔥 Highlights

  • Elevated Comfort Fare: Indulge in high-low luxury with signature dishes like the crispy chicken nuggets topped with caviar, the dry-aged tomahawk steak, and the fan-favorite Proper Burger.
  • Artisanal Beverage Program: Enjoy a sophisticated craft cocktail experience featuring tableside martini service and the refreshing, fan-favorite "Hermes" cocktail.
  • Supper Club Atmosphere: The venue offers a glittering glimpse into the past with white tablecloth service, plush velvet booths, and nightly live piano performances that create a 10/10 atmosphere.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • An elegant dress code is enforced to maintain the sophisticated vibe (no hats or beanies permitted inside).
  • Visit during the weekday happy hour from 5 to 7 p.m. for $9 forced carbonated cocktails, $10 house wines, and $7 draft beers.
