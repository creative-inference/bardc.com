Dog Haus Biergarten Dupont (Washington DC)

Dog Haus Biergarten Dupont (Washington DC)

Category: Beer Gardens, Gastropubs, Happy Hour Spots

Price:

✨ Vibe

A high-energy, modern "craft casual" biergarten that combines the warmth of a cozy neighborhood pub with a lively, social atmosphere perfect for a night out in Dupont.

🍸 Best For

Happy Hour with the crew, gourmet comfort food cravings, craft beer enthusiasts, and casual weekend dinner dates.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Gourmet Menu: Savor signature all-beef Haus Dogs, handcrafted sausages, and Black Angus beef burgers, all served on their famous grilled King’s Hawaiian rolls.
  • Premier Beverage Program: Explore a rotating selection of 24 local and national craft beers on tap, or enjoy signature cocktails designed by celebrity mixologist Phil Wills.
  • Prime Dupont Location: This recently opened 3,200-square-foot venue features a sleek, modern interior and a vibrant outdoor patio situated at the bustling corner of 18th and M Streets.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue features a tech-forward kiosk and mobile ordering system designed for a quick and seamless "craft casual" dining experience.
  • Located just a short walk from the DuPont Circle Metro station, it is an ideal and accessible gathering spot for groups traveling from across the District.
