✨ Vibe

Mpire is a classy and high-energy upscale club in Dupont Circle that pairs a beautiful, modern interior with a welcoming and sophisticated atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Upscale celebrations, out-of-town visitors, high-energy stage performances, and premium late-night cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • Exceptional Talent: Known for phenomenal and engaging dancers who keep the energy high with spectacular performances throughout the night.
  • Top-Tier Service: A dedicated and professional staff—including standout bartenders and welcoming security—ensures every guest feels like a VIP.
  • Pristine Atmosphere: The venue boasts a clean, beautiful, and upscale design that feels luxurious while remaining genuinely friendly and unpretentious.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Consistency is key here, with a full rotation of dancers and active energy even on traditionally slower nights like Mondays.
  • The club features a solid menu of top-notch food and drinks, served with speed and professionalism by a highly-rated bar team.
