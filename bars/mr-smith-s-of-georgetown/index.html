Mr. Smith's of Georgetown

✨ Vibe

A charmingly historic neighborhood saloon with "faded Victorian elegance," offering a cozy and welcoming atmosphere that has made it a Georgetown staple for nearly 60 years.

🍸 Best For

Interactive piano sing-alongs, historic espionage tours, lively group brunches, first dates, and classic American comfort food.

🔥 Highlights

  • Sing-Along Piano Bar: Known as the "friendliest saloon in town," the venue features a signature vintage piano and lively karaoke nights every Friday and Saturday that create a high-energy, communal atmosphere.
  • Historic Espionage Landmark: Steeped in Cold War history, this landmark establishment sits on the legendary site of a former spy meeting spot, making it a key destination for local history and espionage enthusiasts.
  • Legendary Comfort Food: The menu features fan favorites like "must-try" crispy chicken tenders, massive shareable nacho platters, and juicy, delicious burgers that keep locals coming back monthly.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue offers a 24/7 happy hour with $5 cocktail specials and a legendary bottomless brunch every Saturday and Sunday from 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM.
  • Reservations are encouraged for large groups, though the dress code remains relaxed and casual for all guests.
