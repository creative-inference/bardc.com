Off the Record

Category: Hotel Bar, Historic Bar, Lounge

Price: $$$$

Address: 800 16th St NW, Washington, DC 20006

Located in the basement of the luxurious Hay-Adams hotel, Off the Record is a quintessential D.C. power bar. Known for its political caricatures, plush red decor, and clubby atmosphere, it's an iconic spot for quiet, high-powered cocktails just steps from the White House.
