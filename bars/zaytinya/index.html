Zaytinya

Zaytinya

Category: Mediterranean, Happy Hour Spots, Groups

Neighborhood: Gallery Place / Chinatown

Price: $$$

Address: 701 9th St NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A bright, airy, and high-energy Mediterranean oasis that feels both stylishly modern and warmly inviting, featuring soaring ceilings and sun-drenched interiors in the heart of the city.

🍸 Best For

Large group celebrations, pre-game dining near Capital One Arena, lively social happy hours, and sophisticated Mediterranean tapas.

🔥 Highlights

  • Chef José Andrés’ Signature Mezze: A masterfully crafted menu featuring legendary crispy Brussels sprouts, rich bone marrow kibbeh, and fresh, authentic spreads like hummus topped with pomegranate seeds.
  • Stunning Architecture: The multi-level space is a design marvel, boasting floor-to-ceiling windows, a striking metal sculpture entrance, and a chic upstairs dining area that overlooks the bustling main room.
  • Prime Al Fresco Dining: The beautiful outdoor patio offers a perfect vantage point for people-watching across from the National Portrait Gallery, making it one of the most sought-after spots for open-air dining in DC.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended as this remains one of DC’s most popular hotspots, particularly during events at the nearby arena.
  • To experience the full breadth of the menu, consider the Chef’s Tasting Menu, which offers a curated journey through Turkish, Greek, and Lebanese flavors.
