Zaytinya

Category: Mediterranean, Greek, Turkish

Price: $$$

Address: 701 9th St NW, Washington, DC 20001

Another José Andrés classic, Zaytinya offers a stunning, light-filled space for exploring the flavors of the Mediterranean. The menu features a vast selection of Greek, Turkish, and Lebanese mezze (small plates), complemented by a unique list of Mediterranean wines, making it a perennially popular spot for group dining.
