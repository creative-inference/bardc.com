Bossa Bistro + Lounge

Category: Live Music Venue, Lounge, Brazilian

Price: $$

Address: 2463 18th St NW, Washington, DC 20009

A vibrant spot in Adams Morgan, Bossa Bistro + Lounge is known for its live music, featuring everything from Brazilian jazz and salsa to reggae and rock. It offers a menu of Brazilian-inspired cuisine and a lively, intimate atmosphere for dancing and dining.
