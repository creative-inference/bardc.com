Wet Dog Tavern

Category: Taverns, Neighborhood Bars, Dog Friendly

Neighborhood: U Street

Price:

Address: 2100 Vermont Ave NW, Washington, DC 20001, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A welcoming neighborhood tavern with a cool, industrial-chic atmosphere and a spacious, laid-back outdoor patio perfect for social gatherings.

🍸 Best For

Dog lovers, group hangouts, craft beer enthusiasts, happy hour, summer patio vibes, watching sports.

🔥 Highlights

  • Expansive outdoor patio featuring a massive wall projection for sports, corn hole games, and a wrap-around bar that services guests both inside and out.
  • An impressive craft beer selection with a wide variety of IPAs, stouts, and ciders, alongside a "next level" on-site food truck serving delicious tacos.
  • A unique, dog-friendly environment highlighted by a must-see canine-themed mural inspired by Michelangelo's "The Creation of Adam."

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of their excellent happy hour deals and the flexible policy that allows you to bring in your own food to enjoy with friends.
  • The venue is exceptionally welcoming to four-legged friends, making it a top choice for pet owners in the U Street area.
