Perry's

Perry's

Category: Rooftop Bars, Sushi Bars, Japanese

Neighborhood: Adams Morgan

Price: $$$

Address: 1811 Columbia Rd NW, Washington, DC 20009

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An iconic and high-energy Adams Morgan staple where chic modern design meets a lively, year-round rooftop social scene.

🍸 Best For

Rooftop cocktails, award-winning Japanese comfort food, legendary drag brunches, and authentic weekend breakfasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • James Beard-Winning Cuisine: Savor a menu curated by 2024 Emerging Chef winner Masako Morishita, featuring sentimental Japanese family recipes like handmade gyoza and innovative dishes like udon carbonara.
  • Legendary Drag Brunch: Experience a D.C. institution every Sunday with high-octane performances and an all-you-can-eat buffet blending American classics with Japanese treats.
  • Stunning Rooftop Bar: Take in the neighborhood views from the popular rooftop, offering a first-come-first-served bar area perfect for happy hour sake and craft cocktails.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • This is a certified local hot spot, so be sure to book your reservations well in advance—especially for the highly sought-after authentic Japanese breakfast and Sunday brunch.
  • A 20% service charge is added to all checks to directly support the dedicated front-of-house team, ensuring a seamless and friendly service experience.
