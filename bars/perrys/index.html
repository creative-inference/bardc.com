Perry's

Category: Japanese, Sushi, Rooftop Bar

Price: $$$

Address: 1811 Columbia Rd NW, Washington, DC 20009

A long-standing Adams Morgan establishment, Perry's is known for its traditional Japanese cuisine and popular rooftop deck. The rooftop offers city views, a vibrant bar scene, and is a go-to spot for cocktails and sushi in the warmer months. It also hosts a popular Sunday drag brunch.
