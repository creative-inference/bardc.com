Awakening Bar & Grill

Recently making its debut on Barracks Row, Awakening Bar & Grill is a sensational addition to 8th Street, offering a soulful Southern-themed menu by Chef Brittany Davis in a space that perfectly balances sleek design with cozy neighborhood warmth.

✨ Vibe

This convivial gem strikes the perfect balance between a sophisticated cocktail lounge and an inviting neighborhood retreat, featuring local historical decor and an energetic atmosphere often fueled by live jazz.

🍸 Best For

Birthday celebrations, date nights, craft cocktail enthusiasts, live music lovers, and large group gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Elevated Southern Comfort: The kitchen delivers standouts like the blackened salmon with creole sauce, crispy fried green tomatoes with Old Bay dipping sauce, and savory Gouda burger sliders.
  • Expertly Crafted Cocktails: The bar program shines with intentional and balanced creations such as the signature "Metro Mule" and the expertly mixed "Mayor 4 Life."
  • Soulful Entertainment: From the sound of live saxophone performances to the warm, personal welcomes from the owners, the venue offers a high-energy yet intimate experience.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue accepts reservations for both intimate dinners and large parties, making it a premier choice for celebratory group outings.
  • Keep an eye on their schedule for live music sets that perfectly complement the venue's "sing-worthy" Southern cuisine.

Bottomless Mimosa Weekends 🥂

Sat, Sun

Indulge in 2 hours of bottomless classic or flavored mimosas (including flavors like Cherry Blossom, Georgetown Sapphire, and Gibbs Triple Crown) with your brunch.

Soulful Weekend Brunch 🎷

Sat, Sun

Experience a soulful DC brunch tradition with a live local saxophonist. Enjoy classic comfort food with a DC twist, including an omelette station, chicken and waffles, and homemade biscuits.

Happy Hour

Features $4 select draft beers, $8 house mixed drinks, and $2 off all specialty cocktails and bar bites. Food specials include $5 crispy green beans, $7 candied bacon deviled eggs, and $10 Mambo wings (4).
