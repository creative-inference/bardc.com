✨ Vibe

A sun-drenched and vibrant Cuban escape at the heart of The Wharf, blending the energy of a bustling Havana café with the breezy, colorful sophistication of a waterfront cocktail bar.

🍸 Best For

Cuban coffee, waterfront brunch, tropical cocktails, quick bites, afternoon happy hour, social gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Authentic Cuban Flavors: Indulge in highly-praised classics like the signature Cubano, savory Mojo Pork sandwiches, and crispy empanadas that guests rank among the best in the region.
  • Exceptional Coffee Culture: Experience "how Cuba does coffee" with rich, smooth Café con Leche and indulgent lattes finished with a signature dash of dulce de leche.
  • Premier Waterfront Setting: Enjoy a "fine casual" atmosphere featuring a large, welcoming space with plenty of indoor and outdoor seating perfect for soaking in the lively energy of Pearl Street.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • For a seamless visit, take advantage of the convenient QR code system to order and pay for your food and drinks directly from your table.
  • Keep an eye out for seasonal celebrations and community events, as this local favorite is known for its festive spirit and partnerships during Hispanic Heritage Month.

Happy Hour

Cuban-style Happy Hour featuring cocktails, wine, beer, and bites for $8, including mojitos and non-alcoholic options.
