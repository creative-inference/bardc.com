✨ Vibe

Black Whiskey is a high-energy, second-floor sanctuary in Logan Circle that blends the soulful feel of a classic whiskey bar with a vibrant, festive social scene.

🍸 Best For

Whiskey aficionados, festive holiday pop-ups, late-night cocktails, and competitive pool games with friends.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Miracle Pop-Up: Currently hosting the world-famous holiday takeover through December 31st, featuring over-the-top kitschy decor and seasonal hits like the Christmapolitan and gingerbread-scented Old Fashioneds.
  • Expert Craftsmanship: Known for a massive, well-stocked whiskey list and perfectly executed classics, including a highly-recommended Jack Rose and traditional Old Fashioned.
  • Spacious Entertainment: The large, open layout features a dedicated pool table area and live DJs, making it an ideal destination for groups looking for a lively atmosphere.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Look for the entrance at 1410 14th St NW and head up the stairs to find this elevated hidden gem.
  • A true late-night destination, the bar stays open until 1:45 AM on weekdays and 2:45 AM on Fridays and Saturdays.
