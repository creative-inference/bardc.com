Ivy City Smokehouse

Ivy City Smokehouse

Category:

Neighborhood: Ivy City

Price: MODERATE

Address: 1356 Okie St NE, Washington, DC 20002, USA

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A high-energy, industrial-chic destination that masterfully blends a vibrant upstairs tavern and open-air deck with a world-class artisanal seafood market.

🍸 Best For

Live music lovers, rooftop cocktails, large group celebrations, weekend brunch, and seafood enthusiasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Smoked Seafood: As a Michelin Bib Gourmand recipient, the Smokehouse is renowned for its on-site smoking process, featuring signature "candy" salmon, smoked trout, and some of the city's finest bagels and lox.
  • Live Entertainment & Great Energy: The venue is a hub for nightlife in Ivy City, hosting a diverse range of live bands, DJ sets, and cultural events like Latin Rock celebrations in its spacious "Great Room."
  • The Deck & Tavern Experience: The upstairs space offers a seamless indoor-outdoor atmosphere with a lively bar, perfect for enjoying a Strawberry Hennessy Margarita while soaking in the neighborhood's energetic playlist.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended for large groups, and be sure to check the online calendar for upcoming live music tickets and special event schedules.
  • Don't miss the downstairs retail market before you leave to grab their famous smoked fish platters and fresh catches to go.
