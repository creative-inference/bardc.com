Union Stage

Category: Live Music Venue, Taproom, Pizza

Price: $$

Address: 740 Water St SW, Washington, DC 20024

A popular live music venue at The Wharf, Union Stage features a main concert hall for national and local acts and a smaller taproom for more intimate performances. The taproom serves pizza and a wide selection of craft beers, making it a versatile destination for a full night out.
