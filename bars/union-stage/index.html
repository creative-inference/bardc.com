Union Stage

Union Stage

Category: Live Music Venues, Dive Bars, Basements

Neighborhood: The Wharf

Price: $$

Address: 740 Water St SW, Washington, DC 20024

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Union Stage delivers an intimate, "cool basement" aesthetic with a dive-bar soul, featuring projected retro visuals and a welcoming atmosphere that puts fans right at the edge of the stage.

🍸 Best For

Indie music lovers, family-friendly all-ages concerts, themed dance parties (like Emo Night and K-Pop), up-and-coming touring acts, and intimate comedy sets.

🔥 Highlights

  • Exceptional Intimacy: The low stage and lack of barricades create a high-energy, immersive environment where fans can enjoy an up-close and personal connection with their favorite performers.
  • Diverse Entertainment: The venue boasts a versatile calendar ranging from bluegrass festivals and hip-hop tours to Broadway raves and kids’ matinee concerts.
  • Welcoming Atmosphere: Known for its incredibly friendly and helpful staff, the venue provides a professional yet laid-back experience that is perfect for first-time concertgoers and seasoned fans alike.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Check the event schedule for age requirements, as the venue hosts a mix of all-ages shows and 18+/21+ late-night events.
  • Arrive early to snag a spot near the stage, or consider upgrading to VIP for dedicated seating options.
