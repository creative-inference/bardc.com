Smoke & Mirrors

Smoke & Mirrors

Category: Rooftop Bars, Cocktail Bars, Lounges

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price: $$$

Address: 800 E St SE, Washington, DC 20003

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated and vibrant 11th-floor sanctuary that blends sleek, Navy-inspired design with breathtaking, unobstructed panoramic views of the U.S. Capitol and the D.C. skyline.

🍸 Best For

Sunset cocktails, upscale date nights, celebratory group outings, and impressive private events.

🔥 Highlights

  • Unmatched National Vistas: Perched atop the AC Hotel, this venue offers a one-of-a-kind birds-eye view of the U.S. Capitol and the Library of Congress, providing the ultimate backdrop for photos.
  • All-Weather Rooftop Luxury: The expansive outdoor terrace is a year-round destination featuring a retractable canopy, cozy fire pits, and heaters that ensure a comfortable experience in any season.
  • Elevated Craft Program: The menu features high-end American fare by Executive Chef Raymond Melendez—like gold-leaf-topped spicy tuna—paired with a creative cocktail list including smoky Old Fashioneds and the signature Dragon’s Kiss.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended to secure a table with full waiter service and prime views of the city.
  • The venue features a live DJ and a high-energy indoor bar area, making it a top choice for those seeking a lively, upscale nightlife atmosphere.
