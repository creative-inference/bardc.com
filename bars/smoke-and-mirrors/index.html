Smoke & Mirrors

Category: Rooftop Bar, Lounge, American

Price: $$$

Address: 800 E St SE, Washington, DC 20003

Perched atop the AC Hotel in Capitol Hill, Smoke & Mirrors offers breathtaking, panoramic views of the U.S. Capitol and the surrounding cityscape. This stylish rooftop bar and restaurant provides a sophisticated setting for enjoying craft cocktails and modern American cuisine.
