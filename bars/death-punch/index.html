Death Punch

Death Punch

Category:

Neighborhood: Adams Morgan

Price: MODERATE

Address: 2321 18th St NW, Washington, DC 20009, USA

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A high-energy, grunge-styled izakaya that seamlessly blends competitive gaming with a vibrant dance floor, all recently refreshed under new ownership to elevate its unique multi-floor experience.

🍸 Best For

Immersive holiday pop-ups, high-value happy hours, group billiard sessions, and late-night DJ sets.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Miracle Pop-Up: Recognized as one of D.C.’s premier festive destinations, the bar transforms into a stunning holiday wonderland featuring elaborate decorations and creative seasonal cocktails in whimsical glassware.
  • Multi-Floor Entertainment: The venue features a dedicated middle-floor Billiard Room with pool and ping pong, plus a spacious 150-capacity top floor perfect for live music and dancing.
  • Izakaya-Inspired Fare: Guests can enjoy a flavorful menu of fresh ramen and savory bar snacks like Wagyu sliders, perfectly paired with an extensive drink list.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Check out the legendary $20 open bar happy hour, offered twice daily, for incredible value on rail drinks, shots, and select beers.
  • While street parking is limited in Adams Morgan, a paid parking garage is conveniently located nearby at 2421 18th Street NW.
