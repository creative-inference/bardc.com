The Little Grand

The Little Grand

Category: Pizza Bars, Cocktail Bars, Neighborhood Bars

Neighborhood: H Street NE

Price:

Address: 808 7th St NE, Washington, DC 20002, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Tucked away in a charming, well-lit alley off H Street, this "hidden gem" offers a cozy and intimate neighborhood atmosphere where industrial-chic meets a warm, welcoming energy.

🍸 Best For

Casual date nights, neighborhood hangouts, artisanal pizza enthusiasts, Monday happy hours, and craft cocktail lovers.

🔥 Highlights

  • Signature Sourdough Crust: Renowned for their flavorful dough, the kitchen serves up incredible round and square sourdough pizzas, with the pepperoni and unique potato pizzas topping the list of fan favorites.
  • Expertly Crafted Cocktails: The beverage program shines with perfectly balanced classic cocktails and a selective wine list designed to pair seamlessly with their "10/10" bar meatballs and fresh ricotta.
  • Exceptional Hospitality: Living up to their "warm people" motto, the service is famously attentive and accommodating, creating a community-focused vibe that makes every guest feel like a local.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Don't miss "Monday Funday" featuring BOGOHO (Buy One Get One Half Off) pizzas and half-off draft beers and wines by the glass from 5:00 PM to 6:30 PM.
  • Reservations are highly recommended for the intimate indoor seating, though the venue also features a seasonal patio and convenient takeout options.
