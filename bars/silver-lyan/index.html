Silver Lyan

Silver Lyan

Category: Speakeasies, Cocktail Bars, Hotel Bars

Neighborhood: Penn Quarter

Price: $$$

Address: 900 F St NW, Washington, DC 20004

Website
View on Google Maps →

Ranked as one of North America’s 50 Best Bars for 2025, Silver Lyan is a world-class subterranean cocktail lounge from the legendary Ryan "Mr. Lyan" Chetiyawardana, currently captivating the city with its ambitious and inventive "Butterfly Effect" menu.

✨ Vibe

Nestled in a former bank vault beneath the Riggs Washington DC, this upscale speakeasy offers a mysterious and intimate atmosphere characterized by mid-century decor, cozy table lamps, and a sophisticated, secluded energy.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, avant-garde cocktail enthusiasts, upscale pre-dinner aperitifs, and showing off DC's elite nightlife scene to visitors.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Pedigree: Recognized as a top-ranked destination on the "North America’s 50 Best Bars" list, ensuring a premier experience led by a highly knowledgeable and attentive service team.
  • The "Butterfly Effect" Menu: A groundbreaking selection of 12 complex cocktails, featuring imaginative creations like the electrocuted "It’s Alive! 75" and the playful "Pikachu Pop."
  • Elevated Late-Night Bites: A curated menu of sophisticated snacks that pair perfectly with the drinks, including fan favorites like everything pretzel bites, halloumi fries, and crispy fried chicken.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • While walk-ins are welcome via a first-come, first-served waitlist, making a reservation online is highly recommended to secure a spot in the vault.
  • All guests must be 21+ with a valid ID after 7:00 PM.
