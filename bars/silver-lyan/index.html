Located in the historic bank vault of the Riggs Washington DC hotel, Silver Lyan is a subterranean cocktail bar from the internationally renowned bartender Ryan Chetiyawardana (Mr. Lyan). The bar offers a unique menu that reimagines classic cocktails, blending history, culture, and culinary artistry in a stunning, intimate setting.
