✨ Vibe

Gemini offers a chic, European-inspired "tachinomi" (standing room) atmosphere that blends the energy of a curated natural wine shop with an intimate, high-end Italian dining experience.

🍸 Best For

Natural wine enthusiasts, artisanal pasta lovers, gourmet pizza dates, and fans of unique craft ice cream.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Pedigree: Ranked as the #4 "Very Best Restaurant" in D.C. and a consistent fixture on the Resy Hit List for its exceptional Italian-inspired cuisine.
  • Masterful Handmade Pasta & Pizza: Renowned for "air pillow" gnocchi, seasonal ferretti with corn carbonara, and signature sourdough pizza slices.
  • Curated Wine & Treats: Features an unparalleled selection of natural wines led by expert staff, alongside a seasonal sidewalk cart serving inventive artisanal ice creams like olive oil with cocoa crumble.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy an authentic standing-room-only experience at the indoor counters for pasta service, or opt for casual alfresco dining at the outdoor picnic tables.
  • This venue operates as a hybrid wine shop and dinner destination, making it perfect for both a quick gourmet carryout or a vibrant, social evening on-site.

📰 In the News

Food & Wine

Johnny Monis

Gemini — Wine Shop + Dinner (Gemini x Happy Ice Cream) is a casual Washington D.C. concept from Chef Johnny Monis that functions as a wine shop, tapas bar, and pizza carryout. The venue emphasizes simple pleasures like house-made pasta, curated wines, and creative ice cream while maintaining the chef's signature attention to detail.
