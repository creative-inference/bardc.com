Canopy Bar by Hilton

Canopy Bar by Hilton

Category: Hotel Bars, Waterfront, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: The Wharf

Price:

Address: 975 7th St SW, Washington, DC 20024, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated waterfront retreat featuring maritime-inspired design, wood-barn accents, and sweeping panoramic views of the Washington Channel.

🍸 Best For

Pre-show drinks near The Anthem, sunset date nights, waterfront happy hours, and budget-friendly elevated dining.

🔥 Highlights

  • Stellar Waterfront Views: Enjoy prime outdoor seating overlooking the marina and the Potomac River, providing an Instagrammable backdrop for watching boats and the sunset.
  • Exceptional Value: Known for surprisingly affordable pricing in The Wharf, featuring remarkably priced draft beers and high-quality favorites like birria fries, specialty pizzas, and hand-breaded tenders.
  • Personalized Service: The staff is celebrated for their attentive, "eager to please" attitude, often going the extra mile to ensure a memorable experience for hotel guests and locals alike.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Conveniently located on the second level of the Hilton, it is the perfect "go-to" destination for a high-quality meal before or after a concert at The Anthem.
  • For a more elevated experience, visit the hotel's sister rooftop bar, Whiskey Charlie, which features a newly revamped terrace with a retractable roof and monument views.
