✨ Vibe

Rocket Bar is a subterranean, space-themed sanctuary that blends a classic dive bar feel with a high-energy social atmosphere, perfect for escaping the city's hustle and bustle.

🍸 Best For

Pre-game drinks for Capital One Arena events, competitive game nights with friends, budget-friendly happy hours, and casual late-night rock vibes.

🔥 Highlights

  • A Gamer’s Paradise: This expansive venue is packed with entertainment, including multiple pool tables, shuffleboard, darts, Skee-ball, and nostalgic arcade classics like NBA Jam and bubble hockey.
  • The Ultimate Sports Hub: Conveniently located directly across from the arena, it is the premier destination for Capitals and Wizards fans to gather for a "Rocket Fuel" lager before or after the game.
  • Subterranean Social Space: With three full bars and a curated jukebox, this basement haunt offers plenty of room to spread out and enjoy a laid-back, "anything goes" atmosphere.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of the daily Happy Hour until 8 PM featuring $6 rail drinks, wines, and signature Rocket Fuel Lagers.
  • While the bar focuses on drinks and games, they have a friendly "bring your own food" policy, allowing you to carry in snacks from any of the nearby Chinatown eateries.
