✨ Vibe

A vibrant, community-centric outdoor oasis in Navy Yard that blends D.C.’s rich Go-Go culture with a modern, industrial-chic shipping container aesthetic.

🍸 Best For

Spiked cold-pressed juices, sunny happy hours, outdoor DJ sets, group meetups, and supporting premier Black-owned businesses.

🔥 Highlights

  • Innovative Cold-Pressed Cocktails: Famous for their refreshing "next level" spiked juices made with Turning Natural blends, offering a unique and flavorful twist on traditional patio drinks.
  • Award-Winning Cultural Hub: Founded by Ian Callender, a "Washingtonian of the Year," the venue is a celebrated destination for local entrepreneurship and soulful D.C. spirit.
  • Urban Tailgate Atmosphere: Featuring a dedicated DJ booth and shipping container bars, the space serves up fantastic BBQ chicken platters and snacks in a spacious, open-air layout.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Tech-Savvy Ordering: Enjoy a seamless experience with easy mobile ordering directly from your phone for no-contact service.
  • Commuter Friendly: The venue is highly accessible for cyclists and walkers, making it a perfect pit stop or meeting point in the heart of the city.

A New Sandlot Will Come to Anacostia This Spring

Sandlot Southwest was the inaugural location of the Sandlot brand, established in 2019 as an outdoor bar and event space in the Buzzard Point area of Washington, D.C. It operated as an interim cultural hub at the site of the upcoming Verge development, setting the precedent for the brand's model of activating vacant lots slated for future construction.
