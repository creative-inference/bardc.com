✨ Vibe

La Famosa is a warm, colorful, and soulful "love letter" to Puerto Rico, blending vibrant salsa rhythms with a welcoming all-day energy that brings authentic island spirit to the heart of Navy Yard.

🍸 Best For

Authentic Boricua cuisine, rum-focused tropical cocktails, casual weekend brunch, and lively outdoor dining.

🔥 Highlights

  • Chef Joancarlo Parkhurst’s menu features celebrated island staples like the hand-smashed Mofongo and the signature Tripleta sandwich, both recognized for excellence in Tom Sietsema’s Spring Dining Guide.
  • The bar program is a tropical escape, specializing in "on point" Piña Coladas, refreshing Guava Smashes, and a wide selection of traditional Puerto Rican rums.
  • The venue offers a versatile experience, featuring a walk-up coffee counter for morning pastries like quesitos and a spacious wrap-around patio for breezy afternoon Sangrias.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Visit for Happy Hour between 3 PM and 6 PM on weekdays, or stop by for a festive weekend brunch served every Saturday and Sunday from 11 AM to 3 PM.
  • The restaurant utilizes a convenient contactless ordering system via table barcodes, making it easy to enjoy a fast-casual meal in a vibrant, sit-down atmosphere.
