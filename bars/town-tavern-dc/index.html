✨ Vibe

Town Tavern DC is a high-energy Adams Morgan staple that seamlessly blends the welcoming, "where everybody knows your name" feel of a neighborhood pub with the electric atmosphere of a premier sports and dance hub.

🍸 Best For

SF Bay Area sports fans, impromptu karaoke, milestone birthday parties, extended happy hours, and high-energy weekend dancing.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Ultimate Bay Area Sports HQ: Recognized as the go-to destination in DC for Cal and Stanford alumni, as well as fans of the 49ers, Giants, and Warriors to catch every game on massive HDTVs.
  • Legendary Party Atmosphere: Between the DJs spinning late-night "bangers" and the staff passing out microphones for spontaneous karaoke sessions, the venue is famous for turning strangers into backup dancers.
  • Premier Event Hosting: Renowned for exceptional hospitality and "gracious" service, making it a top choice for booking surprise parties and group fundraisers with customized support from the staff.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy one of the city's best extended happy hours Monday through Friday from 5:00 PM to 9:00 PM, featuring $4 domestic bottles, $5 seltzers, and $6 wines.
  • The kitchen stays open late to satisfy cravings, serving until 10:30 PM Sunday-Thursday and 11:30 PM on Friday and Saturday nights.

🤝 Philanthropy Fridays

Fri

Sat, Sun
