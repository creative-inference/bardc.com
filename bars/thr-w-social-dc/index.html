✨ Vibe

THRōW Social DC offers a vibrant, high-energy tropical oasis in Ivy City, blending a fresh tiki aesthetic with an expansive, multi-level playground perfect for social butterflies.

🍸 Best For

Birthday celebrations, live Go-Go music, large group outings, weekend brunch, interactive game nights, and premium bottle service.

🔥 Highlights

  • Dynamic live entertainment featuring local Go-Go bands and a high-fidelity sound system that keeps the dance floor packed and the energy electric.
  • A playful social atmosphere filled with interactive entertainment, including oversized games like Jenga and Connect 4 alongside multiple full-service bars.
  • Versatile indoor and outdoor spaces, including a scenic rooftop, fresh-air patio, and spacious VIP cabanas that can comfortably accommodate parties of up to 20 guests.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • For a premium experience, pre-book a VIP tent or interior cabana to enjoy dedicated seating and bottle service right in the heart of the action.
  • Weekend brunch is a must-try, featuring crowd favorites like chicken and waffles paired with shared carafes of Bellinis and Mimosas.
