✨ Vibe

Kiki is an electric, multi-level LGBTQ+ sanctuary in the heart of the U Street corridor, known for its high-energy dance floors and inclusive, community-driven atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Late-night dancing, drag performances, post-game drinks with sports leagues, and socializing on a spacious outdoor patio.

🔥 Highlights

  • Dynamic Multi-Level Layout: Explore multiple floors of entertainment, including a massive outdoor patio and a dedicated upstairs dance club spinning popular Top 40 remixes.
  • Community Hub: A proud supporter of local organizations like the DC Gay Flag Football League, making it the go-to spot for team celebrations and neighborhood gatherings.
  • Vibrant Drag Scene: Home to regular drag shows that are widely considered a must-see hit for both locals and visitors.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Expansion News: The owner is currently expanding Kiki’s footprint into the neighboring space (formerly Shakers), promising even more room for private events and LGBTQ+ nightlife.
  • Inclusive Environment: While a popular destination for the gay community, the venue is celebrated for being welcoming and friendly to trans, non-binary, and female guests.
