Larry's Lounge

Larry's Lounge

Category: LGBTQ+ Bars, Neighborhood Bars, Lounges

Neighborhood: Adams Morgan

Price: $$

Address: 1836 18th St NW, Washington, DC 20009

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A welcoming, unpretentious neighborhood fixture known for its warm, community-driven atmosphere and laid-back "come as you are" energy.

🍸 Best For

Post-brunch cocktails, strong pours, casual dates with your dog, and meeting friendly locals in an inclusive environment.

🔥 Highlights

  • Spacious, dog-friendly patio perfect for relaxing outdoors with your four-legged friends.
  • A cozy bi-level layout featuring board and card games for a fun, interactive evening.
  • Generous pours and standout favorites like their famously strong and flavorful rum punch.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of their value-driven happy hour deals on classic pub food and drinks.
  • The venue is highly regarded for its personal touch, with owner "Mr. Larry" often on hand to greet patrons.
