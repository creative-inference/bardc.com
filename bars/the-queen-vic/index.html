✨ Vibe

The Queen Vic is a warm and authentic British enclave on H Street, featuring classic wood paneling, soccer scarves, and a vibrant community atmosphere that makes every guest feel like family.

🍸 Best For

Soccer match-days, traditional Sunday roasts, authentic British comfort food, and hearty weekend brunches.

🔥 Highlights

  • Premier Football Destination: As a hub for soccer enthusiasts, the pub offers an electric, high-energy environment for cheering on your favorite clubs alongside fellow fans.
  • Authentic British Fare: From crispy fish and chips with mushy peas to a full English breakfast served with HP sauce, the menu delivers a true taste of the UK in the heart of DC.
  • Exceptional Hospitality: Known for its friendly staff and welcoming ownership, the venue prides itself on personalized service that makes both locals and visitors feel right at home.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy daily Happy Hour from 5pm to 7pm with specials on all drafts, rail drinks, and small plates.
  • The Queen Vic is a cashless establishment, so please plan to use a card or digital payment method for all food and drinks.
