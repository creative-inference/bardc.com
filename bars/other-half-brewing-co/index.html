Other Half Brewing Co.

Other Half Brewing Co.

Category: Breweries, Taproom, Arcade Bars

Neighborhood: Ivy City

Price: $$

Address: 1401 Okie St NE, Washington, DC 20002

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sprawling, high-energy industrial-chic destination that perfectly blends a trendy urban atmosphere with a relaxed, welcoming spirit for beer lovers and families alike.

🍸 Best For

Hazy IPA enthusiasts, large group hangouts, weekend family outings, and stocking up on premium craft cans.

🔥 Highlights

  • Acclaimed Craft Selection: Home to some of DC’s highest-rated brews, including the 4.3-rated "Heroic Dose" Imperial IPA, the taproom features 20 rotating draft lines ranging from world-class hazy IPAs to complex barrel-aged stouts and crisp rice lagers.
  • Massive Indoor/Outdoor Footprint: Housed in a stunning 22,000-square-foot former factory, the venue boasts a sun-drenched taproom and a massive 7,500-square-foot covered outdoor patio and roof deck perfect for catching a game on the TVs.
  • Entertainment for Everyone: More than just a brewery, the space features a fun arcade area for kids (and kids at heart), a dedicated coffee shop, and a curated selection of "Annuelle" wines for those looking for variety beyond hops.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Be sure to visit the massive retail section to pick up their famous four-packs and unique stickers before you head out.
  • The venue is exceptionally family and dog-friendly, making it a go-to Ivy City spot for a relaxed afternoon with the whole crew.
