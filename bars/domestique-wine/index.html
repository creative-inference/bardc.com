✨ Vibe

A bright, light-filled NoMa sanctuary that demystifies the world of viticulture through a community-driven, inclusive, and educational lens.

🍸 Best For

Natural wine aficionados, sourcing unique dinner party bottles, educational tastings, and eco-conscious gifting.

🔥 Highlights

  • Acclaimed National Selection: Recognized as one of the top wine shops in America by Food & Wine and Wine Enthusiast, featuring a curated collection of organic and biodynamic labels sourced directly from over 100 artisanal producers.
  • Expert Personal Consultation: Whether you are a wine club member or a first-time visitor, the knowledgeable staff provides personalized guidance to help you find "cellar gems" or curate the perfect selection for weddings and special events.
  • Exclusive & Rare Finds: Leveraging unique local liquor laws, the shop offers custom cuvées and niche labels from non-traditional regions like Texas and Colorado that are rarely available in other U.S. markets.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Stop by every Sunday for free in-store tastings to discover your next favorite bottle.
  • Stock up and save with a 10% discount on full cases of wine available all day, every day.

Wine Should Be Accessible to Everyone

Domestique Wine is a natural-wine-focused shop in Washington, D.C., dedicated to making the wine retail experience more inclusive and accessible. The shop emphasizes sustainable production and fair labor practices while fostering diversity in the industry through initiatives like the Major Taylor Fellowship for people of color.
