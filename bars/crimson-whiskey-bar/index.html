Crimson Whiskey Bar

Crimson Whiskey Bar

Category: Whiskey Bars, Speakeasies, Hotel Bars

Neighborhood: Gallery Place / Chinatown

Price: $$

Address: 627 H St NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A moody and stylish subterranean speakeasy, this hidden gem features 15-foot ceilings and taxidermy-adorned walls that create a warm, intimate escape from the bustling Chinatown streets.

🍸 Best For

Bourbon aficionados, pre-game cocktails for Capital One Arena events, romantic date nights, and cozy group hangouts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Recognized by The Bourbon Review as one of "America’s Best Bourbon Bars 2024," boasting an elite collection of approximately 150 varieties of American spirits.
  • Expertly crafted whiskey-centric cocktails, including signature house-made Manhattans and Old Fashioneds that locals consider a neighborhood gold standard.
  • A spacious, underground layout that offers a relaxed atmosphere even on busy nights, making it a perfect retreat for those seeking high-quality service and conversation.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located in the basement level of the Motto by Hilton Washington DC City Center.
  • Features a highly-regarded happy hour that is perfect for sampling their extensive whiskey program.
