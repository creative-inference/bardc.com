✨ Vibe

A sophisticated and welcoming spirits sanctuary nestled within The Roost, where expert bartenders elevate cocktail-making into a true craft.

🍸 Best For

Whiskey connoisseurs, adventurous cocktail lovers, and casual dates looking for high-end drinks in a vibrant food hall setting.

🔥 Highlights

  • World-Class Spirits Program: Led by spirits director Nick Farrell, the bar features an impressive collection of 175 whiskeys and a unique, news-making menu dedicated to artisanal vermouth and house-made liqueurs.
  • Elevated Bar Dining: Guests can enjoy "top drawer" cocktails while ordering directly from The Roost’s diverse culinary partners, with the pizza and ribs frequently cited as the perfect pairing.
  • Creative Craftsmanship: From floral margaritas to refreshing lemon highballs, every drink is meticulously prepared by a staff celebrated for their exceptional service and attention to detail.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located inside The Roost food hall in Navy Yard, offering the flexibility to enjoy a high-end bar experience with various food options nearby.
  • Don't miss their specialized Spanish-style vermouth service and signature Vermouth Cobbler for a unique D.C. tasting experience.
