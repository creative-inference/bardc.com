TallBoy

TallBoy

Category: Neighborhood Bars, Beer Gardens, Dive Bars

Neighborhood: Logan Circle

Price: $

Address: 1239 9th St NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A laid-back and welcoming neighborhood haunt where a diverse crowd and high-energy events create a vibrant, "live sitcom" atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Award-worthy wings, budget-friendly beers, late-night comfort food, and lively neighborhood game nights.

🔥 Highlights

  • Far-Out Chicken Wings: Highly acclaimed wings featuring standout flavors like lemon pepper, Memphis hot dry rub, and KBBQ.
  • Rotating TallBoy Cans: An impressive and ever-changing selection of 16-ounce canned beers that encourages patrons to discover new favorites.
  • Signature Sandwiches: Hearty house-cured pastrami and the specialty "Tom and Cheese" grilled cheese, which supports local non-profits through its proceeds.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Look out for their high-energy Bingo nights for a hilarious, interactive experience led by charismatic local hosts.
  • Features a streamlined "speed lane" ordering system designed for quick service and a wallet-friendly, fast-casual bar experience.
