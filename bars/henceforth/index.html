✨ Vibe

Housed in an iconic three-level space on H Street, this "Best New Brewery of 2025" winner offers a sophisticated, midcentury modern retreat that trades the typical warehouse aesthetic for a swanky, conversation-friendly lounge atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Craft beer enthusiasts, group gatherings, upscale date nights, wine lovers, and neighborhood community building.

🔥 Highlights

  • Multi-Level Experience: Each of the three floors offers a unique feel, ranging from a polished first-floor brewpub and a sleek second-floor wine bar to a spacious rooftop patio perfect for sunny afternoons.
  • Diverse Beverage Program: Beyond house-brewed staples like IPAs and stouts, the tap list features rare styles like grodziskie and English pale ales, complemented by an impressive, curated natural wine list.
  • Elevated Gastropub Fare: The menu goes far beyond standard bar snacks, offering refined dishes such as pan-seared halibut, braised beef cheek sandwiches, and their signature luxury hot dog topped with caviar and crème fraîche.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located right next to the historic Atlas Theatre, it serves as a perfect "third place" for the community with an atmosphere designed to inspire connection and the exchange of ideas.
  • Be sure to save room for dessert, particularly the highly-acclaimed orange brûlée, which has quickly become a local favorite.

New & Noteworthy: H Street Gets a Tank to Tap Brewery

Henceforth, is a mid-century modern tank-to-tap brewery and pub located in the former H Street Country Club space in Northeast DC. The venue features a ten-barrel system producing traditional beer styles directly for onsite consumption, complemented by a dedicated wine bar and a full food menu from Chef Walfer Hernandez.

Henceforth Named One of the Nation’s 26 “Best New Breweries”

Henceforth, located on H Street NE, has been named one of the nation's 26 best new breweries for 2025 by VinePair. The lounge-like venue offers an elevated brewpub experience with a diverse selection of craft beers, cocktails, and a food menu featuring sharable snacks and entrees.
