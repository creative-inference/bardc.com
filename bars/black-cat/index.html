✨ Vibe

A legendary U Street staple that blends a storied rock-and-roll legacy with a welcoming, dark-hued alternative energy perfect for fans of all generations.

🍸 Best For

Live independent music, surprise pop-up shows, punk and rock fans, all-ages concert experiences, dive bar enthusiasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Rock Royalty Pedigree: Co-owned by Dave Grohl, this storied venue continues to make history, recently hosting an intimate, high-profile surprise pop-up show by the Foo Fighters.
  • The Red Room Bar: A laid-back, no-cover hangout featuring pinball, darts, and a legendary jukebox paired with an excellent selection of craft brews, bourbon, and scotch.
  • Professional & Welcoming: Renowned for its top-tier sound system and exceptionally friendly staff, the venue provides a safe and exciting atmosphere for both first-time concertgoers and seasoned "old rockers."

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located just three blocks from the U Street/Cardozo Metro Station; always bring a valid government-issued ID if you plan to enjoy the bar, as the venue strictly enforces alcohol policies.
  • The venue offers vegan-friendly food options and typically features a mix of high-energy standing room and side seating for a comfortable viewing experience.

DCist

The Black Cat Is Closing Its Downstairs Space

Black Cat consolidated its operations by closing its downstairs Red Room bar and Backstage music space to focus on its upstairs venue. The shift was designed to adapt to rising labor costs and changing neighborhood dynamics on 14th Street NW while preserving the iconic club's independent spirit.
