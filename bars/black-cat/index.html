A legendary fixture in D.C.'s music scene since 1993, the Black Cat is a major venue for independent and alternative music. Located in the Shaw/U Street neighborhood, it features the Mainstage for larger acts, a smaller concert room, and the Red Room Bar for a more casual experience.
