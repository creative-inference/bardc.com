Mission Dupont Circle

Mission Dupont Circle

Category: Taco Bars, Mexican, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: Dupont Circle

Price:

Address: 1606 20th St NW, Washington, DC 20009, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A high-energy, picturesque Mexican hub featuring a festive multi-level loft space and a twinkling outdoor patio just steps from the Dupont Circle Metro.

🍸 Best For

Large group celebrations, lively bottomless brunches, award-winning happy hours, and private events with no venue fees.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Refreshments: Recognized by Eater DC and Washingtonian as one of the city's premier happy hour spots, Mission is famous for its potent margaritas, pitcher deals, and $5 Taco Tuesday specials.
  • Epic Bottomless Brunch: A local staple featuring a 90-minute flow of mimosas, bloody marys, and margaritas paired with standout dishes like chorizo-loaded waffle fries and crispy churros.
  • Premier Group Hosting: Celebrated for being one of the few DC venues to welcome large groups with no extra venue charges, making it a top choice for rehearsal dinners and birthday celebrations in their dedicated loft space.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue is exceptionally dog-friendly on the patio and offers a seamless online portal for booking private events and mid-size group reservations.
  • Check out their seasonal and holiday specials, as they are a go-to destination for National Margarita Day and local "Liquid Lunch" events.

🔄 Weekly Events

🌮 Taco Tuesday

Special menu featuring a variety of tacos and tequila selections.

🥂 Weekend Bottomless Brunch

Sat, Sun

Includes ninety minutes of mission margaritas, mixed rail drinks, mimosas, miller lites and bloody marys for $27.99. A food item must be purchased to participate (chips don't count).

🎧 Late Night DJs

Fri, Sat

Late night celebrations featuring a DJ booth and dance floor in the Brick Room area.

Happy Hour

Happy hour 4-6pm daily including all night on Mondays, plus late night happy hour Thursday through Saturday.
