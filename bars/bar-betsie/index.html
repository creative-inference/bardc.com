✨ Vibe

A moody, 1960s cabaret-inspired lounge featuring vibrant red neon lighting, theatrical velvet booths, and a swanky, nostalgic party atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Romantic dates, creative cocktails, late-night bites, LGBTQ+ outings, and stylish group gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Expert Mixology: Created by the award-winning team behind Jane Jane, the cocktail program features inventive house originals like the spicy, tequila-based "Devil in a Red Dress" and the rye-forward "Silent Star."
  • Nostalgic Comfort Food: The menu offers a playful selection of "finger foods" including buffalo chicken dip, Lil’ Smokies, and their signature complimentary cheese balls.
  • Acclaimed Atmosphere: Recently recognized by Resy as one of the top spots in D.C., the venue blends a "divey-chic" aesthetic with high-end service and a vibrant, inclusive energy.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • As one of the most anticipated openings in the Union Market district, it is an ideal destination for both pre-dinner cocktails and late-night socializing.
  • The venue is LGBTQ+-owned and operated by a seasoned team of D.C. hospitality veterans known for creating warm, community-focused spaces.

Happy Hour

Happy hour occurs Monday through Thursday, though specific drink or food discounts are not explicitly detailed in the text.
