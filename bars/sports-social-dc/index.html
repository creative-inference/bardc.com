✨ Vibe

A high-energy, tech-forward sports sanctuary in the heart of U Street featuring massive LED screens, a lively social atmosphere, and a premier FanDuel sportsbook experience.

🍸 Best For

Game day watch parties, sports betting, Upstate NY comfort food, late-night cocktails, and group happy hours.

🔥 Highlights

  • Unrivaled Viewing Experience: Don't miss a second of the action with a massive 25-foot high-definition LED media wall and over 30 additional TVs surrounding the venue.
  • "Upstate FTW" Culinary Collaboration: Enjoy a specialized menu from the award-winning chefs behind Chiko and Anju, featuring authentic Rochester-style "garbage plates," Buffalo wings, and beef on weck.
  • Signature Social Drinks: Sip on fan-favorite "Crushes," frozen margaritas, or the Spicy Watermelon Cooler while enjoying a full-service bar with top-tier hospitality.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of the weekday Happy Hour from 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM for half-off domestic beers, house cocktails, and signature snacks like the giant pretzel with beer cheese.
  • The venue features dog-friendly outdoor sidewalk seating, making it a perfect spot to enjoy the U Street energy with your pup.
