✨ Vibe

Now fully renovated and returning to its high-energy sports bar roots, Penn Social stands as Penn Quarter’s ultimate 13,000-square-foot playground for sports, games, and live events.

🍸 Best For

Epic game-watch parties on the big screen, high-energy group outings, competitive arcade nights, and unique community events like Profs & Pints and Story District.

🔥 Highlights

  • The venue features the massive Main Hall, boasting a 22-foot movie screen and professional concert-grade Martin Audio for an unparalleled sports viewing and live music experience.
  • An extensive entertainment floor is packed with social favorites, including skee-ball, hoops, foosball, and organized corn hole leagues.
  • Little Penn provides a specialized space featuring authentic Detroit-style pizza, seasonal cocktails, and an expanded selection of over 30 predominately local craft beers.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Every Friday night, the venue hosts "Firehouse Fridays," featuring the largest dance floor in the DMV for country line dancing lessons and open floor sessions.
  • Check the calendar for regular community programming, including intellectually stimulating "Profs & Pints" lectures on Monday and Tuesday evenings.

Firehouse Fridays

Penn Social is the new host for Firehouse Fridays, a weekly country dancing event featuring line dancing, two-step, and swing lessons followed by open dancing. The venue, which recently underwent renovations to offer the largest dance floor in the DMV area, hosts these sessions every Friday night from January 2 through April 24, 2026.
