Website

✨ Vibe

Nanny O’Brien’s is the quintessential neighborhood Irish pub that serves as the warm, unpretentious heartbeat of Cleveland Park, blending cozy "watering hole" energy with authentic cultural heritage.

🍸 Best For

Watching NFL & NY Giants games, Wing Wednesday, competitive trivia nights, live traditional music, and relaxed neighborhood socializing.

🔥 Highlights

  • Standout Pub Fare: Renowned for serving some of the best wings in the city—particularly their meaty BBQ wings—alongside a storied "Wing Wednesday" tradition that has made the venue a local landmark.
  • Top-Tier Entertainment: Recognized as one of DC’s premier spots for trivia enthusiasts with weekly sessions hosted by Trivia Kings, plus a vibrant schedule of live music every Saturday.
  • Ultimate Sports Hub: A dedicated destination for sports fans featuring Direct TV for all NFL needs, serving as a primary home for NY Giants supporters and a lively gathering spot for Big Ten alumni.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of the Daily Happy Hour offering $1 off draft beers, wines, and rail liquors in a location just one block south of the Cleveland Park metro.
  • The pub maintains a welcoming, casual atmosphere with late-night hours, staying open until 2 AM or 3 AM every night of the week.
