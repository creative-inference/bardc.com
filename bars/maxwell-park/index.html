Maxwell Park

Maxwell Park

Category: Wine Bars, Happy Hour Spots, Romantic

Neighborhood: Shaw

Price:

Address: 1336 9th St NW, Washington, DC 20001, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An approachable, high-energy wine sanctuary that blends expert sommelier curation with a nostalgic, neighborhood feel powered by old-school hip-hop and cozy outdoor fire pits.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, expanding your wine knowledge, casual celebrations with friends, wine connoisseurs and beginners alike.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Excellence: Recently named one of the Top 10 wine bars in the U.S. by USA TODAY 10Best, this Shaw staple is led by renowned sommelier Brent Kroll and recently celebrated its eighth anniversary.
  • Ever-Evolving Menus: The wine list rotates monthly with playful and creative themes, ranging from "UNESCO Heritage Vacation" to pop-culture pairings, ensuring there is always something new to discover.
  • Unique Culinary Pairings: Beyond the grapes, the kitchen serves up inventive and delicious comfort food like artisanal "crunch wrap supremes," dumplings, and gourmet grilled cheese designed to pair perfectly with your pour.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Seating is "old school" style and handled on a first-come, first-served basis as the venue does not take reservations.
  • Check their calendar for monthly wine classes and private tasting opportunities to help expand your palate in a fun, low-pressure environment.
