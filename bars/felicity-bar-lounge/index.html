✨ Vibe

Felicity Lounge captures the authentic, welcoming energy of a local "hole-in-the-wall" gem, blending a cozy neighborhood atmosphere with high-energy global beats and Southern-style hospitality.

🍸 Best For

Sunday day parties, catching up with friends, upbeat happy hours, and late-night dancing.

🔥 Highlights

  • The signature Cognac Wings are a fan-favorite menu item that provides the perfect savory pairing for their cocktail selection.
  • Dynamic DJ sets bring a curated mix of global vibes and high-energy music that keeps the dance floor moving.
  • The bar service is exceptionally attentive and knowledgeable, making it easy to find your new favorite drink in a friendly environment.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Don't miss their popular Sunday sessions, which are highly regarded for their vibrant day-party atmosphere.
  • Located in the heart of the H Street Corridor, it is an ideal destination for those seeking an authentic D.C. nightlife experience.
