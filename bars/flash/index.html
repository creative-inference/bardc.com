Flash

Flash

Category: Nightclub, Dance Clubs, House Music

Neighborhood: U Street

Price: $$

Address: 645 Florida Ave NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A premier multi-level electronic music sanctuary on U Street, dedicated to high-fidelity sound and an immersive, dance-focused atmosphere where the music always takes center stage.

🍸 Best For

House and techno purists, late-night dancing, catching international DJ talent, open-air rooftop socializing.

🔥 Highlights

  • Elite Audio Experience: The venue features a high-performance Funktion-One sound system, specifically tuned to provide a crisp and powerful auditory journey for electronic music fans.
  • Dynamic Multi-Floor Layout: Guests can move between multiple levels, including a high-energy main room and an inviting top-floor rooftop space that offers plenty of room to breathe and dance.
  • Pure Dancefloor Energy: To maintain an authentic club vibe, Flash discourages cell phone use on the dancefloor, ensuring everyone stays present and connected to the rhythm and the artist.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Flash is strictly 21+ and encourages creative attire; while there is no strict dress code, please avoid suits, sports jerseys, or flip-flops to ensure entry.
  • The front box office is cash-only (ATMs are available on-site), but credit cards are welcomed at the bars once you are inside the venue.
