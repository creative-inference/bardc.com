A haven for electronic music enthusiasts in Shaw, Flash is a multi-level nightclub dedicated to high-quality sound and intimate dance experiences. It features a main floor with a state-of-the-art sound system, a more relaxed ground-floor bar (Flash Bar), and a rooftop space (Flashy) for open-air dancing.
