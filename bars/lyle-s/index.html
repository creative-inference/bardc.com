✨ Vibe

Lyle’s offers a sophisticated yet relaxed "upscale-meets-casual" atmosphere in the heart of Dupont Circle, blending sleek modern decor with a warm, welcoming energy perfect for social gatherings.

🍸 Best For

Chic weekend brunches, Instagram-worthy cocktails, stylish date nights, and lively group celebrations.

🔥 Highlights

  • Exceptional Culinary Artistry: Chef Jan and his team curate a menu of beautifully displayed flavor combinations, featuring standout dishes like succulent NY strip, fresh oysters, and indulgent short rib hash.
  • Lively Social Scene: The space hums with energy thanks to curated DJ sets and a popular bottomless mimosa brunch that makes it a premier destination for weekend revelry.
  • Family-Friendly Holiday Spirit: Recently featured for its "Holiday Adventure" package, the venue goes above and beyond with thoughtful touches like in-room teepees and seasonal treats for those looking for a festive getaway.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue is exceptionally accommodating to dietary restrictions and allergies, ensuring a seamless dining experience for all guests.
  • With a spacious layout and large tables, it is an ideal choice for hosting parties of six to eight people.
