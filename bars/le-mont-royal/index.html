Le Mont Royal

Le Mont Royal

Category: Discos, French, Nightclub

Neighborhood: Adams Morgan

Price:

Address: 1815 Adams Mill Rd NW, Washington, DC 20009, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A maximalist French-Canadian "disco-bistro" that blends high-fidelity vinyl beats with neon-soaked, purple-hued decor for the ultimate Adams Morgan party atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Late-night dancing, oyster happy hours, eclectic group celebrations, and vinyl enthusiasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Unrivaled Sound System: Widely regarded as having one of the best custom Hi-Fi setups in DC, the venue features DJs spinning an eclectic mix of funk, jazz, and disco on vinyl every night of the week.
  • Elevated "Highbrow/Lowbrow" Fare: The menu creatively transforms Montreal comfort food into gourmet delicacies, featuring highlights like foie gras poutine, fresh Prince Edward Island oysters, and the "damn good" signature burger.
  • Innovative Beverage Program: Guests can enjoy a curated selection of draft cocktails, natural wines, and unique spirits, including specialized pop-ups like the jalapeño-infused Chacho Outpost.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Visit during their daily happy hour for fantastic deals on half-off oysters and $10 draft cocktails (4:00 PM – 6:30 PM weekdays; 3:00 PM – 7:00 PM weekends).
  • Be sure to experience this neighborhood gem before their three-year anniversary celebration on January 4, 2026.
