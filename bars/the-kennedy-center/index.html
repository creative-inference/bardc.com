The Kennedy Center

The Kennedy Center

Category: Concert Halls, Lounges, Rooftop Bars

Neighborhood: Foggy Bottom

Price: $$$

Address: 2700 F St NW, Washington, DC 20566

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A grand and sophisticated cultural landmark that blends breathtaking modern architecture with a prestigious, world-class atmosphere overlooking the Potomac River.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, Broadway musical enthusiasts, National Symphony Orchestra performances, and sunset cocktails with panoramic city views.

🔥 Highlights

  • The iconic Hall of Nations, which offers a majestic and photogenic welcome with its soaring ceilings and international flag displays.
  • World-class acoustics and production quality in premier spaces like the Opera House, providing an immersive experience for every guest.
  • An incredible legacy and aesthetic, featuring friendly staff and volunteers who help bring the history of this national treasure to life.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Purchase tickets directly through the venue’s website for a seamless experience and the best selection of seating for upcoming shows.
  • Arrive early to enjoy the rooftop bar and lounge areas, which offer some of the most stunning views in all of Washington, DC.

The Kennedy Center is facing public criticism from renowned composer Stephen Schwartz, who announced he will no longer host a scheduled gala at the venue. This decision marks a significant rift between the institution and the Tony Award-winning artist over the center's recent management choices.

Kennedy Center’s turbulent year ends with canceled New Year’s Eve shows and record-low ratings

The Kennedy Center concluded a turbulent year marked by the cancellation of its New Year’s Eve performances and record-low ratings for its signature events.
