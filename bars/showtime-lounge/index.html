Showtime Lounge

Showtime Lounge

Category: Dive Bar

Price: $

Address: 506 V St NW, Washington, DC 20001

★★★★★ 4.5 (385 reviews)

A beloved, no-frills dive bar in Bloomingdale, Showtime Lounge is famous for its vintage soul and funk jukebox and its incredibly welcoming atmosphere. It's a small, intimate spot where the focus is on good music, cheap drinks, and friendly conversation, making it one of D.C.'s most authentic neighborhood bars.
