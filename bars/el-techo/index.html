El Techo

El Techo

Category: Rooftop Bars, Latin, Taco Bars

Neighborhood: U Street

Price: $$

Address: 606 Florida Ave NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A vibrant, tropical "Latin Jungle" rooftop oasis featuring lush greenery, a retractable ceiling for year-round celebrations, and high-energy DJ sets that bring a Tulum-inspired energy to the heart of the city.

🍸 Best For

Bottomless weekend brunch, festive group holiday parties, Taco Tuesday deals, and late-night cocktails with a view.

🔥 Highlights

  • Year-Round Rooftop Escape: Known for its "Selva" winter transformation, the venue features a weatherproof enclosure and lush wisteria, ensuring the tropical atmosphere remains hot regardless of the season.
  • Elevated Mexican Cuisine: From "to die for" carne asada bowls and mini chimis to slow-cooked lamb birria tacos, the menu focuses on bold flavors and house-made corn tortillas.
  • Premium Tequila Program: Recently celebrating its seventh anniversary with major specials, the bar boasts an extensive tequila and mezcal selection alongside signature handcrafted margaritas in flavors like mango and watermelon.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of Happy Hour from Wednesday to Friday (5:00 PM – 7:00 PM) and Saturday (4:00 PM – 6:00 PM), or visit on Tuesday for the $25 taco and drink pairing.
  • Reservations are highly recommended for the popular bottomless brunch and large group celebrations, as the rooftop fills up quickly.

El Techo — Bar Review

El Techo offers a Tulum-inspired atmosphere in Washington D.C., featuring a retractable roof, lush greenery, and high-energy club music. The venue specializes in house-made tacos and a variety of margaritas, including signature eight-serving skull bowls and passion fruit options. Spotlight: El Techo

El Techo is a tropical "treehouse" bar and restaurant in DC’s Shaw neighborhood that offers an Ibiza-inspired atmosphere with hanging plants and high ceilings. The menu features affordable, house-made Mexican favorites like specialty tacos and signature cocktails, including guava Collins and spicy mezcalitas. Originally a lively music venue, it has evolved into a family-friendly destination while maintaining its airy, island-style vibe.
