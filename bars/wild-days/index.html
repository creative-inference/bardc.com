✨ Vibe

Wild Days is a chic and cozy rooftop sanctuary atop the Eaton Hotel, blending breathtaking city views with a laid-back, music-forward atmosphere perfect for any urban explorer.

🍸 Best For

Sunset cocktails, panoramic rooftop views, vibrant DJ sets, and stylish group gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Stunning Skyline Views: Perched high above downtown, the venue features a beautiful indoor-outdoor layout that showcases incredible vistas of the Washington DC skyline.
  • Curated Music Scene: The lounge is a hub for high-energy ambiance, featuring regular DJ sets and public events that keep the energy high well into the night.
  • Top-Tier Hospitality: Guests can expect exceptional service from a fast, friendly, and attentive bar team dedicated to crafting a memorable nightlife experience.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue offers extended weekend hours, staying open until 2:00 AM on Fridays and Saturdays for late-night socializing.
  • For large celebrations or parties of 25 guests or more, it is recommended to contact their events team to secure semi-private or private accommodations.

Wild Days — Bar Review

Wild Days is an artsy rooftop oasis at Eaton DC that features a lush, garden-like atmosphere complete with a glass-enclosed indoor space and a fire pit. The bar serves creative cocktails and local craft beers alongside a playful menu of tacos, nachos, and campfire-style s'mores kits. It remains a popular downtown destination for networking, live DJ nights, and rooftop film screenings.
