✨ Vibe

Surveyor offers a sophisticated nautical-inspired "supper club" atmosphere that beautifully honors the maritime and shipbuilding history of the Navy Yard.

🍸 Best For

Upscale hotel breakfasts, nautical-themed cocktails, Southern-inspired brunch, and pre-concert dining near Nationals Park.

🔥 Highlights

  • Signature Coastal Flavors: Under the leadership of Executive Chef Brian Hatfield, the menu features standout dishes like the flavorful "Shrooms" vegetarian entrée and perfectly prepared French toast loaded with fresh fruit.
  • Nautical Knot Cocktails: Enjoy a unique and creative beverage program where every expertly crafted cocktail is named after a different nautical knot.
  • Sophisticated Interior: Located within the Thompson Washington D.C., the venue provides a polished, maritime-themed setting that is both stylish and welcoming for guests and locals alike.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Securing a reservation is recommended to enjoy a seamless seating experience, particularly during the popular weekend brunch hours.
  • Look out for the Southern-influenced American breakfast classics, which are highly praised for their fresh ingredients and generous portions.
