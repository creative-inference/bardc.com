District Still & Supply

Category: Cocktail Bars, Speakeasies, Basements

Neighborhood: Downtown

Price:

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

This sophisticated underground speakeasy blends industrial-chic elements with lush green velvet decor and a striking iron spiral staircase to create an intimate, "hidden gem" atmosphere in the heart of downtown.

🍸 Best For

Intimate date nights, pre-dinner cocktails, catching up with close friends, and spirit enthusiasts seeking a quiet retreat.

🔥 Highlights

  • Expert Mixology: Highly skilled bartenders craft elevated classics with unique twists, such as Negronis featuring barrel-aged gin and perfectly balanced Oaxacan Old Fashioneds.
  • Stunning Design: The cellar space captures an authentic old-school speakeasy feel with its unique industrial-meets-luxury aesthetic and hidden-door charm.
  • Exclusive Atmosphere: Tucked away from the downtown bustle, this bar offers a peaceful and private setting perfect for meaningful conversation and premium spirit tasting.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located in the cellar of 918 F St NW, this exclusive spot is open Thursday through Saturday evenings.
  • Join their "Inner Circle" mailing list for first access to limited-run spirits, secret menus, and upcoming events like Cocktail Week.
