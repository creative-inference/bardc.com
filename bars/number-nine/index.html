Number Nine

Number Nine

Category: LGBTQ+ Bars, Gay Bars, Lounges

Neighborhood: Logan Circle

Price: $$

Address: 1435 P St NW, Washington, DC 20005

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated and stylish two-story LGBTQ+ lounge in Logan Circle that perfectly balances an upscale "see and be seen" atmosphere with a warm, welcoming energy.

🍸 Best For

Two-for-one cocktails, sophisticated socializing, music video lovers, and prime people-watching on the patio.

🔥 Highlights

  • Exceptional Service: The upbeat bartending crew is renowned for their incredible memory and attentive service, ensuring every guest feels like a regular.
  • Dual-Level Experience: Guests can enjoy the classy, dark-wood paneled lounge on the main floor or head upstairs to the spacious music video bar for a high-energy social scene.
  • Chic Interior Design: The venue features a polished aesthetic with leather banquettes and mirrors, making it one of the most stylish destinations for a night out in DC.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Don't miss the legendary daily happy hour featuring a buy-one-get-one-free deal from 7:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m.
  • Limited outdoor seating is available at the front, offering a perfect vantage point to enjoy the vibrant Logan Circle neighborhood.
