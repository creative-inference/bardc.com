Scarlet Oak Restaurant & Bar

Scarlet Oak Restaurant & Bar

Category: Sports Bars, Happy Hour Spots, American

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price:

Address: 909 New Jersey Ave SE, Washington, DC 20003, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A vibrant and modern Navy Yard social hub that blends sleek, high-energy interiors with a breezy, neighborhood-favorite patio atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Budget-friendly cocktail crawls, game day pre-gaming near Nationals Park, expansive outdoor dining, and lively group celebrations.

🔥 Highlights

  • Trendsetting Happy Hour: Recently featured for its "dirt cheap" $7 martinis, the bar offers exceptional value with $6 local craft beers and $8 artisanal cocktails like the Montengroni and Scarlet Sangria.
  • Premier Outdoor Patios: Recognized as one of the best spots in DC for open-air dining, the venue boasts two spacious patios that offer a perfect view of the bustling Navy Yard neighborhood.
  • Detroit-Style Pizza: Through a unique partnership with Side Door Pizza, guests can enjoy authentic, thick-crust Detroit-style pies, including the fan-favorite Maryland Crab pizza.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Happy Hour runs Monday through Friday from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM; look out for half-off bottles of wine on Wednesdays.
  • Brunch is served daily from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM, featuring popular items like the Passionfruit Crepe Soufflé and the Earlybird Burrito.
