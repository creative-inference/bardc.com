Medina

Medina

Category: Cocktail Bars, Mediterranean, Lounges

Neighborhood: U Street

Price:

Address: 1328 Florida Ave NW, Washington, DC 20009, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An intimate and transportive Moroccan-inspired oasis that pairs a cozy, tented-roof atmosphere with plush couch seating and a nostalgic R&B soundtrack.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, cozy rainy-day cocktails, exploring Mediterranean wines, and sophisticated post-dinner nightcaps.

🔥 Highlights

  • Immersive Mediterranean Ambiance: Designed as the "little sister" to the acclaimed Maydān, this lounge offers a beautiful, "more than meets the eye" experience featuring communal couches and stunning North African-inspired decor.
  • Curated Wine & Spirits: The beverage program shines with a unique wine list inspired by the islands of the Mediterranean—including rare Lebanese pairings—and multisensory craft cocktails like the "Revelry."
  • Savory Shared Plates: Guests can enjoy flavorful, fulfilling Mediterranean dishes such as their signature meat hummus and tender lamb tagine, perfect for a casual meal or a late-night bite.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Making a reservation is highly recommended to secure a spot in this intimate, neighborhood favorite.
  • The venue offers a variety of seating styles, ranging from high-top tables to comfortable lounge couches ideal for catching up with friends.
