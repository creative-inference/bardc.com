✨ Vibe

An enchanting and authentic Spanish escape in the heart of Georgetown, featuring cozy mood lighting, elegant European-inspired decor, and a sophisticated yet social atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, lively group celebrations, happy hour enthusiasts, social sharing, and private events like rehearsal dinners or corporate gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Recently honored as a top D.C. destination for social leisure, specifically recognized for having one of the city's best happy hours featuring $20 sangria pitchers.
  • A mouthwatering menu of traditional tapas, with standout favorites including house-made sangria, succulent lamb chops, garlic shrimp, and authentic Paella Marinera.
  • Versatile dining spaces that include a dark and intimate lounge interior as well as beautiful front and back patios for a stylish al fresco experience.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Online reservations are highly recommended to ensure a prime table, especially for weekend dinner dates and large parties.
  • The venue is a premier choice for celebrations, offering comprehensive catering packages and private party booking options for any occasion.
