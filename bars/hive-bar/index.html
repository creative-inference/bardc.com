✨ Vibe

Hive Bar offers a laid-back, modern retreat in Foggy Bottom featuring a breezy rooftop with panoramic views of the Washington DC skyline.

🍸 Best For

Sunset views, custom pizza parties, casual dates, and low-key group hangouts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Artisan thin-crust pizzas that are perfectly crisp and fully customizable with a wide variety of fresh toppings and gourmet sauces.
  • Breathtaking rooftop scenery that captures the vibrant energy of the city from a relaxing, elevated vantage point.
  • A comfortable and spacious setting that seamlessly blends a chill indoor lounge with an airy outdoor terrace.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy a refreshing selection of frozen margaritas and specialty canned cocktails while soaking in the rooftop breeze.
  • The atmosphere is unpretentious and welcoming, making it a great spot for both solo cravings and lively group gatherings.

Time Out Worldwide

Hotel Hive

Hive Bar, located within Washington D.C.'s Hotel Hive, is operated by &pizza and offers a social atmosphere featuring industrial design elements like exposed brick and rustic beams. The venue provides a complimentary drink to guests who book their stay directly, serving as a key amenity alongside the hotel's on-site fast-casual Italian dining.
