✨ Vibe

Recently opened as a highly anticipated Spring 2025 arrival, Bar Angie brings a glammed-up, SoHo-inspired bistro atmosphere to the West End with its stylish European flair and lively, sophisticated energy.

🍸 Best For

After-work cocktails, upscale group outings, live music lovers, and anyone seeking the perfect martini happy hour.

🔥 Highlights

  • Premier Martini Program: Known for the signature "Angie’s Dirty Duo" and expertly crafted espresso martinis, the bar is a true destination for cocktail enthusiasts.
  • Elevated Bistro Classics: The menu shines with standout dishes like the wagyu French dip, a "legit" top-tier DC burger, and their fan-favorite fluffy flatbreads.
  • Live Jazz & Entertainment: Thursday nights feature live jazz performances that perfectly complement the venue’s chic design and spacious outdoor patio.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Be sure to visit during their popular happy hour, which features a highly-rated half-off martini special.
  • The venue is a prime choice for corporate gatherings and large group celebrations thanks to its spacious, stylish layout.
