RUSH Bar and Restaurant

Category: Cocktail Bars, Lounges, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: U Street

Price:

Address: 2001 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20009, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A vibrant and high-energy LGBTQ+ destination featuring multiple levels of excitement and a modern, tech-forward atmosphere in the heart of the U Street corridor.

🍸 Best For

Spectacular drag shows, rooftop cocktails, late-night dancing, and seamless group celebrations.

🔥 Highlights

  • Dynamic Entertainment: Features some of the city's most captivating drag performances and high-energy DJ sets that keep the dance floor moving.
  • Tech-Savvy Service: Offers ultra-convenient tap-to-pay wristbands, making it easy to grab drinks without the hassle of traditional tabs.
  • Spacious Multi-Level Design: Boasts two levels of vibrant lounge space and a stunning rooftop deck with great views of the surrounding entertainment district.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Recently opened as a premier addition to the 14th Street nightlife scene, offering plenty of space for large groups across its indoor and outdoor areas.
  • Check the schedule for their popular drag showcases, which are widely considered a centerpiece of the venue's nightly energy.
