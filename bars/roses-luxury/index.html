Rose's Luxury

Rose's Luxury

Category: Supper Clubs, American, Romantic

Neighborhood: Barracks Row

Price: $$$

Address: 717 8th St SE, Washington, DC 20003

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Rose’s Luxury offers a whimsical and high-energy atmosphere that seamlessly blends cozy rowhouse charm with sophisticated, Michelin-starred hospitality and a lush indoor garden setting.

🍸 Best For

Romantic dates, milestone birthday celebrations, foodies seeking creative American cuisine, and intimate group dinners.

🔥 Highlights

  • The legendary Pork and Lychee Salad, a "must-order" signature dish that has maintained its status as a D.C. culinary icon for over a decade.
  • A customizable "choose-your-own-adventure" five-course tasting menu featuring inventive delights like truffle madeleines, tableside-carved Korean short ribs, and corn bread fried ice cream.
  • Exceptional, warm service that prioritizes a relaxed and human connection, making even solo diners at the bar feel like the guest of honor.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended through Resy and typically release at noon on the first of every month; however, walk-ins are often welcomed at the bar for a more spontaneous evening.
  • While the venue offers a world-class Michelin-starred experience, the dress code remains approachable and casual, emphasizing comfort and celebration over traditional formality.

🔄 Weekly Events

✨ A La Carte Wednesdays

Wed

Every Wednesday, the restaurant offers its menu a la carte in addition to the classic $128 five-course tasting menu. Guests can choose to snack on favorites, sip something sparkly, or order the full experience.

Chef Aaron Silverman: bio, restaurants, and recipes

Rose's Luxury is Aaron Silverman's flagship neighborhood restaurant on Barracks Row, celebrated for its inventive and approachable comfort food. The menu highlights iconic creations like the Pork and Lychee Salad and a perfected cacio e pepe, which helped the venue earn accolades such as Bon Appétit’s Best New Restaurant in America.
