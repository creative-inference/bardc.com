Jimmy Valentine's Lonely Hearts Club

Category: Dive Bar, Nightclub

Price: $

Address: 1103 Bladensburg Rd NE, Washington, DC 20002

An eccentric and intimate dive bar in the Trinidad neighborhood, Jimmy Valentine's is known for its quirky decor and focus on quality sound for local DJs. It's a small, dark, and dance-friendly spot with a unique, underground vibe.
