Jimmy Valentine's Lonely Hearts Club

Jimmy Valentine's Lonely Hearts Club

Category: Dive Bars, Nightclub, LGBTQ+ Bars

Neighborhood: H Street NE

Price: $

Address: 1103 Bladensburg Rd NE, Washington, DC 20002

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A secretive, red-lit underground sanctuary featuring an eclectic mix of skull decor, kitschy art, and an inclusive "anything-goes" spirit that feels like a hidden neighborhood clubhouse.

🍸 Best For

Late-night EDM dancing, craft beer enthusiasts, an inclusive LGBTQ+ friendly atmosphere, and those seeking a gritty, authentic dive bar experience.

🔥 Highlights

  • Eclectic & Intimate Decor: From its iconic red lighting and skull motifs to the communal costume wall and historical patron notes, the interior offers a visually stunning and cozy backdrop for a night out.
  • Dynamic Soundscapes: Recently highlighted as an ultimate D.C. nightlife destination, the venue features nightly DJ sets ranging from techno and EDM to specialized goth night parties.
  • Unique Drink Offerings: Beyond the impressive beer selection and strong cocktails, guests can enjoy signature root beer slushies and escalating drink specials every Friday.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • This is a cash-only establishment with a discreet, "blink-and-you-miss-it" exterior—look for the blacked-out windows on Bladensburg Road.
  • The venue is "bring your own food" friendly and hits its peak energy after midnight, staying open until 3 am on weekends.
