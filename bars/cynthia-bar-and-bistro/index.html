Cynthia Bar and Bistro

Cynthia Bar and Bistro

Category: Cocktail Bars, Lounges, Event Spaces

Neighborhood: H Street NE

Price:

Address: 502 H St NE, Washington, DC 20002, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Cynthia Bar and Bistro is a warm, aesthetically pleasing neighborhood gem that blends an intimate dining atmosphere with a chic, personality-filled second-story outdoor terrace.

🍸 Best For

Intimate private parties, weekday breakfast dates, craft cocktails, and rooftop lounging.

🔥 Highlights

  • The "Side Piece" Patio: This unique second-story outdoor space is nestled between H Street buildings and features a stylish theme celebrating iconic "Cynthias," from Cynthia Erivo to Cindy Crawford.
  • British-Inspired Breakfast: A fresh partnership with The Bakers’ Lounge brings a new weekday morning menu featuring savory quiches, shepherd’s pie, traditional pasties, and seasonal fruit tarts.
  • Premier Event Hosting: Highly regarded as a perfect venue for wedding parties and celebrations, offering a dedicated team and a cozy environment that makes every gathering feel exclusive and special.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Weekday breakfast and brunch service is available Tuesday through Friday from 8:00 a.m. to 1:30 p.m. for both dine-in and to-go customers.
  • Be sure to explore the creative cocktail menu and the extensive wine list curated to pair perfectly with Chef Fredo’s seasonal small bites.
