L'Enfant Bar & Grill

✨ Vibe

Recently reopened after a stunning five-year overhaul, this modernized hotel bar and grill offers a sophisticated, French-inspired atmosphere within a sleekly redesigned lobby setting.

🍸 Best For

Post-work happy hours, upscale traveler dining, convenient Metro-accessible meals, and relaxed weekend brunches.

🔥 Highlights

  • Refined Culinary Classics: Guests rave about flavor-packed dishes like the butternut squash ravioli, mint-infused lamb chops, and the fresh, house-made Margherita pizza.
  • Sophisticated Modern Aesthetic: The venue features a completely updated interior and polished vibe as part of the Hilton’s significant transformation.
  • Rooftop Amenities: Beyond the main dining area, the venue offers access to seasonal rooftop perks including a pool and fire pit for an elevated DC experience.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located directly above the L’Enfant Plaza Metro station, making it one of the most accessible dining spots for commuters and tourists alike.
  • The staff is noted for being exceptionally attentive and friendly, often providing personalized local recommendations and speedy service without requiring reservations.
