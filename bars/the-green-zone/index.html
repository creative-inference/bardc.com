The Green Zone

The Green Zone

Category: Cocktail Bars, Mediterranean, Hookah Lounges

Neighborhood: Adams Morgan

Price: $$

Address: 2226 18th St NW, Washington, DC 20009

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An elevated Middle Eastern cocktail bar that blends cultural richness with an energetic Adams Morgan energy, featuring two floors of lively music, a dancefloor, and a charming outdoor patio.

🍸 Best For

Unique craft cocktails, flavorful Mediterranean bites, lively weekend dancing, impressive non-alcoholic options, and trendy date nights.

🔥 Highlights

  • Signature Sips: The bar is famous for its refreshing frozen Mint Lemonade (perfect with rum) and the citrusy Lebanese No. 1, alongside some of the best non-alcoholic cocktails in the city.
  • Flavor-Packed Small Plates: Must-try menu items include the legendary Arabic Spiced chicken wings, crispy fried halloumi, and authentic spreads like muhammara and labnah with za'atar.
  • Multi-Level Experience: Guests can enjoy a more intimate atmosphere on the first floor or head to the second floor for a dedicated bar and dancefloor vibe.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • While indoor seating is first-come, first-served, the outdoor patio can be reserved in advance for a guaranteed spot.
  • Large groups and private celebrations are welcome, with dedicated booking options available for parties of 10 or more.

The Green Zone – Bar Review

The Green Zone is a vibrant Middle Eastern cocktail bar in Adams Morgan that features a two-level space including the upstairs Club de la Résistance for cultural events and DJs. The menu highlights regional flavors with signature drinks like the frozen Mint Lemonade & More alongside authentic Iraqi, Lebanese, and Syrian-inspired snacks and wraps. The venue offers a sophisticated yet relaxed atmosphere decorated with vintage posters and traditional Middle Eastern decor.
