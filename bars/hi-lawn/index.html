Hi-Lawn

Hi-Lawn

Category: Rooftop Bars, Game Bars, Pop-ups

Neighborhood: NoMa

Price: $$

Address: 1309 5th St NE, Washington, DC 20002

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sprawling, high-energy rooftop oasis above Union Market that seamlessly transforms from a breezy picnic-style hang into an immersive seasonal wonderland with breathtaking skyline views.

🍸 Best For

Immersive holiday dates, large group gatherings, festive seasonal cocktails, and unique digital art experiences.

🔥 Highlights

  • Snowglow at The Dome: This season, guests can step into a massive 50-foot sphere for a 90-minute immersive experience featuring 360-degree holiday projections, northern lights displays, and cozy lounge seating.
  • Festive Culinary Pop-ups: Enjoy a curated seasonal menu featuring Alpine cheese fondue, spiced eggnog, mushroom custard, and themed treats like the "Snowgroni" and cherry hot chocolate.
  • Dynamic Event Programming: Known for its high-impact transformations, the venue hosts everything from the Capitals SkyRink synthetic ice experience to vibrant night markets and outdoor lawn games.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations for "The Dome" should be booked in advance via Resy, though the heated public lounge area welcomes walk-in guests for drinks and snacks.
  • Convenient free parking is available in the adjacent Union Market lot, and the venue is easily accessible via the rooftop elevator.
