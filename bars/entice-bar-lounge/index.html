✨ Vibe

A stylish and welcoming Ethiopian-inspired lounge in the heart of Adams Morgan that pairs a vibrant, high-energy ambiance with exceptionally fast and friendly service.

🍸 Best For

Happy hour cocktails, authentic Ethiopian dining, live music, and late-night dancing.

🔥 Highlights

  • Authentic Ethiopian cuisine served in a chic, modern setting that transitions seamlessly from an intimate dinner spot to a lively nightlife destination.
  • A dynamic entertainment lineup featuring live music and a dance floor designed for high-energy weekend nights.
  • A charming 25-seat sidewalk café, offering the perfect outdoor perch for cocktails and people-watching on bustling Columbia Road.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located at 1725 Columbia Road NW, the venue offers an intimate yet buzzy atmosphere with a total indoor capacity of 115 guests.
  • Features a popular happy hour and versatile space that caters to both casual after-work drinks and festive group celebrations.
