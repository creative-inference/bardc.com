✨ Vibe

Steeped in history and classic luxury, The Jefferson offers a sophisticated and intimate atmosphere defined by impeccable service, colonial-era elegance, and award-winning refinement.

🍸 Best For

Intimate nightcaps, historic ambiance, sophisticated cocktail hours, and upscale weekend brunches.

🔥 Highlights

  • Quill Bar: An upscale, moody destination featuring expertly mixed creative cocktails and contemporary comfort food, perfect for a refined evening in the city.
  • The Greenhouse: A stunning dining space anchored by a historic skylight, offering a celebrated weekend brunch featuring signature dishes like cast iron baked eggs.
  • The Book Room: A colonial-style library lounge where guests can admire documents signed by Thomas Jefferson and consult with the hotel's historian on Saturday mornings.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended for dining at The Greenhouse, particularly for its popular brunch and breakfast services.
  • Quill provides a full dinner service alongside its signature cocktail menu, offering a more relaxed "no jacket required" luxury experience.
