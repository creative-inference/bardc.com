Category: Italian, Restaurant

Price: $$$

Address: 1063 Wisconsin Ave NW, Washington, DC 20007

A Georgetown landmark for decades, Filomena is a classic, old-world Italian restaurant known for its lavish seasonal decorations and generous portions of house-made pasta. The traditional, welcoming atmosphere and dessert bar make it a go-to for family celebrations and indulgent meals.
