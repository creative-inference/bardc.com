Service Bar

Service Bar

Category: Cocktail Bars, Neighborhood Bars, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: U Street

Price: $$

Address: 926-928 U St NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A chic neighborhood staple that expertly blends high-concept, award-winning mixology with a laid-back, unpretentious atmosphere and a nostalgic pop-punk soundtrack.

🍸 Best For

Award-winning cocktails, legendary fried chicken, casual date nights, pre-show drinks near the 9:30 Club, and elite-tier happy hour deals.

🔥 Highlights

  • World-Class Mixology: Recently ranked No. 23 on the 2025 list of North America’s 50 Best Bars, this spot features inventive, bird-themed creations like the Golden Pheasant and the savory Road Runner, which comes paired with a cooling herbal popsicle.
  • Acclaimed Fried Chicken: Beyond the drinks, the bar is famous for its "incredible" fried chicken thighs and tenders, often cited as some of the best in the city and served with signature house-made sauces.
  • The Snug Room: For a more intimate experience, the venue offers a specialized six-seat private tasting room perfect for exploring their seasonal, elevated riffs on classic spirits.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Stop in for Happy Hour Monday through Friday from 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM for $9 classics like Old Fashioneds and Daiquiris.
  • Reservations are highly recommended for peak hours, though the "grunge-chic" environment welcomes guests in casual attire.

Happy Hour

$9 cocktails (Old Fashioned, Daiquiri, Pisco Punch), $7 High Life combo (shot and a beer), and a featured HH cocktail.
