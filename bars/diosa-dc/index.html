Diosa DC

Category: Dance Clubs, Nightclub, Latin

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated and high-energy subterranean lounge that blends sleek, modern elegance with a vibrant Latin pulse in the heart of the 14th Street corridor.

🍸 Best For

Latin dancing, craft mezcal cocktails, celebratory bottle service, late-night DJ sets, and immersive cultural events.

🔥 Highlights

  • Signature Cocktails & Flavors: Recently featured on Fox 5 DC, the venue boasts a specialized bar program featuring Bolivian Singani and mezcal-based drinks paired with a unique menu of fresh sushi and artisanal empanadas.
  • Dynamic Entertainment: The curated schedule offers everything from high-energy Latin DJ sets and international pop nights to professional dance classes and glamorous drag shows.
  • Chic Subterranean Space: Located beneath its sister restaurant MXDC, this newly opened hidden gem offers an intimate yet lively atmosphere perfect for letting loose with friends.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Diosa DC is open Thursday through Sunday, with late-night hours extending until 2 AM on Fridays and Saturdays.
  • Conveniently located near the U Street and Metro Center stations, making it an easy destination for a night out in Logan Circle.
