Recently opened in May 2025 in the historic Eastern Market neighborhood, Lobby Bar has quickly become the crown jewel of Barracks Row by blending sophisticated cocktail artistry with a warm, "hometown bar" hospitality.

✨ Vibe

An upscale yet approachable neighborhood sanctuary that pairs chic, experimental cocktail culture with a lively and welcoming social energy.

🍸 Best For

Masterfully crafted martinis, trendy group "Girl Dinners," upscale happy hours, and weekend date nights.

🔥 Highlights

  • Innovative Martini Program: Curated by expert Tory Pratt, the menu features creative variations such as olive oil–washed gin, tequila-based martinis, and unique oyster-infused cocktails.
  • Social Media Famous Specials: The venue is home to the viral "Girl Dinner"—a massive $80 curated platter paired with two drinks—and the "Very Happy Meal," which pairs a smash burger or fried chicken sandwich with a glass of Veuve Clicquot.
  • Market-Fresh Ingredients: Located directly across from the historic Eastern Market, the kitchen utilizes hyper-local ingredients for standout dishes like jumbo lump crab cakes, fresh oysters, and their signature weekend prime rib.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Happy Hour runs weekdays from 3 p.m. to 6 p.m., featuring $1 oysters, $10 spicy Sauvignon Blanc, and the popular Veuve Clicquot burger deal.
  • The intimate 66-seat space fills up fast; while the 18-seat bar is held for walk-ins, reservations are highly recommended for tables.
