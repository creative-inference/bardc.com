Reynold’s Bar

Reynold’s Bar

Category: Cocktail Bars, Speakeasies, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: Dupont Circle

Price:

Address: 1320 18th St NW, Washington, DC 20036, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An intimate, midcentury-modern lounge that blends retro-glam wood paneling and velvet seating for a sophisticated, speakeasy-inspired atmosphere tucked inside the Sixty DC hotel.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, craft martini enthusiasts, upscale happy hours, and intimate social gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • A visionary cocktail program specializing in "forgotten classics" and creative signature drinks, including an extensive and adventurous martini menu.
  • High-end, made-from-scratch "bites" that range from comfort favorites like crispy chicken sliders and elevated corn dogs to luxuries like Ossetra caviar and truffle beef tartare.
  • Exceptional, personalized service—led by a team known for making guests feel like the only people in the room—in a mature and "chic" boutique setting.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Keep an eye out for their popular happy hour, which offers high-quality cocktails and bites at an excellent value.
  • The bar is a perfect destination for a "date night cap" or as a transition point for guests dining at the neighboring Casamara restaurant.
