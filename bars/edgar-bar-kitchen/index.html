Category: Hotel Bar, American Restaurant

Price: $$$

Address: 1127 Connecticut Ave NW, Washington, DC 20036

Located inside the historic Mayflower Hotel, Edgar Bar & Kitchen offers a modern take on American brasserie cuisine. Named after the hotel's most famous long-term resident, J. Edgar Hoover, the restaurant and bar provide a sophisticated setting for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.
