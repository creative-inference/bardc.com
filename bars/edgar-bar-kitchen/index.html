Edgar Bar & Kitchen

Edgar Bar & Kitchen

Category: Hotel Bars, Cocktail Bars, American

Neighborhood: Downtown

Price: $$$

Address: 1127 Connecticut Ave NW, Washington, DC 20036

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Nestled within the historic Mayflower Hotel, this upscale American brasserie blends 1920s architectural elegance with a buzzing, modern energy perfect for the city’s power players and socialites.

🍸 Best For

Power lunches, craft cocktails, upscale weekend brunch, business meetings, and sophisticated late-night drinks.

🔥 Highlights

  • Historic Legacy: As the "social nucleus" of the Mayflower Hotel, the venue honors DC history with a sophisticated atmosphere inspired by the legacy of J. Edgar Hoover and a design by the renowned firm AvroKo.
  • Signature Brunch Favorites: Don't miss the legendary Mayflower banana bread, a beloved menu staple that has remained a fixture at this location since 1925.
  • Award-Winning Bar Program: The venue features an award-winning historic bar offering handcrafted artisanal cocktails, a refined wine list, and the "Tolson Bird Bar" experience featuring specialized fried chicken and champagne pairings.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located in the heart of Downtown DC, this is a premier destination for "power lunches" and offers a welcoming environment for solo diners and business travelers.
  • The menu focuses on locally sourced ingredients and modern-classic American brasserie fare served in a refined setting of leather seating and polished wood trim.
