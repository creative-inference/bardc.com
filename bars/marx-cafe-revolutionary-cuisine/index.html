Marx Cafe

Marx Cafe

Category:

Neighborhood: Columbia Heights

Price: MODERATE

Address: 3203 Mt Pleasant St NW, Washington, DC 20010, USA

Website

✨ Vibe

A warm, wood-paneled neighborhood staple that effortlessly transitions from a sun-drenched Mediterranean cafe into a vibrant, disco-ball-lit live music hub with an intimate dance floor.

🍸 Best For

Weekly jazz jams, late-night Latin dancing, Mediterranean small plates, and cheering for international soccer matches.

🔥 Highlights

  • Eclectic Live Music: From soulful weekly jazz jam sessions on Tuesday nights to high-energy Latin genres like psychedelic cumbia and salsa, the venue is a premier destination for local talent.
  • "Revolutionary" Mediterranean Cuisine: The menu features standout small plates like the flaming Cheese Saganaki, handcrafted beef empanadillas, and a diverse selection of craft beers and wines.
  • Community Pillar: Beyond its nightlife, the venue is a neighborhood hub that hosts immersive theatrical performances and local initiatives, earning it a reputation as one of the area's most authentic social spaces.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Check their calendar for special events, including the D.C. Jazz Collective performances and political trivia nights.
  • Sports enthusiasts can enjoy big-screen viewings of UEFA Soccer, MLB, and NFL games in a lively, casual setting.
