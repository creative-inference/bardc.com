Oyamel Cocina Mexicana

Oyamel Cocina Mexicana

Category: Taco Bars, Mexican, Cocktail Bars

Neighborhood: Gallery Place / Chinatown

Price: $$$

Address: 401 7th St NW, Washington, DC 20004

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A festive, high-energy destination where world-class artistry meets authentic Mexican street food culture in a vibrant and enchanting downtown setting.

🍸 Best For

Pre-game drinks for Capital One Arena events, Michelin-recognized small plates, celebratory birthday dinners, and craft margarita enthusiasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Pedigree: Helmed by world-renowned chef José Andrés, this Michelin Bib Gourmand winner is a long-standing favorite of the Obama family, celebrated for bringing authentic Mexico City flavors to the heart of DC.
  • Signature Sips: The legendary "Salt Air" Margarita—featuring a unique, cloud-like salty foam—is a must-try alongside their extensive selection of curated tequila flights.
  • Gourmet Antojitos: Experience a creative menu featuring tableside guacamole, Oaxacan grasshopper tacos, and rich mole sauces paired with house-made tortillas.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended, particularly before events at the nearby Capital One Arena, to ensure your spot in this popular Penn Quarter hub.
  • Be sure to check out "Hora Feliz" (Happy Hour) for high-quality cocktails and authentic snacks at an incredible value.

The Best Mexican Food in Washington, DC

Oyamel Cocina Mexicana, located in the Penn Quarter neighborhood, is a stylish and upscale spot from chef José Andrés inspired by Mexico City's dining scene. The menu features signature "Salt Air" margaritas, various ceviches, and small plates such as potato fries with mole Poblano. Guests can also enjoy a diverse selection of tacos, including grilled marinated chicken thigh and pork belly with guajillo chiles.
