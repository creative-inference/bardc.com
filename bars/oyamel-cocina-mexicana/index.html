Oyamel Cocina Mexicana

Oyamel Cocina Mexicana

Category: Mexican, Tapas Bar, Restaurant

Price: $$$

Address: 401 7th St NW, Washington, DC 20004

Another gem from José Andrés, Oyamel in Penn Quarter celebrates the small plates (antojitos) of Mexico in a bright, festive atmosphere adorned with butterflies. It's known for its unique tacos, fresh ceviches, and an impressive menu of tequilas, mezcals, and creative salt-air margaritas.
