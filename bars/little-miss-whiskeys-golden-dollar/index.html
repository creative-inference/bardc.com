Little Miss Whiskey's Golden Dollar

Little Miss Whiskey's Golden Dollar

Category: Dive Bars, Dance Clubs, Whiskey Bars

Neighborhood: H Street NE

Price: $

Address: 1104 H St NE, Washington, DC 20002

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A New Orleans-inspired haunt bathed in a violet black-light glow, this H Street landmark combines a welcoming dive-bar soul with a magical, high-energy atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Craft beer connoisseurs, whiskey enthusiasts, high-energy dancing, themed karaoke, and cheering on the New Orleans Saints.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Sports Culture: Voted the best sports bar in DC by Washingtonian, this venue is the ultimate destination for community events, crab feasts, and New Orleans Saints game watches.
  • Extensive Beverage Library: Boasts an impressive selection of over 130 craft beers and 60+ whiskeys, alongside their legendary signature "Awesomeness" sweet-tea slushy.
  • Unique Entertainment: Features a second-story dance floor with cover-free DJ nights every Friday and Saturday, plus theatrical "Kostume Karaoke" on Wednesdays complete with props and wigs.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Make sure to explore the "hidden" outdoor patio in the back for a relaxed, open-air experience away from the dance floor.
  • The venue focuses on drinks and vibes rather than a daily food menu, so plan to grab a bite on H Street before heading in for late-night fun.

Little Miss Whiskey’s Golden Dollar

Little Miss Whiskey’s Golden Dollar is a lively H Street dive bar and New Orleans Saints-themed establishment known for its signature "Awesomeness" alcoholic slushies. The venue offers a high-energy atmosphere featuring a variety of events such as stand-up comedy, karaoke, and pig roasts on the patio.

Little Miss Whiskey's Golden Dollar encourages patrons to bring their own food while enjoying an extensive beer selection that ranges from ironic imports to serious craft brews. Located on H Street NE, this dive bar is ideally paired with a porkstrami sandwich from the nearby Red Apron Butchery at Union Market.
