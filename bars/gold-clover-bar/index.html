Gold Clover Bar

Category: Neighborhood Bars, Cocktail Bars, Dive Bars

Neighborhood: NoMa

Price:

Address: 251 Florida Ave NW, Washington, DC 20001, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Gold Clover Bar is a warm and inviting neighborhood sanctuary that masterfully blends the soul of a vinyl-spinning speakeasy with the unpretentious, friendly energy of a community-focused dive bar.

🍸 Best For

Local craft beer lovers, sports enthusiasts (NFL and Premier League), low-key date nights, and cocktail fans seeking creative twists on timeless classics.

🔥 Highlights

  • Expertly Crafted Cocktails: Enjoy beautifully balanced drinks like the signature Manhattan featuring plum wine or a velvety Whiskey Sour with egg white foam, all served with hospitality-driven flair.
  • Music & Culture Focus: Led by industry veteran Antoine Lyers, the bar features an eclectic music selection, a curated library, and a sophisticated decor that pays homage to pop culture and vinyl records.
  • Spacious Beer Garden: The venue boasts a charming outdoor patio perfect for sipping local D.C. brews and enjoying the fresh air in a relaxed, stylish setting.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The bar welcomes a "Bring Your Own Food" policy, making it the perfect spot to pair top-tier cocktails with your favorite takeout from nearby local eateries.
  • Located in the former Truxton Inn space, this Black-owned gem is a premier destination for catching live sports ranging from international soccer to football.
