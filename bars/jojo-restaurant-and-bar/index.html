Jojo Restaurant and Bar

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Recently recognized as one of the best jazz clubs in D.C. for 2025, this intimate two-story venue blends 1920s vintage elegance with a warm, dim-lit neighborhood feel featuring classic mahogany bars.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, live jazz aficionados, top-tier happy hours, Sunday brunch, intimate celebrations.

🔥 Highlights

  • Premier live music performances running Wednesday through Sunday, featuring a captivating mix of local and international acts ranging from soul-influenced jazz to vibrant Brazilian sets.
  • A delicious contemporary American menu by Chef Ben Kibour, highlighted by signature dishes such as herb-grilled salmon, succulent lamb chops, and popular pasta selections.
  • A unique dual-level experience housed in a striking aqua-colored row house, offering a high-energy jazz floor for dinner shows and a relaxed upper level for a quieter dining atmosphere.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy the highly-rated happy hour at the bar for a lively social experience with no cover charge.
  • For the full jazz experience, book a reservation for the lower level where tables are dedicated to guests enjoying both dinner and live performances.
