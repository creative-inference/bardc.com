✨ Vibe

A charming and intimate French escape tucked away in Georgetown, featuring a crackling wood-burning fireplace and a sophisticated, rustic-chic atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Romantic dates, cozy winter evenings, wine enthusiasts, and sophisticated late-night cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Speakeasy Entry: Hidden behind an unassuming entrance and up a set of brick steps, this "hidden gem" offers an exclusive, tucked-away feel right next to the famed Chez Billy Sud.
  • Fireside Hospitality: The bar is anchored by a real log-burning fireplace that fills the room with a pleasant scent and provides a warm, inviting backdrop for enjoying signature drinks like the "Hay Fever" cocktail.
  • Bistro Excellence: Guests can enjoy the full, acclaimed French bistro menu from the adjacent restaurant, including favorites like steak frites, artisanal desserts, and an extensive list of French varietals.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Seating at Le Bar à Vin is entirely first-come, first-served with no reservations accepted, making it an ideal destination for spontaneous walk-ins.
  • Located across from The Ritz-Carlton Georgetown, ride-sharing is highly recommended due to the neighborhood's limited street parking.
