Solly's

Solly's

Category: Dive Bars, Karaoke Bars, Neighborhood Bars

Neighborhood: U Street

Price: $

Address: 1942 11th St NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Solly’s is a warm, high-energy neighborhood staple where red brick walls and low lighting create a quintessential, unpretentious tavern atmosphere that welcomes everyone with open arms.

🍸 Best For

Affordable beer-and-shot specials, legendary themed karaoke, watching live sports, neighborhood camaraderie, and casual comfort food.

🔥 Highlights

  • Kostume Karaoke: A famous U Street tradition held every Thursday where singers perform in costumes provided by the bar, ranging from tiaras to judo gis.
  • Community Spirit: Known for its deep local roots, Solly’s proudly supports veterans, hosts charity-driven trivia nights, and serves as the home base for the Washington Irish Rugby Club.
  • The Covered Patio: One of the best perches in the city for people-watching, this massive outdoor space allows you to soak in the energy of U Street while enjoying a signature Solly's burger.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Keep it casual—this is a "come as you are" venue where the bartenders often become your best friends and there is never a dress code.
  • Game day is a big deal here, featuring $3 High Life specials on "Football Sundays" and occasional free shuttle services for DC United fans.

A recurring trivia night held in the upstairs event space.
