Solly's

Category: Dive Bar, Neighborhood Bar

Price: $

Address: 1942 11th St NW, Washington, DC 20001

A classic, no-frills corner tavern on U Street, Solly's is a staple for cheap beer and a relaxed, neighborhood vibe. This two-story dive bar hosts popular weekly trivia nights and stand-up comedy, attracting a loyal crowd of locals who appreciate its straightforward charm.
