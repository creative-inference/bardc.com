The Gibson

Category: Speakeasy, Cocktail Bar

Price: $$$

Address: 2009 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20009

One of D.C.'s original modern speakeasies, The Gibson is an intimate, reservation-driven cocktail bar on 14th Street. Known for its sophisticated, candlelit ambiance and expertly crafted classic and inventive cocktails, it offers a discreet and upscale drinking experience.
