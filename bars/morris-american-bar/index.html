Morris American Bar

Morris American Bar

Category: Cocktail Bars, Happy Hour Spots, American

Neighborhood: Gallery Place / Chinatown

Price: $$$

Address: 1020 7th St NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A bright and whimsical cocktail den featuring a pastel, Wes Anderson-inspired aesthetic that feels both glamorous and cozy.

🍸 Best For

Craft cocktail enthusiasts, date nights, post-convention unwinding, themed pop-up experiences, and Instagrammable moments.

🔥 Highlights

  • Immersive themed takeovers, including high-profile collaborations like the world’s first official "Elf on the Shelf" holiday bar and Parisian-inspired "Emily in Paris" pop-ups.
  • Meticulous drink craftsmanship featuring hand-cut ice, generous two-ounce pours, and a rotating menu of creative cocktails like the bourbon-based Banana Republic.
  • A sophisticated yet welcoming atmosphere located steps from the Walter E. Washington Convention Center, perfect for a curated tasting experience or a post-show drink.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended, especially for popular seasonal pop-up events which may require a booking fee and offer dedicated time slots.
  • The venue offers a fantastic midweek happy hour featuring strong, flavorful drinks in an exceptionally clean and well-maintained environment.

🔄 Weekly Events

🍸 Cheers from the Shelf: 21+ Holiday Pop-Up

Daily

A nightly adults-only holiday experience featuring themed cocktail journeys (The Naughty List & The Nice List) and elevated nostalgic bites like Pigs in a Blanket and Milk & Cookies.

❄️ Family Brunch Celebration (Mondays)

🥞 Family Brunch Celebration (Weekends)
