View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

The Bullpen offers a legendary "backyard party" atmosphere that feels like a massive, open-air neighborhood festival right in the heart of Navy Yard.

🍸 Best For

Post-Nationals game celebrations, live outdoor concerts, summer festivals, large group gatherings, and casual open-air drinks.

🔥 Highlights

  • Live Entertainment: A diverse and energetic music schedule featuring everything from country concerts and tribute bands to vibrant cultural events like the Amapiano DMV takeover.
  • On-Site Eats: Tasty food options including classic stadium-style hot dogs and fresh tacos and burritos from the popular onsite Surfside Food Truck.
  • Spacious Layout: A massive venue with plenty of room to roam, featuring high-top tables, multiple vendors, and a laid-back environment that handles large crowds with ease.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Don't miss the "Home Game Happy Hour" specials, which offer great deals during every Washington Nationals home game.
  • Now celebrating its 17th season, this iconic Navy Yard staple remains a go-to for affordable drinks and high-energy community vibes.
