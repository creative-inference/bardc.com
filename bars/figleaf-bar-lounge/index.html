Figleaf Bar & Lounge

Figleaf Bar & Lounge

Category: Hotel Bars, Lounges, Brunch Spots

Neighborhood: Logan Circle

Price: $$$

Address: 2 Thomas Circle NW, Washington, DC 20005

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An art-forward social hub located in the world-renowned Hotel Zena, blending sophisticated "future is female" design with a vibrant, welcoming atmosphere in the heart of Logan Circle.

🍸 Best For

Creative happy hours, upscale weekend brunch, sophisticated hotel cocktails, and art-centric social gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Pedigree: Recognized as the signature social space within the tenth-best hotel in the world by Condé Nast Traveler’s 2024 Readers' Choice Awards.
  • Empowering Curation: Features a Latin-meets-Mediterranean menu and a beverage program that intentionally spotlights women-owned alcohol brands and local ingredients.
  • Signature Bites: Home to the famous $10 "Ginsburger," a cheeky culinary tribute to Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg, alongside fan favorites like the Aji Amarillo Caesar salad and lemon garlic shrimp.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take time to explore the lounge’s provocative art collection, which serves as a cultural hub for female empowerment.
  • Popular happy hour specials and a relaxed yet professional service make it an ideal spot for both hotel guests and neighborhood locals.
