✨ Vibe

A sophisticated and warm haven in Navy Yard that seamlessly blends mid-century elegance with cozy, modern hospitality, perfect for everything from intimate date nights to festive celebrations.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, upscale pre-game dining near Nationals Park, private milestone celebrations, and leisurely weekend brunches by the Anacostia.

🔥 Highlights

  • Artisanal House-Made Pastas: Experience authentic Italian flavors with pasta made fresh in-house daily, featuring standout dishes like the signature Lemon Linguini with Lobster and rich, delicate ravioli.
  • Exceptional Event Hosting: Renowned for a dedicated management team and personalized service, Jackie offers a stunning private space and seamless coordination for unforgettable birthdays and anniversaries.
  • Chef-Driven Culinary Fusion: Executive Chef Domenico Cornacchia delivers a "wow" factor with beautifully plated dishes, including the melt-in-your-mouth Beef Short Ribs and expertly seared Sea Scallops.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Visit daily from 4pm to 6pm for one of Navy Yard’s best happy hours, or enjoy the "Daily Value Meal," an incredible three-course dinner experience for just $24.50.
  • The venue is cashless and features a beautiful, heated enclosed patio that is dog-friendly, allowing you to dine with your furry friends year-round.
