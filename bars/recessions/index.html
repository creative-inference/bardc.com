Recessions

Recessions

Category: Dive Bars, Karaoke Bars, Pool Halls

Neighborhood: Downtown

Price: $

Address: 1823 L St NW, Washington, DC 20036

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A high-energy, "speakeasy" style basement dive that pulses with an electric atmosphere and a welcoming, authentic local heart.

🍸 Best For

High-octane karaoke, unbeatable happy hour deals, casual pool games, and late-night socializing in a gritty-chic setting.

🔥 Highlights

  • Legendary Karaoke: Celebrated as a top-tier destination for singers, the nightly sessions offer an electric environment where the friendly crowd genuinely cheers for every performer.
  • Unbeatable Value: A true downtown gem for those seeking "un-fancy" prices, featuring famous happy hour specials like their massive 28oz beers for just $9.
  • Beyond the Stage: When you aren't singing, you can enjoy a game of pool on their well-maintained tables, often hosted by the gracious and welcoming owner, Muhammad.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Karaoke begins at 8:00 PM Tuesday through Saturday and 7:00 PM on Sundays, running until closing time.
  • Located in a cozy lower-level space on L Street, this "hidden gem" is the perfect spot for a relaxed, budget-friendly night out with colleagues or friends.
