✨ Vibe

A stylish yet laid-back oasis on 14th Street that seamlessly blends contemporary charm with a lush, inviting patio perfect for soaking in the energy of Logan Circle.

🍸 Best For

Value-driven happy hours, outdoor group hangs, casual date nights, and transitioning from morning coffee to evening cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • Premier Outdoor Patio: Boasting fun decorations and ample seating, the expansive outdoor space is a neighborhood favorite for "kicking back" and relaxing in a vibrant urban garden setting.
  • Exceptional Happy Hour Value: Locals rave about the well-poured, affordable cocktails, including $7 Elderflower G&Ts and refreshing frozen specialty drinks.
  • All-Day Community Hub: From expertly brewed morning coffee to savory comfort food like wings and fresh ceviche, this venue offers a welcoming "community" atmosphere from sunrise to late night.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue utilizes a convenient, tech-forward QR code ordering system at every table for a streamlined and efficient dining experience.
  • Open daily starting at 8:00 AM, making it a versatile spot for early birds seeking a quiet morning workspace or a lively late-night weekend scene until 2:00 AM.
