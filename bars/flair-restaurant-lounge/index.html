✨ Vibe

A sophisticated yet inviting dual-level sanctuary that effortlessly blends a chic lounge aesthetic with a high-energy U-Street pulse.

🍸 Best For

Happy hour, Group celebrations, After-work drinks, Weekend nightlife, Intimate dinners.

🔥 Highlights

  • A spacious two-story layout featuring a beautiful dining room, a trendy lounge area, and an outdoor patio perfect for a breezy city experience.
  • A flavorful menu of American favorites, including signature shrimp tacos, perfectly seasoned wings, and premium steaks meticulously crafted for a high-end culinary experience.
  • Exceptional hospitality highlighted by a high-energy management team and attentive service that ensures a personalized and welcoming atmosphere.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • This new addition to the 14th Street corridor is a popular destination for major events and holiday celebrations.
  • The venue offers a dedicated happy hour menu featuring curated appetizers and signature cocktails in a chill, post-work setting.
