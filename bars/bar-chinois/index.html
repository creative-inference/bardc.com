✨ Vibe

A sultry and energetic "velvet-smirk" sanctuary where French cocktail chic meets a high-octane modern Chinese dim sum parlor.

🍸 Best For

Daily Happy Hour, Award-Winning Mixology, Late-Night Eats, Trendy Weekend Brunch, Post-Workout Refueling

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Excellence: Recognized as the 2024 RAMMY Award Winner for Best Bar in DC and featured in the prestigious Michelin Dining Guide USA.
  • Culinary Fusion: An evolving menu featuring fan favorites like the $1 happy hour dumplings, "1970s Garlic Noodles," Gyoza de Boeuf, and a decadent Matcha Brûlée.
  • World-Class Bar Program: Expertly crafted French-inspired cocktails and smooth Espresso Martinis served by a staff frequently praised for their "cream of the crop" service.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Happy Hour is hosted daily from 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM, offering a legendary dollar-dumpling deal and 50% off select cocktails.
  • Open late until 1:00 AM on weekdays and 2:00 AM on Saturdays, with a popular weekend brunch served from 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM.
