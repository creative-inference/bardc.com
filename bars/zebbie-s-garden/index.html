✨ Vibe

Zebbie's Garden is a whimsical and electric "indoor-outdoor" paradise that transports guests into an upscale wonderland featuring dangling pink cherry blossoms, neon rosebuds, and faux grass floors.

🍸 Best For

Birthday celebrations, house and techno music lovers, Instagrammable photo ops, bottle service, and high-energy late-night dancing.

🔥 Highlights

  • Immersive Whimsical Decor: Experience a truly unique atmosphere with bucket swings, hand-sculpted seats, and stunning floral installations that make it one of the most aesthetic spots in the city.
  • Top-Tier Talent: The venue regularly hosts a curated lineup of both local and internationally renowned house and techno artists, ensuring the dance floor energy stays electric all night long.
  • The Ultimate Night Out: Guests can enjoy a seamless evening by starting with an elegant dinner at the connected Mayflower Club before heading upstairs to dance the night away in the Garden.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Operating hours are Thursday (10:00 PM - 2:00 AM) and Friday through Saturday (10:00 PM - 3:00 AM).
  • Bottle service is highly recommended for groups celebrating special occasions to ensure a premium, accommodated experience.
