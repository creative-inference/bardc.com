✨ Vibe

This award-winning bistro blends Parisian charm with a high-energy, retro-chic aesthetic, featuring signature LED-lit wine walls and a warm, convivial atmosphere that feels like a vibrant escape to France.

🍸 Best For

After-work happy hours, sophisticated date nights, French country brunch, and wine connoisseurs looking for rare small-batch labels.

🔥 Highlights

  • A world-class wine program curated by Director Olivier Filograsso, boasting over 75 wines by the glass with a focus on biodynamic selections and boutique French wineries.
  • Refined bistro fare from Alain Ducasse alumnus Executive Chef Anthony Lemortellec, including fan-favorites like the baked Camembert, garlic-white wine mussels, and the signature "Profitonut" for dessert.
  • A dynamic social experience that ranges from soulful weekend brunches with live DJs to an authentic French pastis service and a high-energy dining room led by the welcoming Maitre D, Fabio.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of their popular Happy Hour featuring $10 house wines and $12 flights, which is also available during their new Thursday and Friday lunch service.
  • The venue is a Logan Circle favorite and fills up quickly—especially by 5 PM—so booking a reservation via Resy is highly recommended to guarantee a table.
