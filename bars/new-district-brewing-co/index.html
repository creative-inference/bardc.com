New District Brewing Co.

Category: Brewery, Taproom

Price: $$

Address: 2709 S Oakland St, Arlington, VA 22206

Arlington's first production brewery in a century, New District Brewing Co. is a community-focused brewery with a laid-back taproom and beer garden. They offer a range of ales and lagers and host regular events like trivia, yoga, and food trucks.
