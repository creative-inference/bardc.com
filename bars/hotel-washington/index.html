Hotel Washington

Hotel Washington

Category: Hotel Bar, Rooftop Bar, Lounge

Price: $$$$

Address: 515 15th St NW, Washington, DC 20004

The Hotel Washington is home to VUE Rooftop, a spectacular bar offering unparalleled, iconic views of the White House and the Washington Monument. This upscale venue provides a chic setting for enjoying handcrafted cocktails and light fare while taking in one of the best vistas in the city.
