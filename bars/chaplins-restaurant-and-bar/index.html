Chaplin's Restaurant & Bar

Category: Japanese, Ramen, Cocktail Bar

Price: $$

Address: 1501 9th St NW, Washington, DC 20001

A lively restaurant and bar in Shaw that pays homage to silent film star Charlie Chaplin, Chaplin's is known for its delicious ramen and creative cocktails. The 1920s-themed decor and popular happy hour make it a fun and festive dining and drinking destination.
