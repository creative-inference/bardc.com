Chaplin's Restaurant & Bar

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Chaplin’s Restaurant & Bar is a high-energy, cinematic escape that blends the charm of a 1920s Prohibition-style speakeasy with the artistry of a master-crafted Tokyo ramen house.

🍸 Best For

Ramen enthusiasts, craft cocktail aficionados, dog-friendly patio dates, high-value weekend brunch, and group celebrations.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Culinary Pedigree: Recently named one of DC’s best ramen spots, the menu features authentic bowls like the spicy "Chaplin A.S.S." and the "Stamina Spicy Ramen," crafted by chef Myo Htun using techniques learned from Tokyo masters.
  • Inventive "Drafty Spirits": The bar program stands out with complex on-tap cocktails and silent-film-inspired drinks like the absinthe-based "A Woman of Paris" and the strawberry-infused "Cure."
  • Cinematic Dog-Friendly Patio: Recognized as one of the best pet-friendly spots in the city, the venue provides a welcoming outdoor space where four-legged friends are greeted with water bowls while owners enjoy a "Drafty Spirit" under the stars.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended for weekend evenings and can be easily booked online to secure a spot in the cozy, Old Hollywood-themed interior.
  • Look for their popular weekend brunch, which offers incredible value including an appetizer and entrée for a set price.
