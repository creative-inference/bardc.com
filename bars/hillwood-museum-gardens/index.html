Hillwood Museum & Gardens

Hillwood Museum & Gardens

Category: Museum, Cafe

Price: $$

Address: 4155 Linnean Ave NW, Washington, DC 20008

While primarily a museum featuring the decorative arts collection of Marjorie Merriweather Post, Hillwood's Merriweather Café offers a lovely daytime spot for a meal, coffee, or a glass of wine. It's a serene bar experience surrounded by beautiful gardens and art.
