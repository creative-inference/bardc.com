✨ Vibe

A warm and welcoming sanctuary in the heart of downtown that masterfully blends the charm of a traditional British pub with a lively, modern sports energy.

🍸 Best For

Authentic British pub fare, watching live soccer and football matches, post-work happy hours, and dog-friendly patio dining.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Comfort: Recently recognized as one of the top destinations for authentic British-style Shepherd’s Pie, the kitchen excels at traditional staples like crispy Fish & Chips and their signature Ale Onion Soup.
  • The Poutinerie: A unique Canadian-inspired menu feature offering specialized takes on poutine, including creative variations like butter chicken and savory bacon-topped fries.
  • Lively Match Atmosphere: With multiple big screens and a dedicated match schedule, this is a premier downtown destination for catching international football and domestic sports in a vibrant, community-focused setting.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are available and recommended for large groups or during major sporting events to secure the best view of the screens.
  • The spacious outdoor patio is dog-friendly, providing a perfect spot for you and your furry companions to enjoy the downtown atmosphere.
