Tiffany's Bar and Bistro

Tiffany's Bar and Bistro

Category: Cocktail Bars, Brunch Spots, American

Neighborhood: H Street NE

Price:

Address: 709 H St NE, Washington, DC 20002, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A charming and flower-filled hidden gem on the H Street corridor that blends cozy, multi-level elegance with the warm, welcoming feel of a family-owned bistro.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, celebratory anniversary dinners, aesthetic weekend brunches, and craft cocktail enthusiasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Global-Inspired Cuisine: The menu features a sophisticated blend of flavors including Istanbul Royal family recipes, with standout dishes like Duck Shawarma with forbidden rice and savory amba sauce.
  • Whimsical Decor: From an abundance of fresh floral arrangements and cozy upstairs nooks to a fun indoor swing, the space is designed for both comfort and social-media-ready moments.
  • Exceptional Service: Known for its intentional and warm hospitality, the staff and owners go above and beyond to make every guest feel like part of the family.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue offers a beautiful outdoor patio that is highly recommended for special occasions and evening dining.
  • Be sure to explore the creative cocktail list, featuring seasonal favorites like the "Silk Road" and "Winter’s Solstice."
