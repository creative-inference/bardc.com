Wok and Roll Karaoke

✨ Vibe

A high-energy, affordable karaoke destination that blends a lively party atmosphere with a unique piece of American history in the heart of Chinatown.

🍸 Best For

Birthday celebrations, themed karaoke parties, budget-friendly happy hours, and large group outings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Unbeatable Happy Hour: Famous for offering one of the most competitive happy hour deals in the city, providing incredible value for singers and diners alike.
  • Historic Landmark Setting: Sing your heart out in a storied building once home to the Mary Surratt Boarding House, a famous site connected to the history of President Abraham Lincoln.
  • Private Karaoke Suites: Clean and vibrant private rooms equipped with fun extras like tambourines to keep the energy high throughout your session.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Booking is seamless and host-friendly with no upfront deposit required to secure your private room.
  • The venue features a full Asian Fusion restaurant menu, allowing you to pair your performance with a variety of delicious dishes.
