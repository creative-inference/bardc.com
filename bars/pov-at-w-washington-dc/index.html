POV at W Washington DC

Category: Rooftop Bar, Lounge, Hotel Bar

Price: $$$$

Address: 515 15th St NW, Washington, DC 20004

POV was the former name for the iconic rooftop bar at the W Hotel, now known as VUE Rooftop at the Hotel Washington. It remains one of D.C.'s most sought-after nightlife destinations, offering breathtaking, close-up views of the White House and Washington Monument from its chic, upscale terrace.
