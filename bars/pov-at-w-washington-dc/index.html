✨ Vibe

Perched atop the historic Hotel Washington, Vue Rooftop is a sophisticated and high-energy lounge that offers an elegant, front-row seat to the city’s most iconic landmarks.

🍸 Best For

Romantic dates, entertaining out-of-town guests, chic cocktail hours, and celebratory dinners with a view.

🔥 Highlights

  • Unbeatable Panoramic Views: Marvel at breathtaking, close-up vistas of the White House lawn, the Washington Monument, and the Treasury Building as they glow against the D.C. skyline.
  • Elevated Culinary Artistry: Experience a fresh, sophisticated menu curated by Executive Chef Jerome Grant, featuring standout dishes like the lobster-loaded Atlantic Linguine and perfectly seared Branzino.
  • Signature Craft Cocktails: Sip on creative, world-class pours like the "Under Review" or the "Witness Tampered" from a bar program recognized as one of the chicest in the District.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended to secure the best vantage points for dinner or sunset cocktails.
  • Valet parking is available through Hotel Washington, providing easy access to this premier downtown destination.
