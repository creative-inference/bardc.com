DC9

Category: Live Music Venue, Nightclub, Rooftop Bar

Price: $$

Address: 1940 9th St NW, Washington, DC 20001

A multi-level nightclub and live music venue near the U Street corridor, DC9 features a main bar on the first floor, an intimate concert space on the second, and a covered and heated rooftop deck. It hosts a variety of indie bands, DJs, and dance parties.
