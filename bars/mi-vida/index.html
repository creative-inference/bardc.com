Mi Vida

Category: Mexican, Restaurant, Waterfront

Price: $$$

Address: 98 District Square SW, Washington, DC 20024

Located at The Wharf, Mi Vida offers an elevated Mexican dining experience in a stunning, multi-level space with dramatic riverfront views. The menu features modern takes on classic Mexican dishes, complemented by a robust tequila and mezcal program in a vibrant, stylish setting.
