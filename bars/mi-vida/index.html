✨ Vibe

Mi Vida at The Wharf is a high-energy, three-story Mexican masterpiece that blends sophisticated Mexico City soul with breathtaking panoramic views of the Potomac River.

🍸 Best For

Waterfront date nights, lively group celebrations, sunset cocktails, and festive bottomless brunches.

🔥 Highlights

  • Chef-Driven Cuisine: Helmed by acclaimed Chef Roberto Santibañez, the menu features standout dishes like the legendary crispy Brussels sprouts with caramelized pecans and lime crema, savory Quesabirria, and sizzling lobster pozole.
  • Stunning Multi-Level Design: Recently featured in the 2025 Resy Guide for its bustling atmosphere, the venue boasts eclectic decor, a dramatic "Tree of Life" installation, and an enviable third-floor terrace overlooking the water.
  • Exceptional Beverage Program: Enjoy a curated selection of tequila and mezcal, featuring "Mezcal Mondays" with half-off specialty cocktails and "Fiesta de Bebidas" bottomless brunch deals.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Visit during the weekday Happy Hour from 11:30 AM to 6:00 PM at the bar to enjoy $4.50 Taco Tuesdays and drink specials.
  • Reservations are highly recommended for the upper levels to ensure a front-row seat for the best river views in the District.
