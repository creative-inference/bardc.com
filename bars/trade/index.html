Trade

Category: LGBTQ+ Bar, Dive Bar

Price: $

Address: 1410 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20005

A popular gay bar on the 14th Street corridor, Trade is known for its edgy, industrial-chic vibe and its famously strong 'giant-sized' happy hour drinks. It offers a fun, high-energy atmosphere that attracts a diverse and lively late-night crowd.
