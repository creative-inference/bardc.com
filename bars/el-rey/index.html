✨ Vibe

A vibrant, community-cherished U Street staple known for its high-energy Latin atmosphere, industrial-chic design, and spirited crowd.

🍸 Best For

Group outings, happy hour margaritas, pre-concert fuel, sharing plates with friends, and casual weekend dates.

🔥 Highlights

  • Signature Birria Tacos: Celebrated for their aromatic seasoning and perfectly crisp tortillas, these are a must-order for any taco enthusiast.
  • Exceptional Hospitality: Known for its friendly bar staff and welcoming management, the venue provides a warm, "cared-for" environment that keeps locals coming back.
  • Dynamic Shareables: The menu is packed with social favorites like loaded carne asada nachos and golden corn croquettes, ideal for a table full of friends.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of the popular happy hour deals on margaritas and appetizers for a high-value U Street experience.
  • Building on its U Street success, El Rey recently opened a massive new waterfront "oasis" location in Navy Yard featuring a 166-seat outdoor patio.
