✨ Vibe

An authentic and high-energy "bear haven" that blends rustic-modern style with an inclusive, multicultural atmosphere where everyone is celebrated.

🍸 Best For

Rooftop socializing, Sunday fundays, karaoke nights, themed bear parties, and lively trivia competitions.

🔥 Highlights

  • Multi-Level Entertainment: Whether you're dancing downstairs to music videos with high-energy bartenders or lounging on the popular rooftop patio, there is a perfect space for every mood.
  • Top-Tier Weekly Events: From professional-grade karaoke and trivia nights featuring excellent AV systems to signature bear-themed parties, the venue offers a rotating calendar of engaging entertainment.
  • Elevated Fare & Creative Drinks: Enjoy a standout menu featuring "elevated" bar favorites like crispy fried chicken and sliders, alongside refreshing frozen cocktails and beautifully crafted classic drinks.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Join the local crowd for the legendary "Sunday Beer Bust," a staple of the U-Street nightlife scene known for its vibrant, friendly energy.
  • Reservations are recommended for groups planning to attend their popular karaoke or trivia nights to ensure a great view of the action.
