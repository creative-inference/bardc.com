Jackpot

Jackpot

Category: Dive Bars, Sports Bars, Neighborhood Bars

Neighborhood: Gallery Place / Chinatown

Price: $

Address: 726 7th St NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A welcoming southern-style dive bar that blends an intimate, chill atmosphere with the unpretentious comfort of a classic neighborhood hangout.

🍸 Best For

Pre-concert drinks near Capital One Arena, large group celebrations, casual sports viewing, and craft beer enthusiasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Complimentary Popcorn: Enjoy unlimited servings of what regulars call the "best popcorn in the city," served warm and fresh from a classic popcorn maker.
  • Lightning-Fast Service: The expert bartending team is famous for their friendly hospitality and some of the quickest pours in DC, ensuring you never wait long for a drink.
  • Curated Beverage List: Explore an impressive, rotating selection of craft beers and ciders at some of the most competitive price points in Chinatown.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of their daily Happy Hour from 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM, featuring $2 off beers and $5 rail drinks.
  • The venue is strictly 21+ and has a flexible policy that allows guests to bring in their own food from nearby local eateries.
