Jack Rose Dining Saloon

Category: Whiskey Bars, Cocktail Bars, Gastropubs

Neighborhood: Adams Morgan

Price: $$$

Address: 2007 18th St NW, Washington, DC 20009

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A world-renowned "whiskey library" that masterfully balances upscale sophistication with a warm, rustic atmosphere, featuring floor-to-ceiling shelves of rare bottles and a cozy, inviting energy.

🍸 Best For

Whiskey connoisseurs, impressive date nights, group celebrations on the rooftop, and enthusiasts seeking rare or unobtainable spirits.

🔥 Highlights

  • The legendary collection of over 2,700 whiskey expressions, officially recognized as one of the largest in the Western Hemisphere, offering everything from accessible $10 drams to ultra-rare independent bottlings.
  • A versatile three-story layout that includes a refined main dining saloon, a year-round glass-enclosed rooftop terrace with DJs and happy hours, and an intimate cellar tasting room known as Premier Drams.
  • Exceptional contemporary American cuisine and Southern-inspired snacks, with highly-praised dishes such as bone marrow, whiskey-glazed wings, and their signature bourbon bread pudding.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended for the Dining Saloon, while the rooftop terrace offers a more casual, high-energy environment for cocktails and late-night bites.
  • Whiskey enthusiasts can visit the Premier Drams cellar shop to purchase exclusive Jack Rose single-barrel bottlings and other retail selections to take home.
