Jack Rose Dining Saloon

Category: Whiskey Bar, Rooftop Bar, Restaurant

Price: $$$

Address: 2007 18th St NW, Washington, DC 20009

A whiskey lover's paradise in Adams Morgan, Jack Rose Dining Saloon boasts one of the largest whiskey collections in the Western Hemisphere. The multi-level space includes a main dining room, a popular open-air rooftop terrace, and a basement tiki bar, offering a variety of experiences.
