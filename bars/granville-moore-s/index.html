✨ Vibe

This legendary H Street mainstay is a cozy, rustic-chic Belgian tavern known for its warm hospitality, historic "hole-in-the-wall" character, and soulful gastropub energy.

🍸 Best For

Authentic moules frites, Belgian beer aficionados, intimate date nights, and neighborhood happy hours.

🔥 Highlights

  • World-Class Mussels: A Resy Hit List favorite featuring fragrant pots of mussels in diverse styles—like Thai or Blue Cheese—with a unique option to add noodles to your leftover broth.
  • Expertly Curated Drinks & Bites: An incredible Belgian beer list that pairs perfectly with their signature "double-cooked" crispy fries and award-nominated fried chicken sandwiches.
  • Exceptional Leadership & Service: This celebrated women-owned gem is renowned for having some of the most welcoming and pleasant staff in the city.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of half-price mussel nights on Mondays and the specialized Food & Beer Happy Hour held Sunday, Monday, and Thursday from 5:00 PM to 6:30 PM.
  • The venue operates Thursday through Monday (5:00 PM – 9:00 PM) and offers a "Kids Happy Hour" during the same early evening window.
