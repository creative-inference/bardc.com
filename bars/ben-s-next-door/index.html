✨ Vibe

A high-energy, soul-infused "fun dining" landmark on U Street where the sophisticated ambiance of a cocktail lounge meets the lively, communal spirit of a neighborhood staple.

🍸 Best For

Bottomless mimosa brunch, game day watch parties, late-night cocktails, soul food cravings, and high-energy group celebrations.

🔥 Highlights

  • DC’s Longest Bar: Take a seat at the venue's centerpiece—an impressive 53-foot bar—where expert bartenders serve up strong cocktails and an extensive drink menu in a stylish, dimly lit setting.
  • Elevated Comfort Classics: The menu shines with "Black Excellence" Chicken + Waffles, flash-fried calamari with a signature sweet sauce, and perfectly fried wings featuring their famous sweet and tangy Mambo sauce.
  • Curated R&B Atmosphere: Known for its incredible music and curated playlists, the venue offers a "lively vibe" that transforms brunch into a soulful social event with a rhythmic, club-like energy.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Conveniently located directly next to the Metro on U Street, making it a perfect spot for those planning to enjoy the bottomless mimosas or happy hour specials.
  • Reservations are highly encouraged for their popular weekend brunch, and keep an eye out for weekly events like Tequila Taco Tuesday.
