Grand Central

Grand Central

Category: Sports Bar, Neighborhood Bar

Price: $$

Address: 2447 18th St NW, Washington, DC 20009

A welcoming neighborhood sports bar in Adams Morgan, Grand Central is known as a home for University of Wisconsin and Green Bay Packers fans. It features three floors, including a rooftop deck, plenty of TVs, and a menu of American pub fare.
