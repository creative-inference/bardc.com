The Blaguard

The Blaguard

Category: Irish Pubs, Dive Bars, Sports Bars

Neighborhood: Adams Morgan

Price: $$

Address: 2003 18th St NW, Washington, DC 20009

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A warm and unpretentious neighborhood staple that perfectly blends the cozy charm of an Irish pub with the high-energy excitement of a premier sports bar.

🍸 Best For

Game days, budget-friendly happy hours, late-night hangouts, weekend brunch, and authentic pub vibes.

🔥 Highlights

  • The legendary "Totchos," a house-favorite tater tot alternative to nachos featuring generous portions and flavors like Buffalo blue cheese and bacon cheddar.
  • Renowned for pouring a "perfect Guinness" alongside award-winning Buffalo wings served with a signature must-try homemade blue cheese dressing.
  • An upgraded two-story layout designed for entertainment, complete with shuffleboard, multiple widescreen TVs for 365-day sports coverage, and a polished upstairs bar.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of their celebrated weekday happy hour from 4 to 8 p.m., offering an incredible $10 "drink and a snack" combo with $5 appetizers, drafts, and rail drinks.
  • A long-standing Adams Morgan favorite for over a decade, it is recognized by locals and major publications alike as one of the city's top destinations for football fans.
