✨ Vibe

Decades is a high-energy, multi-level dance destination in Dupont Circle that offers a nostalgic and vibrant atmosphere across four floors of non-stop musical excitement.

🍸 Best For

Group celebrations, themed dance parties, late-night cocktails, and lively birthday bashes.

🔥 Highlights

  • Four spacious floors of dance floors and bars, providing a variety of musical vibes and plenty of room to move.
  • Exceptional service from friendly bartenders like Marvin, who are known for crafting amazing drinks and sharing the layout of the venue's multiple levels.
  • Easy access to the connected "Rewind" restaurant, which serves delicious late-night favorites like fried pickles, salmon, and mashed potatoes.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Bottle service and VIP passes are available for those looking to elevate their nightlife experience with a private section.
  • Located on Connecticut Avenue, this spot is perfect for those seeking an all-in-one destination for dinner, drinks, and dancing.
