✨ Vibe

A sophisticated yet versatile neighborhood gem that balances a high-energy wine market and bar downstairs with a tranquil, intimate dining space upstairs perfect for deep conversation.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, artisanal wine flights, weekend brunch with friends, and boutique bottle shopping.

🔥 Highlights

  • The "Corkyard" Patio: Recently debuted as a "secret" outdoor oasis, this full-service patio features its own dedicated rosé bar and a seasonal small-plates menu.
  • Award-Winning Pairings: Savor world-class comfort food like their famous crispy fried chicken and truffled fries, expertly paired with a rotating selection of over 50 wines by the glass.
  • Expert Sommelier Service: The incredibly knowledgeable staff and on-site market offer access to over 350 hand-selected labels, with experts ready to help you discover rare finds and "licorice-bomb" gems to take home.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended for the upstairs dining room if you prefer a quieter, candlelit atmosphere for your meal.
  • Be sure to check the chalkboard at the top of the stairs for the most current wine flight listings, as these curated pairings change frequently.

Cork Wine Bar

Cork Wine Bar and Market on Washington, D.C.’s 14th Street offers a unique wine-centric dining and retail experience with over 50 wines by the glass and 250 bottles from small producers. The venue features European-inspired shared plates by Chef Cicile Mendy alongside a market offering organic wines, artisanal cheeses, and educational classes. Guests can also enjoy a patio, two bars, and weekly specials like Fried Chicken and Champagne Sundays.
