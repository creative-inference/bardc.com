Atlas Brew Works Navy Yard Brewery & Tap Room

Atlas Brew Works Navy Yard Brewery & Tap Room

Category: Breweries, Sports Bars, Taproom

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price:

Address: 1201 Half St SE suite 120, Washington, DC 20003, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A high-energy, baseball-centric taproom that combines the friendly charm of a neighborhood local with the industrial-chic pulse of the Navy Yard district.

🍸 Best For

Pre-game rallies, post-work happy hours, award-winning craft beer, Tuesday night trivia, and groups looking for world-class pizza.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Solar-Powered Brews: Sip on sustainably crafted beers from "DC's Most Awarded Brewery" (2023–2025), featuring fan favorites like the Bullpen Pilsner and the Citra-hopped Dance of Days IPA.
  • World-Class On-Site Dining: Pair your pints with legendary NY-style slices from Andy’s Pizza, including standout menu items like burrata-topped pies and flavor-packed Old Bay dry rub wings.
  • Prime Game-Day Hub: Located directly across from Nationals Park, the spacious taproom offers a laid-back, community-focused atmosphere that is the ultimate destination before, during, or after a ballgame.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Test your knowledge during weekly trivia nights every Tuesday at 7 PM, featuring $6 drink specials and gift card prizes (note: trivia is paused during Nationals home games).
  • Don't miss the Happy Hour value, where you can grab a beer and two slices of Andy’s Pizza for a budget-friendly $11.
