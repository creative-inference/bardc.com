The Anthem

The Anthem

Category: Live Music Venues, Concert Halls, Event Spaces

Neighborhood: The Wharf

Price: $$$

Address: 901 Wharf St SW, Washington, DC 20024

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An electric, multi-level powerhouse that combines stunning riverfront architecture with the high-energy atmosphere and world-class production of a premier arena.

🍸 Best For

Major international concert tours, premium VIP viewing experiences, and high-energy nights out at the heart of The Wharf.

🔥 Highlights

  • World-Class Production: Renowned for its state-of-the-art sound engineering and hypnotic visual displays, creating an immersive environment for everything from soulful R&B sets to expansive psychedelic rock.
  • Premier Multi-Level Design: The stunning three-floor layout features bars on every level and meticulously maintained facilities, ensuring a seamless and upscale experience for thousands of fans.
  • Exceptional Service & Safety: A highly trained, professional staff and attentive security team provide a welcoming, community-focused environment where guests can focus entirely on the music.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • For the ultimate experience, look for "Super Excellent" or third-floor Box Seats to enjoy direct, unobstructed views of the stage and extra legroom.
  • Make it a full evening by taking advantage of the venue's prime location, surrounded by top-tier Wharf dining like Hank’s Oyster Bar and Shake Shack just steps from the entrance.
