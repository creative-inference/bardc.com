Chacho Spirits

Chacho Spirits

Category: Distilleries, Cocktail Bars, Tequila Bars

Neighborhood: Manor Park

Price: $$

Address: 6031 Kansas Ave NW, Washington, DC 20011

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A vibrant, llama-themed sanctuary in Manor Park that blends the industrial energy of a production distillery with the cozy, low-lit intimacy of an artist’s studio and vinyl lounge.

🍸 Best For

Spicy cocktail aficionados, immersive distillery tours, local art browsing, and laid-back date nights.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Spirits: Recognized as Washington City Paper’s "Best Distillery" and "Best Margarita" in 2023, the bar is famous for its signature Jalapeño Aguardiente—a fiery, Colombian-inspired firewater crafted on-site.
  • Owner-Led Experiences: Founder Daniel Ziegler frequently helms the bar and leads personal tours, sharing the brand's unique story alongside tastings of house-infused spirits and bourbon-barrel-finished variations.
  • Art & Soul: The colorful space features a rotating gallery of South American and local artwork for sale, perfectly complemented by a soundtrack of curated vinyl records.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Complement your spicy "Chacho-rita" with a menu of authentic Colombian snacks and small bites sourced from local favorites.
  • Keep an eye out for seasonal specials like the "Coco Caliente" and be sure to snap a photo with Chacho the llama mascot before you leave.
