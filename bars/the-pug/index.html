The Pug

The Pug

Category: Dive Bars, Neighborhood Bars, Late Night

Neighborhood: H Street NE

Price: $

Address: 1234 H St NE, Washington, DC 20002

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A quintessentially cool H Street hole-in-the-wall that pairs a laid-back dive bar atmosphere with eclectic decor and a welcoming, homey energy.

🍸 Best For

Affordable drinks, late-night hangs, neighborhood vibes, meeting locals, casual solo drinks.

🔥 Highlights

  • An impressive and reasonably priced beer selection featuring everything from reliable cheap cans to seasonal treats like their famous spiked hot apple cider.
  • A warm and inclusive environment with a diverse crowd and friendly staff that make you feel like a regular the moment you walk in.
  • Tasty and convenient food options, including an upstairs kitchen and popular local pop-ups like Starvin Marvin on Sunday nights, Mondays, and Wednesdays.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Plan your visit for Sunday evening, Monday, or Wednesday to catch the highly-rated food pop-up service.
  • The atmosphere is strictly casual and "come as you are," making it one of the most unpretentious and inviting spots in the city.
