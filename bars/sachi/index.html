Sachi

Sachi

Category: Nightclub, Themed, Late Night

Neighborhood: Downtown

Price: $$$

Address: 727 15th St NW, Washington, DC 20005

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Sachi is a stunning subterranean "secret garden" oasis in downtown D.C., blending a lush Asian rainforest aesthetic with state-of-the-art sound and light production.

🍸 Best For

High-energy dancing, VIP bottle service, immersive themed events, and late-night escapes blocks from the White House.

🔥 Highlights

  • Breathtaking Decor: The ornate venue features an elaborate "Asian Rainforest" design complete with golden birch trees, immersive moss and Buddha installations, and a massive 27-foot bar.
  • World-Class Tech: Guests are treated to a premium sensory experience powered by a state-of-the-art DAS Audio sound system and a mesmerizing, elaborate ceiling light show.
  • Dynamic Atmosphere: The 400-capacity dance floor stays vibrant with a diverse rotation of music including EDM, Hip-hop, Latin, and Afrobeats.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • A chic and fashionable dress code is strictly enforced to match the venue's upscale, "secret garden" atmosphere.
  • The club frequently hosts high-profile themed celebrations and holiday events featuring premium open bars and VIP packages.
