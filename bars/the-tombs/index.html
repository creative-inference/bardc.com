The Tombs

The Tombs

Category: Taverns, American, College

Neighborhood: Georgetown

Price: $$

Address: 1226 36th St NW, Washington, DC 20007

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A quintessential Georgetown institution, this historic underground tavern blends classic American charm with a high-energy collegiate spirit that has welcomed locals and students for over 35 years.

🍸 Best For

Georgetown game days, historic university traditions, lively late-night dancing, and casual American comfort food with friends.

🔥 Highlights

  • Iconic Tavern Classics: The menu features beloved favorites like the famous breaded zucchini chips, Philly cheesesteak egg rolls, and signature "Hoya Blue" shooters.
  • Lively Weekly Events: Experience a packed calendar of entertainment, including Tuesday night Trivia, Wednesday Karaoke, and high-energy DJ sets with dancing every Thursday night.
  • Georgetown Tradition: As a local landmark, it offers a legendary atmosphere for alumni and fans to gather, especially after football games or for celebratory weekend brunches.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of Happy Hour specials Monday through Friday from 3 PM to 6 PM, featuring discounted pitchers and appetizers.
  • While the tavern is a popular walk-in spot, making a reservation is highly recommended for groups to ensure a table during busy evening and weekend hours.
