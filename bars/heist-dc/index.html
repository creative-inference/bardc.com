✨ Vibe

Located in the heart of Dupont Circle, Heist DC is an intimate, high-energy underground lounge that blends award-winning luxury with a sophisticated, star-studded dance floor atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

VIP bottle service, milestone birthday celebrations, house music enthusiasts, and spotting A-list celebrities.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Excellence: Recognized as the "#1 Lounge in America," this venue features a premier rotation of hand-selected national DJs spinning house, R&B, and global hits.
  • Celebrity Magnet: A preferred destination for superstars like Drake, Kevin Durant, and Julia Fox, offering an elite environment where you can experience the heartbeat of DC’s exclusive nightlife.
  • Elevated VIP Experience: Known for its seamless table service, the club provides an intimate setting perfect for medium-sized groups looking for dedicated hospitality and space to dance.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Advance table reservations are highly recommended to secure entry and enjoy the full boutique nightclub experience.
  • Dress to impress, as this high-end venue maintains a sophisticated atmosphere befitting its status as one of the city's most exclusive spots.
