✨ Vibe

Easy Tiger Tavern is a cozy, wood-paneled neighborhood gem that perfectly blends a laid-back, welcoming atmosphere with the high-energy spirit of a dedicated sports bar.

🍸 Best For

Game days (specifically Arsenal and LSU fans), weekly trivia nights, group celebrations, outdoor lounging, and late-night cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • Elevated Pub Fare & Signature Drinks: Recently opened in June 2024 as a sibling to the beloved Exiles, the tavern serves up "no-fuss" favorites like tempura-battered fish and chips and wings alongside expertly crafted Orange Crushes and Espresso Martinis.
  • Versatile Social Spaces: Whether you're enjoying the fresh air on the front porch or hosting a private event in the spacious upstairs dining room, the venue offers the perfect layout for both intimate chats and large group gatherings.
  • Weekly Trivia: Every Wednesday at 7 PM, the tavern hosts paper-format trivia sessions by Capital City Showcase, where participants can enjoy $2 off all draft beers while they compete.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Open daily from 11:30 AM to 2:00 AM, making it a reliable spot for everything from weekend brunch to late-night bites.
  • A premier destination for soccer fans, known for its friendly "Arsenal bar" reputation and welcoming environment for Liverpool and Champions League matches.
