✨ Vibe

Set within the prestigious Four Seasons Hotel, The Lounge at Bourbon Steak offers a sophisticated and exclusive atmosphere that perfectly captures the refined elegance of Georgetown.

🍸 Best For

Executive dinners, celebratory events, high-end craft cocktails, and late-night decadent desserts.

🔥 Highlights

  • The iconic signature duck fat fries, a must-order fan favorite that features perfectly crisp textures and rich, savory flavors.
  • Premium gourmet burgers, including succulent Wagyu and Prime steak options, prepared with expert culinary skill.
  • An inventive dessert menu featuring showstopping treats like the 24k molten sundae and artfully presented smoked s'mores.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are available and recommended to ensure your spot in this high-demand lobby destination.
  • The venue offers elegant private dining and event spaces that can accommodate everything from intimate dinners to standing receptions for up to 50 guests.
