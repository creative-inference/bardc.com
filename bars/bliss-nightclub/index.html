Category: Nightclub, Dance Clubs, Late Night

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Bliss Nightclub is a high-energy Ivy City staple known for its expansive multi-level layout and its reputation as a premier destination for grand-scale urban nightlife and celebrity-hosted events.

🍸 Best For

Celebrity sightings, live hip-hop performances, bottle service, late-night dancing, large group celebrations.

🔥 Highlights

  • Exclusive Live Performances: As a hub for major artists and local legends, the venue frequently hosts high-profile shows and unique, headline-making tribute events that celebrate DC's vibrant music culture.
  • Spacious Multi-Level Design: The club features an impressive footprint with plenty of room for high-octane dancing on the main floor and elevated perspectives from the upper levels.
  • Premium VIP Atmosphere: Dedicated bottle service sections offer an upscale home base for groups looking to experience the party with a dedicated server and a prime view of the stage.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • For the best experience during high-profile celebrity shows, arriving early is recommended to secure your place in the heart of the action.
  • Table reservations are a popular choice for those celebrating special occasions and seeking a dedicated seating area for their party.
