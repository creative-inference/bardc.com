Echostage

Category: Nightclub, Live Music Venue, Concert Hall

Price: $$$

Address: 2135 Queens Chapel Rd NE, Washington, DC 20018

A massive, world-renowned music venue in Northeast D.C., Echostage is a premier destination for major electronic dance music DJs and a wide range of live bands. The 30,000-square-foot space features a state-of-the-art sound system, advanced lighting, and multiple bars, offering an unparalleled concert experience.
