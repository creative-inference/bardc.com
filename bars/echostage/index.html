Echostage

Echostage

Category: Nightclub, Live Music Venues, Event Spaces

Neighborhood: Ivy City

Price: $$$

Address: 2135 Queens Chapel Rd NE, Washington, DC 20018

Website
View on Google Maps →

Consistently ranked as the #1 North American Concert Venue by DJ Mag, Echostage is a world-class destination that brings the energy of a global music festival to the heart of Washington, D.C.

✨ Vibe

This massive, high-energy warehouse venue offers an electrifying atmosphere where cutting-edge technology meets an enthusiastic, festival-ready crowd.

🍸 Best For

Electronic dance music enthusiasts, high-production concerts, late-night dancing, milestone celebrations, and seeing global superstar DJs.

🔥 Highlights

  • State-of-the-Art Production: The venue features a massive 30-foot LED wall and a professional-grade Funktion-One sound system, delivering an immersive sensory experience that is second to none.
  • Spacious & Efficient Design: Boasting a 3,000-person capacity, the well-organized layout includes bars surrounding the entire dance floor, ensuring quick and easy access to refreshments throughout the night.
  • Elite Talent Roster: As a premier stop for international tours, the venue hosts legendary artists such as Porter Robinson, Fisher, and Subtronics, making it the district's go-to spot for top-tier talent.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Most events are 18+ and the venue offers convenient amenities like on-site locker rentals and premium VIP table service for a more exclusive experience.
  • To secure a prime spot near the front of the stage and take advantage of the dedicated parking lots, arriving early is highly recommended.
