✨ Vibe

Barrel is a warm, rustic Capitol Hill mainstay that blends the cozy charm of a neighborhood haunt with the sophisticated expertise of a premier whiskey destination.

🍸 Best For

Bourbon connoisseurs, lively weekend brunches, private birthday celebrations, and educational whiskey tastings.

🔥 Highlights

  • The "Whiskey Bible": An impressive curated collection of over 200 whiskey varieties, featuring rare bourbons, ryes, and international spirits from Japan, Ireland, and beyond.
  • Bottomless Sunday Brunch: A crowd-favorite experience featuring DIY champagne service at the table and indulgent comfort food like the honey butter chicken biscuit and savory fried rice.
  • Private Tasting Experiences: A secluded downstairs bar perfect for hosting guided tastings where knowledgeable staff share the history and craftsmanship behind every bottle.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue offers a popular $28 per person bottomless brunch deal that includes a full champagne bottle and juice carafes for the table.
  • Reservations are highly recommended for their private basement bar, which is an ideal spot for intimate group events and special celebrations.

🔄 Weekly Events

🥂 Bottomless Mimosa Brunch

Sat, Sun

Enjoy a Southern-inspired scratch kitchen brunch menu featuring shrimp and grits, carbonara, and real bottomless mimosas.

🥃 Private Whiskey Tastings

Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat

Tailored tasting experiences in the cozy basement bar with curated selections and rare pours, led by whiskey experts.
