✨ Vibe

A transportive, multi-level pan-Mediterranean escape featuring a lush rooftop "garden oasis," vibrant global decor, and a hip, high-energy atmosphere in the heart of the 14th Street corridor.

🍸 Best For

Group celebrations, rooftop cocktails, weekend brunch, adventurous tapas dates, and elevated happy hours.

🔥 Highlights

  • Stunning Year-Round Rooftop: Boasting a massive 1,100-square-foot "garden oasis" patio, this space offers a beautiful, covered setting for dinner and drinks regardless of the season.
  • Acclaimed Culinary Pedigree: Featured in Tom Sietsema’s Dining Guide for its distinctive value, the kitchen is helmed by alumni of the prestigious José Andrés restaurant group.
  • Global Small Plates: An eclectic and flavorful menu of Spanish, Lebanese, and Greek-inspired tapas, highlighted by signature dishes like the lamb hummus, crispy cauliflower, and tuna crudo.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue is a premier choice for hosting birthday dinners and group events; reservations for the upstairs rooftop bar and dining area are highly recommended.
  • Be sure to visit for their popular weekend brunch to experience unique Mediterranean-inspired favorites like the Tunisian hot chicken and shakshouka.
