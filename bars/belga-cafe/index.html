Belga Cafe

Category: Belgian, Restaurant, Beer Bar

Price: $$$

Address: 514 8th St SE, Washington, DC 20003

The first authentic Belgian restaurant in D.C., Belga Cafe on Capitol Hill is a bustling brasserie known for its mussels, frites, and extensive list of Belgian beers. Chef Bart Vandaele offers a menu of traditional and modern Belgian dishes in a lively, European-style cafe setting.
