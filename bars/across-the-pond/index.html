✨ Vibe

Across the Pond is an authentic, high-energy Irish neighborhood hub that seamlessly blends the warmth of a local pub with the electric atmosphere of a premier international sports destination.

🍸 Best For

Soccer and rugby fans, District Trivia enthusiasts, weekend brunch, NY Giants and Boston Celtics game days, and a welcoming neighborhood drink.

🔥 Highlights

  • Recently recognized as one of D.C.’s best sports bars, this venue is a premier destination for international soccer fans, offering a dedicated environment for teams like Everton and Borussia Dortmund.
  • Enjoy a standout drink experience featuring expertly poured Guinness—complete with custom foam art—and a popular weekend brunch featuring bottomless mimosas and Irish-inspired classics.
  • The pub hosts a highly popular District Trivia night every Wednesday at 7:30 PM, where teams compete for prizes in a lively, tech-forward competition.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of the weekday happy hour from 4 PM to 8 PM, featuring $6 house cocktails and beer specials.
  • While the bar focuses on a stellar beverage program, guests are encouraged to carry in their favorite food from local Dupont Circle eateries to enjoy during the match.
