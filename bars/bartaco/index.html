✨ Vibe

A beachy-meets-urban sanctuary with rustic wood accents, soft lighting, and floor-to-ceiling windows that capture the breezy, vacation-style energy of the Potomac River.

🍸 Best For

Waterfront dates, girls' nights, lively weekend brunches, and happy hours with a coastal escape feel.

🔥 Highlights

  • Stunning Waterfront Setup: Recently opened as part of The Wharf’s phase two, this location features a 40-seat bar overlooking the water and a year-round patio equipped with private cabanas for the ultimate riverfront experience.
  • Fresh, Beach-Inspired Fare: The menu shines with flavorful Latin street food, ranging from signature duck birria and Baja fish tacos to indulgent desserts like tres leches and key lime pie jars.
  • Handcrafted Cocktails: Every drink is elevated by fresh-pressed juices, with standouts like the Maui Express and the refreshing watermelon margarita providing a bright, boozy accompaniment to the meal.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located at 645 Wharf Street SW, the venue is conveniently situated near a parking deck for easy access to the waterfront's vibrant dining and walking paths.
  • The staff is highly regarded for their attentive service, so don't hesitate to ask your bartender for seasonal recommendations or "secret" menu favorites.
