Tai Chi Bar

Tai Chi Bar

Category: Karaoke Bars, Speakeasies, Game Bars

Neighborhood: Georgetown

Price:

Address: 1357 Wisconsin Ave NW, Washington, DC 20007, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A hidden Georgetown gem that seamlessly blends a cozy, welcoming atmosphere with a high-energy "speakeasy" basement lounge perfect for socializing.

🍸 Best For

Karaoke enthusiasts, group icebreakers, casual dinner-and-drinks, and late-night social gaming.

🔥 Highlights

  • Interactive Entertainment: From the thrill of spinning the "Shot Wheel" to a variety of tabletop icebreaker games, this bar is designed to turn strangers into friends.
  • Subterranean Speakeasy: The downstairs area offers a distinct, intimate vibe that is perfect for hosting high-energy karaoke performances and group celebrations.
  • Eclectic Menu: Guests can enjoy a unique fusion of comfort food and trendy sips, ranging from savory ramen and chicken rice bowls to refreshing boba tea and craft cocktails.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Make your way to the downstairs bar to experience the full speakeasy aesthetic and participate in the karaoke sessions.
  • The venue is highly praised for its personalized service—don't hesitate to ask for John or chat with the owner for a truly local experience.
