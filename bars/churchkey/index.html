ChurchKey

Category: Gastropubs, Beer Gardens, Taproom

Neighborhood: Logan Circle

Price: $$

Address: 1337 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20005

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Widely regarded as the East Coast’s epicenter for craft beer, this sophisticated Logan Circle staple offers a warm, meticulously curated atmosphere where passion for brewing history meets modern gastropub elegance.

🍸 Best For

Craft beer connoisseurs, celebratory welcome parties, rare international imports, and elevated date nights.

🔥 Highlights

  • Unrivaled Curation: Boasts a massive collection of 500 hand-picked labels and 50 temperature-controlled draft lines, all expertly categorized by flavor profile and served in style-specific glassware.
  • Refreshed Gastropub Fare: Following a recent renovation, the kitchen offers a fantastic menu featuring tavern-style pizzas, shrimp and grits, and specialty event-themed dishes.
  • Exclusive Industry Events: A premier destination for rare releases and tap takeovers, frequently hosting legendary brewery founders and world-class bottle shares.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Visit the on-site retail beer shop to purchase rare bottles, cans, and exclusive to-go options from their world-class inventory.
  • The venue is highly sought after for private events and welcome parties, known for its communicative staff and seamless group hospitality.

ChurchKey

ChurchKey is a plush Washington, D.C. beer bar that features an extensive selection of approximately 500 beers, including 50 taps and five casks. The draft list is uniquely organized by flavor profiles such as "tart and funky" or "smoke" to help patrons navigate global offerings served at precise temperatures. This venue is a perennial favorite in the 10Best Readers' Choice Awards, consistently ranking as one of the top beer bars in the country.
