Washington's oldest saloon, founded in 1856, Old Ebbitt Grill is a legendary institution just steps from the White House. The Victorian-style restaurant features multiple bars, including the famous wooden Main Bar and the bustling Oyster Bar, serving classic American food and drinks to a mix of politicians, journalists, and tourists.
