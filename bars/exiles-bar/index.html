Exiles Bar

Exiles Bar

Category: Sports Bars, Neighborhood Bars, American

Neighborhood: U Street

Price: $$

Address: 1610 U St NW, Washington, DC 20009

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A non-traditional Irish tavern that blends high-energy sports culture with a warm, welcoming neighborhood feel, famously recognized as the 2023 RAMMY award winner for Best Bar in DC.

🍸 Best For

Catching Liverpool FC or Buffalo Bills games, Penn State alumni gatherings, authentic Buffalo-style wings, lively patio drinks, and private parties.

🔥 Highlights

  • Premier Sports Atmosphere: As an official watch bar for the Nittany Lions and Liverpool FC, the energy is electric across two floors and an outdoor patio featuring multiple projectors and TVs.
  • Award-Winning Excellence: Celebrated for its "friends-not-just-drinks" philosophy, this U Street staple offers a friendly, quick-service environment that earned it top honors for the city's best bar.
  • Signature Fare & Sips: Guests rave about the "Buffalo-quality" wings and the Irish chicken curry, paired perfectly with a bucket of Blue or a signature Dan’s Orange Crush.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The bar opens early on weekend mornings to accommodate Premier League soccer fans and offers rotating game-day specials on buckets and rail drinks.
  • With two distinct floors and a spacious back patio, the venue is highly versatile for both casual hangouts and private event rentals.
