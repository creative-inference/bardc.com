✨ Vibe

Jane Jane is a warm and nostalgic cocktail gem in Logan Circle that blends mid-century Southern hospitality with "radically welcoming" charm, featuring honey-colored wood, plaid mosaic tiles, and dim, intimate lighting.

🍸 Best For

First dates, intimate celebrations, craft cocktail aficionados, solo outings, and stylish happy hours.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Atmosphere: Recently recognized as a finalist for Washingtonian’s "Best First Date Bar" and one of DC’s most chic cocktail spots, it offers a perfectly buzzy yet cozy environment for conversation.
  • Creative & Classic Sips: The menu features inventive standouts like the "Gin Soaked Felon," the floral "Sunflower," and the fan-favorite "Earl Grey Martini," all crafted with precision and high-quality ingredients.
  • Playful Southern Snacks: Every visit is elevated by charming touches like complimentary cups of Cheez-Its and nostalgic house-made favorites including rich French onion dip and zesty pickled okra.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • This intimate neighborhood favorite operates on a walk-in basis with no reservations, making it the perfect spot for a spontaneous night out.
  • Be sure to snag one of their collectible matchbooks or vintage-inspired "Sip, Snack, Fun" shirts from their popular merchandise collection.
