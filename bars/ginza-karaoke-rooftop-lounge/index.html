✨ Vibe

Ginza Karaoke & Rooftop Lounge is a high-energy, multi-level destination that masterfully blends a vibrant Japanese-themed karaoke bar with a sophisticated third-floor rooftop patio and private singing suites.

🍸 Best For

Girls' trips, group celebrations, late-night karaoke, Japanese BBQ enthusiasts, rooftop cocktails

🔥 Highlights

  • Premier Private Suites: Eight professionally equipped private karaoke rooms can accommodate groups of up to 28 people, offering dedicated drink service for an exclusive singing experience.
  • Elevated Culinary Program: The menu features high-quality Japanese BBQ, lamb skewers, and yakisoba, and has even hosted prestigious 18-course omakase pop-ups led by Michelin-rated chefs.
  • Dynamic Multi-Level Layout: Guests can transition from a professional karaoke session on the second floor to a scenic outdoor rooftop lounge on the third floor for specialty cocktails and "light-up" drinks.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Open until 3:00 AM on weekends, making it the go-to spot for late-night entertainment and singing in the Barracks Row area.
  • Reservations are highly recommended for private rooms to ensure your group secures a dedicated space for celebrations.
