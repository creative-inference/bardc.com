Kbird DC

Kbird DC

Category: Cocktail Bars, Lounges, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: Logan Circle

Price:

Address: 1333 P St NW, Washington, DC 20005, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A stylish and incredibly cute neighborhood gem that blends chic decor with a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere perfect for the 14th Street crowd.

🍸 Best For

Pre-theater drinks, cozy date nights, craft cocktail enthusiasts, casual group gatherings, and weekend wind-downs.

🔥 Highlights

  • Creative Mixology: Enjoy an extensive range of expertly prepared drinks, from signature cocktails like the pisco sour and "Last Word" to refreshing zero-proof options and artisanal coffee.
  • Versatile Spaces: A rare find for the area, featuring a charming loft for intimate conversations and a lovely new outdoor patio for soaking in the Logan Circle energy.
  • Personalized Service: Known for its exceptionally charismatic and friendly staff who create a warm, community-focused environment and even craft bespoke drinks for their regulars.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Conveniently located right next to Studio Theatre, making it the premier choice for a sophisticated drink or a bite to eat before or after a performance.
  • Boasts a spacious, laid-back environment with plenty of seating options, a unique luxury for the bustling 14th Street corridor.
