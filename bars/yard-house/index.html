✨ Vibe

Yard House offers a high-energy, modern American gastropub experience with a boisterous bar scene and a vibrant, sports-centric atmosphere in the heart of Chinatown.

🍸 Best For

Late-night dining, sports viewing parties, happy hour specials, large group gatherings, craft beer fans.

🔥 Highlights

  • Incredible Appetizers & Entrees: A massive, diverse menu featuring standout items like the exceptional poke nachos, lime cilantro chicken, and gourmet burgers.
  • Dynamic Sports Environment: Featuring numerous large-screen TVs and a lively crowd, it is a premier destination to catch a game in a high-energy setting.
  • Top-Tier Service: Renowned for a friendly and attentive staff that ensures a seamless experience and quick service even on the busiest nights.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of their popular happy hour, which features half-priced appetizers and specialty drink deals like the signature Lavender Drop.
  • A reliable staple for late-night dining in DC, this venue remains open late, making it the perfect spot for post-game or post-theater bites.
