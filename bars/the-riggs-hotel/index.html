The Riggs Hotel

The Riggs Hotel

Category: Hotel Bars, Cocktail Bars, Luxury

Neighborhood: Downtown

Price: $$$

Address: 900 F St NW, Washington, DC 20004

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated blend of historic grandeur and modern boutique charm, this former bank building offers a stunning, high-design atmosphere right in the heart of downtown.

🍸 Best For

Vault-inspired cocktail experiences, elegant brasserie dining, luxury staycations, and impressive business meetings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Prestige: Recently recognized with a Michelin Key, the property is celebrated for its exceptional character and dedication to a unique guest experience.
  • Iconic Basement Bar: The "fancy" cocktail lounge located in the basement vault is a must-visit for world-class mixology in a one-of-a-kind subterranean setting.
  • Historic Grandeur: From the beautiful ground-floor cafe to the nostalgic architecture, the hotel masterfully preserves its history as a grand bank while offering a chic, vibrant energy.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly encouraged for the basement cocktail bar and weekend dining at the popular ground-floor restaurant.
  • The central downtown location provides effortless access to the city’s top museums and landmarks, making it an ideal hub for explorers.
