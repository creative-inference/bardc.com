✨ Vibe

A stylish and modern three-story oasis on U Street that blends an upscale American eatery with a vibrant, string-lit atmosphere and a premier rooftop view.

🍸 Best For

Bottomless weekend brunch, sunset rooftop cocktails, lively group celebrations, and contemporary American dining.

🔥 Highlights

  • Unbeatable Brunch: Experience one of DC's favorite weekend traditions with $28 bottomless mimosas and brunch cocktails featuring delicious fruit juice pairings like peach and strawberry.
  • Elevated Comfort Cuisine: The menu features beautifully presented favorites, including crispy quesabirria tacos, savory brunch skillets, and fresh Pura Vida bowls with blackened salmon.
  • Instagrammable Moments: From the playful "Rubber Ducky" signature cocktail to the breezy, view-filled rooftop bar, every corner of this multi-level venue is designed for a great photo.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended for the popular Saturday and Sunday brunch, which features a generous two-hour window for bottomless drinks.
  • The rooftop bar is a prime destination for early Saturday evening hangouts, offering a friendly crowd and excellent views of the U Street corridor.
