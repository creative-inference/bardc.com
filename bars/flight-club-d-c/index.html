Flight Club D.C.

Flight Club D.C.

Category: Game Bars, Speakeasies, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: Gallery Place / Chinatown

Price:

Address: 641 New York Ave NW, Washington, DC 20001, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A whimsical and upscale fusion of a traditional London pub and a Victorian fairground, featuring a sophisticated speakeasy aesthetic and high-energy "Social Darts®" competition.

🍸 Best For

Social Darts®, corporate happy hours, connecting date nights, group celebrations, and craft cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • Proprietary Social Darts® technology that automates scoring and captures instant replays across various games like Sneak, Donkey, and Killer, making competition effortless and fun.
  • An elevated menu of globally inspired shareables, including fan favorites like buffalo chicken sliders, lobster elote dip, poke tacos, and truffle fries.
  • A spacious 8,300-square-foot historic venue that hosts high-profile activations, such as the "Flight Club Fridays" music series featuring D.C. native Wale.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of the popular Happy Hour from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM for excellent specials on rail drinks, liquor options, and bar snacks.
  • Reservations are highly recommended to guarantee a semi-private playing area for groups of 2–12, while the venue can accommodate events for up to 300 guests.
