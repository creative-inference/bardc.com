Segreto DC Speakeasy Bar

Segreto DC Speakeasy Bar

Category: Speakeasies, Cocktail Bars, Pizza Bars

Neighborhood: U Street

Price:

Address: 1932 9th St NW, Washington, DC 20001, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated and intimate underground sanctuary featuring sleek blue and gold decor, recently recognized as one of the 14 must-try speakeasies in D.C.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, intimate catch-ups, secret-seekers, and craft cocktail enthusiasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Hidden Entrance: This true speakeasy experience begins by walking through a faux fridge door beneath Minya’s Pizza and descending into a sleek, hidden lounge.
  • Masterful Mixology: Enjoy beautifully presented cocktails and mocktails made with fresh ingredients and finesse by expert bartenders who specialize in flavor and flair.
  • Speakeasy Eats: Pair your drinks with delicious offerings from the kitchen upstairs, including flavorful spicy wings and fresh prosciutto pizzas available late into the night.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Due to the intimate size and limited seating, making a reservation is highly recommended; keep an eye out for the secret code required for entry.
  • Be sure to check out the happy hour menu for exceptional deals on their high-quality drink selections and appetizers.
