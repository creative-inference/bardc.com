✨ Vibe

Recently recognized as one of DC’s most romantic restaurants in 2025, this Logan Circle staple blends a sophisticated rustic interior with a high-energy social scene that transforms from a chic dining room into a lively late-night destination.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, late-night dining, weekend brunch, group gatherings, and wine enthusiasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Chic Indoor-Outdoor Atmosphere: The venue features an alluring design with a wood-and-stone interior and a popular patio equipped with a fire pit, heat lamps, and blankets for a year-round Mediterranean feel.
  • Authentic Tapas & Late-Night Kitchen: An award-winning menu of Spanish favorites like paella, savory empanadas, and charcuterie, with the rare perk of a kitchen that stays open late to satisfy night owls.
  • Curated Global Wine List: A world-class selection focused on the unique regions of Spain, Portugal, and South America, paired with "immaculate" music and top-tier service that makes every visit feel like a celebration.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • While a top-tier spot for dinner and late-night drinks, don't miss their highly-rated weekend brunch featuring shareable plates that are perfect for groups and families.
  • Located in the heart of high-traffic Logan Circle, making a reservation is highly recommended to secure a spot at this DC "must-do."
