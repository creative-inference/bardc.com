Madhatter

Category: Bar, Restaurant, Sports Bar

Price: $$

Address: 1319 Connecticut Ave NW, Washington, DC 20036

An 'Alice in Wonderland'-themed bar and restaurant near Dupont Circle, Madhatter offers a whimsical escape with multiple floors of dining and drinking space. It's a popular spot for brunch, happy hour, and watching sports, with a menu of classic American pub fare.
