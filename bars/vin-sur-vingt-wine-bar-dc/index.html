✨ Vibe

A sophisticated yet welcoming neighborhood French bistro and wine bar with a chic patio and a relaxed, upscale atmosphere perfect for unwinding in the heart of Logan Circle.

🍸 Best For

Wine enthusiasts, happy hour dates, French-inspired dinners, patio lounging, neighborhood meetups.

🔥 Highlights

  • An exceptional and extensive wine list featuring a vast selection of French varietals with a highly knowledgeable staff ready to provide expert pairing recommendations.
  • Elevated French fare including standout dishes like the duck confit, fresh Wellfleet oysters, and a "must-try" chicken and liver mousse charcuterie board.
  • A spacious and inviting outdoor patio that provides a relaxing escape for sipping wine and enjoying the vibrant neighborhood energy.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of the fantastic happy hour menu featuring $10 wine specials and $1.50 oysters, as well as periodic deals like 25% off full bottles.
  • The venue is spacious and welcoming for walk-ins, offering a more intimate and accessible alternative to some of the busier nearby hotspots.
