Capo Speakeasy

Category: Speakeasy, Cocktail Bar

Price: $$$

Address: 715 Florida Ave NW, Washington, DC 20001

Hidden behind the freezer door of an Italian deli in Shaw, Capo Speakeasy is an intimate, dimly lit bar known for its classic cocktails and playful, often politically themed, drink creations. The cozy, old-school atmosphere provides a unique and exclusive experience.
