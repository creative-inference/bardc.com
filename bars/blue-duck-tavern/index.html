✨ Vibe

An elegant and sophisticated urban oasis within the Park Hyatt, blending modern wood décor and an open-kitchen concept to create a warm, refined atmosphere perfect for any upscale occasion.

🍸 Best For

Celebratory brunches, milestone birthdays, high-end business lunches, and intimate late-night cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • Michelin-Recognized Cuisine: Enjoy a masterfully crafted contemporary American menu featuring standout dishes like the signature duck confit hash, jumbo lump crab cakes, and the decadent beef bone marrow with roasted garlic.
  • World-Class Tea Program: Experience one of the city’s most unique offerings in the Tea Cellar, where an on-staff specialist curates over 35 rare and vintage teas served with precision in traditional clay pots.
  • Sophisticated Lounge: The Blue Duck Lounge offers a stylish retreat for seasonal cocktails—such as the Duck Fat Old Fashioned—and features intimate "Capitol Hill" glass-enclosed booths for a more private gathering.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Advance reservations are highly recommended to secure preferred seating, such as the cozy, secluded booths perfect for special celebrations.
  • The venue offers a seamless full-day dining experience, ranging from award-winning breakfast and weekend brunch to refined late-night bites served until 11:00 PM.

Blue Duck Tavern

Blue Duck Tavern is an upscale American restaurant located in the Park Hyatt Washington D.C., known for its sleek, urbane interior and open kitchen featuring a wood-burning oven. The menu focuses on high-quality regional products, highlighting signature dishes such as duck leg confit and whole-roasted fish alongside an extensive selection of nearly 30 rare teas.
