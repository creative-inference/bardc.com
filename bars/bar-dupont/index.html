✨ Vibe

Doyle is a sophisticated mid-century lounge that masterfully blends the upscale elegance of a cocktail bar with the warm, welcoming charm of a classic Irish pub.

🍸 Best For

Craft cocktail enthusiasts, stylish date nights, girls' night out, and people-watching with panoramic views of Dupont Circle.

🔥 Highlights

  • Recently named one of the "10 Best Hotel Bars in Washington, DC," celebrated for its inventive cocktail menu and stunning "living room" aesthetic.
  • Must-try signature drinks include the fan-favorite Espresso Martini topped with custom white chocolate foam and the bold El Zorro featuring mezcal and habanero bitters.
  • An exceptional service team known for their warm hospitality and creating unforgettable experiences for birthdays and special celebrations.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue features a beautiful outdoor patio that offers a prime vantage point for enjoying the energy of the neighborhood.
  • Check out the food menu for elevated Irish-inspired bites like boxty and dressed crab, or the local favorite kale Caesar with steak.
