Rappahannock Oyster Bar

Rappahannock Oyster Bar

Category: Oyster Bars, Seafood Bars, Waterfront

Neighborhood: The Wharf

Price:

Address: 1150 Maine Ave SW, Washington, DC 20024, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated yet breezy waterfront destination that perfectly blends modern maritime style with a relaxed atmosphere, offering stunning views of the Potomac River and a refreshing open-air feel.

🍸 Best For

Waterfront date nights, birthday celebrations, fresh raw bar cravings, golden hour cocktails, and impressive seafood towers with friends.

🔥 Highlights

  • Michelin-Recognized Shellfish: Experience the best of the Chesapeake with proprietary, sustainably farmed oysters like the sweet Rappahannock River Oysters and the briny Olde Salts, served fresh at their signature raw bar.
  • Expertly Crafted Seafood: From seasoned Oysters Rockefeller and grilled octopus to some of the largest, lump-filled crab cakes in the city, the menu consistently delivers high-impact flavor and quality.
  • Premier Bar Program: Enjoy a "perfect" seasonal peach sangria or a classic martini at the stylish wraparound bar while soaking in the vibrant energy of the Wharf.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • As the flagship Washington D.C. location for the renowned Rappahannock Oyster Co., this venue is a top-tier choice for those seeking a premium view and authentic "tide-to-table" dining.
  • The professional and accommodating staff are well-versed in celebratory dining, making it a go-to spot for special occasions and group gatherings.
