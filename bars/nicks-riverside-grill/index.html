Nick's Riverside Grill

Category: Happy Hour Spots, Waterfront, American

Neighborhood: Georgetown

Price: $$

Address: 3050 K St NW, Washington, DC 20007

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A lively and contemporary waterfront destination where rustic barn wood accents and floor-to-ceiling windows create a relaxed, sun-drenched atmosphere overlooking the Potomac River.

🍸 Best For

Scenic happy hours, large group celebrations, casual waterfront dining, and legendary 90s-inspired cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Hospitality: Experience top-tier service led by standout staff like Terrell Springs, recently named a runner-up for Best Bartender in the Washington City Paper's Best of DC 2025 awards.
  • Iconic Cocktails & Cuisine: Indulge in their famous "Mind Eraser" cocktails or dive into a diverse menu featuring fresh glazed salmon, signature Detroit-style deep dish pizza, and their "must-try" loaded nachos.
  • Premier Waterfront Views: Following an extensive multi-million dollar renovation, the venue offers a stunning indoor-outdoor experience with a sleek concrete bar and a spacious patio perfect for sunset drinks at Washington Harbour.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The kitchen serves a special weekend breakfast every Friday, Saturday, and Sunday from 9 AM to 12 PM.
  • Known for being highly efficient with large parties, it is an ideal choice for group bookings and organized event inquiries.
