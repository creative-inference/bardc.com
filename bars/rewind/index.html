Rewind

Category: Speakeasy, Retro Bar, Nightclub

Price: $$$

Address: 1219 Connecticut Ave NW, Washington, DC 20036

Located on the lower level of the Decades nightclub, Rewind is a speakeasy-style bar with a focus on nostalgic video games and 80s/90s pop culture. It offers a more intimate setting than the main club floors, with retro decor and themed cocktails.
