The LINE Hotel

The LINE Hotel

Category: Hotel Bars, Rooftop Bars, Event Spaces

Neighborhood: Adams Morgan

Price: $$$

Address: 1770 Euclid St NW, Washington, DC 20009

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A masterfully designed blend of historic grandeur and modern artistic flair, housed in a 110-year-old church at the epicenter of Adams Morgan’s vibrant social scene.

🍸 Best For

Rooftop sunset cocktails, sophisticated weddings, spacious staycations, and stylish all-day dining.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Rooftop & Mezzanine: Breathtaking outdoor views and unique architectural levels provide a dramatic and picturesque backdrop for both casual drinks and grand celebrations.
  • No Goodbyes: A standout culinary hub featuring a highly-praised kitchen that serves everything from elegant plated breakfasts to vibrant, high-quality dinner fare.
  • Exceptional Event Spaces: With the ability to host ceremonies, cocktail hours, and receptions all in one location, the venue is a favorite for seamless, high-end event planning.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • For the best experience and exclusive perks, book directly through their website to take advantage of The LINE Rewards program, which offers cash-back benefits.
  • The hotel is located in the heart of Adams Morgan, making it the perfect home base for exploring one of DC’s most famous nightlife corridors.
