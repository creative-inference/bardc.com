Union Trust

Union Trust

Category: Neighborhood Bars, Bourbon Bars, Cocktail Bars

Neighborhood: Downtown

Price: $$$$

Address: 740 15th St NW, Washington, DC 20005

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A hip and sophisticated yet unpretentious neighborhood gem in the heart of downtown, featuring a dimly lit, historic interior that offers a relaxing escape from the city bustle.

🍸 Best For

Intimate conversations, bourbon aficionados, after-work happy hours, and craft cocktail enthusiasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • An impressive top-shelf bourbon and whiskey selection, curated for enthusiasts and showcased in perfectly balanced Old Fashioneds.
  • Expertly crafted signature drinks, including their fan-favorite Moscow Mules and a diverse, rotating selection of cold craft beers.
  • A welcoming and social atmosphere where the music and crowd levels are kept at a perfect volume for coherent, meaningful conversation.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Visit during happy hour to enjoy premium pours and a relaxing environment to watch major sporting events on their well-placed TVs.
  • This venue focuses exclusively on the art of the drink, making it the perfect spot for a pre-dinner cocktail or a sophisticated nightcap.
