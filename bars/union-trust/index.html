Union Trust

Category: Event Space, Historic

Price: $$$$

Address: 740 15th St NW, Washington, DC 20005

Housed in a magnificent, historic bank building downtown, Union Trust is not a daily operating bar but a premier private event venue. Known for its soaring ceilings, grand architecture, and elegant ballroom, it hosts upscale weddings, galas, and corporate events.
