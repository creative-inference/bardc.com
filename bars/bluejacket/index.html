Bluejacket

Category: Brewery, Restaurant, Bar

Price: $$

Address: 300 Tingey St SE, Washington, DC 20003

Housed in a historic 1919 factory building in the Navy Yard, Bluejacket is a three-story brewery and restaurant offering a constantly rotating selection of 20 drafts and cask ales. Known for its extensive variety of house-made beers and locally sourced American fare, it's a popular destination for brunch, dinner, and drinks.
