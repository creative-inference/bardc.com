✨ Vibe

Located in the heart of the Union Market district, this massive, high-octane venue blends sleek neon aesthetics with cutting-edge technology to create a sophisticated, high-energy racing world for both fans and newcomers alike.

🍸 Best For

Interactive date nights, high-energy corporate events, birthday surprises, F1 watch parties, and competitive group outings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Elite Racing Simulation: Experience the thrill of the track with over 80 state-of-the-art, full-motion simulators that offer realistic feedback for every skill level, from "Rookie" to "Seasoned Speedster."
  • Gourmet "Pit Stop" Menu: Enjoy a premium dining experience featuring upscale sharing plates like cauliflower wings and steak tacos, paired with F1-inspired cocktails such as the spicy Miami Heat.
  • The Arcadian Hub: Level up your visit by creating a Driver Profile to earn "Arcadians," a unique digital currency used to unlock in-venue games, exclusive merchandise, and VIP rewards.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Plan to arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled race time to check in and set up your driver profile.
  • Take advantage of the daily Happy Hour from 4 PM to 7 PM, featuring $7 deals on cocktails and appetizers.
