Irish Channel

Irish Channel

Category: Irish Pubs, Sports Bars, Gastropubs

Neighborhood: Gallery Place / Chinatown

Price: $

Address: 500 H St NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A cozy and welcoming neighborhood gem in the heart of Gallery Place that blends traditional pub warmth with a chill, laid-back atmosphere perfect for relaxing.

🍸 Best For

Authentic Irish comfort food, pre-game drinks near Capital One Arena, a hearty weekend breakfast, and casual weeknight dinners.

🔥 Highlights

  • The World-Famous Spice Bag: Recently celebrated as a premier global destination for this viral Irish-Chinese fusion dish, featuring a flavorful mix of spicy fried chicken, fries, and Sichuan peppercorns.
  • Hearty Irish Staples: Home to one of the most affordable and authentic Full Irish Breakfasts in DC, alongside fan-favorites like savory Shepherd’s Pie and Bangers and Mash served with delicious curry sauce.
  • Exceptional Service: Known for a friendly and attentive staff that provides lightning-fast service, ensuring you get your favorite brew and a hot meal without the wait.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Conveniently located within the Fairfield Inn & Suites, making it an easy and accessible meeting spot in the Chinatown area.
  • An ideal choice for a quick, high-quality dinner or appetizer (like their famous artichoke dip) before a show or event.
