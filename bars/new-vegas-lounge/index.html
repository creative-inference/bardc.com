New Vegas Lounge

New Vegas Lounge

Category: Lounges, Live Music Venues, Blues Bars

Neighborhood: Logan Circle

Price: $$

Address: 1415 P St NW, Washington, DC 20005

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A high-energy, historic Logan Circle staple where soulful live music and a passionate atmosphere create an unforgettable night of dancing and blues.

🍸 Best For

Live Blues and Motown, Weekend Dancing, Soulful Atmosphere, Late-Night Cocktails

🔥 Highlights

  • Electric Live Performances: Renowned for incredibly talented musicians who pour their passion into every set, covering classics from BB King and Johnny Gill to Frankie Beverly and Maze.
  • Long-Standing DC Legacy: A neighborhood fixture for over 50 years, this legendary venue recently underwent a 2021 exterior refresh to keep its iconic P Street presence looking sharp.
  • Infectious Energy: The venue is celebrated for its "best vibes" where the band takes requests and the dance floor stays packed with a crowd in a great mood.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Plan your visit for Friday or Saturday nights when the lounge is most lively and the live entertainment is at its peak.
  • Expect a modest cover charge that goes directly toward supporting the high-caliber local artists performing on stage.
