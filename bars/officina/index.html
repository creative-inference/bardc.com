Officina

Officina

Category: Italian, Cocktail Bars, Upscale

Neighborhood: The Wharf

Price:

Address: 1120 Maine Ave SW, Washington, DC 20024, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated tri-level Italian sanctuary blending midcentury art deco design with a lively, upscale energy that transitions seamlessly from a sunny marketplace to an elegant rooftop lounge.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, professional business dinners, sunset cocktails, weekend drag brunch, and special milestone celebrations.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Tri-Level Experience: Enjoy a unique culinary journey across three floors, featuring a street-level gourmet market and cafe, a chic second-floor trattoria, and a stunning year-round rooftop bar with scenic views.
  • Michelin-Inspired Italian: Conceived by Chef Nicholas Stefanelli, the menu features artisanal house-made pastas like Bucatini All’Amatriciana and Tagliatelle Bolognese, alongside high-end dry-aged ribeye and fresh seafood.
  • Curated Wine & Spirits: Explore a world-class wine collection featuring rare vintages and a sophisticated cocktail program crafted by a professional, attentive bar team.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended for the Trattoria and Rooftop dining areas; an elevator is available to ensure easy access to all three levels.
  • Check out the vibrant happy hour and the popular Sunday family-style dinner menu for a curated taste of authentic Italian heritage.
