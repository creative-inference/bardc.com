✨ Vibe

Moreland’s Tavern is a warm, rustic neighborhood cornerstone that beautifully blends historic DC roots with a cozy, two-story community atmosphere perfect for locals and visitors alike.

🍸 Best For

Neighborhood hangouts, private events and rehearsal dinners, local craft beer enthusiasts, and family-friendly dining.

🔥 Highlights

  • Elevated Comfort Fare: The menu features high-quality American classics, including a standout customizable burger and an innovative, highly-praised vegan "crab cake" made from lion’s mane mushrooms.
  • Premier Event Space: The spacious second floor is a dedicated venue for private gatherings, offering personalized touches like custom slideshows and a seamless transition from sit-down dinners to casual mixers.
  • Local Flavor & History: Named after a Civil War-era tavern, this spot celebrates the area’s heritage with a rotating selection of local craft beers and seasonal cocktails like the pumpkin-spiced White Russian.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • This resilient neighborhood staple remains a community favorite for indoor dining Tuesday through Sunday, even as they navigate local street improvements.
  • For those planning a celebration, their team is highly recommended for creating budget-friendly, high-impact private events with exceptional hospitality.
