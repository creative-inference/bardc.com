✨ Vibe

Moonraker is a sophisticated rooftop oasis atop the Pendry Hotel, blending chic Japanese-inspired design with breathtaking, 360-degree panoramic views of the Potomac River and DC skyline.

🍸 Best For

Sunset cocktails, celebratory toasts, upscale dates, late-night drinks with live DJs, and groups looking for premium waterfront scenery.

🔥 Highlights

  • Unparalleled vistas of the National Mall and Potomac River from the 14th-floor terrace, featuring cozy outdoor fire pits perfect for year-round lounging.
  • An elevated Japanese-themed menu featuring standout dishes like the fan-favorite hamachi puffs, Tokyo fried chicken, and a curated library of rare Japanese whiskies.
  • A glamorous circular bar that serves as the centerpiece for a refined late-night atmosphere, often complemented by live music and a stylish, high-energy crowd.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended for prime sunset seating, though the stylish secondary bar area offers a great spot to grab a drink while waiting for a table.
  • Keep an eye out for unique "arrival experiences," as guests have frequently enjoyed complimentary sake samples during the elevator ride up to the lounge.
