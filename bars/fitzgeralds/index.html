Fitzgerald's

Category: Sports Bar, Japanese, Irish Pub

Price: $$

Address: 1990 M St NW, Washington, DC 20036

An upscale sports bar in the West End, Fitzgerald's offers a unique blend of Irish hospitality and Japanese cuisine. The multi-level space features numerous TVs, a traditional bar, and a menu that includes everything from sushi to classic pub fare.
