Fitzgerald's

Fitzgerald's

Category: Cocktail Bars, Happy Hour Spots, Lounges

Neighborhood: Georgetown

Price: $$

Address: 1990 M St NW, Washington, DC 20036

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated, mahogany-clad sanctuary that blends vintage sporting charm with a sleek, modern polish, creating an inviting and intimate atmosphere in the heart of Georgetown.

🍸 Best For

Refined date nights, upscale happy hours, intimate group gatherings, whiskey enthusiasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Expert Mixology: A cocktail-centric menu featuring perfectly balanced classics like the Manhattan alongside creative house originals and a distinguished collection of rare Japanese whiskies.
  • Elevated Fusion Fare: Innovative dishes that go beyond standard bar bites, including "best in town" lobster rolls, Peking duck burgers, and addictive Korean chicken wings.
  • Sophisticated Decor: A warm, dimly lit interior outfitted with plush club chairs, spacious corner booths, and curated collegiate memorabilia for a uniquely preppy yet cozy feel.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy Happy Hour specials every Friday and Saturday from 5:00 PM to 6:30 PM, featuring $12 signature cocktails and $7 draught beers.
  • Managed by the renowned team behind 1789 and The Tombs, ensuring high-level hospitality and a curated, top-tier experience.

Happy Hour

$12 cocktails (Martini, Old Fashioned, Manhattan, Blood Orange Lavender Margarita), $7 draught beers, and $12 select wines by the glass
