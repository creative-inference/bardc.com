Hook Hall

Category: Beer Garden, Event Space, Bar

Price: $$

Address: 3400 Georgia Ave NW, Washington, DC 20010

A massive indoor/outdoor venue in Park View, Hook Hall is known for its festive, themed pop-ups and spacious beer garden. It offers private cabanas, fire pits, a wide selection of drinks, and serves as a versatile space for everything from casual drinks to large private events.
