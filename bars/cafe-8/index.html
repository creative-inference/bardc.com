✨ Vibe

A relaxed and unpretentious neighborhood lounge that feels like a home away from home, offering a laid-back atmosphere perfect for unwinding in Southeast DC.

🍸 Best For

Hookah enthusiasts, affordable happy hours, late-night socializing, and strong, reasonably priced cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • Exceptional Service: Guests frequently rave about the attentive staff and proactive hookah management, where fresh coals are delivered seamlessly to keep the experience going.
  • Expanded Space: Recently expanded into the building next door, the venue now offers even more room to enjoy its vibrant energy and neighborhood charm.
  • Local Favorite: Known for its "Southeast soul," this spot perfectly captures the local vibe with food, drinks, and music tailored to its loyal community.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • A fantastic destination for a casual happy hour meetup with friends looking for a "Generation Y" friendly environment.
  • Open seating is often available, making it a great choice for spontaneous late-night plans.
