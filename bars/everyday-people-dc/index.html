Everyday People DC

Everyday People DC

Category: Neighborhood Bars, Hip Hop, Soul Food

Neighborhood: U Street

Price:

Address: 1013 U St NW, Washington, DC 20001, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A soulful, high-energy neighborhood lounge where 90s hip-hop nostalgia meets genuine DC hospitality and a "feel like family" atmosphere in the heart of U Street.

🍸 Best For

Nostalgic Hip Hop lovers, soulful dinner dates, signature lemon drops, and anyone seeking an authentic, welcoming community space.

🔥 Highlights

  • Recently Opened Excellence: Launched in April 2025 at the former Lounge of Three site, this spot has quickly become the talk of U Street for its vibrant energy and community-focused culture.
  • Nostalgic Soundscapes: Featuring live sets from local favorites like DJ Scientific, the music focuses on banging 90s and 2000s Hip Hop classics that keep the crowd singing along.
  • Soulful Food & Drink: From "consistently fire" soul food that feeds the spirit to their "to die for" signature Lemon Drop cocktails, the menu is crafted with love and expertise.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Founded by industry veterans Monisa and Reggie, the venue prides itself on a "neighborhood feel" where the owners frequently greet guests personally.
  • Located at 1013 U St NW, it is a perfect destination for a lively Thursday evening or a high-energy weekend night out.
