Boardwalk Bar & Arcade

Category: Arcade Bars, Waterfront, Happy Hour Spots

Price:

Website

✨ Vibe

A nostalgic beach party brought to the D.C. waterfront, blending high-energy arcade games with a vibrant, sun-soaked carnival atmosphere and stunning views of the Potomac River.

🍸 Best For

Group celebrations, happy hour, family-friendly outings, bachelor and bachelorette parties, and sunset cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • Playful Arcade & Gaming: An impressive selection of classic entertainment including Skee-ball, Dance Dance Revolution, and a whimsical claw machine where guests can win tiny rubber ducks to use as DIY drink garnishes.
  • Coastal Eats & Signature Sips: A menu packed with "seaside" favorites like Sriracha slaw dogs and loaded nachos, complemented by their famous Orange Crush cocktails, "adult happy meals," and customizable cocktail flights.
  • Premier Waterfront Setting: Boasting a massive outdoor bar and a picnic-style patio, it is the perfect destination for capturing summer vibes and enjoying scenic views along the Washington Channel.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue is exceptionally accommodating for families and children, offering a dedicated kids' menu and a welcoming environment for all ages.
  • For larger groups or special events, the spacious upstairs area provides a more private setting with dedicated bartender access and retro tabletop games.
