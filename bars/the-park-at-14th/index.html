The Park at 14th

Category: Nightclub, Restaurant, Lounge

Price: $$$

Address: 920 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20005

A sophisticated four-story restaurant and lounge downtown, The Park at 14th offers a vibrant nightlife experience that blends dining and dancing. It transforms from a happy hour spot and restaurant into a full-fledged nightclub with multiple floors of music and VIP service.
