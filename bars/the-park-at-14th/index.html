The Park at 14th

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated four-story "celebration destination" that blends modern glam with high-energy nightlife, featuring upscale Caribbean-inspired design overlooking historic Franklin Park.

🍸 Best For

Birthday celebrations, high-octane group brunches, late-night dancing, bottle service, and professional private events.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Ultimate Brunch Experience: Renowned for its legendary weekend brunch featuring unlimited favorites like "fall-off-the-bone" jerk chicken wings, blackened salmon, and buttery, "15/10" French toast.
  • Versatile Multi-Level Design: Four floors of world-class entertainment where guests can enjoy a warm, high-design dining atmosphere before transitioning into a vibrant nightlife scene with top-tier DJs.
  • Top-Tier Hospitality: A dedicated and professional staff that excels in creating a welcoming, attentive environment for special occasions and VIP celebrations.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended for the popular two-hour unlimited brunch experience on Saturdays and Sundays.
  • A 20% service charge is applied to all checks to support the venue's commitment to increased wages for the entire staff.
