The Salt Line

The Salt Line

Category: Seafood Bars, Oyster Bars, Waterfront

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price:

Address: 79 Potomac Ave SE, Washington, DC 20003, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated yet relaxed nautical retreat right on the Anacostia River, featuring a stylish dining room and an expansive, string-lit patio with stunning waterfront views.

🍸 Best For

Riverfront dates, seafood-centric happy hours, Nationals game day pre-gaming, and celebratory group dinners.

🔥 Highlights

  • Exclusive Raw Bar: Indulge in a rotating selection of fresh Chesapeake and New England oysters, including the signature "Dancing Molly" oyster available only at The Salt Line.
  • New England Classics: The menu shines with perfectly executed coastal favorites like the buttery lobster rolls, crispy Brussels sprouts, and the crowd-pleasing baked pimento crab dip.
  • Prime Waterfront Seating: Enjoy the river breeze on the outdoor corner couches or stay cozy on the heated, insulated patio designed for year-round comfort.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Visit during "Happy Afternoon" (Monday–Friday until 6 PM) for discounted local oysters and beverage specials; the bar also opens early for any weekday Nationals game.
  • Reservations are highly recommended for the main dining room, especially during the summer and on game days, though the bar and outdoor lounge offer a great casual vibe for walk-ins.

🔄 Weekly Events

⚾ Weekday Gameday Early Open

Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri

On any weekday afternoon gameday, The Salt Line opens early at least one hour prior to gametime with happy hour specials in effect.

⚾ Weekday Gameday Specials

Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri

On any weekday afternoon gameday, the restaurant opens early (at least one hour prior to gametime) and happy hour specials are in effect.

🥞 Weekend Brunch

Sat, Sun

Featuring classics like the Portuguese Egg Sandwich, Smoked Salmon Benedict, and 'The Perfect Storm' ultimate brunch cocktail (double bloody mary with shrimp, oyster, and coddie).

Happy Hour

Discounted local oysters, small bites ($5-$10), and beverage specials including $6 draft beers, $10 wines, and $12 cocktails.
