✨ Vibe

Llamabar Cafe is a stunningly designed, bohemian-chic oasis in Navy Yard that seamlessly transforms from a light-filled, tranquil workspace into a high-energy "coffee rave" destination with a vibrant social pulse.

🍸 Best For

Weekend day parties, remote work sessions, health-conscious brunching, and social networking.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Coffee Rave Experience: As a pioneer of the "coffee rave" trend, the venue hosts live DJs every Saturday, creating a high-energy, club-like atmosphere perfect for those who love EDM and social daytime energy.
  • Gourmet Menu Fusion: The kitchen serves a sophisticated blend of elevated comfort food and wellness items, including the signature Wagyu Chopped Cheese, Crabby Patty BLT, and organic, fair-trade açaí bowls.
  • Artisanal Drink Program: Beyond traditional coffee, guests can enjoy innovative creations like the Cracking Latte with melted chocolate, Cookie Butter Lattes, and a refreshing array of cold-pressed juices and protein smoothies.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Visit on a Saturday afternoon to experience the venue’s signature DJ sets and lively social scene.
  • The space is highly commuter-friendly and centrally located in Navy Yard, making the Metro or alternative transit an easy way to arrive for events.

Event Traffic Issues Dominate

Llamabar Cafe recently received support from ANC 8F for a new liquor license and entertainment endorsement to host daytime events with DJs. The establishment has agreed to implement crowd control measures to prevent guests from blocking local parking entrances and will promote alternative transportation options to mitigate traffic issues.
