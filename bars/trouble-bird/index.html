Trouble Bird

Trouble Bird

Category: Cocktail Bars, Speakeasies, Neighborhood Bars

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price:

Address: 1346 4th St SE, Washington, DC 20003, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An intimate and artsy speakeasy that seamlessly blends cozy neighborhood warmth with the high-energy pulse of Navy Yard.

🍸 Best For

Craft cocktail enthusiasts, romantic date nights, chill group hangouts, and cozy outdoor drinks by the fire.

🔥 Highlights

  • Expert Mixology: Highly skilled bartenders craft perfectly balanced drinks like the "Water Lily," "Midori Sour," and "Painkiller," with a full range of creative NA options also available.
  • Whimsical Touches: The bar is famous for its thoughtful service and the signature "little duck" that accompanies every check, adding a playful spark to the sophisticated atmosphere.
  • Cozy Outdoor Fire Pit: The patio features a welcoming fire pit, offering a relaxed and lively spot to enjoy drinks tucked away from the main street bustle.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Seating is first-come, first-serve, so arrive early to snag a spot at the bar or by the fire pit.
  • Take advantage of Happy Hour all day on Mondays, or Tuesday through Friday from 5:00 PM to 6:30 PM.
