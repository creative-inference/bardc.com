Lady Bird

Lady Bird

Category: Rooftop Bars, Cocktail Bars, Hotel Bars

Neighborhood: Dupont Circle

Price:

Address: 1315 16th St NW, Washington, DC 20036, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated rooftop sanctuary inspired by First Lady Lady Bird Johnson’s floral legacy, blending plush velvet seating and lush greenery with panoramic, unobstructed views of the DC skyline.

🍸 Best For

Golden hour cocktails, romantic date nights, chic group gatherings, and visitors seeking a picturesque monument backdrop.

🔥 Highlights

  • Stunning architectural vistas featuring clear lines of sight to the White House and the Washington Monument from the outdoor terrace.
  • An elevated menu of seasonal small plates, including standout chicken lollipops, deviled eggs, and French-inspired goat-cheese beignets.
  • Expertly crafted signature cocktails, such as the refreshing lychee martini and the classic Negroni, served by a warm and attentive team.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Securing a reservation is recommended to guarantee seating on the outdoor patio for the best sunset experience.
  • Located atop the Kimpton Banneker Hotel, this venue offers a cozy indoor-outdoor layout complete with fire pits and blankets for cooler evenings.

📰 In the News

National Park Service (.gov)

Beautification: A Legacy of Lady Bird Johnson

Lady Bird Johnson’s Beautification Project transformed Washington, D.C. through the installation of massive floral displays, the planting of urban tree canopies, and the rehabilitation of public parks. This legacy includes the creation of the Floral Library and the greening of major gateways to improve the city's environmental and aesthetic quality.
