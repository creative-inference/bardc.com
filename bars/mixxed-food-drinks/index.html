Mixxed Food & Drinks

✨ Vibe

This vibrant, Black-owned Adams Morgan hotspot delivers a high-energy, inclusive atmosphere featuring stunning graffiti-style decor and a social mixer spirit that transitions seamlessly from a lively brunch to a high-octane late-night scene.

🍸 Best For

Bottomless weekend brunch, high-energy karaoke, inclusive LGBTQ+ outings, affordable date nights, and group celebrations with 90s/2000s R&B vibes.

🔥 Highlights

  • Legendary Bottomless Brunch: Recently recognized for one of the city's best brunch experiences, guests can enjoy unlimited fruit-infused mimosas—with the peach flavor being a standout—alongside decadent Biscoff pancakes and savory shrimp and grits.
  • Elevated Comfort Food: The menu features "out of this world" bodega-style chopped cheese sandwiches (available with vegan cheese), signature smash burgers, and crispy sweet chili wings that are a local favorite.
  • Dynamic Entertainment: As a premier nightlife destination, the venue offers a "lit" atmosphere with live DJs, premium hookah service, and popular karaoke nights that keep the energy high until late.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • To enjoy the bottomless mimosa special, the entire table must participate; however, the staff is happy to provide unlimited mocktails so everyone can stay part of the vibe.
  • Located in the heart of the 18th Street corridor, the venue is conveniently situated next to a Colonial Parking garage for those looking to avoid Adams Morgan's competitive street parking.
