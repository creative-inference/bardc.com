✨ Vibe

Green Lantern is a quintessential downtown LGBTQ+ staple that masterfully blends the welcoming feel of a neighborhood dive bar with the high-energy pulse of a premier dance club.

🍸 Best For

After-work drinks, themed leather nights, high-energy dancing, budget-friendly cocktails, local Pride celebrations.

🔥 Highlights

  • A versatile dual-level layout featuring a relaxed downstairs lounge with a complimentary popcorn machine and an expansive upstairs dance floor.
  • Exciting themed events and specialty nights, including popular leather and gear parties that attract a spirited and diverse crowd.
  • An electric atmosphere known for being a go-to destination during peak city events, consistently drawing a fun and attractive crowd.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy daily Happy Hour specials from 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM, featuring $3.50 rail drinks and domestic beers.
  • Check the event calendar for regular karaoke nights and high-energy dance parties.
