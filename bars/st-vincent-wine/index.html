St. Vincent Wine

✨ Vibe

A lush, New Orleans-inspired wine garden in the heart of Petworth that blends a relaxed house-party energy with sophisticated live jazz and a serene, multi-level outdoor oasis.

🍸 Best For

Group gatherings, romantic date nights, wine enthusiasts, live jazz lovers, dog-friendly outings, and weekend brunch.

🔥 Highlights

  • A sprawling 4,000-square-foot outdoor garden featuring twinkle lights, a heated tent for year-round enjoyment, and a serene upper balcony perfect for taking in the atmosphere.
  • A unique "choose your own adventure" retail model where guests can browse an extensive global collection in the wine shop before enjoying their bottle alongside curated charcuterie and artisanal small plates.
  • Vibrant live music programming, including intimate indoor jazz performances on Mondays and frequent outdoor sets that create a premier "D.C. by way of New Orleans" experience.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • While walk-ins are enthusiastically encouraged, making a reservation is recommended for groups, especially to secure a prime spot for live music.
  • The venue features a convenient app-based ordering system and a seamless "shop-to-table" service, plus popular weekly events like $10 wine tastings on Wednesdays.

🔄 Weekly Events

Live Jazz Music 🎷

St Vincent Wine features live jazz music that can be enjoyed both indoors and in the garden courtyard.

Brunch & Mimosas 🥂

A popular brunch featuring a 'bucket of mimosa' for the table, pastry baskets with lemon poppyseed fritters, and savory dishes like frittatas and crispy potatoes.

Garden Brunch 🥂

A recurring brunch service featuring buckets of mimosas for the table, pastry baskets with warm flaky biscuits, and lemon poppyseed fritters in a dog-friendly atmosphere.

Live Jazz 🎷

Enjoy live jazz music performances in a vibrant atmosphere, available both indoors and in the outdoor garden courtyard.

Condé Nast Traveler

St. Vincent Wine — Bar Review

St. Vincent Wine offers an unpretentious and convivial atmosphere in a converted trio of 1920s row houses, focusing on off-the-beaten-path labels and funky wine finds. The beverage program prioritizes exploration over regional focus and is complemented by a selection of cheese boards and snacks from chef Sam Molavi.
