A vibrant Peruvian gastropub near Dupont Circle, Pisco y Nazca specializes in modern Peruvian cuisine, including a variety of ceviches. The bar is a central feature, serving creative cocktails with a focus on its namesake spirit, pisco, in a lively, contemporary setting.

Happy Hour

Happy Hour (Hora Loca) is offered daily from 4-7 pm with half-off house wines, beer, and liquor. At the Kendall location, there's an extended happy hour on Thursdays. Some locations have additional happy hour food specials available at the bar.
