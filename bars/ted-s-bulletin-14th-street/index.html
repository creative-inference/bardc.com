Ted's Bulletin - 14th Street

Ted's Bulletin - 14th Street

Category: Brunch Spots, American, Casual

Neighborhood: U Street

Price:

Address: 1818 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20009, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A boisterous and welcoming Art Deco-inspired retreat that blends vintage charm with modern energy, featuring brick-lined walls, classic films on the screens, and a cozy, nostalgic atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Boozy weekend brunches, family-friendly outings, indulgent late-night desserts, and casual group celebrations.

🔥 Highlights

  • Signature Ted Tarts: Famous house-made pop-tarts available in rotating flavors like strawberry and blueberry cheesecake, crafted fresh daily at this primary production hub.
  • Elevated Comfort Classics: An extensive menu featuring oversized cinnamon rolls, hearty "All Day Breakfast," and decadent favorites like steak frites and crispy calamari.
  • Boozy Milkshakes & Retro Decor: Enjoy creative adult milkshakes at the bar while surrounded by unique vintage touches, including old ticket windows from the Philadelphia Civic Center.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Making a reservation is highly recommended as this popular 14th Street corridor spot fills up quickly during peak brunch and lunch hours.
  • Take advantage of the dedicated bakery entrance for a quick grab-and-go selection of fresh doughnuts and their iconic "Ted Tarts."
