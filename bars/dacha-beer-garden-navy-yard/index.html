Dacha Beer Garden (Navy Yard)

Dacha Beer Garden (Navy Yard)

Category: Beer Gardens, Happy Hour Spots, Dog Friendly

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price:

Address: 79 Potomac Ave SE, Washington, DC 20003, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A lively and expansive waterfront destination that blends traditional Bavarian beer garden energy with chic mid-century modern "Space Race" aesthetics overlooking the Anacostia River.

🍸 Best For

Pre-game drinks for Nationals Stadium, dog-friendly weekend outings, lively group celebrations, riverfront happy hours, and family-friendly afternoon snacks.

🔥 Highlights

  • An impressive beverage program featuring 26 draft beers and 14 curated cocktails, including the fan-favorite option to enjoy your brew in a massive, iconic glass boot.
  • A multi-faceted menu of modern American comfort food and Bavarian classics, ranging from bratwurst and giant pretzels to high-quality burgers and refreshing mocktails.
  • Prime outdoor seating with spacious bench-style tables and lush greenery, providing a vibrant open-air atmosphere perfect for social meetups and community events like their weekly beer club.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Don't miss the fantastic Happy Hour specials, which feature $5 beers, $10 boots, and great deals on burgers and fries.
  • The venue offers a seamless, modern experience with QR code ordering, making it easy to grab a quick bite and a drink before heading to the stadium.
