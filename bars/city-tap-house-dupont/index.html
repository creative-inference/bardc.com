✨ Vibe

A polished yet high-energy gastropub that combines a sophisticated craft beer program with the spirited, welcoming atmosphere of a premier DC sports hub.

🍸 Best For

Game day viewing, craft beer exploration, celebratory group dinners, and upscale pub fare.

🔥 Highlights

  • Massive Craft Selection: A rotating draft list of over 30 taps featuring local DMV gems, rare "dope" beers, and seasonal winter ales for every palate.
  • Acclaimed Sports Viewing: Recently recognized as one of the best sports destinations in the city, providing an electric environment to catch every major athletic event.
  • Standout Hospitality: Renowned for attentive, friendly service that goes above and beyond, ensuring a memorable experience for birthday celebrations and group gatherings.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy a variety of specialized menus throughout the week, including daily happy hour, weekend brunch, and dedicated late-night options.
  • Located just steps from the Dupont Circle station, it offers an accessible and clean setting for a quick bite or an all-day sports marathon.
