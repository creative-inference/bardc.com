Kramerbooks & Afterwords Cafe

✨ Vibe

An iconic Dupont Circle landmark that seamlessly blends the intellectual charm of a legendary independent bookstore with the high-energy pulse of a modern, all-day bistro and cocktail bar.

🍸 Best For

Literary-themed dates, late-night cocktails, weekend brunch, and browsing for books with a drink in hand.

🔥 Highlights

  • A Culturally Storied Setting: Browse a curated collection in a venue that has served as a DC institution for over 40 years, famously hosting luminaries like Maya Angelou and Andy Warhol.
  • Elevated All-Day Dining: From "the best waffles in DC" and signature Soho omelettes to a "Super Fun High Tea," the kitchen delivers top-tier comfort food that pairs perfectly with their famous pumpkin spice lattes.
  • Revamped Bar Program: Sip on creative, pre-batched cocktails at the modernized 26-seat bar, featuring standout drinks like the Cold Brew Martini and spicy Margaritas infused with local hot honey.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • A true late-night destination, the venue stays open until 3:00 AM on Friday and Saturday nights for night owls and book lovers alike.
  • While the bookstore is a staple, the All Day by Kramers restaurant accepts reservations, which are highly recommended for their popular Sunday brunch.
