✨ Vibe

District Alley is a high-energy, intimate boutique lounge on the U Street corridor that blends the charm of D.C.’s historic alleyways with a vibrant, "feel-like-family" social atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Premium hookah service, high-energy DJ sets, happy hour cocktails, and late-night dancing.

🔥 Highlights

  • Exceptional Soundscape: Renowned for its live DJs who maintain a perfect vibe all night, making it a top destination for those who want to dance and enjoy the best music in the city.
  • Attentive "Family" Service: Guests consistently rave about the personalized and welcoming service, featuring proactive hookah refills and friendly staff that make every visitor feel like a regular.
  • Intimate Tavern Charm: Located slightly off the beaten path, this "hidden gem" features a cozy indoor setting for 50 guests and a beautiful seasonal summer garden for open-air socializing.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Be sure to arrive early to take advantage of their popular happy hour specials and secure a spot in this buzzy, intimate space.
  • Keep an eye on their event calendar for special themed nights and RSVP opportunities to guarantee entry during busy weekend sets.

Happy Hour

Happy Hour options are available everyday and are designed to encourage a networking atmosphere for young professionals.

District Alley has been blocked from opening as a 1,200-person nightclub and strip club following a ruling by the D.C. Court of Appeals. The court found that a restrictive covenant prevents the building, located next to JPMorgan’s D.C. headquarters, from being used for such entertainment purposes.
