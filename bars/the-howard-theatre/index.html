The Howard Theatre

The Howard Theatre

Category: Live Music Venue, Historic Theatre, Supper Club

Price: $$$

Address: 620 T St NW, Washington, DC 20001

A historic landmark in the Shaw neighborhood, the beautifully restored Howard Theatre has hosted legendary Black artists since 1910. Today, it operates as a concert hall and supper club, offering a full food and drink menu during performances by a diverse lineup of musicians and entertainers.
