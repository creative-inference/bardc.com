Franklin's Restaurant, Brewery and General Store

Franklin's Restaurant, Brewery and General Store

Category: Brewpub, American Restaurant, General Store

Price: $$

Address: 5123 Baltimore Ave, Hyattsville, MD 20781

A unique destination in Hyattsville, Maryland, Franklin's combines a restaurant, a craft brewery, and a quirky general store all under one roof. The family-friendly brewpub serves a wide variety of house-brewed beers and classic American fare in a bustling, cavernous space.
