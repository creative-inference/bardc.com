Residents Cafe & Bar

Price:

Website

✨ Vibe

Tucked into a historic Dupont Circle row house, this immigrant-founded oasis offers a "casual-chic" multi-sensory experience where lush botanical greenery, curated house playlists, and stylish design create a welcoming, home-away-from-home atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Michelin Bib Gourmand dining, Instagrammable brunch dates, seasonal outdoor cocktails, and high-value multi-course meals.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Cuisine: A Michelin Bib Gourmand recipient celebrated for its hyper-seasonal, globally inspired menu featuring standout dishes like charred octopus with red chermoula and vibrant watermelon and heirloom tomato salad.
  • Exceptional Beverage Program: Home to a renowned espresso martini and a creative cocktail selection that perfectly complements its sophisticated yet approachable dining experience.
  • Immersive Seasonal Decor: Known for its stunning patio transformations, including immersive floral displays and themed menus during Washington D.C.’s iconic Cherry Blossom season.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly encouraged for both the indoor dining room and the popular heated patio to ensure a spot at this local favorite.
  • The venue offers an affordable fine-dining experience, with multi-course meals frequently available for under $60.

🔄 Weekly Events

🥞 Weekend Brunch

Sat, Sun

A casual-chic brunch experience featuring seasonal ingredients. Highlights include French Toast Bites, Smashed Avocado Toast, Turkish Eggs, and signature brunch cocktails like the Chai Espresso Martini.

Brunch 🥂

A comprehensive brunch service featuring signature dishes like French Toast Bites, Turkish Eggs, Smashed Avocado Toast, and the Residents Smash Burger, accompanied by specialized brunch cocktails like the Chai Espresso Martini and Lavender Bee.

Happy Hour

$8 house wine and bubbles, $9 cocktails including Espresso Martinis and Moscow Mules, and $8-$18 bites such as pea fritters, lamb meatballs, and chicken schnitzel.
