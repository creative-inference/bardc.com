✨ Vibe

A sophisticated, Snarkitecture-designed cultural hub that seamlessly blends a high-end barbershop and designer boutique with an intimate, neon-lit speakeasy aesthetic.

🍸 Best For

Intimate dates, craft cocktail aficionados, stylish grooming sessions, designer shopping, creative networking

🔥 Highlights

  • Multi-Concept Excellence: This unique hybrid destination offers a "Socializing, Selfcare, and Style" experience where you can shop curated luxury apparel and enjoy premium grooming services before heading to the hidden bar.
  • Bespoke Beverage Program: The speakeasy, "Out of Office," features an inventive cocktail menu categorized by "vibes," with expert bartenders who specialize in crafting personalized off-menu drinks and premium mocktails.
  • Design-Forward Atmosphere: Featuring a sleek interior with terrazzo countertops and signature archways, the venue’s prestige is further cemented by its recent high-profile expansion into a massive sibling location in Union Market.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended for the speakeasy and grooming services, as the intimate space fills up quickly, especially on weekends.
  • The venue transitions from a specialty coffee shop featuring Black Acres Roastery beans during the day to a refined cocktail lounge in the evening.
