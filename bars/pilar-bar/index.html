✨ Vibe

Inspired by Ernest Hemingway’s global travels, this nautical-themed two-story haunt offers a cozy, soul-filled atmosphere that transitions seamlessly from a relaxed neighborhood hang to a high-energy nightlife destination.

🍸 Best For

Bottomless Brunch, Craft Cocktails, After-Work Socials, Late-Night Dancing

🔥 Highlights

  • Hemingway-Inspired Refresh: Experience a newly revitalized space and menu celebrating the writer’s travels, featuring house-made Cubanos, whole fried rockfish, and sophisticated rum-spiked espresso martinis.
  • Acclaimed Brunch Scene: A local favorite for weekend celebrations, boasting a variety of flavored bottomless mimosas—including pomegranate and blackberry—and standout dishes like blueberry lemon ricotta pancakes.
  • Award-Winning Pop-Ups: The venue keeps the energy high with frequent high-profile collaborations, such as recent kitchen takeovers by award-winning partners like Taqueria Habanero.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Open until 2:00 AM on weekdays and 3:00 AM on weekends, making it a staple for those looking to keep the night going on the 14th Street corridor.
  • Reservations are highly encouraged for the popular weekend brunch service, which runs Saturdays and Sundays from 12:00 PM to 4:00 PM.
