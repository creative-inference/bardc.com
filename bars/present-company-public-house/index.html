Present Company

Present Company

Category: Neighborhood Bars, Burger Bars, Brunch Spots

Neighborhood: Gallery Place / Chinatown

Price:

Address: 438 Massachusetts Ave NW, Washington, DC 20001, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Housed in a historic 1855 firehouse, this neighborhood gem offers an unpretentious yet cool atmosphere with perfectly leveled background music and high-quality pub energy.

🍸 Best For

Casual dates, weekend brunch, group trivia, craft beer enthusiasts, and savoring one of the city's top-rated burgers.

🔥 Highlights

  • A standout culinary menu featuring what locals call the "best burger in DC," spicy Nashville hot chicken, and a legendary, "impossible to share" cinnamon roll.
  • An exceptional bottomless brunch every Saturday and Sunday where guests can enjoy 90 minutes of unlimited mimosas, Bloody Marys, or Miller High Lifes for just $20 with an entree.
  • A robust beverage program boasting over 50 beer selections and creative original cocktails like the refreshing Cucumber Smash.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Table reservations on Resy are highly recommended for the popular "Trivia Tuesdays" hosted by Geeks Who Drink.
  • Happy Hour runs Wednesday through Friday from 5–7 PM and features extended weekend hours starting at 3 PM on Saturdays and Sundays.

🔄 Weekly Events

🥂 Bottomless Brunch

Sat, Sun

Available for $20 with the purchase of an entree. Choose from unlimited Mimosas, Bloody Mary's, or Miller Highlifes for 90 minutes.

🧠 Trivia Tuesdays

Tue

Hosted by Geeks Who Drink. Trivia is held inside only and fills up quickly, so reservations via Resy are recommended.

Georgetown Law Student Discount ⚖️

Sun

Georgetown Law Students receive 10% off their checks every Sunday.

Happy Hour

Happy Hour menu featuring various appetizers, tacos, and snacks including wings, nachos, and pimento tater tots.
