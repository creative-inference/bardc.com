Duke's Grocery

Duke's Grocery

Category: Gastropubs, Burger Bars, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: Dupont Circle

Price: $$

Address: 1513 17th St NW, Washington, DC 20036

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

This cozy, East London-inspired neighborhood pub blends high-energy gastropub charm with a friendly, welcoming atmosphere that makes everyone feel like a regular.

🍸 Best For

Award-winning burgers, bottomless mimosa brunch, relaxed patio dining, and lively happy hours.

🔥 Highlights

  • Ten-Year Award Winner: Home to the legendary "Proper Burger," a signature masterpiece of Angus beef and Gouda that has been voted the Best Burger in DC for ten consecutive years.
  • British Gastropub Classics: Beyond the burgers, enjoy an authentic menu featuring white truffle macaroni and cheese, fish and chips, and fresh "Golden Grain" salads prepared-to-order.
  • Exceptional Service & Atmosphere: Known for its attentive staff and cozy interior, the venue offers a peaceful patio perfect for enjoying the Dupont Circle neighborhood.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Visit during Happy Hour for fantastic deals on signature food and drinks, or stop by for their phenomenal bottomless mimosa brunch service.
  • The patio is a neighborhood favorite; while pups are welcome, DC Health Code requires four-legged friends to remain outside the patio railing.
