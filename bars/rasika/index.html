Rasika

Rasika

Category: Indian, Restaurant, Fine Dining

Price: $$$

Address: 633 D St NW, Washington, DC 20004

One of D.C.'s most acclaimed and influential restaurants, Rasika offers a modern and elegant approach to Indian cuisine. With locations in Penn Quarter and the West End, it's famous for signature dishes like palak chaat, setting the standard for fine Indian dining in the city.
