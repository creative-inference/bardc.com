Category: Brewery, Taproom

Price: $$

Address: 327 S St NE, Washington, DC 20002

Housed in a historic former Nabisco factory near Union Market, Lost Generation Brewing Company focuses on classic, old-world beer styles. Their beautiful, spacious taproom offers a wide range of lagers and ales in a setting that pays homage to the building's industrial past.
