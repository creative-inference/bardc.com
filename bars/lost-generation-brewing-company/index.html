Lost Generation Brewing Company

Lost Generation Brewing Company

Category: Breweries, Taproom, Dog Friendly

Neighborhood: NoMa

Price: $$

Address: 327 S St NE, Washington, DC 20002

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Housed in a historic former Nabisco factory along the Metropolitan Branch Trail, this woman- and minority-owned brewery blends a 1920s speakeasy aesthetic with literary charm and a warm, "low-tech" community energy.

🍸 Best For

Post-trail bike rides, dog-friendly hangouts, literary enthusiasts, live jazz fans, Sunday trivia, and group celebrations.

🔥 Highlights

  • Literary-Inspired Brews & Vintage Decor: Enjoy meticulously crafted lagers and IPAs—including the "Grave Shift" dark lager—in a space decorated with jazz-age antiques, Gertrude Stein quotes, and a nostalgic atmosphere perfect for reading.
  • Premier Community Events: Recently recognized as the "Best Brewery on the MBT," the venue hosts free live jazz on Saturday nights, popular Sunday trivia with $75 prizes, and serves as the official craft beer partner for WorldPride 2025.
  • Expert Draft Experience: The taproom features a specialized Lukr side-pull faucet system for authentic European-style pours, alongside a diverse selection of sours, seltzers, and traditional German digestifs.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Stop by on Sundays for trivia at 7 PM to take advantage of $5 "Shift" beer specials starting at 6 PM.
  • The venue is highly dog-friendly and perfectly situated for a 20-minute walk from the NoMa-Gallaudet U Metro station along the street-art-filled Metropolitan Branch Trail.
