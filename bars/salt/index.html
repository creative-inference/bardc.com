Salt

Category: Cocktail Bar, Speakeasy

Price: $$$

Address: 4000 Wilson Blvd Suite C, Arlington, VA 22203

Hidden within The Salt Line restaurant in Ballston, Salt is an intimate, 18-seat speakeasy-style cocktail bar. It offers a more focused and elevated drinking experience than the main restaurant, with a menu of classic and inventive cocktails in a cozy, exclusive setting.
