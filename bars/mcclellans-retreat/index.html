McClellan's Retreat

McClellan's Retreat

Category: Cocktail Bars, Speakeasies, Neighborhood Bars

Neighborhood: Dupont Circle

Price: $$

Address: 2031 Florida Ave NW, Washington, DC 20009

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Intimate and evocative, this Civil War-themed "officers' retreat" features dim lantern lighting, flannel banquettes, and a cozy speakeasy atmosphere that makes it a quintessential Dupont Circle neighborhood favorite.

🍸 Best For

Whiskey connoisseurs, intimate date nights, creative seasonal cocktails, and relaxed happy hours with friends or colleagues.

🔥 Highlights

  • Expertly Crafted Rotating Menus: Recently hailed as one of the best bars in DC, the venue features a beverage program that evolves every six weeks, offering everything from era-inspired classics to elaborate summer tiki drinks.
  • Unrivaled Spirits Selection: Guests can explore an extensive library of barrel-aged spirits, rare whiskeys, and unique rums, all navigated by a knowledgeable bar team known for their incredible bartending skills and personalized recommendations.
  • Charming Bar Bites: The menu pairs high-quality drinks with delightful snacks, including white cheddar grilled cheese, curated charcuterie boards, and their signature whale crackers.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue maintains a dedicated seating policy to ensure every guest has a stool or table, preserving a relaxed and comfortable environment for conversation.
  • Don't miss their legendary Happy Hour, which features a unique house punch that changes every single day.

Happy Hour

$8 Old Fashioneds and Hammonds, $9 Palomas and Aperitivo Spritzes, and $10 Martinis.
