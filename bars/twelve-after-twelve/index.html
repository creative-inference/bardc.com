✨ Vibe

Twelve After Twelve is a sophisticated and transcendent multi-level sanctuary that masterfully blends the legacy of a classic DC lounge with modern, upscale design and world-class sound.

🍸 Best For

Live Salsa nights, multi-genre music fans, upscale group celebrations, and late-night dancing on a climate-controlled outdoor patio.

🔥 Highlights

  • Expansive Multi-Sensory Design: A sprawling 8,000-square-foot venue featuring four distinct levels, five bars, and a stunning outdoor lounge with a retractable roof and floral-themed art installations.
  • Elite Sound & Performance: Outfitted with premier Das Audio and Funktion-One sound systems to showcase a diverse rotation of live jazz, funk, soul, and world-class DJ sets.
  • Musical Diversity: A true "musical haven" offering everything from high-energy Thursday night Salsa bands to weekend lineups featuring acid jazz, house, and deep grooves.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • This is a 21+ upscale venue where guests are encouraged to explore all four floors to experience the unique character and decor of each space.
  • Table service and bottle menus are available for those looking for a more intimate, high-energy experience in the heart of Dupont Circle.
