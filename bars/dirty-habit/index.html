Dirty Habit

Dirty Habit

Category: Cocktail Bars, Brunch Spots, Hotel Bars

Neighborhood: Penn Quarter

Price: $$$

Address: 555 8th St NW, Washington, DC 20004

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Tucked away in the historic 1841 General Post Office building, this film noir-inspired venue offers a chic, modern escape with a sleek, dim-lit interior and an expansive, fire-pit-adorned urban courtyard.

🍸 Best For

Lively sing-along brunches, stylish date nights, dog-friendly "Yappy Hour" gatherings, and sophisticated pre-game cocktails across from Capital One Arena.

🔥 Highlights

  • New Culinary Direction: Recently appointed Executive Chef Derek Watson (formerly of Rose’s Luxury and Morimoto) has introduced a bold, globally-inspired menu featuring a creative fusion of Japanese, Latin, and American Southern flavors.
  • Cutting-Edge Bar Program: Experience a progressive drink menu known for inventive creations like "The Cheesy Grin"—a savory-sweet cocktail featuring Parmigiano-Reggiano foam—alongside their famous signature Brunch Punch and bottomless mimosas.
  • Premier Urban Oasis: The sprawling, wraparound courtyard is one of DC’s top outdoor spots, hosting high-energy events like Tasting Tuesdays and "Riviera Royale" patio activations with shared roulette-style tasting plates.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of the weekday happy hour from 3 PM to 7 PM, featuring $9 glasses of wine and $13 signature margaritas and old-fashioneds.
  • Reservations are strongly recommended for the high-energy Sing-Along Brunch every Sunday from 12 PM to 4 PM, featuring live DJs spinning R&B and pop favorites.
