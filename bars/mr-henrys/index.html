Mr. Henry's

Mr. Henry's

Category: Live Music Venues, Jazz Clubs, American

Neighborhood: Capitol Hill

Price: $$

Address: 601 Pennsylvania Ave SE, Washington, DC 20003

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A charming and nostalgic Capitol Hill landmark where vintage decor meets a soul-stirring jazz history, creating a cozy "everyone knows your name" neighborhood atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Live jazz performances, classic American comfort food, historic D.C. sightseeing, casual patio dining, and intimate musical evenings.

🔥 Highlights

  • A Musical Legend’s Legacy: Recently celebrated as the historic venue where Roberta Flack was discovered, it features a dedicated second-floor music room built specifically for her rise to fame, complete with signature church-pew seating.
  • Acclaimed Burgers & Bites: Frequently praised for having some of the city's best burgers and patty melts, complemented by crispy tater tots and fan-favorite buffalo chicken dip.
  • Warm Community Spirit: Known for its exceptionally tenured staff and welcoming environment, offering both a nostalgic indoor setting and a breezy outdoor patio perfect for soaking in the Pennsylvania Avenue energy.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Live music events at "Upstairs @ Mr. Henry's" often feature world-class jazz talent; tickets are recommended for these intimate performances.
  • Conveniently located near the Eastern Market Metro, the venue offers a popular half-priced burger night and serves food until the kitchen closes at 10:00 PM.

Mr. Henry's in Washington, D.C.

Mr. Henry's is a historic Capitol Hill landmark that has operated continuously since 1966, famously serving as the venue where Roberta Flack launched her career. This neighborhood staple features Victorian-inspired decor and continues to host live jazz performances on its refurbished second floor alongside a menu of pub favorites and signature cocktails.
