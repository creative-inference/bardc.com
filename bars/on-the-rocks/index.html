✨ Vibe

On The Rocks is a dynamic H Street destination where a chill first-floor lounge meets a high-energy second-floor party scene, topped off with a stylish rooftop perfect for social gatherings.

🍸 Best For

Late-night dancing, hookah enthusiasts, flavorful Cajun-style dining, birthday celebrations, and energetic live performances.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Famous Pasta: A must-try local favorite, their Seafood Cajun Pasta features a rich cream sauce with peppers and spinach that regulars find absolutely addictive.
  • Multi-Level Entertainment: The venue offers a versatile experience with a relaxed main floor for dining and a vibrant upstairs lounge that hosts private events and talented performers.
  • Flavor-Packed Menu: Beyond the signature pasta, guests rave about the jerk shrimp, shrimp tacos, and a specialized brunch menu featuring chicken and waffles.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Stop by for Happy Hour from 6:00 PM to 9:00 PM for great deals on sliders and wings, or visit on Tuesdays for their popular $2 taco specials.
  • The rooftop and second-floor lounge are available for private event rentals, making it a go-to spot for curated celebrations and parties.
