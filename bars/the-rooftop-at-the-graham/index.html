The Rooftop at the Graham

Category: Rooftop Bars, Cocktail Bars, Hotel Bars

Neighborhood: Georgetown

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated yet laid-back open-air lounge offering breathtaking panoramic views of the DC skyline and an energetic, welcoming atmosphere in the heart of historic Georgetown.

🍸 Best For

Sunset cocktails, group gatherings, scenic sightseeing, upscale date nights

🔥 Highlights

  • Stunning 360-degree views overlooking the Potomac River, the Kennedy Center, and the city's iconic monuments.
  • A chic and comfortable setting featuring plush couches, high-top tables, and stylish decor perfect for lingering with friends.
  • A curated selection of signature craft cocktails and light bites served with quick, attentive service in a luxury boutique hotel setting.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The rooftop opens at 3:30 PM Monday through Saturday and 2:00 PM on Sundays; arriving early is highly recommended to secure a prime spot for the sunset.
  • This popular destination features a mix of open-air and covered sections, making it a reliable choice for enjoying Georgetown's skyline in various conditions.
