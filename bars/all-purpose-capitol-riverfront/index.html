All-Purpose Capitol Riverfront

All-Purpose Capitol Riverfront

Category: Pizza Bars, Rooftop Bars, Waterfront

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price:

Address: 79 Potomac Ave SE, Washington, DC 20003, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A scenic and lively waterfront destination that blends the comfort of a classic Italian-American pizzeria with breathtaking views of the Anacostia River.

🍸 Best For

Pre-game gatherings, scenic rooftop drinks, casual waterfront dates, and family dinners.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Pizza: Recognized as a leader in D.C.’s pizza renaissance, the kitchen serves deck-oven pies featuring a signature 72-hour fermented dough and creative toppings like New Jersey clams or brunch-ready eggs.
  • Prime Riverfront Views: The venue features both a spacious outdoor patio and a popular rooftop deck, offering some of the best scenic dining vistas in the Navy Yard.
  • Inventive Beverage Program: Enjoy a refreshing selection of "Frozies," house-made "Dirty Shirleys," and nostalgic Italian Water Ice cocktails inspired by classic Nintendo characters.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Ideally located near Nationals Park and Audi Field, making it a favorite for sports fans; reservations are recommended on game days and during nice weather.
  • The restaurant utilizes a service-inclusive model where staff are paid directly by the employer, meaning additional tipping is not expected.
