Silver Social

Silver Social

Category: Rooftop Bars, Sports Bars, Cocktail Bars

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price:

Address: 1250 Half St SE, Washington, DC 20003, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An upscale, 21-and-over rooftop oasis in the heart of Navy Yard, offering a sophisticated "American tapas" experience with stunning bird's-eye views of Nationals Park from a sleek, climate-controlled terrace.

🍸 Best For

Pre-game cocktails, rooftop weekend brunch, post-concert celebrations, and stylish group social gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Premier Stadium Views: The 1,700-square-foot heated outdoor terrace provides one of the most picturesque vantage points in the city, looking directly over Nationals Park.
  • Elevated Shareable Plates: The menu features high-quality "American tapas" like crispy Old Bay wings, Korean tacos, and grilled shrimp flatbreads, all developed through local focus group research.
  • Top-Tier Service & Spirits: Known for its exceptionally attentive staff and craft cocktail program, including fan favorites like the $7 game-day lemon drops and perfectly balanced mezcal creations.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Silver Social is strictly a 21+ venue, providing a mature and elevated lounge atmosphere situated directly above the all-ages Silver Diner.
  • Look for special game-day pricing on signature cocktails and seasonal selections during Nationals home games.
