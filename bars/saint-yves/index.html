Saint Yves

Saint Yves

Category: Cocktail Bars, Lounges, Nightclub

Neighborhood: Dupont Circle

Price:

Address: 1220 Connecticut Ave NW, Washington, DC 20036, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated 5,000-square-foot "clubstaurant" that blends historic elegance with high-energy nightlife, featuring striking design elements like custom art installations and soaring arched windows in the heart of Dupont Circle.

🍸 Best For

Luxury bottle service, high-profile social events, professional networking, gourmet happy hours, and late-night dancing.

🔥 Highlights

  • Stunning Aesthetic: The venue features a breathtaking interior design characterized by a custom gold chain chandelier, a signature ceiling tulle sculpture, and elegant vintage glassware.
  • High-Profile Event Hub: A magnet for D.C.'s influential professional and creative classes, Saint Yves recently made headlines for hosting massive, high-profile celebrations featuring major talent like Rick Ross.
  • Inclusive & Diverse Programming: Under the vision of mogul David S. Chung, the club offers a vibrant rotation of themed nights ranging from hip-hop and electronic dance to international and LGBTQ+ events.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of the weekday happy hour for a refined experience featuring gourmet snacks, caviar service, and specialty cocktails.
  • For those looking for an exclusive experience, the venue is known for its high-end table service and rigorous professional management standards.
