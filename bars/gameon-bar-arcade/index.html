GameOn bar+arcade

GameOn bar+arcade

Category: Arcade Bars, Cocktail Bars, Game Bars

Neighborhood: U Street

Price:

Address: 1330 U St NW, Washington, DC 20009, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A high-energy, nostalgic playground in the heart of U Street that seamlessly blends classic 80s and 90s arcade charm with a modern, social bar atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Nostalgic date nights, group outings, social gaming leagues, and retro arcade enthusiasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Extensive Gaming Collection: Spread across two levels and 3,400 square feet, the venue features over 20 classic cabinets and favorites like Ms. Pac-Man, Galaga, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, and pinball.
  • Creative Themed Cocktails: Enjoy a unique drink menu featuring playful creations like the "Smores Old Fashion," the "Reboot," and fan-favorite "Butter Beer" alongside a full kitchen menu.
  • Social Competition Hub: Recently ranked among the top 21 arcades in the U.S., the venue is the official home of DC Fray’s winter skeeball league, hosting community-focused competitions every Tuesday night.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Look out for daily specials and rotating game titles to keep your high-score hunting fresh.
  • Located conveniently near the U Street Metro in the former Fainting Goat space, offering two bars to ensure quick service during busy weekends.
