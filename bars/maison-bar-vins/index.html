Maison Bar À Vins

Maison Bar À Vins

Category: Wine Bars, French, Romantic

Neighborhood: Adams Morgan

Price:

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated yet intimate French sanctuary housed in a moody, candle-lit Victorian row home that pairs historic brownstone elegance with a warm, homey atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Romantic dates, organic wine enthusiasts, pre-dinner cocktails, and refined solo dining at the bar.

🔥 Highlights

  • World-Class Wine Program: Recently opened by the acclaimed Popal Group, this destination features an ambitious cellar of over 1,000 labels with a focus on organic and biodynamic vintages.
  • Chef-Driven French Fare: Beyond the wine, the kitchen serves elevated classics like brioche-stuffed roast chicken, monkfish with crispy capers, and their "must-order" warm bread and butter.
  • Artful Attention to Detail: From the custom-logo wine glasses to the high-end Le Labo products in the downstairs lounge, every inch of the three-story venue is meticulously curated for a luxury experience.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • While the venue offers a full dining experience, the bar is a prime spot for walk-ins and watching the expert staff craft gin martinis and provide spot-on wine recommendations.
  • Located in the heart of Adams Morgan, it serves as a more casual, accessible sibling to the award-winning Lutèce.
