Atomic Billiards

Atomic Billiards

Category: Pool Halls, Dive Bars, Game Bars

Neighborhood: Cleveland Park

Price: $

Address: 3427 Connecticut Ave NW, Washington, DC 20008

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A cozy and dependable subterranean neighborhood anchor, Atomic Billiards offers a welcoming "basement-energy" atmosphere perfect for unwinding with friends over classic bar games.

🍸 Best For

Casual dates, neighborhood hangouts, solo nights out, and competitive billiards enthusiasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Extensive Gaming Variety: Beyond the well-spaced 8-foot pool tables, the venue features professional dart boards, shuffleboard, and a renowned vintage jukebox for the ultimate low-key entertainment experience.
  • Inclusive Community Hub: Since 1993, this basement gem has been a cornerstone of Cleveland Park, proudly hosting the gay Stonewall Billiards league on Mondays and APA leagues on Tuesdays.
  • Generous Membership Perks: Frequent visitors can take advantage of an annual membership program that offers incredible value, including free daily play before 7 p.m. and exclusive bar tab discounts.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Happy Hour specials run daily from 4 p.m. to 7 p.m. on select drafts, rail cocktails, and house wines, with pool play prorated by the minute so you only pay for the time you use.
  • Open 361 days a year, this "Libation Station" focuses on drinks and games; while food is limited to snacks like chips, the surrounding neighborhood offers plenty of nearby dining options.
