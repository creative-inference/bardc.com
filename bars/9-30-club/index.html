9:30 Club

9:30 Club

Category: Live Music Venues, Nightclubs, Historic

Neighborhood: U Street

Price: $$

Address: 815 V St NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A legendary and intimate historic treasure that feels like a "second home" for both world-class artists and fans, recently honored as the 2025 Pollstar Nightclub of the Year.

🍸 Best For

Live music enthusiasts, seeing arena-level bands in a personal setting, high-energy themed dance parties, and indie rock showcases.

🔥 Highlights

  • World-Class Sound & Sightlines: Highly praised by audio experts for its exceptional acoustics and a multi-level layout that ensures every spot in the house feels close to the stage.
  • Rich Musical Heritage: A cornerstone of DC culture that has evolved from an integrated jazz hall hosting Duke Ellington to an iconic stage for legends like Nirvana and the Red Hot Chili Peppers.
  • Full-Service Concert Experience: Beyond the music, guests enjoy professional and friendly staff, a dedicated record and memorabilia collection, and a solid menu of refreshments and food.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Skip the online service fees by purchasing tickets in person at the venue box office during their weekday hours (12:00 pm – 7:00 pm).
  • The venue is all general admission, so arriving early is the best way to snag a prime spot on the balcony or right at the stage rail.
