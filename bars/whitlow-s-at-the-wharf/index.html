Whitlow’s at the Wharf

Whitlow’s at the Wharf

Category: Sports Bars, Burger Bars, Waterfront

Neighborhood: The Wharf

Price:

Address: 949 Wharf St SW, Washington, DC 20024, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A high-energy waterfront social hub featuring buzzing crowds, scenic river views, and an upbeat atmosphere perfect for any occasion.

🍸 Best For

Sports fans, waterfront cocktails, post-monument walks, pre-concert gatherings, and people-watching on the patio.

🔥 Highlights

  • Exceptional Hospitality: Renowned for top-tier service, the management and staff are celebrated for their attentive, friendly, and "masterclass" approach to guest experience.
  • Prime Wharf Location: Boasts a spacious waterside patio offering some of the best river views and people-watching in the District.
  • Craft Drinks & Comfort Food: Features an impressive beer and cocktail list perfectly paired with crowd-pleasing favorites like signature burgers and loaded nachos.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Conveniently located within a scenic 15-minute walk of the Jefferson Memorial, making it the ideal reward after a day of sightseeing.
  • Ideally situated in the heart of The Wharf’s entertainment district, it is a go-to destination for pre-show food and drinks before heading to a nearby concert.
