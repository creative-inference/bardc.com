Mercy Me

Mercy Me

Category: Hotel Bars, Lounges, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: Foggy Bottom

Price: $$$

Address: 1143 New Hampshire Ave NW, Washington, DC 20037

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sleek and magnetic "sorta South American" lounge where modern hotel chic meets vibrant Latin-inspired energy and soulful live music.

🍸 Best For

Swanky happy hours, milestone birthday celebrations, intimate conversations in plush lounge seating, and seasonal outdoor escapes.

🔥 Highlights

  • Refined Latin Cuisine: A revamped menu led by Culinary Director Robert Curtis featuring bold, "sorta South American" flavors like feijoada balls, choripan, and Peruvian chicken.
  • El Chalet Pop-Up: A stunning Patagonia-inspired patio experience featuring crackling fire pits, hearty regional cuisine, and a festive menu of hot cocktails.
  • Immersive Soundtrack: An elevated atmosphere powered by live samba music and a dedicated private vinyl listening room for a unique auditory experience.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located within the Yours Truly DC hotel, this venue offers versatile spaces including cozy couch spots and a stylish bar area that are perfect for groups.
  • Reservations are recommended for both the main lounge and the seasonal outdoor chalet to secure the best seating for live performances.

🏔️ El Chalet Winter Pop-Up

Daily
