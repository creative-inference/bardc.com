Lupo Verde

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Lupo Verde is a lively and rustic two-story Italian osteria that blends the charm of a neighborhood cheese shop with an intimate, upscale atmosphere featuring Carrara marble bars and floor-to-ceiling windows.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, birthday celebrations, wine enthusiasts, and authentic Southern Italian dining.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Authentic Fare: Recently recognized as one of the top Italian restaurants in DC, the kitchen serves mouthwatering, hand-made pastas like their signature gnocchi, cacio e pepe, and savory salt-crusted branzino.
  • In-House Cheese Shop: The first floor features a premiere market where guests can enjoy or take home over 50 varieties of imported and house-made cheeses, artisanal salumi, and fresh house-made sauces.
  • Boutique Beverage Program: Enjoy a curated list of 100 Italian-focused wines alongside innovative cocktails crafted with house-made vermouth and liquors aged in oak barrels.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • While walk-ins are welcome, reservations are highly recommended via Open Table, especially for the popular "Sophia Loren" cupola dining table which seats up to eight.
  • Located just three blocks from the U Street Metro, the venue offers a gated outdoor patio perfect for spring and summer dining.
