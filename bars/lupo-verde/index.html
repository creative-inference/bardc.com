Lupo Verde

Category: Italian, Restaurant, Wine Bar

Price: $$$

Address: 1401 T St NW, Washington, DC 20009

A rustic and charming Italian restaurant near the 14th Street corridor, Lupo Verde specializes in authentic, house-made pastas and wood-fired dishes. Its cozy dining room and extensive list of Italian wines and amari make it a favorite for intimate, flavorful dinners.
