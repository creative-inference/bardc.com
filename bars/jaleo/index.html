The restaurant that launched celebrity chef José Andrés's empire in America, Jaleo in Penn Quarter is a vibrant celebration of traditional and modern Spanish tapas. Known for its lively, festive atmosphere and signature dishes like paella and gambas al ajillo, it remains a cornerstone of D.C. dining.
