Jaleo

Jaleo

Category: Spanish, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: Penn Quarter

Price: $$$

Address: 480 7th St NW, Washington, DC 20004

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A vibrant and artistic Penn Quarter landmark that captures the festive spirit of Spain through its lively atmosphere and playful, José Andrés-designed decor.

🍸 Best For

Group celebrations, authentic Spanish tapas, innovative gin and tonics, and sophisticated happy hours.

🔥 Highlights

  • MICHELIN-Recognized Excellence: As the cornerstone of Chef José Andrés' culinary empire for over 30 years, this venue is a celebrated staple of the DC dining scene and a recurring favorite on "Best Spanish Restaurant" lists.
  • Signature Tapas & Paella: Indulge in legendary dishes like the 48-month cured Jamón Ibérico de Bellota, creamy chicken croquetas, and authentic wood-fired paellas that are announced by the ringing of a traditional cowbell.
  • Interactive Dining Features: The restaurant offers a unique experience with table-side sangria presentations and iconic glass-topped foosball tables that serve as functional dining surfaces.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Securing a reservation is highly recommended as this popular destination is a favorite for both locals and visitors and tends to fill up quickly.
  • For the ultimate experience, opt for the "José's Way" tasting menu to enjoy a curated journey through the chef’s most beloved Spanish flavors.

🔄 Weekly Events

🔔 Paella Time!

Daily

Every time a new paella is ready from the open-fire wood grill, the team rings the famous Paella Cowbell. Wood-fired paellas change daily and are available by the plate.

🎭 É by José Andrés Tasting Menu

Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat, Sun

A clever and creative tasting menu of Spanish avant-garde dishes served in a private room for only nine guests. Cooking is treated as a performance against a dramatic red glass wall.

💃 Flamenco Mondays

Mon

Experience the excitement and spectacle of traditional Spanish Flamenco performances every Monday.

Happy Hour

Sangria Hour featuring an exclusive Specials Menu with specially priced drinks and tapas available at the bar.
