✨ Vibe

Code Red is a glamorous, moody Prohibition-era speakeasy in Adams Morgan that transports guests back to the 1920s with its intimate red lighting, plush velvet couches, and authentic vintage decor.

🍸 Best For

Date nights, craft cocktail enthusiasts, secret hideaways, and immersive historical experiences.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Secret Entrance: Guests experience true speakeasy mystery by knocking on a heavy steel door and providing a secret password to a doorman through a sliding grate to gain entry.
  • Era-Inspired Libations: The "Bill of Fare" is presented as a vintage newspaper featuring theatrical cocktails like the Southside and Mary Pickford, often served in elegant crystal glassware.
  • Upscale Small Plates: The menu features sophisticated bites ranging from fresh $1 Tuesday oysters and tuna tartare to wagyu sliders and charred Madagascar prawns.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Daily "Bootlegger Hour" runs from 5 PM to 7 PM, featuring discounted craft cocktails, draft beers, and "sacramental" wines.
  • Reservations are strongly encouraged to secure a spot in this intimate space, particularly for those desiring the cozy antique booth seating.
