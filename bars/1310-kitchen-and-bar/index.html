✨ Vibe

This stylish Georgetown mainstay blends a relaxed, cozy atmosphere with a modern edge, featuring a recently refurbished dining room adorned with a striking photo gallery of iconic female rockers.

🍸 Best For

Celebrity spotting, weekend brunch, gourmet grab-and-go, and refreshing craft mocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • Chef-Driven Cuisine: Chef Jenn Crovato crafts a menu that celebrates local DC favorites, incorporating Call Your Mother bagels and Ivy City Smokehouse salmon into elevated dishes like Shakshuka and ginger coconut curry.
  • Star-Studded Appeal: Known as a neighborhood favorite for high-profile guests, don't be surprised to spot celebrities like Conan O’Brien enjoying casual breakfast tacos in the vibrant dining space.
  • 1310 Provisions: Beyond the table, the venue features a gourmet market offering house-made vegan pastries and the famous "Jenn’s Homemade" freezer-to-oven entrées like chicken pot pie and tofu lasagna.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located within the historic Georgetown Inn, this prominent women-owned establishment is a centerpiece of the neighborhood’s essential dining scene.
  • If you’re on the move, take advantage of their online ordering for savory favorites and gourmet take-home meals.
