Category: Rooftop Bars, Lounges, Event Spaces

Price:

✨ Vibe

A vibrant, Mexican-inspired rooftop sanctuary in the heart of Barracks Row, combining Oaxacan flair with a high-energy nightlife atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Day parties, late-night DJ sets, agave spirit tasting, and chic weekend outings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Newly Developed Gem: This anticipated addition to the Barracks Row corridor brings a fresh energy to the neighborhood with a focus on vibrant decor and panoramic views.
  • Agave-Focused Bar Program: Boasts an impressive collection of over 65 tequilas and 30 mezcals, complemented by handcrafted cocktails and seasonal Mexican small bites like ceviches and tacos.
  • All-Weather Versatility: Features a sophisticated retractable roof and a seamless indoor-outdoor layout, ensuring a perfect setting for social gatherings regardless of the season.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Currently finalizing construction for its highly anticipated debut; keep an eye out for the official launch of their weekend DJ programming.
  • Expect a chic, upscale atmosphere ideal for group celebrations and monumental sunset views over the neighborhood.

Lucha Rosa

Lucha Rosa is an upscale Mexican-inspired rooftop bar located atop the Moxy Hotel in downtown Washington, D.C., featuring a retractable roof for all-season enjoyment. The venue offers one of the city's largest selections of agave spirits alongside a fresh menu of ceviches, crudos, and tacos inspired by the culture of Southern Mexico.
