Denson Liquor Bar

Denson Liquor Bar

Category: Cocktail Bars, Speakeasies, Basements

Neighborhood: Gallery Place / Chinatown

Price: $$$

Address: 600 F St NW, Washington, DC 20004

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Denson Liquor Bar is a sultry, subterranean sanctuary that blends Art Deco glamour with the intimate, moody atmosphere of a 1920s speakeasy.

🍸 Best For

Romantic dates, post-theater cocktails, after-work drinks, and late-night charcuterie near Capital One Arena.

🔥 Highlights

  • Theatrical Craft Cocktails: Renowned for a sophisticated drink list ranging from the herbaceous "The Princess and the Monk" to a dramatic "Green Fairy" absinthe service featuring traditional equipage and fire.
  • Elevated Speakeasy Bites: The menu features creative, high-end small plates like deviled eggs drizzled with white truffle oil and trout roe, and a signature grilled cheese with a sweet fig twist.
  • Award-Winning Aesthetic: Recognized by Architectural Digest and Washington City Paper, the "Great Gatsby"-style interior features plush leather booths and a refined design that feels like a hidden DC secret.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are specifically for the leather booths and have a two-hour time limit; bar seating and high-tops are available for walk-ins on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • The venue is strictly 21+ and observes a business casual dress code, prohibiting any clothing or hats displaying political affiliations.
