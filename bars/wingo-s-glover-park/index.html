✨ Vibe

A lively and welcoming two-story neighborhood hub where sports fans and locals gather for high-energy karaoke and a friendly, community-focused atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Game day viewing, Tuesday night karaoke, group celebrations, customizable comfort food, casual happy hours

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Wings: Choose from over 25 fresh, house-made sauces and seasonings, including standouts like Mango Habanero and Old Bay Honey.
  • Legendary Karaoke Nights: Every Tuesday, the venue transforms into the neighborhood's favorite stage for a high-energy karaoke party with an awesome atmosphere.
  • Versatile Two-Floor Layout: Enjoy the cozy main bar for catching a game or head upstairs to the spacious second floor, which is perfect for hosting larger parties and private events.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of fantastic happy hour specials and a rewarding loyalty program for regular visitors.
  • The online ordering system makes it incredibly easy to customize their famous wraps, sandwiches, and wings for a fresh meal on the go.
