Nellie's Sports Bar

Category: Sports Bar, LGBTQ+ Bar, Rooftop Bar

Price: $$

Address: 900 U St NW, Washington, DC 20001

★★★★★ 4.4 (1427 reviews)

A landmark gay sports bar on the U Street corridor, Nellie's is a vibrant, multi-level venue known for its popular rooftop deck. It offers a lively atmosphere for watching games, weekly events like drag brunch and bingo, and serves as a major hub for the LGBTQ+ community.
