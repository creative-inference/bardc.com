Urbana

Category: Italian, Wine Bar, Restaurant

Price: $$$

Address: 2121 P St NW, Washington, DC 20037

Located in the Kimpton Hotel Palomar near Dupont Circle, Urbana is a chic and vibrant restaurant and wine bar. It specializes in coastal Italian cuisine, including house-made pastas and wood-fired pizzas, and is known for its extensive happy hour, making it a popular neighborhood gathering spot.
