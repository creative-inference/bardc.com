✨ Vibe

A welcoming campus-centered tavern with a warm neighborhood feel that is perfect for lifting your spirits after a long day.

🍸 Best For

Georgetown students, Thursday night trivia, casual campus dining, and social happy hours.

🔥 Highlights

  • Exceptional Service: Friendly and dedicated staff members like MJ and Janai are known for their attentive care, ensuring every guest feels welcome and leaves with a smile.
  • Engaging Weekly Events: The venue is a local favorite for its popular Thursday night trivia, offering a fun and competitive atmosphere for the campus community.
  • Consistent Pub Favorites: Known for serving "solid" and "great food," this spot is a reliable choice for those looking for a satisfying meal paired with a great beer selection.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located in the Healey Family Student Center, the space has recently been revitalized as a social hub designed to foster campus community through affordable dining.
  • Conveniently situated in Georgetown, it remains a top choice for a casual beer and a friendly neighborhood atmosphere.
