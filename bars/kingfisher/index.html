Kingfisher

Kingfisher

Category: Dive Bars, Neighborhood Bars, Late Night

Neighborhood: Logan Circle

Price: $

Address: 1414 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20005

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Tucked away in a Logan Circle basement, Kingfisher is a high-energy, retro-inspired neighborhood pub that perfectly blends old-school nostalgia with a lively, community-focused atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

After-work drinks, charity trivia enthusiasts, budget-friendly nights out, and late-night snacking.

🔥 Highlights

  • Engaging Weekly Events: Dive into the fun with Monday Night Bingo or Tuesday Charity Trivia hosted by Geeks Who Drink, where $0.50 from every drink sold is donated to local designated charities.
  • Signature Cans & Combos: Explore an extensive and curated selection of canned beers, wines, and nostalgic "beer-and-shot" combos that offer a fun, high-value drinking experience.
  • Complimentary Treats: Enjoy a legendary neighborhood perk with unlimited free hot popcorn available to all guests throughout the night.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of the daily happy hour featuring specials on cans and cocktails that runs until 8 PM.
  • The venue operates without a kitchen, so patrons are encouraged to bring in their favorite outside food to enjoy at the bar.

🔄 Weekly Events

🧠 Charity Trivia & Cans for a Cause

Tue

A weekly trivia night where drinking helps the community. The bar donates $0.50 from every item sold and $1 from specific featured cans and cocktails to a rotating monthly charity partner.

🧠 Charity Trivia Tuesdays

Tue

Test your knowledge while supporting a good cause. Kingfisher donates $.50 from everything sold and $1 for select cocktails and cans to a rotating monthly charity. Seats fill up fast, so arrive early.

🎱 Monday Bingo

Mon

Hosted by The Regular App, this 'least skill-based sport' allows guests to win prizes like free drinks just for listening for letters and numbers. Buy a drink, grab a chair, and play!

🎱 Monday Night Bingo

Mon

Participate in the world’s least skill-based sport! Buy a drink, sit in a chair, and listen for numbers to win prizes like free drinks. Hosted by The Regular App.

Happy Hour

$2 off beer, wine, and rail drinks. Additionally, $2 off beer all night Sunday and $2 off whiskey all night Wednesday.
