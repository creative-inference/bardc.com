Kingfisher

Category: Dive Bar, Beer Bar

Price: $

Address: 1414 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20005

A subterranean craft beer bar in Logan Circle, Kingfisher offers a relaxed, no-frills environment to enjoy a great selection of local and craft brews. With vintage arcade games and a bring-your-own-food policy, it's a comfortable and casual basement hideaway.

Happy Hour

Happy hour is offered 7 days a week. Weekdays it's 4pm-8pm, weekends 5pm-8pm. Deals include $2 off all beers all night on Sundays, $2 off all beers, wine, and rails during daily happy hour, and $2 off all bourbon, scotch, and whiskey all night on Wednesdays.
