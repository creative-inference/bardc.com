Garden District

Category: Beer Garden, BBQ

Price: $$

Address: 1801 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20009

★★★★★ 4.5 (475 reviews)

A seasonal, outdoor-only beer garden and barbecue spot at the corner of 14th and S Streets, Garden District offers a lively and casual atmosphere. It's known for its German beers, pulled pork, and brisket sandwiches, making it a popular warm-weather destination. Note: Operates seasonally.
