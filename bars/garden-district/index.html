Garden District

Garden District

Category: Beer Gardens, Burger Bars, Dog Friendly

Neighborhood: Logan Circle

Price: $$

Address: 1801 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20009

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Garden District is a vibrant, "hipster cozy" urban oasis on the corner of 14th and S, offering a laid-back, communal atmosphere that captures the essence of a traditional Bavarian beer garden with a modern DC twist.

🍸 Best For

Al fresco drinking, dog-friendly outings, group gatherings, authentic barbecue cravings, and weekend afternoon hangouts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Fare: Renowned for serving some of the city's best burgers and a phenomenal fried chicken sandwich, alongside pitmaster-style brisket and traditional German bratwurst.
  • Curated Beverage Program: A high-end selection of German and American craft beers served in giant glassware, complemented by a unique wine list and specialized spirits.
  • Premier Outdoor Patio: A quintessential 14th Street staple featuring iconic blue picnic tables and heaters for cooler months, providing a social environment that is welcoming to both humans and their four-legged friends.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue is fully accessible via street ramps and is a favorite local destination for dog owners.
  • While they open at 5:00 PM on weekdays, arrive early for 12:00 PM weekend service as the patio is one of the most popular spots in Logan Circle.
