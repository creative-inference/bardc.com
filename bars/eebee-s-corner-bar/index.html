Eebee's Corner Bar

Eebee's Corner Bar

Category:

Neighborhood: Shaw

Price: $$

Address: 1840 6th St NW, Washington, DC 20001, USA

Website

✨ Vibe

Eebee’s Corner Bar is a nostalgic, "Cheers-style" neighborhood parlor that perfectly blends a cozy, lived-in dive aesthetic with intentional vintage decor and warm hospitality.

🍸 Best For

Late-night industry drinks, neighborhood hangs, elevated pub grub, icy martinis, and burger enthusiasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Signature Burger: Widely hailed as one of the best in DC, this "stunner" features a dry-aged Pat LaFrieda patty served on a fresh, in-house made bun.
  • Curated Beverage Program: Enjoy a selection of four distinct martini styles or premium Czech-style lagers poured from authentic imported faucets.
  • Whimsical Design: The space is filled with personal touches, from family photos and a trophy marlin to uniquely themed Broadway and baseball bathrooms with their own soundtracks.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The kitchen serves its "elevated pub grub" menu until 2 a.m., making it a top-tier destination for late-night dining in Shaw.
  • Look for the buzz: This recently opened spot from beer industry veteran Emily Brown has already earned high praise from Eater DC and Washingtonian.
