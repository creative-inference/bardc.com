A Georgetown institution for nearly three decades, Cafe Milano is a high-end Italian restaurant famous for being a playground for politicians, journalists, diplomats, and celebrities. It offers refined Italian cuisine and a sophisticated lounge atmosphere where seeing and being seen is part of the experience.
