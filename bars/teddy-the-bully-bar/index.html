Teddy & The Bully Bar

Teddy & The Bully Bar

Category: Cocktail Bars, Happy Hour Spots, Oyster Bars

Neighborhood: Dupont Circle

Price:

Address: 1200 19th St NW, Washington, DC 20036, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

A standout staple in Dupont Circle’s recent neighborhood resurgence, Teddy & The Bully Bar seamlessly blends presidential history with modern sophistication, offering a stylish "industrial farmhouse" atmosphere inspired by the life of Theodore Roosevelt.

✨ Vibe

Sophisticated yet approachable, this beautifully designed space features rustic-chic decor like rifle lights and a monocle chandelier, creating a warm, upscale environment where history meets hospitality.

🍸 Best For

Happy Hour gatherings, bottomless weekend brunches, barrel-aged cocktail enthusiasts, and festive private celebrations.

🔥 Highlights

  • Premier Cocktail Program: Known for its expertly crafted barrel-aged spirits and innovative draft selections like the "Lady Outlaw," featuring elderflower, lemon sour, and lavender bitters.
  • Signature Sunday Brunch: A high-energy experience featuring a house DJ and a personalized omelet and waffle station where the Head Chef often adds a personal touch.
  • Refined American Comfort: A seasonal menu that celebrates locally sourced ingredients with highlights like fresh-shucked oysters, Maryland crab cakes, and the fan-favorite mambo-sauced chicken bites.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended to secure a table, particularly for their popular weekend brunch and special holiday dining events.
  • Be sure to visit during Happy Hour for exceptional values on their signature barrel-aged cocktails and a wide variety of discounted small plates.
