Takoda

Takoda

Category: Rooftop Bars, Happy Hour Spots, American

Neighborhood: U Street

Price: $$

Address: 715 Florida Ave NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A vibrant and inclusive rooftop sanctuary in the heart of Shaw, offering stunning sunset views and a high-energy, "sports-bar chic" atmosphere perfect for social gatherings.

🍸 Best For

Sunset cocktails, group happy hours, weekend brunch, people watching, and LGBTQ+ friendly nightlife.

🔥 Highlights

  • Stunning Rooftop Views: Enjoy one of the best vantage points in the city for watching the sunset over the U Street corridor while sipping on well-balanced, signature cocktails.
  • Exceptional Hospitality: Known for a warm and eclectic staff that provides personalized service, making every guest feel welcome whether you're dining solo at the bar or with a large party.
  • Top-Tier Happy Hour: Take advantage of a standout happy hour featuring an impressive selection of cocktails under $10, making it one of the neighborhood's best values.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Happy hour runs Wednesday through Sunday from 5:00 PM to 7:30 PM.
  • This is a completely cashless venue, and reservations are recommended for prime rooftop seating.

🔄 Weekly Events

🥂 Bottomless Brunch

Sat, Sun

Enjoy bottomless brunch offerings including Challah French Toast, Chicken & Waffles, and Breakfast Burritos. A 20% service fee applies to parties participating in bottomless brunch.

Happy Hour

$6-$7 select beers, $7 rail drinks, $7-$8 select cocktails and wine, and $6-$9 food specials including fries and truffle tater tots.
