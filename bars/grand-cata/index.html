✨ Vibe

A chic and culturally vibrant oasis in the heart of La Cosecha that celebrates the spirit of Latin America through curated wines, warm hospitality, and a modern market atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Latin American wine discovery, casual happy hours, celebratory wine flights, post-Mystics game drinks, and boutique pantry shopping.

🔥 Highlights

  • Premier Latin American Curation: Discover an expansive selection of hand-picked bottles from the Caribbean, Central America, and South America, guided by an inclusive education philosophy that focuses on flavor profiles and food pairings.
  • Authentic Regional Bites: Pair your vintage with delicious, traditional small plates including savory empanadas, choripanes, and artisanal charcuterie boards.
  • Exceptional Value Flights: Experience the "Vino y Mercado" lifestyle with popular $20 wine flights and expert recommendations from a friendly staff known for their deep knowledge and cheerful service.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located within the contemporary La Cosecha market, making it an ideal anchor for a broader afternoon of Latin cultural exploration.
  • Look into their specialized Wine Club memberships or on-demand wine classes for a deeper dive into regional viticulture and stories.

🔄 Weekly Events

🍷 Daily Cata Tasting

Daily

🍷 #DAILYCATA Wine Tasting

Daily

Every day, Grand Cata has something open to taste at the counter. A great reason to stop by, taste something new, and discover your new favorites.

🍷 Wine Club Release Tasting

Sat

An exclusive wine tasting connected to the themed wine of the month at the flagship location. A chance to meet other club members while collecting your wine club package.

🎉 Wine Club Release Party

Sun

Exclusive tastings, mercadito samples, and in-store pick-up gatherings for wine club members. Note: During months with big holidays, the party moves to the Sunday before the holiday.

🎓 Wine Classes on Demand

Daily

Private classes led by Catadores including options like 'Sit, Sip and Learn', 'Stand, Mingle, Converse', and 'Class for Two'. Includes systematic wine analysis and region history. Requires 48 hours notice.
