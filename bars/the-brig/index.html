the brig

Category: Beer Gardens, Sports Bars, Dog Friendly

Price:

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A spacious and welcoming neighborhood beer garden that combines a lively outdoor sports-watching environment with authentic German hospitality.

🍸 Best For

Game days, large group gatherings, dog-friendly weekend outings, and pre-Nationals game drinks.

🔥 Highlights

  • Premier Beer & Food Selection: Features an impressive rotation of authentic German imports and local craft brews, perfectly paired with fan favorites like sliders, bratwurst, and giant pretzels.
  • Versatile Outdoor Space: Offers a massive 300-person capacity open-air garden equipped with umbrellas and heated, enclosed sections to provide a comfortable atmosphere regardless of the season.
  • Top-Tier Sports Viewing: Boasts numerous high-definition outdoor TVs and a dedicated following for Bayern Munich matches, making it one of the city's best spots to catch a game.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are available online for groups up to 20, while the venue can accommodate massive private buyouts for up to 300 guests.
  • The garden is exceptionally pet-friendly and conveniently located within walking distance of both the Eastern Market and Navy Yard Metro stations.
