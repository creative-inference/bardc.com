✨ Vibe

Addis Glo Lounge offers a high-energy, late-night atmosphere in Shaw where traditional Ethiopian hospitality meets a vibrant modern lounge scene.

🍸 Best For

Late-night dancing, weekend birthday celebrations, authentic Ethiopian music, and premium hookah experiences.

🔥 Highlights

  • Premier Entertainment: Experience a top-tier DJ rotation on Friday and Saturday nights spinning a seamless mix of Ethiopian hits and global chart-toppers.
  • Warm Hospitality: Owned and operated by a dedicated team, this "hidden gem" is celebrated for its welcoming staff, friendly ownership, and professional service.
  • Vibrant After-Hours Scene: The energy peaks after 11 PM, making it the perfect destination for those seeking a lively weekend getaway with great food and high-impact vibes.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Secure your spot by arriving early or calling ahead for table reservations, as the lounge is a local favorite that fills up fast on weekends.
  • Take advantage of the Friday night festivities from 9 PM to 3 AM, which frequently offer free entry for a premium Shaw nightlife experience.
