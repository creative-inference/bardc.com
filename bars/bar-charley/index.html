Bar Charley

Category: Cocktail Bars, Neighborhood Bars, Brunch Spots

Neighborhood: Adams Morgan

Price: $$

Address: 1825 18th St NW, Washington, DC 20009

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated yet cozy speakeasy-style destination that blends exposed brick and vintage-inspired charm with the welcoming, high-energy atmosphere of a beloved neighborhood bar.

🍸 Best For

Craft cocktail enthusiasts, bottomless brunch dates, all-night Monday happy hours, and intimate group dinners.

🔥 Highlights

  • World-Class Drink Program: Boasts an extensive 11-page spirits menu featuring over ten varieties of Manhattans and Martinis, alongside creative tropical favorites like the "Giggle Water."
  • Value-Packed Bottomless Brunch: Renowned for one of the city’s best brunch deals, featuring high-quality dishes like salmon Benedict and potato cakes paired with pomegranate mimosas or Bloody Marys.
  • Weekly Special Events: A local favorite for its themed nights, including "All-Night Happy Hour" Mondays, Sunday Night Steaks, and "Pasta & Prosecco" Wednesdays.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue features a comfortable, all-season heated patio that is perfect for walk-ins, though reservations are highly recommended for weekend brunch and indoor dining.
  • Take advantage of the legendary Monday Happy Hour, which offers discounted craft cocktails and beer from 5 PM until closing.
