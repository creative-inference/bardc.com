✨ Vibe

An opulent "mini Versailles" in the heart of downtown, SAX offers a sumptuous Moulin Rouge-inspired ambiance featuring 60-foot ceilings, gold-leaf decor, and a breathtaking castle-like atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Bachelorette parties, milestone birthday celebrations, celebrity-spotting, immersive dinner theater, luxury bottle service, and high-energy late-night dancing.

🔥 Highlights

  • World-Class Entertainment: The venue features a 20-foot glass infinity stage positioned above the bar, showcasing a rotating cast of aerialists, modern ballet dancers, and live cabaret performers.
  • Exquisite Design: Every inch of the space is designed for luxury, from the ornate gold-leaf accents and red velvet paneling to the provocative murals and grand theatrical lighting.
  • Premier Event Atmosphere: Known for hosting high-profile afterparties and private events, SAX seamlessly blends a sophisticated lounge vibe with a high-energy dance floor and attentive VIP service.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Advanced reservations are highly encouraged for dinner theater shows and late-night bottle service to secure the best views of the stage.
  • Expect a sophisticated, upscale environment featuring theatrical liquid nitrogen cocktails and a menu of gourmet small plates.

District of Columbia | Attorney General (.gov)

Attorney General Schwalb Announces Seven Settlements Protecting DC Workers and Important Workplace Rights

SAX, a dinner theater and restaurant in downtown D.C., has settled with the Office of the Attorney General following investigations into wage theft, minimum wage violations, and the use of unlawful arbitration clauses. The venue will pay $66,000 in restitution to approximately 70 workers and $30,000 in penalties while committing to overhauling its payroll and workplace practices.
