Sook DC

Sook DC

Category: Cocktail Bars, Wine Bars, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: U Street

Price:

Address: 1346 T St NW, Washington, DC 20009, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

Recently opened in the historic townhouse that formerly housed the legendary Compass Rose, Sook DC is a stunning all-day global cafe, market, and wine bar that masterfully blends nostalgia with a fresh, vibrant new identity.

✨ Vibe

A warm and eclectic neighborhood sanctuary that pairs the cozy, welcoming energy of a historic home with the bustling charm of a global marketplace.

🍸 Best For

Afternoon co-working with specialty lattes, intimate date nights over natural wine, unique retail shopping, and enjoying shareable global bites with friends.

🔥 Highlights

  • Iconic Legacy Dishes: Fans of the previous tenant will be delighted to find the signature Georgian khachapuri still on the menu, now joined by playful new additions like potato chip nachos and Lebanese breakfast items.
  • Curated Wine & Spirits: The evening transition features an impressive selection of low-intervention natural wines and refreshing spritzes, perfect for pairing with their globally-inspired comfort food.
  • The "Souk" Experience: Beyond the food and drink, the space functions as a retail market where guests can shop for specialty Middle Eastern spices, oils, and bottles of wine to take home.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Seating is handled on a first-come, first-served basis with a casual counter-service model, so no reservations are required for a spontaneous visit.
  • Guest WiFi is available, making it an ideal "third space" for remote work during the cafe’s daytime hours.

Happy Hour

Golden Hour features $12 wine pours, $12-$14 spritzes, $16 espresso martinis, and discounted food items including $5 fries and $14 mini snacks.
