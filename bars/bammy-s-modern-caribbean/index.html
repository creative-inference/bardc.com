Bammy's Modern Caribbean

Bammy's Modern Caribbean

Category: Rum Bars, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price:

Address: 301 Water St SE Suite 115, Washington, DC 20003, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

Recognized on the prestigious "Hit List" of DC dining, Bammy’s Modern Caribbean is a waterfront gem in Navy Yard where Executive Chef Peter Prime delivers authentic island flavors with a sophisticated, high-energy twist.

✨ Vibe

A vibrant and transportive tropical escape that combines authentic Caribbean "yard" energy with the sleek, breezy atmosphere of the DC waterfront.

🍸 Best For

Birthday celebrations, festive happy hours, group dinners, and authentic island cravings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Chef-Driven Cuisine: Savor island-inspired masterpieces by Executive Chef Peter Prime, including the signature Trinidadian "bake and shark," authentic jerk pork, and creamy coconut milk collards.
  • Tropical Beverage Program: Refresh your palate with a curated selection of Caribbean favorites, from ice-cold Red Stripes to their famous frozen guava daiquiris.
  • Gold-Standard Hospitality: Renowned for its exceptionally warm and comedic staff, the team creates an inviting "Bammy’s Fam" atmosphere that turns first-time visitors into regulars.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Happy Hour specials are a local favorite—be sure to ask your server for the daily offerings and upcoming seasonal events.
  • The venue is highly accommodating for large parties and milestones; you can book the space for private corporate events or birthday celebrations through their website.
