The Golden Age

The Golden Age

Category: Cocktail Bars, Speakeasies, Live Music Venues

Neighborhood: Dupont Circle

Price:

Address: 1726 Connecticut Ave NW, Washington, DC 20009, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated yet cozy old-school DC retreat featuring a moody speakeasy atmosphere, plush back-lounge seating, and live music that perfectly complements easy conversation.

🍸 Best For

Craft cocktail enthusiasts, late-night dining, intimate date nights, and upscale group outings for the 30-and-up crowd.

🔥 Highlights

  • Expertly mixed cocktails ranging from the signature "Spicy Bee's Knees" to flawlessly executed classics like the Sazerac and Daiquiri.
  • An impressive full-service kitchen that serves high-quality dishes all night long, including a celebrated double-patty pub burger and tender lamb kabobs.
  • A warm, inviting service style that makes every guest feel like part of the family in a beautifully lit, "hidden gem" setting.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • While walk-ins are often accommodated shortly after opening, making a reservation is highly recommended to secure a spot in the cozy lounge.
  • The venue offers a fantastic happy hour, making it an ideal destination for an early evening cocktail and small plates.
