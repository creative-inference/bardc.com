Bourbon Steak

Category: Steakhouse, Hotel Bar, American

Price: $$$$

Address: 2800 Pennsylvania Avenue NW, Washington, DC 20007

Located in the Four Seasons Hotel in Georgetown, Bourbon Steak is a contemporary American steakhouse from celebrity chef Michael Mina. Known for its upscale ambiance and wood-fired prime cuts, it features a sophisticated bar and a garden patio with fire pits.
