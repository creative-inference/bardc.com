✨ Vibe

A masterclass in modern luxury, Bourbon Steak blends the sophisticated elegance of the Four Seasons with a warm, high-caliber atmosphere defined by plush leather seating and world-class service.

🍸 Best For

Milestone celebrations, romantic date nights, power lunches, and bourbon aficionados.

🔥 Highlights

  • Nationally Recognized Excellence: Recently honored as one of the "Top 100 Hotel Restaurants in America" for 2025, this Michael Mina destination features a menu of prime cuts and A5 Wagyu finished on a signature wood-burning grill.
  • Legendary Sides & Starters: Every meal begins with a flourish, from the buttery, sea-salt-topped truffle rolls to the iconic trio of duck-fat fries seasoned with garlic-herb, smoked paprika, and spicy ketchup.
  • The District's Premier Burger: For a more casual yet decadent experience, the Prime Steak Burger with red wine shallot compote is a must-try lunch and lounge favorite, frequently cited as one of the best in the city.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Valet parking is available via the hotel entrance, offering a seamless arrival for those visiting the heart of Georgetown.
  • Reservations are highly recommended for the main dining room, particularly for evening celebrations and weekend dining.
