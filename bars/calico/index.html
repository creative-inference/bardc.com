An urban backyard-style bar located in the historic Blagden Alley, Calico is known for its spacious patio and casual, friendly vibe. It's a popular spot for groups, offering picnic tables, lawn games, and a menu of draft cocktails and adult 'juice boxes'.

Happy Hour

Sunday Spritz: All-day happy hour with specially-priced spritzes and spritz + tomato pie combos. 4th of July (July 4th): 2-6pm extended happy hour with $2 off Adult Juice Boxes, individually-bagged cocktails, draft beer, and wines by the glass. Arts & Drafts (Mondays, 4-10pm): All-day artsy happy hour with free craft projects (with any food/drink order) and discounted draft drinks. Note that one Monday's event has a $5 fee for supplies.
