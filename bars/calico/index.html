Calico

Calico

Category: Beer Gardens, Cocktail Bars, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: Gallery Place / Chinatown

Price: $$

Address: 50 Blagden Alley NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A hidden urban backyard oasis in Blagden Alley that blends a chic, cozy brick interior with a festive, string-lit patio perfect for year-round socializing.

🍸 Best For

Large group gatherings, spirited happy hours, casual dates, weekend trivia, and playful seasonal events.

🔥 Highlights

  • Recently recognized as one of the "33 Best Bars in Washington, DC," the venue is famous for its signature "Adult Juice Boxes" and refreshing draft lavender lemonades.
  • A vibrant social calendar that keeps the energy high with weekly trivia, bingo, sip-and-paint nights, and board games.
  • Delicious, elevated bar fare including their fan-favorite loaded tots, massive pizzas, and Philly-style tomato pie.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of the weekday happy hour (Tuesday–Friday, 4–6 PM) for $3 tomato pie slices and $2 discounts on select drafts and pouches.
  • Exceptionally group-friendly, the space accommodates up to 30 guests on weekdays without food or drink minimums.

Calico - Bar Review

Calico is a hip patio bar tucked away in D.C.'s Blagden Alley, offering a playful outdoor party vibe with greenhouse seating and string lights. The menu features creative drinks like boozy juice boxes and lavender lemonade alongside picnic-inspired fare such as tomato pie, smoked ribs, and bratwurst.

First Look: Drink Up The Nostalgia In Calico’s Urban Backyard

Calico is an urban backyard-themed restaurant in Blagden Alley offering a nostalgic atmosphere inspired by Eastern Shore backyard barbecues. The venue features a 3,000-square-foot outdoor space with a vintage greenhouse and serves creative offerings like "adult juice boxes," Maryland crab soup, and Philly-style tomato pie.
