✨ Vibe

A high-energy, fast-casual waterfront destination that brings Gordon Ramsay’s signature British flair and lively spirit to the heart of DC’s buzzing Wharf district.

🍸 Best For

Quick waterfront lunches, celebrity chef fans, family-friendly dining, casual seafood cravings, and grab-and-go treats before a show.

🔥 Highlights

  • Signature Seafood & "Dirty" Chips: Enjoy perfectly crispy golden battered fish and succulent shrimp paired with indulgent truffle fries or specialty "dirty" chips seasoned to perfection.
  • Artisanal Dipping Sauces: Elevate your meal with a variety of signature sauces, including the highly-recommended mango curry and a top-tier tartar sauce that fans rave about.
  • Exceptional Quality & Service: As a standout expansion for Gordon Ramsay, the venue maintains incredibly high standards of cleanliness and professional, friendly service that ensures every guest feels valued.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Ordering is streamlined through efficient touch-screen kiosks, making it a reliable and quick option for a high-quality meal in a bustling neighborhood.
  • Don't miss out on the unique dessert menu, which features signature sweet treats like the decadent Biscoff and Sticky Toffee shakes.
