✨ Vibe

Amsterdam Lounge is a high-energy urban hotspot on U Street featuring a vibrant multi-level layout and a pulse for the city's hip hop scene.

🍸 Best For

Birthday celebrations, late-night dancing, happy hour specials, private events, urban nightlife experiences.

🔥 Highlights

  • Multi-Level Entertainment: Enjoy a spacious venue with two distinct floors, two full-service bars, and an outdoor patio perfect for socializing.
  • Eclectic Music & Energy: Known for its great rotation of hip hop and regional favorites that keep the crowd moving and the atmosphere electric.
  • Affordable Fun: A popular destination for those seeking a high-energy, authentic DC nightlife experience with budget-friendly drinks and a chill 21+ crowd.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Stop by for happy hour for a more relaxed social scene, or visit late-night to experience the full energy of the club.
  • The venue is highly recommended for hosting private events and birthday parties with a beautiful turnout.
