View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

This chic and welcoming boutique café offers an immaculate aesthetic and cozy energy, perfectly tucked away just off the main Georgetown thoroughfare.

🍸 Best For

Artistic coffee dates, sophisticated afternoon tea, light weekend brunch, and elegant happy hour cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Excellence: Recently honored as one of the "Top 25 Best Restaurants in Washington, D.C.," this venue is a premier destination for those seeking the capital's best culinary experiences.
  • Custom Latte Art: Skilled artisan baristas can create exquisite, custom-created latte art, including the ability to print any photo of your choice directly onto your drink.
  • Turkish-Inspired Savories: The menu features unique Mediterranean flavors, such as traditional layered cheese borek, alongside premium La Colombe coffee and specialty drinks like the Blueberry Lavender Iced Tea.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Beyond coffee, the venue offers a full bar and curated wine list, making it a seamless transition from morning caffeine to evening happy hour.
  • The space is available for private event bookings, including birthday parties and celebrations, with convenient online reservations for groups.
