QrtzBar

QrtzBar

Category: Tiki Bars, Hookah Lounges, Cocktail Bars

Neighborhood: U Street

Price:

Address: 1914 9th St NW, Washington, DC 20001, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A stunning and stylish hidden gem on U Street that seamlessly blends the tropical energy of a tiki bar with the chic, intimate elegance of a high-end quartz lounge.

🍸 Best For

Private events, milestone celebrations, premium hookah, creative cocktails, and cozy late-night hangouts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Picture-Perfect Cocktails: Guests can enjoy expertly crafted drinks that feature their own photos printed directly on the beverage for a truly personalized touch.
  • Luxury Hookah Service: Known for offering high-end, expensive hookah pipes and a top-tier experience that outshines standard hookah establishments.
  • Stunning Multi-Level Design: The venue features a gorgeous cocktail bar upstairs and a vibrant tiki bar downstairs, offering beautiful, ready-to-use decor perfect for wedding receptions and private parties.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • An excellent choice for private bookings, featuring helpful event staff and a stylish atmosphere that eliminates the need for extra decorations.
  • Look for this tucked-away spot to escape the U Street crowds for a more intimate, high-quality experience.
