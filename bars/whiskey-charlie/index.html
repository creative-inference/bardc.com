Whiskey Charlie

Whiskey Charlie

Category: Rooftop Bars, Lounges, Whiskey Bars

Neighborhood: The Wharf

Price: $$$

Address: 975 7th St SW, Washington, DC 20024

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated nautical-themed rooftop retreat offering stunning 10th-floor views of the Potomac River and the Washington DC skyline.

🍸 Best For

Sunset cocktails, upscale date nights, whiskey connoisseurs, waterfront sightseeing, social group gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Unrivaled panoramic vistas of the Wharf marina and DC’s iconic monuments from both the intimate outdoor deck and the stylish indoor "Cabin" lounge.
  • An elite spirits collection featuring a massive library of premium bourbons, rare scotches, and creative reimagined classics like the signature "Waterfront Queen."
  • A lively atmosphere elevated by curated music—ranging from Afro beats to Caribbean rhythms—and exceptional service from a welcoming, attentive hospitality team.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located atop the Canopy by Hilton, this 21+ venue offers a "Happy Hour" menu featuring specialty pricing on select craft cocktails and savory small plates like brisket bao buns.

🔄 Weekly Events

🥂 Private Event Buyouts

Mon, Tue, Wed

The 10th-floor rooftop lounge is available for partial and full private buyouts for groups of 40 to 120 people. Events include access to both the outdoor rooftop deck and the indoor 'Cabin' lounge with panoramic views of The Wharf.

Happy Hour

$10 select cocktails, $9 wine by the glass, $5 beer and seltzer, and food specials ranging from $4 to $8 including brisket bao buns, chicken taquitos, and soup dumplings.

Inside DC Refined's cocktail class with Whiskey Charlie

Whiskey Charlie hosts monthly cocktail classes in partnership with DC Refined, featuring guided tastings of spirits like bourbon, scotch, and rye. These educational sessions offer guests the opportunity to learn about the history and production of different liquors while enjoying the venue's rooftop atmosphere.
