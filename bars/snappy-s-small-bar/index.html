Snappy’s Small Bar

Snappy’s Small Bar

Category: Dive Bars, Neighborhood Bars, Late Night

Neighborhood: Petworth

Price:

Address: 3917 Georgia Ave NW , Washington, DC 20011, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

Recently recognized as one of D.C.’s "Most Lovable Dive Bars" and currently expanding with a second location in Logan Circle, Snappy’s Small Bar is a beloved Petworth gem that perfectly captures the inclusive, high-energy spirit of a neighborhood local.

✨ Vibe

A nostalgic and thoughtfully curated dive bar that packs a big personality into an intimate, dimly lit space filled with quirky vintage decor and a welcoming community atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Late-night hangouts, budget-friendly dates, unique cocktails, and casual neighborhood gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Nostalgic Snacks & Sips: Indulge in fan favorites like air-fried Dino-shaped chicken nuggets, the signature "Spaghett" cocktail, or frozen coffee drinks, all served alongside a vintage Snoopy telephone at the bar.
  • Diverse Seating Options: While the interior is cozy and "strangely clean" for a dive, the venue surprises guests with a spacious front patio and a vibrant, mural-adorned backyard.
  • Unbeatable Value: Founded by industry veterans to prioritize affordability, the menu features draft beers, "Low Life" combos, and creative cocktails that are among the most reasonably priced in the city.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Snappy’s is a dog-friendly venue that stays open until 2 a.m. on weeknights and 3 a.m. on Fridays and Saturdays.
  • Located just a short walk from the Georgia Ave-Petworth Metro stop, the entrance is tucked behind a charming, nondescript door on Georgia Avenue.
