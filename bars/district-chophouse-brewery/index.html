District Chophouse & Brewery

Category: Steakhouse, Brewpub, American

Price: $$$

Address: 509 7th St NW, Washington, DC 20004

Situated in a historic 1890s bank building in Penn Quarter, the District Chophouse & Brewery combines a classic steakhouse with an in-house brewery. It offers a menu of prime steaks, seafood, and handcrafted ales in a handsome, two-story space with a billiard room.
