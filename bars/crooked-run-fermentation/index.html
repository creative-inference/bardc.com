✨ Vibe

A welcoming, high-energy neighborhood hub in the Union Market District that combines the expertise of a craft brewery with the warmth of a local hangout where the staff treats everyone like a regular.

🍸 Best For

Craft beer enthusiasts, vinyl lovers, casual group hangouts, pizza and wing pairings, and low-key date nights.

🔥 Highlights

  • Exceptional Fermentation Program: Enjoy a diverse rotation of award-winning mixed-fermentation sours, natural wines, and the signature "Alora" Italian pilsner, the brewery's first D.C.-produced beer.
  • Artisan Pizza & Wings: The "brew-staurant" concept features handmade NYC-style slices and personal pan pizzas from J&J Pizza, alongside wings that have become a local favorite.
  • Curated Weekly Events: The taproom hosts popular Vinyl Nights every Thursday and maintains a fun, nostalgic atmosphere by screening a mix of women’s basketball and cult classic TV shows.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • While famous for their beers, the venue also offers a full beverage program including natural wine and craft cocktails.
  • Keep an eye out for their highly sought-after canned releases like "Cold Like War" and "Mango Sticky Rice" to take the experience home.
