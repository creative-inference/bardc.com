Steeped in more than a century of political lore at the Willard InterContinental, the Round Robin Bar is a legendary D.C. gathering place. Its iconic circular mahogany bar and esteemed mint juleps have been enjoyed by countless historical figures, from Mark Twain to Abraham Lincoln.
