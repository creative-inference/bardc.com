Round Robin Bar

Round Robin Bar

Category: Hotel Bars, Cocktail Bars, Historic

Neighborhood: Downtown

Price: $$$

Address: 1401 Pennsylvania Avenue NW, Washington,DC 20004

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A historic "Oval Office of Bars" that exudes political and literary prestige with its polished mahogany circular bar and a sophisticated, time-honored atmosphere that feels quintessentially DC.

🍸 Best For

Upscale after-work drinks, political history buffs, legendary Mint Juleps, sophisticated date nights, and impressing out-of-town guests.

🔥 Highlights

  • A Living History Landmark: Established in 1850 and recognized by Historic Hotels of America, this storied institution has hosted everyone from Civil War generals to modern presidents, earning accolades such as the 2023 Condé Nast Traveler Readers’ Choice award.
  • The Iconic Mint Julep: Famed as the location where the Mint Julep was first introduced to Washington, D.C., the bar remains the gold standard for this signature cocktail, served fresh by master bartenders.
  • Masterful Craftsmanship & Elevated Fare: Beyond the classics, enjoy expert mixology like smoked Old Fashioneds and dirty martinis paired with upscale dishes including grilled octopus, pork belly, and artisanal cheese plates.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Look out for "History Happy Hours" led by legendary bartender Jim Hewes, which offer a fascinating deep dive into the political and literary stories of the District.
  • Located inside the prestigious Willard InterContinental, it is an ideal post-museum retreat just steps away from the National Mall and the White House.
