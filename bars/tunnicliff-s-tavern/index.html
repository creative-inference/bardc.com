✨ Vibe

Tunnicliff's Tavern offers a warm, classic neighborhood pub atmosphere that makes locals and visitors feel right at home in the heart of Barracks Row.

🍸 Best For

Game day sports viewing, hearty weekend brunch, casual family dinners, and perfectly poured pints of Guinness.

🔥 Highlights

  • Exceptional pub fare including a legendary crab cake sandwich and crispy fish and chips that keep regulars coming back for more.
  • A standout brunch menu featuring decadent French toast and savory eggs Benedict, ideal for a relaxing Saturday morning.
  • Welcoming and attentive service that pairs perfectly with generous portions and a cozy, lived-in tavern charm.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Perfectly located just steps from Eastern Market, making it an ideal stop for a cocktail or breakfast before browsing the local vendors.
  • The menu offers a fantastic price point for the Capitol Hill area, providing high-quality comfort food in a relaxed setting.
