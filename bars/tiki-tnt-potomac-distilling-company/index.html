Website

✨ Vibe

A vibrant, three-story tropical oasis at The Wharf that blends industrial-cool with classic tiki kitsch, offering breathtaking waterfront views of the Potomac River.

🍸 Best For

Rum enthusiasts, waterfront sunset cocktails, lively group gatherings, tropical happy hours, and casual dockside dining.

🔥 Highlights

  • In-House Distillery Experience: As the home of Thrasher’s Rum, guests can enjoy expert-led rum tastings and learn about the craft spirits distilled right on-site.
  • High-Value Tropical Libations: The cocktail program features potent, liquor-forward favorites like the signature "TNT Problem Forgetter" and classic Blue Hawaiians that offer great value for the DC area.
  • Pacific-Inspired Fare: The kitchen serves up fresh, flavorful bites including a popular Pupu platter, crunchy coconut shrimp, and zesty chicken tacos that perfectly complement the island drinks.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The atmosphere truly transforms at night, making it the perfect time to experience the full effect of the tiki decor and the sparkling lights of the wharf.
  • Located conveniently near several concert venues, it is an ideal spot for a pre-show drink or a laid-back happy hour with friends.
