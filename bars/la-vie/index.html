La Vie

Category: Brunch Spots, Cocktail Bars, Waterfront

Neighborhood: The Wharf

Price:

Address: 88 District Square SW Floor 4.6, Washington, DC 20024, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sprawling 10,000-square-foot luxury oasis where Mediterranean elegance meets a high-energy waterfront lounge atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Bottomless group brunches, milestone celebrations, scenic date nights, and sophisticated late-night cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • Michelin-Starred Culinary Direction: Under the guidance of a culinary director with Michelin-starred credentials, the menu features elevated Mediterranean signatures like generous lobster risotto, a premium raw bar, and a curated carving station.
  • Breathtaking Waterfront Vistas: Located on the 4th floor, the venue offers panoramic views of the Potomac from uniquely themed spaces, including the chic Conservatory, the Chandelier Room, and the stunning outdoor Terrace Garden.
  • The Ultimate Brunch Experience: A local favorite for its "bottomless everything" Sunday brunch, featuring family-style plates, free-flowing mimosas, and a high-energy vibe perfect for large parties.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly sought after and should be booked weeks in advance, especially for weekend brunch and the seasonal winter igloos.
  • The venue maintains an upscale dress code; guests are encouraged to dress to impress and avoid casual sneakers.
