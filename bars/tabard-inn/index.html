Category: Historic Bar, Restaurant, Hotel Bar

Price: $$$

Address: 1739 N St NW, Washington, DC 20036

★★★★★ 4.5 (1298 reviews)

The bar at the Tabard Inn, located in D.C.'s oldest continuously running hotel, is a uniquely cozy and historic spot near Dupont Circle. Known for its fireplace, plush armchairs, and live jazz nights, it offers a romantic and intimate escape with a menu of classic cocktails and seasonal dishes.
