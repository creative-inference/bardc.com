Tabard Inn

Tabard Inn

Category: Hotel Bars, Live Music Venues, Romantic

Neighborhood: Dupont Circle

Price: $$$

Address: 1739 N St NW, Washington, DC 20036

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A charming European-style oasis steeped in history, featuring cozy wood-burning fireplaces and eclectic, time-honored decor nestled within three historic 19th-century townhomes.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, fireside winter cocktails, live jazz, and leisurely weekend brunches.

🔥 Highlights

  • The legendary lounge area, recently featured as a top D.C. destination for enjoying craft cocktails by a crackling wood-burning hearth.
  • Exquisite seasonal dining led by Executive Chef Sarah Kwon, highlighted by the inn's famous house-made doughnuts and locally sourced dishes like mushroom risotto and pan-seared scallops.
  • A vibrant live music scene, where guests can enjoy soulful jazz performances in an intimate, historic setting that feels worlds away from the city bustle.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Happy Hour runs Monday through Friday from 4:30 PM to 6:00 PM, and dinner reservations are highly recommended to secure a spot in the romantic dining room or garden.
  • Be sure to visit for breakfast or brunch to experience their award-winning garden patio dining.

DC's Tabard Inn Seeks Landmark Status

The Tabard Inn, a historic 35-room hotel in DC’s Dupont Circle, sought landmark status for its three interconnected rowhouses built between 1887 and 1900. The property is notable for being one of the city's oldest woman-owned hotels and for hosting the early career of organic food pioneer Nora Pouillon.
