Kabin Lounge

Kabin Lounge

Category: Lounges, Nightclub, Hip Hop

Neighborhood: Dupont Circle

Price:

Address: 1337 Connecticut Ave NW, Washington, DC 20036, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated après-ski escape in the heart of the city, blending the cozy rustic charm of an Aspen mountain lodge with the high-energy pulse of a premier DC nightclub.

🍸 Best For

Birthday celebrations, premium bottle service, late-night dancing, and stylish group outings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Immersive Alpine Decor: The 3,200-square-foot space features stylish cabin-themed elements, including digital fireplaces and posh mountain retreat aesthetics that transport guests to the French Alps.
  • Dynamic Musical Landscape: Expert DJs keep the energy high with a versatile rotation of Top 40, Hip Hop, House, and nostalgic 80s and 90s hits.
  • Signature Après-Ski Drinks: Guests can enjoy a unique cocktail menu featuring the traditional "Three Pins" herbal liqueur shot, perfect for a high-energy mountain-inspired toast.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue maintains a trendy and fashionable dress code; reservations for one of the 14 VIP tables are recommended for the ultimate experience.
  • Arrive early on Friday and Saturday nights to enjoy the beautiful bar and beat the peak weekend rush.
