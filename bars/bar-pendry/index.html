✨ Vibe

Bar Pendry offers a sophisticated, modern sanctuary that strikes a perfect balance between high-end elegance and a welcoming, down-to-earth atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Elegant boozy tea parties, refined happy hour drinks, chic girls' outings, and sophisticated pre-dinner cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • The signature Boozy Tea Experience, a creative twist on high tea featuring unique seasonal blends, themed pairings, and complimentary champagne.
  • An impressive spirits program, including curated "Flight of the Day" selections and expertly crafted cocktails like the District Noir.
  • A stunning, contemporary setting inside the Pendry hotel that provides a chic, upscale backdrop for socializing along the waterfront.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of the popular Happy Hour, which features premium handmade cocktails at a special price point.
  • Reservations are highly recommended for the themed tea services and special event brunches to ensure your table is ready.

Pendry Washington DC — Hotel Review

Bar Pendry is an intimate ground-floor lounge at the Pendry Washington DC – The Wharf, featuring a cozy fireplace and private nooks for enjoying classic cocktails and light bites. Pendry Washington DC - The Wharf Review: You Won’t Find Better Waterfront Views Than This

Bar Pendry, located on the lobby level of the Pendry Washington DC - The Wharf, serves signature cocktails like the mezcal-based 'Where There’s Smoke' alongside whiskey and bourbon flights. The venue also hosts a weekly Boozy Tea on Saturdays, offering tea-infused cocktails and a tiered selection of savory and sweet pastries in a refined atmosphere.
