✨ Vibe

Vagabond is a vibrant, globally-inspired surf oasis in Adams Morgan featuring a breezy open-air rooftop and a high-energy, social atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Rooftop cocktails, lively weekend brunches, value-driven happy hours, and group meetups.

🔥 Highlights

  • The stunning "Swell Season" rooftop bar provides an energetic open-air setting perfect for enjoying drinks with friendly, fast-paced service.
  • The menu features standout global comfort food, including umami-rich sliders and crispy yucca fries that offer high-quality flavor at an approachable price point.
  • A premier brunch destination known for 10-out-of-10 cocktails and fluffy Pura Vida eggs, making it a top choice for large parties and weekend celebrations.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy a rare Sunday happy hour or book reservations for parties of up to 12 through Resy to secure your spot.
  • The kitchen stays active late into the night, with the venue remaining open until 2:00 AM on Fridays and Saturdays.
