Price:

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Nestled inside the historic Omni Shoreham Hotel, this Woodley Park treasure offers an upscale, old-school lounge atmosphere defined by polished wood, elegant glass accents, and a warm, "jewel box" feel that exudes classic D.C. hospitality.

🍸 Best For

Historic ambiance, holiday celebrations, sophisticated business cocktails, and intimate evening retreats.

🔥 Highlights

  • Festive Transformations: The lounge is currently hosting "Blitzen’s," a holiday pop-up featuring a seasonal cocktail menu designed by acclaimed mixologist Julie Reiner, including the bourbon-based Naughty or Spice.
  • Masterful Mixology: Renowned for a top-tier drink list that features creative signatures like the Cardamom Old Fashioned and the gin-based Mistletoe Martini.
  • Gourmet Bar Fare: Guests enjoy high-quality comfort food with beautiful presentation, specifically the highly recommended burgers and signature wings.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Conveniently located within the Omni Shoreham Hotel, making it an easy and elegant meeting point for both locals and travelers.
  • The bar is known for its exceptionally welcoming staff and personalized service, often remembered for its cozy, "at-home" hospitality.
