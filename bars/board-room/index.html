✨ Vibe

Board Room is a cozy, vintage-inspired Dupont Circle staple that perfectly blends the nostalgic fun of a massive board game library with the high-energy atmosphere of a classic neighborhood sports bar.

🍸 Best For

Group hangouts, ice-breaker dates, tabletop game enthusiasts, casual happy hours, and Washington Capitals fans.

🔥 Highlights

  • Games Galore: Access a legendary "game closet" filled to the brim with an extensive library of vintage and modern board games for all ages and eras.
  • Award-Winning Selection: Sip on award-winning beers, craft cocktails, and seasonal favorites like their popular boozy spiked lemonades while catching the game on nine big-screen TVs.
  • Rotating Events: Join a vibrant community for unique weekly programming, including Chess Mondays, monthly lock-picking workshops, and themed game nights.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of one of the city's best daily Happy Hours, running until 7:00 PM every day, featuring $5 rail drinks and select drafts.
  • While the venue focuses on its robust drink menu, it is a "bring your own food" friendly spot—local favorite Tatianna’s Pizza is located right across the street for easy delivery or pickup.
