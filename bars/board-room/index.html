Board Room

Category: Game Bar, Sports Bar

Price: $$

Address: 1737 Connecticut Ave NW, Washington, DC 20009

Situated in Dupont Circle, Board Room is a popular bar where patrons can enjoy a wide variety of board games along with cocktails and a large selection of draft beers. It features numerous TVs for watching sports and hosts events like weekly chess nights.
