Dacha Beer Garden

Dacha Beer Garden

Category: Beer Gardens, Happy Hour Spots, Dog Friendly

Neighborhood: Shaw

Price: $$

Address: 1600 7th St NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Dacha is a vibrant, European-inspired outdoor oasis in the heart of Shaw, radiating "Gemutlichkeit" with its festive atmosphere and welcoming community spirit.

🍸 Best For

Large group gatherings, outdoor day drinking, dog-friendly outings, weekend brunch, and festive seasonal markets.

🔥 Highlights

  • World-Class Brews: Features an elite, curated beer menu headlined by a partnership with Germany’s Weihenstephan—the world's oldest brewery—alongside local craft favorites and meads.
  • Award-Winning Atmosphere: Recognized by USA Today as one of the "Top 10 Beer Gardens in the U.S.," the venue boasts an iconic open-air design that is perfect for soaking up the neighborhood energy.
  • Bavarian-Inspired Bites: The kitchen serves up crowd favorites like their signature giant soft pretzels and highly-rated brunch specials, including the popular brisket benedict and $5 happy hour mimosas.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Dacha is proudly dog and family-friendly, ensuring a welcoming environment for both four-legged friends and kids.
  • For those hosting special occasions, the "Dacha Loft" provides a unique, elegant private event space tucked right above the bustling garden.
