The Big Hunt

Category: Dive Bar, Beer Bar, Comedy Venue

Price: $

Address: 1345 Connecticut Ave NW, Washington, DC 20036

A long-standing dive bar in Dupont Circle, The Big Hunt was known for its safari-themed decor, multiple bars, extensive beer list, and for hosting comedy shows. The venue is now permanently closed.
