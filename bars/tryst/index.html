✨ Vibe

A legendary Adams Morgan institution, Tryst offers a cozy, bohemian "living room" atmosphere that transitions seamlessly from a productive morning workspace to a vibrant social hub.

🍸 Best For

Remote work, morning coffee, casual brunch dates, board games with friends, evening cocktails

🔥 Highlights

  • Signature Touches: Every coffee drink is served with their iconic animal-shaped graham crackers, adding a charming and nostalgic touch to your experience.
  • Exceptional Drink Selection: From creamy Dirty Chai Frappes and refreshing London Fogs to expert espresso, there is a perfectly crafted beverage for every palate.
  • Spacious & Versatile: Featuring two large indoor rooms and inviting outdoor seating, the venue accommodates everything from solo reading sessions to lively group gatherings.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Early Risers Welcome: One of the neighborhood’s few spots to open early, it is a staple for morning meetings and early-day coffee runs.
  • Work-Friendly Amenities: Guests can take advantage of free Wi-Fi and an atmosphere that is highly conducive to productivity and creative work.

📰 In the News

American University, Washington, D.C.

Hallowed (Coffee) Grounds

Tryst serves as a pioneering community gathering space in Adams Morgan, blending the concepts of a coffeehouse, bar, and lounge into a relaxed 'third place' for locals. The venue is celebrated for its comfortable living-room atmosphere and its role as the flagship establishment of Constantine Stavropoulos's Washington D.C. eatery empire.
