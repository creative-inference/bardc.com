✨ Vibe

Penn Quarter Sports Tavern is a high-energy, quintessential D.C. sports destination where an electric atmosphere and walls of televisions make it the premier hub for game-day excitement.

🍸 Best For

Game day viewing parties, group outings near Capital One Arena, outdoor happy hours, post-museum dining, and classic American pub fare.

🔥 Highlights

  • Unmatched Viewing Experience: Boasting over 40 high-definition TVs throughout the venue—including the upstairs lounge and even the restrooms—this tavern ensures you never miss a second of the action.
  • Fan-Favorite Menu: Dig into a robust menu featuring signature hot honey and buffalo wings, the massive Triple Double smash burger, and the highly-rated Shark Bite Mahi Mahi bites.
  • A Local Institution: Recognized as a community pillar, the venue recently hosted high-profile celebrations like the Washington Nationals "Round on Riz" event, cementing its status as the go-to spot for D.C. sports fans.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The spacious outdoor sidewalk seating is perfect for people-watching and enjoying the lively atmosphere near the National Mall and Capital One Arena.
  • For a more elevated experience with stunning views and a beautiful bar top, head to the second-floor seating area which is ideal for larger groups.
