✨ Vibe

Sinners and Saints is a vibrant, QTBIPOC-owned basement sanctuary that blends a "Brooklyn hole-in-the-wall" aesthetic with an edgy, inclusive energy perfect for radical self-expression.

🍸 Best For

Sapphic socialites, T4T events, alternative fashion, high-energy dancing, and "scary strong" craft cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • Inclusive Programming: A rotating calendar of diverse, community-focused events including Desi drag nights, kink-themed art sessions, and dedicated bisexual-themed parties.
  • Atmospheric Dance Floor: An electric underground space featuring a curated rotation of DJs who bring a fresh, diverse sound to the Adams Morgan nightlife strip.
  • A Resilient Sanctuary: Recently recognized as a vital community pillar, this venue serves as a "for the girls, gays, and theys" home that prioritizes QTBIPOC joy and resistance.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Operating hours can vary depending on the event schedule, so be sure to check their Instagram for the latest updates, ticketed events, and DJ lineups.
  • The vibe is a welcoming "elevated dive" where alternative outfits are highly encouraged and the staff is known for being incredibly friendly.
