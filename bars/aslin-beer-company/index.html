Aslin Beer Company

Aslin Beer Company

Category: Breweries, Taproom, Groups

Neighborhood: Logan Circle

Price: $$

Address: 1740 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20009

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A vibrant, modern-industrial social hub in the heart of Logan Circle that seamlessly transitions from a bright, community-focused coffee bar to a high-energy evening beer garden.

🍸 Best For

Large group gatherings, craft beer connoisseurs, all-day remote work sessions, and lively weekend hangs on the 14th Street corridor.

🔥 Highlights

  • Extensive Craft Portfolio: Featuring 21 rotating taps including Aslin’s world-class experimental IPAs and sours, alongside exclusive R&D batches brewed on a dedicated one-barrel system right in the District.
  • Unique Culinary Partnerships: In addition to the on-site IZZI kitchen and forthcoming food trucks, guests can enjoy seamless table delivery from local 14th Street icons Chicken + Whiskey and Doi Moi.
  • The Driven Collective Era: Following a high-profile merger with New York’s Torch & Crown, this location serves as a premier flagship for the new Driven Collective, offering an expanded hospitality experience and top-tier distribution.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The full-service Aslin Coffee bar opens daily at 8:00 AM, offering house-made syrups, pastries, and a perfect environment for morning meetings.
  • The 211-seat venue features a spacious indoor/outdoor layout, including a welcoming patio that is ideal for enjoying the bustling atmosphere of downtown DC.
