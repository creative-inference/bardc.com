✨ Vibe

A quintessential Capitol Hill mainstay since 1979, this historic tavern blends a classic neighborhood atmosphere with the bustling energy of DC’s political hub.

🍸 Best For

Hill staffers, post-tour lunches, affordable happy hours, outdoor patio dining, casual business meetings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Exceptional Sandwiches: From the highly acclaimed TK Chicken Sandwich to their signature Maryland-style crab cakes, the menu offers elevated American classics served piping hot.
  • Icy Cold Brews: Features an impressive selection of beers and ciders served at the perfect temperature, making the outdoor seating a premier destination for a refreshing summer drink.
  • Speedy Service: Renowned for an incredibly efficient kitchen and attentive staff, it is the go-to spot for those needing a high-quality meal without the long wait.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located directly across from the Capitol South Metro station, providing easy access for commuters and tourists exploring the nearby National Mall.
  • Maintains some of the most competitive pricing in the area with an all-day selection of wines, beers, and ciders ranging from $6 to $10.
