Mission - Navy Yard

Mission - Navy Yard

Category: Taco Bars, Mexican, Sports Bars

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price:

Address: 1221 Van St SE, Washington, DC 20003, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A massive and energetic multi-level Tex-Mex powerhouse that blends industrial-modern style with a high-octane atmosphere perfect for sports fans and party-goers alike.

🍸 Best For

Nationals Park pre-gaming, large-scale corporate events, late-night dancing with DJs, daily happy hour, and festive weekend brunch.

🔥 Highlights

  • Architectural Grandeur: The venue features four distinct bars, including a record-breaking 150-foot bar on the second level and a stunning two-story atrium with breezy outdoor balconies.
  • Elevated Tex-Mex: From signature shrimp tacos and potato empanadas to loaded sheet pan nachos, the kitchen serves up crowd-pleasing favorites with excellent vegan-friendly options.
  • Elite Event Hosting: Renowned for its professional hospitality staff and versatile floor plans, it is a premier destination for everything from alumni gatherings to large-scale wedding after-parties.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Happy Hour is a neighborhood staple, offered daily from 4 PM to 6 PM, including weekends and game days.
  • On Friday and Saturday nights, the restaurant transforms into a nightlife hub with some of the city’s best DJs performing after the dinner service ends.
