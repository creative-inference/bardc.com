Solace Outpost

Solace Outpost

Category: Sports Bars, Gastropubs, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price:

Address: 71 Potomac Ave SE, Washington, DC 20003, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A massive, industrial-style taproom with a lively energy, offering scenic waterfront views and an expansive wraparound patio perfect for game days and social gatherings.

🍸 Best For

Sports fans, craft beer enthusiasts, waterfront happy hours, group networking, and pre-game fuel near Nationals Park.

🔥 Highlights

  • Premier Beer Selection: An impressive rotation of Virginia-brewed craft favorites like the Cloudy AF DIPA and Mosaic Wave Blonde, alongside creative beer-based cocktails and refreshing ciders.
  • Elevated Ballpark Fare: A gourmet twist on classic bar bites, featuring standout items like crispy chicken thighs, jumbo pretzels with beer cheese, and premium bratwurst sourced from Georgetown’s famous Stachowski Market.
  • The Ultimate Fan Hub: Recently host to major events like the Denver Broncos' Orange Herd Fan Rally, this spacious venue is a prime destination for Commanders watch parties and catching live sports on numerous TVs.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy unbeatable Happy Hour specials Monday through Friday until 5 PM and all day on Sundays, featuring discounts on drafts, wine, and $7 bar snacks.
  • Weekly entertainment includes Trivia Night every Thursday and 1/2 price growler fills every Wednesday.
