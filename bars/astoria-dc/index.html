Astoria DC

Astoria DC

Category: Cocktail Bars, Asian Fusion, Romantic

Address: 1521 17th St NW, Washington, DC 20036

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Astoria DC offers a sleek and intimate railroad-style setting that blends rustic charm with the sophisticated energy of a premier Dupont Circle cocktail den.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, Michelin-level Sichuan dining, bespoke craft cocktails, and stylish group outings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Michelin-Recognized Sichuan Flavors: Honored as a 2025 Bib Gourmand recipient, the kitchen shines with standout dishes like water-boiled beef, Dan Dan noodles, and the fan-favorite salt and pepper eggplant.
  • Expertly Curated Bar Program: From classic Vieux Carrés and tropical tiki-style drinks to personalized cocktails tailored to your specific palate, the drink menu is a masterpiece of craft mixology.
  • Warm, Professional Service: The dedicated staff is celebrated for their friendly, high-energy service, ensuring every guest feels like a VIP in this cozy and exclusive atmosphere.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations open exactly one week in advance and are highly recommended; however, arriving right at opening is a great way to snag a walk-in seat at the bar.
