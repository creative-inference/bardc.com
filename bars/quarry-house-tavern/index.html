Quarry House Tavern

Category: Dive Bar, Whiskey Bar, Burger Bar

Price: $

Address: 8401 Georgia Ave, Silver Spring, MD 20910

A legendary, subterranean dive bar in downtown Silver Spring, Quarry House Tavern is beloved for its incredible selection of whiskeys and beers, fantastic burgers, and a dark, cozy, old-school atmosphere. It's a cherished institution for locals and those in the know.
