Red Room Bar

Red Room Bar

Category: Bar, Live Music Venue

Price: $$

Address: 1811 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20009

The street-level bar inside the legendary Black Cat music venue, the Red Room Bar offers a more casual, laid-back experience than the main concert hall. It's a great spot to grab a drink on its own or before a show, with a classic dive-bar feel, pinball machines, and a good beer selection.
