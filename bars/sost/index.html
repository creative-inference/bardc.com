SOST

SOST

Category: Live Music Venues, Speakeasies, Rooftop Bars

Neighborhood: U Street

Price:

Address: 1901 9th St NW, Washington, DC 20001, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Sophisticated yet soulful, SOST is a stunningly designed three-story cultural sanctuary that blends Pan-African heritage with elevated jazz-inspired decor and a vibrant community pulse.

🍸 Best For

Immersive vinyl listening sessions, solo dining, chic date nights, Ethiopian coffee breaks, and lively group dinners with a cultural edge.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Vinyl Room & Speakeasy: A moody lower-level listening lounge where guests can enjoy expertly crafted smoked cocktails while spinning a curated selection of neo-soul, Afro-piano, and jazz-funk records.
  • A Masterful Fusion Menu: Recently recognized for its elevated Pan-African cuisine, the kitchen delivers bold flavors like the Grandma’s Suya platter, spicy sambusas, and the signature sweet potato croquette.
  • A Multi-Story Cultural Hub: This celebrated women-owned venue acts as a bridge for the African Diaspora, seamlessly transitioning from a sunny daytime café and art gallery into a vibrant evening resto-lounge and rooftop destination.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located in the heart of the U Street corridor, the venue is highly popular for its live music sets and DJ performances; booking a reservation is recommended for the restaurant level.
  • The space offers four distinct levels of entertainment, including a hybrid café that serves authentic Ethiopian honey lattes by day before transforming into a wine bar at night.
