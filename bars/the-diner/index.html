✨ Vibe

A quintessential, no-frills neighborhood staple in Adams Morgan offering a warm, welcoming atmosphere and friendly service any time of day or night.

🍸 Best For

Late-night cravings, family weekend brunch, boozy diner breakfasts, and quick neighborhood bites.

🔥 Highlights

  • Iconic 24/7 service that makes it the ultimate destination for post-nightlife fuel or early morning starts near the National Zoo.
  • A versatile menu of American classics, including "premium breakfasts" with fluffy pancakes, hearty sandwiches, and indulgent milkshakes for the kids.
  • A full-service bar featuring expertly crafted cocktails and a selection of beers, perfect for a lively and attentive Sunday brunch experience.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, providing reliable service and large portions around the clock.
  • Offers a variety of seating options to suit your mood, including indoor bar seating and breezy outdoor tables.
