Denizens Brewing Co.

Category: Brewery, Gastropub

Price: $$

Address: 2440 18th St NW, Washington, DC 20009

Denizens Brewing Co. operated a two-story taproom and gastropub in Adams Morgan. It featured a wide selection of their craft beers brewed in Maryland and a menu of globally inspired pub fare. This location is now permanently closed.
