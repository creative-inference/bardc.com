Opera Ultra Lounge

Opera Ultra Lounge

Category: Nightclub, Lounges, Late Night

Neighborhood: Downtown

Price: $$$

Address: 1400 I St NW, Washington, DC 20005

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Opera Ultra Lounge offers a sophisticated and high-energy subterranean escape in the heart of downtown DC, blending an upscale lounge atmosphere with a cutting-edge nightclub experience.

🍸 Best For

Late-night dancing, premium bottle service, group celebrations, and high-end weekend nightlife.

🔥 Highlights

  • Immersive audio experience featuring a 30,000-watt world-class Funktion One sound system and high-tech lighting.
  • Visually stunning 6,000-square-foot interior designed by Francois Frossard, featuring a unique circular amphitheater layout and custom-upholstered couches.
  • An upscale destination that hosts a rotating lineup of premier local and international DJs in a vibrant, high-energy setting.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Table reservations and bottle service are highly recommended for groups looking for an elevated, curated experience.
  • The venue focuses on a premium beverage program featuring signature house cocktails and top-shelf spirits.
