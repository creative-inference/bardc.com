✨ Vibe

Nido is a chic Mediterranean-inspired gem with warm Andalusian décor, blending the charm of a European specialty market with a welcoming, high-energy neighborhood social hub.

🍸 Best For

Natural wine lovers, artisanal gift shopping, casual lunches, tinned fish connoisseurs, and Friday night social tastings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Iconic Spanish BLT: A crowd-favorite signature sandwich featuring savory Serrano ham on light, airy fresh-baked focaccia.
  • Expertly Curated Market: Shelves are stocked with world-class tinned fish, imported European pantry staples, and a natural wine selection managed by knowledgeable "wine geniuses" Thea and Morgan.
  • Award-Winning Neighborhood Flavor: Featured as a premier destination in Mount Pleasant—recently named one of Time Out’s "coolest neighborhoods in the world."

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Don't miss their popular Friday night wine tastings, which offer a rotating and expert-led deep dive into unique global bottles.
  • Limited indoor seating is available, making it a perfect spot to enjoy a glass of wine and their famous Basque burnt cheesecake on-site.
