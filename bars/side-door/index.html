✨ Vibe

Recently featured as one of the best vinyl bars in D.C., Side Door offers a nostalgic "cool grandpa’s basement" aesthetic complete with wood paneling, midcentury decor, and an incredible library of over 1,000 records.

🍸 Best For

Vinyl enthusiasts, hidden gem seekers, creative cocktail connoisseurs, and intimate date nights.

🔥 Highlights

  • Themed & Bespoke Cocktails: Enjoy a rotating list of creative, themed drinks and barrel-aged classics, or ask the expert bartenders to whip up a custom creation based on your personal flavor profile.
  • Immersive Audio Experience: The walls are adorned with an extensive vinyl collection that fuels a curated, on-brand soundtrack for a truly 10/10 musical atmosphere.
  • Modern Gastropub Fare: While focusing on drinks, the venue serves high-end comfort food like Creamy Rigatoni Mac & Cheese, Buttermilk Scallion Hush Puppies, and the signature "Heidi" crispy chicken sandwich.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • To find this subterranean speakeasy, walk through the Pub & the People patio, look for the retro stripe on the ivy-covered wall, and head down the stairs.
  • Reservations are highly recommended and can be found on the Pub & the People Resy page.
