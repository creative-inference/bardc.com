Jake's Tavern

Jake's Tavern

Category: Taverns, Sports Bars, Neighborhood Bars

Neighborhood: Shaw

Price:

Address: 1606 7th St NW, Washington, DC 20001, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A maritime-inspired neighborhood gem in Shaw that offers a refreshingly unpretentious atmosphere focused on honest drinks, friendly energy, and high-spirited sports fandom.

🍸 Best For

D.C. United match days, competitive trivia nights, large group gatherings, and casual weekend hangouts on the patio.

🔥 Highlights

  • Premier Soccer Destination: As an official host for D.C. United Away Day Watch Parties, the tavern is a high-energy hub for the local soccer community and sports fans of all stripes.
  • Expanded Entertainment Space: A newly launched second-floor venue provides a dedicated area for DJs, private celebrations, and exclusive game-day events.
  • Elevated Bar Fare & Drinks: Renowned for serving the "best orange crushes in town" alongside authentic Nashville hot chicken, gourmet cheese curds, and local specialties.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Don't miss the Tuesday night "Shaw Showdown" trivia starting at 7:30 PM, with happy hour specials on gumbo and $6 drafts running from 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM.
  • The venue features a unique nautical-themed interior and a standout back patio equipped with a boat, perfect for outdoor socializing.
