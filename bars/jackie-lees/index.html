Jackie Lee's

Category: Neighborhood Bar, American

Price: $$

Address: 116 Kennedy St NW, Washington, DC 20011

A welcoming and colorful neighborhood bar in Brightwood Park, Jackie Lee's offers a relaxed setting for craft cocktails, beer, and inventive pub fare. It features a comfortable main bar and a spacious back patio, making it a beloved local hangout.
