Beuchert's Saloon

Category: Restaurant, Bar, Historic Bar

Price: $$$

Address: 623 Pennsylvania Ave SE, Washington, DC 20003

A neighborhood restaurant in Capitol Hill, Beuchert's Saloon serves a 'farm American' dining experience with ingredients sourced from local farms. The establishment pays homage to the original 1880s saloon and speakeasy that once occupied the same space.
