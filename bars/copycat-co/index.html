Copycat Co.

Category: Cocktail Bar, Chinese

Price: $$

Address: 1110 H St NE, Washington, DC 20002

An intimate, bi-level spot on H Street, Copycat Co. features a ground-floor counter serving Northern Chinese street food like potstickers and bao, while the upstairs bar offers expertly crafted classic and contemporary cocktails in a dimly lit, stylish setting.
