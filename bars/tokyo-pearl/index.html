✨ Vibe

Tokyo Pearl is a chic and high-energy hybrid of a Japanese-inspired sushi bar and a trendy lounge, featuring stunning aesthetics, fun lighting, and live DJs that keep the atmosphere electric.

🍸 Best For

Extensive happy hours, group birthday celebrations, lively weekend nights out, and photo-ready cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • Unbeatable Happy Hour: Known for its incredible value, the venue offers a wide selection of $7 sushi rolls and snacks alongside generous drink specials.
  • Insta-Worthy Decor: The interior features beautiful aesthetics, including unique hanging basket chairs and a stylish back patio with a retractable roof.
  • High-Energy Soundtrack: Frequent live DJ sets seamlessly transition the space from a sleek dining destination into a vibrant late-night lounge.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Happy hour is a major draw here, running from 4:00 PM to 10:00 PM on weekdays.
  • Given its popularity in Dupont Circle, making a reservation is highly recommended for weekend nights and special occasions.
