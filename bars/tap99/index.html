Tap99

Tap99

Category: Sports Bars, Taproom, Beer Gardens

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price:

Address: 1250 Half St SE, Washington, DC 20003, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

High-energy and interactive, this tech-forward taphouse offers a vibrant, social atmosphere directly across from the stadium, perfect for sports fans and craft beverage enthusiasts alike.

🍸 Best For

Pre-game drinks, sampling rare craft beers, social group outings, casual date nights, holiday pop-up experiences

🔥 Highlights

  • The Self-Pour "Drink Wall": Featuring 99 rotating taps and a unique RFID card system, you can explore a massive selection of local craft beers, wines, and pre-mixed cocktails while paying only by the ounce.
  • Prime Stadium Proximity: Located directly across from the Nationals Park main gates, it’s the ultimate destination for game-day crowds looking for a lively indoor-outdoor environment with plenty of TVs.
  • Artisan Brick-Oven Eats: Complement your custom pours with a menu of neo-Neapolitan pizzas, crispy chicken sandwiches, and fan-favorite shareables like their signature fried pickles.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Keep an eye out for immersive seasonal transformations, such as the festive "Whoville Hideaway" holiday pop-up featuring immersive décor and themed cocktails.
  • The digital card system allows you to track your running tab in real-time, making it easy to sample a wide variety of drinks at your own pace.
