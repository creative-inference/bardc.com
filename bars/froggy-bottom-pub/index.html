✨ Vibe

A welcoming and relaxed neighborhood institution that blends a classic, no-frills college pub atmosphere with the heartfelt warmth of a family-owned "home away from home."

🍸 Best For

George Washington University students, budget-friendly happy hours, late-night dining, and unique Vietnamese-American fusion comfort food.

🔥 Highlights

  • Eclectic Culinary Fusion: The menu offers a fantastic mix of traditional favorites, including "best-ever" chicken wings and burgers, alongside authentic Vietnamese specialties like Pho, Bánh Mì, and $7 crispy spring rolls.
  • Deep Community Roots: A true Foggy Bottom staple, the pub is celebrated for fostering a sense of belonging, famously serving as a long-standing gathering place for veterans and university students alike.
  • Signature Sips & Spaces: Guests can enjoy the popular Froggy Bottom Pub Amber Ale at the spacious hardwood bar or relax on the expanded outdoor patio for a casual West End hangout.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Join the fun for double happy hours featuring $6 Guinness and rail drinks, running Monday through Saturday from 4:00 PM – 7:00 PM and again from 11:00 PM – Close.
  • The venue offers huge portions and a unique dining experience that is both surprisingly affordable and highly welcoming to newcomers and regulars alike.
