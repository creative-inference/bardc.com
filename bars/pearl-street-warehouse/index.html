Pearl Street Warehouse

Pearl Street Warehouse

Category: Live Music Venues, American, Waterfront

Neighborhood: The Wharf

Price: $$

Address: 33 Pearl St SW, Washington, DC 20024

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An intimate, industrial-chic "back alley" music hall that brings you face-to-face with world-class artists in the heart of The Wharf’s vibrant waterfront.

🍸 Best For

Up-close live music experiences, curated indie concerts, casual waterfront dates, and high-energy private event receptions.

🔥 Highlights

  • Now part of the prestigious Union Stage Presents family, this venue offers a professional, top-tier sound system with stellar acoustics that ensure every drum beat and bass line hits perfectly.
  • The versatile multi-level layout features an "all-access" feel where the front row is just inches from the stage, plus an elevated mezzanine for birds-eye views of the performance.
  • Enjoy a diverse and packed calendar of events ranging from free bluegrass festivals and comedy nights to high-profile touring acts like Margaret Glaspy and the upcoming Gubbulidis tour.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Arrive early to snag a coveted spot along the upstairs mezzanine railing for the absolute best sightlines in the house.
  • Most shows are all-ages, and the venue features a unique outdoor seating area where you can still see and hear the performers while enjoying the fresh air.
