Pearl Street Warehouse

Pearl Street Warehouse

Category: Live Music Venue, American, Bar

Price: $$

Address: 33 Pearl St SW, Washington, DC 20024

An intimate live music venue at The Wharf, Pearl Street Warehouse showcases a mix of rock, country, folk, and blues artists. The diner-style venue offers American comfort food and cocktails, with a focus on creating a close-up and personal concert experience.
