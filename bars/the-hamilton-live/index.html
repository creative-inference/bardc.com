The Hamilton Live

Category: Live Music Venue, Supper Club

Price: $$$

Address: 600 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20005

Located beneath The Hamilton restaurant downtown, The Hamilton Live is a state-of-the-art music venue that hosts a diverse range of local, national, and international artists. It offers a supper-club experience, allowing guests to enjoy food and drinks during the performances.
