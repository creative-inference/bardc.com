✨ Vibe

Salazar is a high-energy, multi-story Mexican haven that transitions seamlessly from a sun-drenched rooftop social spot to a pulse-pounding basement dance floor.

🍸 Best For

Large group celebrations, bottomless party brunches, late-night dancing, tequila enthusiasts, and sunset drinks on the roof.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Iconic Ice Shot: A must-do U Street tradition where guests take a shot and hurl the ice at a rooftop bell for a celebratory ring.
  • Multi-Level Energy: With expert DJs in the basement "never missing" a beat and a rose-red brick rooftop perfect for margarita pitchers, there is a floor for every mood.
  • Premier Event Hosting: Renowned for seamless coordination and friendly staff, it is a top-tier destination for stress-free birthday parties and large gatherings.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of Happy Hour specials on margaritas and tacos until 7 PM on weekdays, 6 PM on weekends, and all night long every Thursday.
  • Salazar is a cashless establishment and offers one of the city's most popular bottomless brunches on weekends from 11 AM to 4 PM.
