✨ Vibe

Abigail is a sleek and modern 5,000-square-foot destination in the heart of downtown that blends high-energy clubbing with an inclusive, multicultural atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

VIP bottle service, birthday celebrations, hip-hop and Afrobeats fans, and upscale private events.

🔥 Highlights

  • Curated weekly theme nights including "Abby Fridays" and "AfroBeats Saturdays" that showcase a diverse mix of hip-hop, electronic, and international sounds.
  • A spacious and plush interior featuring premium VIP sections designed for ultimate comfort, style, and high-end champagne service.
  • An inclusive nightlife experience led by mogul David S. Chung, specifically designed to celebrate Washington D.C.’s vibrant multicultural demographic.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue is conveniently situated in the Central Business District, just a short walk from the Dupont Circle and Farragut North metro stations.
  • For the ultimate experience, guests can reserve one of the many plush bottle service sections to enjoy dedicated attention and premium spirits.
