✨ Vibe

A sophisticated and stylish rooftop sanctuary perched atop the Rosewood Hotel, offering an upscale atmosphere with some of the most stunning panoramic views in Georgetown.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, sunset cocktails, refined small bites, and festive seasonal pop-ups.

🔥 Highlights

  • Wolfgang Puck Culinary Excellence: Indulge in an elevated menu featuring signature items like fresh ceviche, the famous CUT burger, and decadent desserts including churros with dulce de leche and the acclaimed chocolate soufflé.
  • The Lodge Winter Pop-Up: During the cooler months, the space transforms into a cozy, ski-inspired hideaway featuring festive décor, holiday cocktails, and a heated, covered rooftop experience.
  • Iconic Panoramic Views: Enjoy a breathtaking backdrop that includes the Potomac River, the Kennedy Center, the Washington Monument, and the glittering Virginia skyline.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended to secure the best seating for golden hour and to minimize wait times.
  • The venue is a premier choice for private events, accommodating up to 100 guests for outdoor soirées and cocktail receptions.

The Lodge at CUT Above

CUT Above at the Rosewood hotel in Georgetown presents "The Lodge," a festive, ski-inspired rooftop pop-up featuring seasonal cocktails and light bites. The immersive space includes a red ski-lift chair for photo opportunities and offers panoramic views of the Washington Monument and Kennedy Center.

Raise a glass at D.C.'s hottest rooftop bars

CUT Above offers an elevated rooftop experience at the Rosewood Washington D.C., featuring a culinary program by Wolfgang Puck. Guests can enjoy artistic cocktails and refined bar bites while taking in enchanting views of Georgetown, the Potomac River, and the Kennedy Center.
