✨ Vibe

Zeba Bar is a lively and unpretentious neighborhood gem in Columbia Heights, known for its high-energy dance floors and relaxed multi-level lounge atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Late-night dancing, group hookah sessions, affordable happy hours, and breezy patio drinks.

🔥 Highlights

  • High-Energy Dance Parties: Home to legendary events like the OMG BOYZ parties, where the music and atmosphere keep the crowd dancing all night long.
  • Excellent Hookah Service: Renowned for consistently smooth, high-quality shisha and a dedicated, spacious upstairs lounge perfect for social smoking.
  • Versatile Outdoor Space: Features a charming upper back patio that offers a great escape for enjoying frozen cocktails and snacks during the warmer months.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of standout happy hour deals, including half-price wine bottles and great specials on bar bites.
  • The venue features multiple floors, with the upstairs area offering a more spacious, relaxed setting for groups and hookah enthusiasts.
