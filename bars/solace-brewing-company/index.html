Category: Brewery, Taproom

★★★★★ 4.5 (447 reviews)

A prominent craft brewery based in Northern Virginia, Solace Brewing Company is known for its wide variety of well-crafted beers, especially its IPAs. Their original location in Sterling and their outpost in Falls Church both feature spacious taprooms and a lively, welcoming atmosphere for beer lovers.
