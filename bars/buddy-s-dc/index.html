✨ Vibe

Buddy’s DC is a warm and inclusive Columbia Heights neighborhood hub that perfectly balances the spirited energy of a sports bar with a relaxed, community-first atmosphere where everyone feels like a local.

🍸 Best For

Music bingo, game-day viewing, hosting alumni events, daily happy hours, and weekend brunch.

🔥 Highlights

  • Elevated Comfort Food: The menu features standout dishes like perfectly seasoned fried catfish, Old Bay wings, and the fan-favorite Avocado Caesar, along with inclusive options like the hearty Vegan Bowl.
  • Engaging Weekly Events: Beyond the sports on TV, the venue draws a crowd for its "shockingly good" music bingo nights held twice a week and its vibrant, high-energy atmosphere.
  • Exceptional Hospitality: Known for its attentive owners and friendly staff like Brenda, Buddy’s excels at making large groups and private events feel seamless and personalized.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Stop by for "Work Hard, Happy Hour Harder" specials running daily from 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM.
  • The venue is closed on Tuesdays; weekend brunch kicks off at 11:30 AM on Saturdays and Sundays.
