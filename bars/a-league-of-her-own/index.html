A League of Her Own

A League of Her Own

Category: Lesbian Bars, Sports Bars, Dance Clubs

Neighborhood: Adams Morgan

Price: $$

Address: 2319 18th St NW, Washington, DC 20009

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A vibrant and inclusive multi-level lesbian sports bar that combines a welcoming community atmosphere with high-energy dance floors and a passion for queer nightlife.

🍸 Best For

Late-night dancing, queer community connection, retro gaming, sports fans, and Thursday night trivia.

🔥 Highlights

  • Expansive Multi-Level Venue: Spread across three floors, the space features two bar areas, two dedicated dance floors, and rooftop access, offering a variety of environments to explore.
  • Interactive Entertainment: Recently recognized as one of the best lesbian bars in the USA, the venue keeps guests entertained with gaming consoles like Mario Kart, numerous TV screens for sports, and high-impact events like drag shows and karaoke.
  • Affordable & Social: Known for its fairly priced drinks and affordable shots, it’s a go-to spot for meeting new people and enjoying a lively, youthful crowd in the historic Adams Morgan neighborhood.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The bar operates as a specialized space within the larger Pitchers establishment, creating a massive LGBTQ+ complex for a full night out.
  • As one of the few remaining dedicated lesbian bars in the country, it serves as a historic and essential hub for the DC queer community.
