✨ Vibe

Sudhouse DC is a charming, "mom-and-pop" style neighborhood bar that pairs a rustic, bohemian atmosphere with the unhurried, welcoming spirit of the U Street corridor.

🍸 Best For

Happy hour enthusiasts, private parties and fundraisers, local neighborhood vibes, budget-friendly drinks, and classic American comfort food.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Value: Recently featured for its "unconventional" happy hour deals, Sudhouse is a premier destination for those seeking high-quality, budget-friendly drink and food specials in the District.
  • Premier Event Space: The venue offers a spacious 900-square-foot private second floor that accommodates up to 90 guests, featuring a full-service private bar and the ability to customize your own music.
  • Elevated Pub Fare: Moving beyond basic bar bites, the kitchen serves highly-rated dishes like the avocado shrimp wrap and classic American fare that consistently exceeds expectations.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy "All-Day" happy hour every Monday, with additional specials running Tuesday through Friday (4 PM–8 PM) and weekends (2 PM–6 PM).
  • Widely recognized as one of the best free event spaces in DC, it is an ideal local spot for coordinating stress-free celebrations or nonprofit fundraisers.
