✨ Vibe

Ultrabar is a high-octane, multi-level dance destination in Gallery Place-Chinatown that offers a dynamic nightlife experience with distinct musical atmospheres on every floor.

🍸 Best For

18+ clubbing, multi-genre music fans, VIP bottle service, group celebrations, late-night dancing.

🔥 Highlights

  • Four distinct levels each featuring a different DJ and musical style, ensuring a fresh energy and "fire" vibes as you move through the club.
  • Unique lounge environments, including the stylish second-floor "bedroom" area equipped with chic beds and benches for a one-of-a-kind aesthetic.
  • Premium VIP table service and "Baller Packages" that provide an elevated experience for groups of up to eight guests.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • A strict dress code is enforced (no athletic wear), so be sure to dress to impress for the weekend.
  • Doors open at 10:00 PM on Fridays and Saturdays, with early arrival or VIP reservations recommended to beat the crowds.
