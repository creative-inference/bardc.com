Blues Alley

Category: Jazz & Blues Club, Supper Club

Price: $$$

Address: 1073 Wisconsin Ave NW, Washington, DC 20007

Founded in 1965, Blues Alley is the nation's oldest continuously operating jazz supper club. Located in a historic 18th-century carriage house in Georgetown, this intimate, 125-seat venue has hosted legendary artists like Dizzy Gillespie and Sarah Vaughan and serves a Creole-inspired menu.
