Iron Gate

Category: Restaurant, Wine Bar, Mediterranean

Price: $$$

Address: 1734 N St NW, Washington, DC 20036

★★★★★ 4.5 (1242 reviews)

Tucked away in a historic former stable near Dupont Circle, Iron Gate is one of D.C.'s most romantic dining spots. It features a wisteria-covered patio, a cozy dining room with a fireplace, and a menu of Greek and Italian-inspired dishes, complemented by an extensive wine list.
