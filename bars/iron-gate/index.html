Iron Gate

Iron Gate

Category: Mediterranean, Wine Bars, Romantic

Neighborhood: Dupont Circle

Price: $$$

Address: 1734 N St NW, Washington, DC 20036

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Set in a historic 19th-century stable, Iron Gate offers a romantic and secluded Mediterranean oasis featuring a candlelit dining room and a legendary patio canopy of wisteria and grapevines.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, family celebrations, wine enthusiasts, and upscale garden dining.

🔥 Highlights

  • Historic Ambiance: As one of DC’s oldest continuously operating restaurants, the venue provides a unique atmosphere split between a cozy, firelit carriage house and a lush, heated outdoor patio with fire pits at every table.
  • Award-Winning Cuisine: Chef Anthony Chittum, a three-star recipient from the Washington Post, blends Mid-Atlantic ingredients with Mediterranean traditions to create standout dishes like the signature beetroot ravioli and housemade garlic focaccia.
  • Exceptional Service & Wine: Recognized by the Restaurant Association of Washington DC for both Best Service and Best Wine Program, the staff is known for a warm, attentive approach that often includes thoughtful celebratory touches.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended for both the sumptuous indoor dining room and the popular wisteria-covered patio.
  • Beyond their nightly Mediterranean "Family Table" tasting menu, the restaurant offers a refined weekend brunch and a weekday lunch service.
