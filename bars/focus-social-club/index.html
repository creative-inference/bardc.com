Recently making waves as one of Washington, D.C.’s newest Black-owned social hubs, Focus Social Club on H Street NE offers a sophisticated, art-filled environment designed for everything from professional networking to high-energy weekend celebrations.

✨ Vibe

An energetic, multi-level sanctuary that blends a chic, art-forward aesthetic with a vibrant urban nightlife pulse.

🍸 Best For

Bottomless brunch enthusiasts, weekend socializing, hip-hop lovers, and lively group celebrations.

🔥 Highlights

  • Multi-Level Experience: With three distinct floors to explore, the venue offers a dynamic layout that provides different vantage points and moods for dining and mingling.
  • Premier Brunch Destination: Recognized as a standout Black-owned brunch spot, the weekend experience features a well-seasoned buffet and flowing mimosas in a high-energy atmosphere.
  • Sophisticated Social Hub: A versatile venue that serves as a community anchor, seamlessly transitioning from a curated co-working space to a premier nightlife destination with live DJs.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Advance ticket purchases are recommended for their popular weekend brunch to ensure a seamless entry for you and your group.
  • The venue spans several floors, making it a great choice for those who enjoy a lively, exploratory environment.
