Crown & Crow

Category: Bar, Lounge

Price: $$

Address: 1317 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20005

A bar on 14th Street with a vintage, literary-inspired theme, Crown & Crow offers a classic, old-school atmosphere. Its decor features taxidermy and antique books, creating a cozy and unique setting for enjoying classic cocktails and conversation.
