The Fireplace

The Fireplace

Category: LGBTQ+ Bars, Dive Bars, Karaoke Bars

Neighborhood: Dupont Circle

Price: $

Address: 2161 P St NW, Washington, DC 20037

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A legendary and welcoming LGBTQ+ dive bar in the heart of Dupont Circle that balances a cozy, two-story neighborhood feel with high-energy urban celebrations.

🍸 Best For

LGBTQ+ culture, affordable cocktails, late-night karaoke, neighborhood socializing, weekend dance parties.

🔥 Highlights

  • Wednesday Karaoke: Known as one of the most spirited karaoke nights in DC, where the local community brings incredible energy and talent to the stage.
  • The "DC Swamp Water": A must-try signature cocktail that serves as a fan-favorite staple for regulars and newcomers alike.
  • Legendary Longevity: A true Washington institution since 1989, featuring a multi-level layout that offers both intimate lounge spots and vibrant party spaces.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • While the venue focuses exclusively on the bar experience, guests can conveniently grab a meal at the iconic Z-Burger located right next door.
  • Visit during the weekday happy hour for a more relaxed, "chill" atmosphere with some of the friendliest bartenders in the city.
