✨ Vibe

An intimate and welcoming neighborhood gem that offers a cozy, "home away from home" atmosphere perfect for relaxing in the heart of the U Street corridor.

🍸 Best For

Late-night cocktails, casual dates, solo reading with a drink, and artisanal non-alcoholic refreshments.

🔥 Highlights

  • Customized Cocktails: The talented bar team specializes in bespoke mixology, frequently crafting personalized drinks tailored to your specific taste and mood.
  • Sophisticated Sips & Snacks: Beyond their acclaimed spirit list, they offer a delicious house-made citrus and ginger non-alcoholic drink alongside classic bar bites like seasoned almonds and olives.
  • Perfect for Book Lovers: Recognized as one of the best bars in the city to "lose yourself in a book," the environment strikes a rare balance between social hub and peaceful retreat.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy extended late-night hours until 3:00 AM on Fridays and Saturdays for those looking to cap off their evening in style.
  • The bar operates with a 22% service charge included, ensuring the dedicated staff is consistently supported while you enjoy the neighborhood hospitality.

All Souls Bar | Bars in Shaw, Washington DC

All Souls Bar is a no-nonsense drinking den in DC's Shaw neighborhood known for serving high-quality classic cocktails like the Negroni at affordable prices. While the bar does not serve its own food, patrons are encouraged to bring their own meals to enjoy in the welcoming, modestly decorated atmosphere.
