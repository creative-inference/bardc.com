Next Level DC

Category: Live Music Venues, Nightclub, Hookah Lounges

Neighborhood: NoMa

Price:

Address: 15 K St NE, Washington, DC 20002, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A high-energy, multi-level urban sanctuary in NoMa where sleek neon lighting and soulful live performances meet a vibrant, celebratory nightclub atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Live music performances, late-night dancing, poetry slams, hookah enthusiasts, and VIP group celebrations.

🔥 Highlights

  • Dynamic Entertainment: From excellent live bands and soulful poetry nights to high-energy DJ sets, the professional stage is a focal point for top-tier local talent.
  • Sleek Multi-Level Design: The venue features a stylish interior filled with contemporary art, vibrant lighting, and versatile seating options across multiple floors, including an outdoor area.
  • Premium Amenities: Guests can enjoy a curated experience with dedicated VIP sections, efficient parking services, and a lively, social crowd that keeps the energy high all night.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Dedicated parking is available in the rear lot, providing a convenient arrival experience in the heart of the city.
  • Booking a VIP section in advance is highly recommended for groups looking to enjoy the full "Next Level" experience with a dedicated home base.
