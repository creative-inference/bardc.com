✨ Vibe

Proper 21-K Street offers an elevated American bistro experience that blends a sleek modern aesthetic with a vibrant, high-energy atmosphere perfect for transitioning from the workday to a night out.

🍸 Best For

Post-work happy hours, high-end sports viewing, sophisticated group dinners, and outdoor alfresco dining.

🔥 Highlights

  • Signature Gourmet Fare: Indulge in fan favorites like the famous "Proper Burger," innovative build-your-own lotus buns, and fresh, seasonally inspired bowls like the Ahi Tuna Poke or Beef Bulgogi.
  • Premier Outdoor Space: Boasting a massive 2,000-square-foot outdoor patio, it is one of Foggy Bottom's top destinations for open-air cocktails and social gatherings.
  • Refined Cocktail Program: Enjoy a sophisticated beverage menu featuring forced carbonated cocktails, craft drafts, and house wines served in a stunning gold and dark green-accented space.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Happy Hour is available weekdays from 5:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m., offering great deals on signature drinks and house favorites.
  • The venue is highly accessible and reservation-friendly, making it a reliable and seamless choice for both routine visits and special occasions.
