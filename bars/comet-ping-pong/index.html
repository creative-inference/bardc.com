Comet Ping Pong

Category: Pizza Restaurant, Live Music Venue, Bar

Price: $$

Address: 5037 Connecticut Ave NW, Washington, DC 20008

A neighborhood fixture in Upper Northwest, Comet Ping Pong combines a family-friendly pizzeria with a live music venue and bar. It's known for its gourmet pizzas, ping-pong tables, and for hosting a wide array of local and touring indie rock bands.
