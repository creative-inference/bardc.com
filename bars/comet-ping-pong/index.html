Comet Ping Pong

Comet Ping Pong

Category: Live Music Venues, Pizza Bars, Game Bars

Neighborhood: Tenleytown

Price: $$

Address: 5037 Connecticut Ave NW, Washington, DC 20008

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

This bustling Upper Connecticut Avenue haunt radiates a laid-back, creative energy where wood-fired ovens and indie rock riffs create the ultimate neighborhood hangout.

🍸 Best For

Family-friendly dinners, late-night indie concerts, artisanal pizza cravings, and spirited ping pong tournaments.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Wood-Fired Pies: Recently named one of the 16 Best Pizza Joints in DC, Comet specializes in Neapolitan-style crusts with creative toppings like the "Spring Chick" and the fan-favorite "Steel Will."
  • Intimate Live Music Scene: A beloved destination for discovery, the venue's dedicated performance space hosts a curated lineup of local and touring indie rock bands for high-energy, mid-week and weekend shows.
  • Craft Food & Drink: Beyond the tables, enjoy "melt-in-your-mouth" wings, garlic knot meatball sliders, and exclusive local brews like the Right Proper Comet IPA, followed by sundaes made with local Honey's ice cream.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue is walk-in only, making it a perfect spot for spontaneous gatherings, though it can get lively during peak dinner and brunch hours.
  • Performance times typically begin at 10:00 PM; check their "Live Music" calendar for upcoming ticketed sets and local showcases.
