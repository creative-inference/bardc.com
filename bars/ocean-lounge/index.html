✨ Vibe

Ocean Lounge is a high-energy, multi-level sanctuary on H Street that masterfully blends a chic, sophisticated atmosphere with the vibrant, welcoming spirit of a premier black-owned nightlife destination.

🍸 Best For

Birthday celebrations, VIP bottle service, networking with local professionals, late-night dancing, and catching live local entertainment.

🔥 Highlights

  • Four Versatile Levels: With four distinct floors to explore, you can transition seamlessly from a high-energy dance floor with "poppn" beats to chill lounges designed for networking and socializing.
  • The "Mecca of $1 Wings": Beyond the impressive bar and signature cocktails, guests rave about the incredible food offerings, including their famous $1 wing specials and collaborations with popular local vendors.
  • Sophisticated Energy: The venue strikes the perfect balance between a high-end club experience and a friendly community hub where "everyone compliments each other" and the "sexy" bartending staff provides top-tier service.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • For the ultimate weekend experience, secure a VIP table reservation in advance, especially for Friday and Saturday nights when the energy stays high until 3:00 AM.
  • The venue offers a diverse schedule, including lively Sunday sessions starting at 12:00 PM that are perfect for day-party enthusiasts.
