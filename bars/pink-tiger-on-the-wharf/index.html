✨ Vibe

Pink Tiger is a whimsical, retro-glam waterfront oasis that pairs 30-foot ceilings and plush pink accents with a high-energy atmosphere that transitions from sun-drenched patio lounging to a vibrant late-night music lounge.

🍸 Best For

Waterfront date nights, celebratory group dinners, late-night cocktails after a show at The Anthem, and lively happy hours with a view.

🔥 Highlights

  • Interactive Dining: The tableside Wagyu “Hot Rock” and garlic Parmesan noodles are standout fan favorites that combine premium ingredients with a memorable presentation.
  • Live Entertainment: The venue features an incredible resident performer, Jeremy Ragsdale, and hosts live music and weekend DJs that keep the energy high until 2:00 AM.
  • Chef-Driven Fusion: Executive Chef Joseph Castro blends Asian and Southern-American influences, resulting in unique creations like miso deviled eggs, fried red snapper, and signature lychee martinis.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Happy Hour is available at the bar and select patio seating Monday through Friday (starting at 11:30 AM most days, and 5:00 PM on Tuesdays).
  • Reservations are highly recommended for weekend nights when the live music and waterfront views draw a consistent crowd.
