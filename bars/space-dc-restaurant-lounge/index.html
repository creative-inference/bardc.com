✨ Vibe

An upscale, three-story cultural destination that seamlessly blends Ethiopian-Eritrean heritage with a modern, high-energy lounge atmosphere in the heart of the U Street Corridor.

🍸 Best For

Late-night dancing, curated vinyl sets, group celebrations, Ethiopian-Eritrean fusion dining, and premium hookah sessions.

🔥 Highlights

  • Multi-Level Experience: A sophisticated three-story layout featuring a dedicated vinyl lounge for audiophiles, an elegant restaurant, and a vibrant upper-level club space.
  • Innovative Fusion Menu: Signature culinary offerings like the "Berber-Q" braised chicken and creative coffee-based cocktails that celebrate the flavors of the Black diaspora.
  • Cultural Hub: Recently opened as a community-focused "third place" designed for networking events, live music performances, and high-end entertainment.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located at the historic 1355 U Street NW, this venue offers distinct environments on every floor, making it perfect for transitioning from dinner to a late-night party.
  • Check their schedule for special DJ sets and vinyl mixing sessions that showcase local and international talent.
