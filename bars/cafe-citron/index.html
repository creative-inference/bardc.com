Cafe Citron

Cafe Citron

Category: Dance Clubs, Latin, Nightclub

Neighborhood: Dupont Circle

Price: $$

Address: 1343 Connecticut Ave NW, Washington, DC 20036

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A vibrant, multi-level Latin oasis in the heart of Dupont Circle that seamlessly transforms from a colorful South American restaurant into a high-energy dance club with infectious rhythms and tropical flair.

🍸 Best For

Salsa and Bachata enthusiasts, lively birthday celebrations, group night outs, Spanish language practice, and late-night dancing to Latin hits.

🔥 Highlights

  • Complimentary Dance Lessons: Master the Latin two-step with free, high-energy introductory salsa and bachata classes offered every Wednesday, Friday, and Saturday night.
  • Multi-Level Sonic Experience: Explore three to four floors of music where dedicated DJs spin a diverse mix of reggaeton, Latin house, salsa, bachata, and EDM across different rooms.
  • Themed Latin Fiestas: Enjoy signature weekly events like the "Drums of DC" on Saturdays and the "Macarena Fiesta Latina" on Wednesdays, paired with authentic South American bites like empanadas and ceviche.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue transitions from dinner service to a full nightclub atmosphere around 10:00 PM; arrive early to take advantage of the late-night happy hour specials.
  • Located directly across from the Dupont Circle Metro (South Exit), it is a premier destination for private events and large groups with no room fees for buyouts.
