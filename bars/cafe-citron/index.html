A colorful and lively Latin bar in the heart of Dupont Circle, Cafe Citron transforms from a restaurant serving authentic Latin American dishes into a high-energy nightclub. It's known for its daily happy hour, salsa and samba lessons, and DJs spinning Latin music across its multiple floors.
