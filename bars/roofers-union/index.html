✨ Vibe

Recently rebooted under new ownership, Roofers Union is a multi-level neighborhood gem that blends industrial-chic decor with a high-energy atmosphere, featuring floor-to-ceiling windows and a Mediterranean-inspired rooftop deck.

🍸 Best For

Craft cocktails, sunset views, large group gatherings, weekend brunch, and live music.

🔥 Highlights

  • Exciting New Era: Freshly reopened with a revamped American menu by Chef Jose Molina and a rebranded "Cocktail Lab" focusing on sophisticated craft mixology.
  • Iconic Views: Whether you're on the Mediterranean-inspired rooftop or in the main dining room with its massive windows, you'll enjoy some of the best panoramic vistas of Adams Morgan.
  • Lively Entertainment: The venue is a hub for local energy, hosting live cover bands twice a week, trivia nights, and popular surprise comedy shows on the second floor.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Happy Hour runs from 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM, Tuesday through Saturday, offering a great selection of drinks in a buzzing communal setting.
  • Located in a historic building, all levels including the dining room and rooftop are accessible exclusively via a staircase.

Roofers Union

Roofers Union in Adams Morgan offers classic American fare with a German gastropub influence, featuring house-made sausages and seasonal dishes designed by Chef Marjorie Meek-Bradley. The venue features three distinct levels, including a cozy downstairs lounge, a second-floor dining room, and an al fresco rooftop overlooking the vibrant 18th Street Corridor.
