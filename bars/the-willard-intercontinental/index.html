The Willard InterContinental is a legendary hotel housing the iconic Round Robin Bar, a historic nexus of D.C. political life for nearly 200 years. Known for its circular bar, classic cocktails like the Mint Julep, and stately atmosphere, it offers a truly timeless Washington experience.

Happy Hour
