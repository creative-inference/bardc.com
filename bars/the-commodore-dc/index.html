The Commodore DC

The Commodore DC

Category: Neighborhood Bars, Sports Bars, Dog Friendly

Neighborhood: Dupont Circle

Price:

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A welcoming "five-star dive" in Dupont Circle that perfectly blends a cozy nautical-pub aesthetic with high-energy neighborhood charm and friendly, attentive service.

🍸 Best For

Relaxed nights out, watching the big game, dog-friendly patio dining, and immersive seasonal holiday pop-ups.

🔥 Highlights

  • Innovative Comfort Food: Under new ownership and Chef Steve Kim, the kitchen serves up a refreshed menu featuring Southern comfort with Asian influences, highlighted by signature smash burgers and draft espresso martinis.
  • Immersive Holiday Experiences: The venue is home to "Caroled," a multi-level Christmas Carol-themed pop-up featuring Victorian-style tavern decor and festive, nostalgic cocktails.
  • Vibrant Weekly Programming: There is always something happening, from lively Trivia Tuesdays and industry nights to popular monthly drag bingo sessions.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The spacious outdoor seating area is fully dog-friendly, offering a perfect spot for you and your pets to enjoy a meal together.
  • As a local favorite for sports and holiday events, the bar has a bustling atmosphere, so arriving early is recommended to grab a table during peak times.
