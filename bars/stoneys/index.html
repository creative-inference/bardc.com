Stoney's

Stoney's

Category: Sports Bars, Neighborhood Bars, Dive Bars

Neighborhood: Logan Circle

Price: $$

Address: 1433 P St NW, Washington, DC 20005

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A beloved neighborhood institution with over 50 years of history, offering a welcoming, unpretentious atmosphere where locals and visitors gather for sports, camaraderie, and legendary hospitality.

🍸 Best For

Game days, local DC history, late-night bites, lively trivia nights, and casual neighborhood hangs.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Legendary Super Grilled Cheese: A must-try signature sandwich loaded with tomato, bacon, and onion that has been a DC staple for decades.
  • Resilient Neighborhood Legacy: Recently reopened and refreshed, this storied pub has been a Logan Circle fixture since 1968, famously serving everyone from local reporters to the Secret Service.
  • Classic Pub Comforts: From juicy burgers and crispy fries to hand-spun milkshakes and a solid selection of craft beers and bourbons, the menu hits every comfort food high note.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Late-Night Hours: A go-to destination for night owls, staying open until 2:00 AM on weekdays and 3:00 AM on weekends.
  • Local Sports Hub: Known for its "local football camaraderie" and numerous TVs, making it a prime spot to catch any game of the day.

Down Goes the Streatery at Stoney’s (and so many more)

Stoney's has removed its outdoor streatery on P Street as part of a city-wide phase-out of temporary pandemic-era dining structures. The Logan Circle establishment continues to offer late-night service until 2:00 AM on weekdays and 3:00 AM on weekends.
PoPville Aug 15, 2025

Closed Since a Fire on July 6th, Stoney’s is REOPENING this morning at 11am!!

Stoney's on P Street has officially reopened following a closure caused by a fire on July 6th. The Logan Circle neighborhood favorite has resumed full operations, offering daily service starting at 11:00 AM with late-night hours until 3:00 AM on weekends.
PoPville Jul 7, 2025

Fire on Roof above Stoney’s in Logan Circle Last Night!!

Stoney's in Logan Circle was impacted by a roof fire involving an air conditioning unit in July 2025, which required an emergency response from DC Fire and EMS. The incident prompted a Health Department inspection of the food establishment to determine its operational status following the fire.
Washington City Paper Mar 8, 2021

Set in Stoney’s: Beloved Neighborhood Bar Marks 50 Years in D.C.

Stoney’s is a long-standing Washington D.C. neighborhood institution celebrated for its welcoming atmosphere and signature "Super Grilled Cheese" sandwich featuring tomato, bacon, and onion. Originally opened in 1968 and now located on P Street NW, the bar is famous for its legendary trivia nights and a rich history of serving local reporters, police, and Secret Service members.

A Stoney’s Waiter Is a Contestant on “The Bachelorette”

Stoney’s, a popular watering hole on 14th Street, gained spotlight when part-time waiter Luke Stone was cast as a contestant on "The Bachelorette." General manager Anthia Smith shared insights into Stone's tenure at the bar and the possibility of his return to the staff following his stint on reality television.
