Velvet Lounge

Category: Live Music Venue, Dive Bar, Nightclub

Price: $

Address: 915 U St NW, Washington, DC 20001

A long-standing, no-frills live music venue on the U Street corridor, the Velvet Lounge is a pillar of D.C.'s underground music scene. This bi-level dive bar features a small stage for indie bands and DJs, offering a gritty, authentic, and intimate concert experience.
