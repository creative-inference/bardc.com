DC Brau Brewing Company

DC Brau Brewing Company

Category: Breweries, Taproom, Live Music Venues

Neighborhood: Ivy City

Price: $$

Address: 3178 Bladensburg Rd NE, Washington, DC 20018

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

This historic Ivy City gem offers a welcoming, industrial-chic atmosphere where a laid-back neighborhood feel meets the energy of a world-class production brewery.

🍸 Best For

Craft beer aficionados, family outings, Thursday night trivia, live music lovers, and community-driven events.

🔥 Highlights

  • Sip on greatness at D.C.’s top-selling craft brewery, home to the city's #1 rated beer, "On The Wings Of Armageddon," and an extensive lineup of award-winning IPAs, porters, and unique hard seltzers.
  • Enjoy a vibrant entertainment calendar that features everything from weekly high-stakes trivia and live "Dead Days" music to creative "Sip & Sculpt" pottery workshops and burlesque performances.
  • Experience a spacious, no-frills taproom and outdoor area equipped with games like shuffleboard and giant Connect Four, complemented by delicious weekend food pop-ups like Taco Man Fred.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Join the crowd every Thursday at 7:00 PM for free-to-play trivia featuring $3.50 core pints and the chance to win brewery gift cards.
  • The taproom is family-friendly and offers plenty of space for large groups, with curbside pickup available for those wanting to enjoy their favorite brews at home.

Breweries to Check Out In & Around Washington, DC

DC Brau Brewing Company is a pioneering craft brewery in Northeast Washington, D.C., recognized as the city's first packaging brewery since 1956. The facility produces signature brews like The Public American Pale Ale and The Corruption IPA, and is open Friday through Sunday for visitors to enjoy pints and growler fills.
