DC Brau Brewing Company

Category: Brewery, Taproom

Price: $$

Address: 3178 Bladensburg Rd NE, Washington, DC 20018

★★★★★ 4.6 (238 reviews)

The first modern production brewery in the District of Columbia, DC Brau has been a leader in the city's craft beer scene since 2011. Their brewery and taproom in Northeast DC offer tours, tastings of their flagship and seasonal beers, and host various events.
