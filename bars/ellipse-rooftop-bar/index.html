Ellipse Rooftop Bar

Category: Rooftop Bars, Cocktail Bars, Hotel Bars

Neighborhood: Downtown

Price:

Address: 1522 K St NW, Washington, DC 20005, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An inviting and stylish downtown escape featuring chic neon lights, comfortable lounge seating, and some of the most stunning views of the capital skyline.

🍸 Best For

Golden hour cocktails, upscale appetizers, social group hangouts, and a sophisticated hotel bar experience.

🔥 Highlights

  • Phenomenal Views: Situated atop the Hyatt Place, this venue offers a breathtaking vantage point of the downtown DC architecture, perfect for sightseeing while you sip.
  • Crafted Cocktails & Bites: Enjoy high-quality, made-to-order drinks paired with standout small plates like lamb meatballs, Maryland-style crab cakes, and crispy parmesan garlic fries.
  • Curated Atmosphere: The venue is known for its upbeat music playlists and modern aesthetic that create a vibrant, high-energy rooftop energy.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • This is a seasonal rooftop destination, so be sure to check current operating hours and weather updates before planning your visit.
  • Located at 1522 K St. NW, it is a prime spot for those looking for a sophisticated drink within walking distance of the White House.

Happy Hour

Enjoy $7 Draft & Domestic Beers, $7 House Wines, and $11 Specialty Cocktails. Featured bar bites like Loaded Nachos, Crabcake Sliders, and Wings are also available.
