✨ Vibe

Bistro Du Jour is a charming, all-day Parisian escape where Art Deco elegance meets breezy waterfront views, offering an authentic "little piece of France" right on the Potomac.

🍸 Best For

Sophisticated waterfront brunches, authentic French dinners, celebratory family meals, and premium espresso breaks with artisanal pastries.

🔥 Highlights

  • Acclaimed French Fare: Recognized by Bon Appétit as one of the city's best, the menu shines with masterpieces like the tender Coq Au Vin, perfectly cooked Steak Frites, and world-class fingerling potatoes.
  • Legendary Bottomless Brunch: Experience the ultimate weekend luxury with a $32.99 bottomless brunch featuring 90 minutes of free-flowing cocktails paired with Parisian classics like tarte flambée.
  • Stunning Waterfront Ambiance: Whether dining inside among the magnificent Art Deco floors or on the scenic outdoor patio, you’ll enjoy breathtaking views of the water and boats.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Outdoor seating is a must-try for the full Wharf experience and prime people-watching along the docks.
  • The venue features an impressive selection of over 130 French wines and a full marble bar specializing in elegant Champagne cocktails.

Bistro du Jour Brings Paris to the Wharf

Bistro du Jour is a Parisian-style café and bistro located at 99 District Square SW in Washington D.C.’s Wharf neighborhood. Modeled after the chic Paris café Carette, the venue features pastries from New York’s Mah-Ze-Dahr bakery by day and transitions to a classic French dinner menu featuring steak frites and coq au vin.
