✨ Vibe

A sophisticated yet soulful neighborhood gem featuring dim, romantic indoor lighting and a lively, all-weather terrace energized by resident DJs spinning smooth 70s and 80s melodies.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, natural wine connoisseurs, leisurely weekend brunches, and refined craft cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • World-Class Wine & Spirits: Boasting an impressive curated list of over 350 natural wines and a creative mixology program recently showcased on FOX 5 DC for its seasonal innovation.
  • Elevated American Fare: A unique menu of American small plates with Southern influences, featuring standout items like their "best in memory" buttermilk biscuits, wagyu steak frites, and divine panna cotta.
  • The All-Season Terrace: A stunning outdoor lounge featuring a high-tech retractable canopy and mounted heaters, ensuring a perfect alfresco atmosphere regardless of the weather.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located within the elegant Hotel AKA Washington Circle, this is a prime spot for those who enjoy a leisurely dining pace and want to linger over their drinks.
  • Keep an eye out for their specialized events, such as the A.BBQ Series, which features guest chefs and patio grilling.
