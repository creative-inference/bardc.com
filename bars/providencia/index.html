✨ Vibe

Providencia is an intimate, globally-inspired cocktail gem where high-end mixology meets a warm, nostalgic atmosphere fueled by a lively Latin soundtrack and masterful storytelling.

🍸 Best For

Intimate date nights, adventurous cocktail enthusiasts, sophisticated late-night desserts, and small groups seeking a unique fusion experience.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Fusion Concept: Recently added to the 2025 D.C. Michelin Guide, this alleyway row house masterfully blends Salvadorian, Dominican, and Taiwanese influences through inventive dishes like roasted eggplant pupusas and the "Big Chip and Salsa."
  • Playful & Creative Mixology: The beverage program features imaginative drinks like the "Ni De Aqui Ni De Alla" (a bourbon-mezcal blend with plantain sweetness) and the Espresso Martini garnished with Kopiko candy, with some drinks even featuring "adoptable" toy kittens.
  • Stellar Pastry Pedigree: Co-owned by world-class pastry chef Paola Velez and Erik Bruner-Yang, the menu offers "big dessert energy" with showstoppers like the Baked Alaska kakigori, featuring guava and coconut lime shaved ice.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • With a limited 22-seat capacity across its bar and upstairs tables, reservations are highly recommended to secure a spot in this cozy neighborhood favorite.
  • The venue focuses on a curated selection of substantial small plates and sophisticated snacks rather than a traditional three-course meal format.

📰 In the News

MICHELIN Guide

Providencia

Providencia is a contemporary Washington D.C. restaurant led by chefs Erik Bruner-Yang and Paola Velez that blends Asian, Caribbean, and Latin American culinary traditions. The menu features creative dishes like crab-filled pan de playa and a Japanese-inspired baked Alaska made with kakigori-style shaved ice and guava puree.
Fine Dining Lovers

Chef Isabel Coss’s Guide to the Best Desserts in D.C.

Providencia features pastry chef Paola Velez’s signature Baked Alaska shaved ice, a standout dish highlighted in Chef Isabel Coss’s guide to D.C. sweets. The venue is recognized for its unique concept that allows guests to enjoy sophisticated desserts alongside high-quality cocktails.
