✨ Vibe

The Mayflower Club is a sophisticated and fashionable multi-level lounge that seamlessly blends a chic, decorative aesthetic with the high-energy spirit of a world-class Latin nightclub.

🍸 Best For

VIP birthday celebrations, romantic date nights, Latin-inspired dinner and drinks, late-night dancing, group outings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Exceptional Latin-American Cuisine: Indulge in a menu of standout dishes like the crispy beef empanadas and the refreshing Ceviche Tropical, crafted with fresh fish, mango, and habanero.
  • Two Experiences Under One Roof: Start your evening with refined cocktails at The Mayflower before heading upstairs to Zebbie's Garden, a vibrant garden-themed dance space.
  • First-Class Hospitality: From the welcoming door staff to the attentive servers, guests are treated to a personalized and engaging nightlife experience that makes everyone feel like a VIP.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Dinner service is offered Friday and Saturday from 6:00 PM to 11:45 PM; booking a reservation in advance is recommended for a seamless evening.
  • Valet or street parking is available, but arriving via rideshare is encouraged to enjoy the extensive cocktail menu.
