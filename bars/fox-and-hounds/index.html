Fox and Hounds

Category: Dive Bar, Neighborhood Bar

Price: $

Address: 1537 17th St NW, Washington, DC 20036

A classic, no-frills neighborhood dive bar near Dupont Circle, Fox and Hounds offers an unpretentious atmosphere and affordable drinks. It's a long-standing local favorite for a casual beer, with a worn-in, comfortable feel that has been serving the community for decades.
