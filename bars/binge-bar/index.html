Binge Bar

Recently recognized as one of the 13 best non-alcoholic bars in the United States, Binge Bar is Washington D.C.’s premier alcohol-free venue and Filipino-inspired bistro, offering a sophisticated and inclusive social experience on the H Street Corridor.

✨ Vibe

Intimate, cozy, and effortlessly chill, this warm space features soft lighting, plush seating, and an intentional atmosphere that celebrates connection without the hangover.

🍸 Best For

Sober-curious dates, wellness-focused social outings, intimate birthday celebrations, and vegan-friendly dining.

🔥 Highlights

  • Artisanal Zero-Proof Cocktails: From the soothing CBD-infused sangria and Blackberry Moscow Mules to a masterfully crafted "New Fashioned," the menu features creative, high-quality ingredients that rival any traditional cocktail bar.
  • Unique Fusion Bites: The kitchen serves a delicious blend of Filipino and Creole-inspired dishes, with guest favorites including traditional lumpia, savory wings with homemade sauce, and seitan adobo.
  • Exceptional Hospitality: Led by founder Vergie "Gigi" Arandid, the venue is known for its warm, personalized service and community-driven events ranging from floral workshops to wellness activations.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of the popular Happy Hour menu for great deals on signature non-alcoholic drinks and tasty bar snacks.
  • The bar is conveniently located in the heart of H Street NE, approximately a 10-minute walk from Union Station.
