Whitlow's

Category: Bar, Restaurant, Rooftop Bar

Price: $$

Address: 901 U St NW, Washington, DC 20001

After a long run in Arlington, Whitlow's has been reborn on the corner of 9th and U Street. The new location continues the tradition of a lively neighborhood bar, featuring a spacious rooftop deck, island-inspired cocktails, and a menu of casual American fare.
