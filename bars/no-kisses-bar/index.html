No Kisses Bar

No Kisses Bar

Category: Cocktail Bars, Neighborhood Bars, Patios

Neighborhood: Columbia Heights

Price:

Address: 3120 Georgia Ave NW, Washington, DC 20010, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

This psychedelic neighborhood lounge pairs moody forest-themed decor with color-changing LED ceiling panels and plush forest-green velvet banquettes to create a groovy, intimate atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Natural wine enthusiasts, low-key date nights, vinyl lovers, and sunny afternoon patio sessions with friends.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Pizza Garden: A sprawling, shared backyard patio featuring a vintage Citroen van converted into a bar, where guests can enjoy world-class pan pizzas delivered directly from sister establishment Sonny’s Pizza.
  • Exceptional Bottle Shop: Beyond the bar, discover a curated selection of funky natural wines and a rare collection of amari and vermouths available for tasting and purchase.
  • Vinyl & Atmosphere: Recently named one of the best wine bars in D.C., the venue hosts regular vinyl-spinning nights that perfectly complement its "secret forest" aesthetic.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • No Kisses operates with a standard 20% living wage charge, ensuring a seamless experience where additional tipping is not expected.
  • Happy hour features great deals on cocktails and beer, making it a popular neighborhood go-to for an early evening drink.
