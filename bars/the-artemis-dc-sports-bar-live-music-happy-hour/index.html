✨ Vibe

The Artemis is a high-energy, two-story neighborhood hub in Columbia Heights that perfectly blends the spirit of a premier soccer pub with the excitement of a lively entertainment lounge.

🍸 Best For

Catching NFL and Premier League games, Saturday night karaoke, live R&B performances, and unbeatable weekday happy hour deals.

🔥 Highlights

  • Elite Sports Tech: Boasting 20 high-definition screens and a state-of-the-art matrix system, the venue ensures every guest has a crystal-clear line of sight for everything from Champions League soccer to horse racing.
  • Weekly Entertainment: The calendar is packed with local culture, featuring Live R&B Band Wednesdays, Open Mic Comedy, and high-spirited Karaoke Saturdays.
  • Cajun-Inspired Comfort: Beyond standard bar fare, the kitchen serves up flavorful specialties including gumbo, étouffée, and their fan-favorite signature catfish sandwich.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Don't miss the "Power Happy Hour" on weekdays, which kicks off with incredible $1 beers during the first hour of operation, followed by $5 rail drinks and craft specials until 7 PM.
  • The venue opens early for major international soccer matches; reservations are highly recommended to secure a table for morning Premier League and Champions League action.
