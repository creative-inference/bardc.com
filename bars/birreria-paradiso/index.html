Birreria Paradiso

Birreria Paradiso

Category: Pizza Bars, Game Bars, Taproom

Neighborhood: Georgetown

Price: $$

Address: 3251 P St NW, Washington, DC 20007

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A lively Georgetown staple that blends a classic, warm Italian pizzeria atmosphere with a high-energy subterranean game bar perfect for retro fun.

🍸 Best For

Craft beer aficionados, retro gaming enthusiasts, casual group hangouts, and diners seeking top-tier gluten-free pizza options.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Paradiso Game Room: This expansive basement space features arcade classics like Skee-Ball, pinball, and Big Buck Hunter, all set against a custom mural for the ultimate casual nightlife experience.
  • Exceptional Wood-Fired Pizza: Renowned for authentic Neapolitan-style dough and fresh ingredients, the menu offers everything from the meat-heavy "macellaio" to some of the city's most celebrated gluten-free crusts.
  • Elite Beer Curation: Boasting a massive selection of over 50 canned craft beers and rotating taps, the venue provides expert-level pairings and tasting flights for every palate.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Stop by for the $12 "Express Lunch Pair" available Monday through Friday from 12–4 PM for a quick and delicious mid-day meal.
  • Outdoor patio seating is available for those who want to soak in the iconic Georgetown street vibe while they dine.

🔄 Weekly Events

Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri

Quick lunch combo: Choice of a 7" mini pizza or half sandwich paired with a 1/2 Insalata Mista. Add a soda for $2 or a draft beer/prosecco for $7. Available at Dupont Circle, Georgetown, and Spring Valley.

🎮 Georgetown Game Room Hours

Fri, Sat

The downstairs Paradiso Game Room at Georgetown is open for shuffleboard and arcade games.

🕹️ Georgetown Paradiso Game Room

Fri, Sat

Enjoy beer or wine while playing shuffleboard or arcade games in the downstairs game room at the Georgetown location.

🍕 Wednesday Meal Deal

Wed

Midweek pizza specials: 2 large pizzas + 2 apps for $50 or 1 large + 1 small pizza + 1 app for $40 (up to 20% off).

🧒 Kids Eat Free Mondays

Mon

Up to two kids under 12 eat free (choice of kids' pizza, pasta, or grilled cheese) with the purchase of an adult entree. Available at Hyattsville and Spring Valley locations.

🍕 Wednesday Midweek Meal Deal

Wed

Special midweek pricing: 2 large pizzas + 2 apps for $60, or 1 large + 1 small pizza + 1 app for $45.

🍳 Wood-Fired Brunch

Sat, Sun

Weekend brunch featuring breakfast pizzas with sunnyside-up eggs, oven-baked omelets, calzones, and classic brunch cocktails like Mimosas and Bloody Marys.

$3 off drafts, wine by the glass, and select cocktails; discounts on wings, meatballs, and garlic knots.
