The Fountain Inn

The Fountain Inn

Category: Cocktail Bars, Supper Clubs, Romantic

Neighborhood: Georgetown

Price: $$$

Address: 1000 C St SW, Washington, DC 20024

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

This elegant candlelit haven masterfully blends 18th-century tavern history with modern sophistication, offering an intimate and welcoming atmosphere perfect for refined storytelling.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, rare spirit tastings, elevated seasonal dining, historical cocktail tours, and sophisticated low-key evenings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-winning culinary excellence led by 2025's "Best Chef" winner Greg Heitzig, featuring a celebrated seasonal menu and an exceptional umami-driven tasting experience.
  • A premier spirits library housing over 700 rare bottles, recently earning a prestigious one-pin distinction from The Pinnacle Guide for global excellence in hospitality and drinks programming.
  • Unique "Historical Cocktail Tours" and expert-led spirits flights—including both rare bourbon line-ups and curated zero-proof experiences—that celebrate the venue’s rich 1783 heritage.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Advanced reservations are highly recommended for their signature spirits flights and tasting experiences, as the venue offers a cozy and intimate setting.
  • The kitchen concludes service between 9:00 PM and 10:00 PM nightly, though the cocktail bar remains open later on Thursday through Saturday.

🔄 Weekly Events

🥃 Blanton's Bourbon Flight Tasting Experience

Daily

A guided tasting of the Blanton's lineup, including the standard single barrel release plus two rare expressions: Blanton's Gold and Blanton's Straight From The Barrel. Includes three 1oz pours.

📜 Historical Cocktail Tour Experience

Daily

An exploration of three cocktails that shaped history: Fish House Punch, Syllabub, and a rotating Seasonal Historical Cocktail.

🚫 Zero-Proof Cocktail Tour Experience

Daily

A journey through American history told via three curated zero-proof cocktails designed by historical spirits advisors.

🥃 Blantons Bourbon Flight Tasting Experience

Explore the Blantons lineup by tasting the standard bottling alongside 2 rare expressions. Includes a one-ounce pour of Blantons Single Barrel, Blantons Gold, and Blantons Straight From The Barrel. $120 per person.
