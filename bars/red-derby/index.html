Red Derby

Red Derby

Category: Dive Bars, Neighborhood Bars, Rooftop Bars

Neighborhood: Petworth

Price: $

Address: 3718 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20010

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A quintessential neighborhood staple that blends nostalgic dive bar charm with a warm, communal energy and a breezy, multi-level rooftop escape.

🍸 Best For

Affordable drinks, rooftop lounging, board game nights, late-night hangs, weekend brunch, local atmosphere

🔥 Highlights

  • Celebrate a D.C. institution that recently marked its 18th anniversary, continuing its legacy as a beloved 14th Street haunt for craft cans and comfort food.
  • Enjoy fantastic daily themed specials, including "Tendie Tuesdays" with half-priced chicken tenders and "Throwback Thursdays" featuring discounted grilled cheese sandwiches.
  • Experience a unique multi-level layout offering everything from a cozy interior filled with board games and books to a lively rooftop perfect for fresh air and city vibes.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of the legendary 24/7 Happy Hour featuring $3.50 "Lucky Pick" beers and rail shots every day.
  • Visit on the weekend for a popular rooftop brunch featuring $3 mimosas, bloody marys, and signature French toast sticks.

Red Derby | Bars in Columbia Heights, Washington DC

Red Derby is a beloved Columbia Heights watering hole known for its relaxed communal vibe, affordable drink deals like the $25 Lucky Bucket, and half-price chicken tenders on Tuesdays. This multi-level bar offers frequent entertainment through weekly trivia nights, board games, and movies projected onto the walls.
