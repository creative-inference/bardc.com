Trusty's

Trusty's

Category: Dive Bars, Neighborhood Bars, Rooftop Bars

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price: $

Address: 1420 Pennsylvania Ave SE, Washington, DC 20003

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Trusty’s is a legendary, unpretentious neighborhood gem that perfectly blends a cozy, "everyone knows your name" dive atmosphere with a quirky, elevated rooftop energy.

🍸 Best For

Casual group hangouts, budget-friendly craft beers, game day viewing, and unique rooftop photo-ops.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Iconic Bus Bar: The second floor features a unique, roofed outdoor space where a repurposed school bus serves as a one-of-a-kind bar specializing in a wide variety of canned craft beers.
  • Award-Worthy Bar Grub: Known for serving up some of the city's favorite comfort food, including their famous buffalo chicken cheese sub and authentic DC half smokes.
  • Weekly Entertainment: Join the locals for high-energy Trivia Tuesdays at 8:00 PM or head to the Bus Bar on Wednesdays for live music and costume karaoke.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Furry friends are always welcome at this dog-friendly spot, and there is never a need to dress up—the atmosphere is strictly casual and welcoming.
  • Visit on the weekends for a laid-back brunch featuring spicy Bloody Marys and mimosas, served every Saturday and Sunday from 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM.
