View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Prima DC is a stylish and intimate two-story gem on U Street that masterfully blends cozy neighborhood warmth with a high-energy, Dominican-inspired rhythmic soul.

🍸 Best For

Craft cocktails, late-night dancing, interactive game nights, private celebrations, and vibrant rooftop socializing.

🔥 Highlights

  • Signature Sips & Bites: Indulge in expert-level mixology with the fan-favorite "Smokey Sunset" cocktail, perfectly paired with unique culinary twists like their acclaimed Salt & Vinegar wings.
  • Authentic Rhythms: Experience a high-energy dance floor featuring Saturday night Bachata sessions that bring a vibrant, international energy to the heart of the District.
  • Interactive Entertainment: The venue offers a playful social scene with weekly karaoke nights and a curated selection of tabletop games including Uno, Jenga, and Mancala.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue features two distinct levels, including an intimate upstairs lounge and a rooftop deck perfect for open-air cocktails.
  • The high-energy dancing and late-night atmosphere hit their peak after 11:00 PM, making this a go-to destination for the U Street night owl.
