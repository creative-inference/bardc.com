Midlands Beer Garden

Category: Beer Garden, Sports Bar

Price: $$

Address: 3333 Georgia Ave NW, Washington, DC 20010

★★★★★ 4.4 (1008 reviews)

A large, dog-friendly beer garden in Park View, The Midlands offers a spacious outdoor patio and a cozy indoor bar. With 26 draft lines, a wide selection of cans, and a menu of bar snacks, it's a popular neighborhood hangout for casual drinks and watching sports.
