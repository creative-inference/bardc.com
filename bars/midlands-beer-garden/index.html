Midlands Beer Garden

Midlands Beer Garden

Category: Beer Gardens, Sports Bars, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: Columbia Heights

Price: $$

Address: 3333 Georgia Ave NW, Washington, DC 20010

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A massive, 6,000-square-foot indoor-outdoor community hub that seamlessly blends the high-energy atmosphere of a premier sports bar with the relaxed, sun-drenched charm of a classic beer garden.

🍸 Best For

Game days, large group gatherings, weekend brunch, happy hour, dog lovers, family outings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Elite Sports Coverage: Featuring 18 TVs (including 7 outdoors), NFL Sunday Ticket, and status as an official home for Michigan Wolverines and Detroit Lions fans, it is the ultimate destination for everything from the Premier League to NCAA matchups.
  • Award-Winning Draft List: A 2023 Rammy Finalist for Best Beer Program, the venue boasts 26 rotating taps focusing on local and European craft brews, alongside creative frozen cocktails and $20 ice-cold buckets.
  • Elevated Gastropub Menu: The kitchen serves high-quality comfort food that goes far beyond standard bar fare, including "phenomenal" Ribeye Gyros, giant pretzels with bratwurst, and a dedicated weekend brunch.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of Happy Hour seven days a week, including a special four-hour session every Friday and rare weekend discounts from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM.
  • The spacious outdoor garden is famously dog-friendly, providing a welcoming environment for your furry friends to join the party.
