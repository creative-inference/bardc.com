H Street Pub

Category: Neighborhood Bars, Dive Bars, Taproom

Neighborhood: H Street NE

Price:

Address: 600 H St NE, Washington, DC 20002, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

This freshly reimagined multi-level destination blends a sleek mid-century modern aesthetic with the welcoming, laid-back energy of a classic neighborhood taproom.

🍸 Best For

Tank-to-tap craft beer, weekend brunch, industrial-chic dates, and casual group hangouts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Ultra-Fresh Brewing: As a premier tank-to-tap brewery, the bar features a ten-barrel brewing system where most selections are poured directly from onsite tanks to your glass.
  • Standout Hospitality: The venue is known for its friendly, top-tier service, with local regulars frequently praising the expertise and welcoming nature of the bartending team.
  • Multi-Level Experience: Beyond the main taproom, guests can enjoy a dedicated wine bar and look forward to the breezy upcoming roof deck overlooking H Street NE.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The full kitchen is a major draw, serving up high-quality menus for both dinner and weekend brunch.
  • Located in the expansive former H Street Country Club space, it offers plenty of room for both intimate drinks and larger social gatherings.
