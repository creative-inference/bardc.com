Royal Sands Social Club

Royal Sands Social Club

Category: Tiki Bars, Happy Hour Spots, Event Spaces

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price: $$

Address: 26 N St SE, Washington, DC 20003

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A massive, 14,000-square-foot tropical paradise in Navy Yard that transports you straight to a sun-drenched Florida beach resort with its vibrant, high-energy atmosphere and "pool-side" decor.

🍸 Best For

Large group events, high-energy happy hours, Nationals Park game days, "Pitch-A-Friend" dating nights, and buzzing watch parties.

🔥 Highlights

  • Stunning experiential design featuring a sunken tiled bar made to look like a swimming pool and a signature 30-foot-tall lifeguard station DJ booth.
  • An expansive two-story layout including a rooftop Cabana Bar that offers fantastic views and a high-energy club transition on weekend nights.
  • A unique coastal menu featuring ahi wonton nachos, sushi, and gator bao buns paired with expertly crafted frozen cocktails and tropical libations.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy a standout weekday Happy Hour from 4 PM to 7 PM with great specials on wings, nachos, and signature drinks.
  • Known for exceptional hospitality, this venue is a premier spot for private events, offering dedicated coordinators and custom themed cocktail menus for large groups.
