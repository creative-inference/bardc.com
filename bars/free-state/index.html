Free State

Free State

Category: Beer Bar, Regional

Price: $$

Address: 501B G St NW, Washington, DC 20001

Tucked away in a Chinatown alley, Free State is a cozy bar that celebrates the Mid-Atlantic region. It exclusively serves beers, ciders, wines, and spirits from Maryland, Delaware, Virginia, and Pennsylvania, paired with a menu of regional snacks.
