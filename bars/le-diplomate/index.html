Category: French, Brasserie, Restaurant

Price: $$$

Address: 1601 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20005

★★★★★ 4.6 (7386 reviews)

A bustling and beautiful Parisian-style brasserie on 14th Street, Le Diplomate is one of D.C.'s most popular and acclaimed restaurants. It offers a stunningly authentic setting for enjoying French classics like steak frites, onion soup, and fresh-baked bread, attracting a constant crowd for brunch, lunch, and dinner.
