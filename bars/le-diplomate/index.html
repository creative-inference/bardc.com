Le Diplomate

Le Diplomate

Category: French, Brunch Spots, Romantic

Neighborhood: Logan Circle

Price: $$$

Address: 1601 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20005

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A transportive Parisian brasserie that blends the shimmering romance of a "Midnight in Paris" dream with the vibrant, high-energy pulse of D.C.’s Logan Circle.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, award-winning weekend brunch, late-night Bordeaux, and sophisticated neighborhood networking.

🔥 Highlights

  • Recipient of the 2025 RAMMY award for Best Brunch in Washington, D.C., celebrated for its exquisite menu and iconic impact on the city's dining scene.
  • Masterfully executed French classics including signature steak frites, a decadent duck ragu, and a world-class French onion soup that rivals the best in New York City.
  • Exceptional service and a sophisticated 14th Street atmosphere that makes it a premier destination for both special occasions and high-end social encounters.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended and can be booked via Resy to secure a spot in the elegant dining room or on the vibrant covered patio.
  • The bar is an excellent choice for a solo "main character" moment or a spontaneous glass of champagne in a bustling, chic environment.
