✨ Vibe

V14 Street Lounge is a stunningly elegant U Street destination that masterfully balances a warm, welcoming atmosphere with an energetic, high-octane nightlife spirit.

🍸 Best For

Lively weekend brunches, premium hookah sessions, celebratory group dinners, late-night bites, and high-energy DJ sets.

🔥 Highlights

  • Gourmet Lounge Fare: Unlike typical nightlife venues, V14 offers a top-tier menu featuring "can't-miss" juicy burgers and beautifully presented shrimp pasta that rivals fine-dining standards.
  • Unmatched Hospitality: The owner-led team and attentive staff are famous for creating a "like family" environment, providing personalized service that makes every guest feel like a VIP.
  • Premium Hookah & Brunch: Enjoy a sophisticated hookah experience with smooth pulls and expert flavor recommendations alongside a generous, well-paced bottomless mimosa deal during brunch.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Be sure to check out their weekend brunch for a perfect mix of great food, live DJs, and a high-energy "turn-up" vibe.
  • The staff is highly knowledgeable about their extensive hookah menu—don't hesitate to ask for a custom blend crafted specifically to your taste buds.
