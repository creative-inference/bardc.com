✨ Vibe

A beloved 40-year neighborhood landmark and LGBTQ institution, this Dupont Circle staple blends rustic Italian warmth with a high-energy community spirit along the bustling 17th Street corridor.

🍸 Best For

Happy Hour, Authentic Italian Dining, LGBTQ+ Community Hub, Karaoke & Drag Performances, People-Watching.

🔥 Highlights

  • Culinary Soul: Recently ranked as one of the top Italian restaurants in D.C. by Tripadvisor, the kitchen serves up "sacredly honest" classics like velvet-textured Cacio e Pepe, wood-fired pizzas, and fresh seafood.
  • The Iconic DIK Bar: The legendary upstairs space is a sanctuary for nightlife lovers, featuring high-energy karaoke nights, drag events, and prime views of local celebrations.
  • 17th Street Patio: The popular outdoor seating area offers the perfect vantage point for neighborhood energy, complemented by a sophisticated wine list and refreshing cocktails.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of the daily Happy Hour featuring $8 margaritas and excellent wine selections in a casual, welcoming atmosphere.
  • The venue is currently expanding its storied legacy with the opening of "Dupont Cantina" next door, adding a fresh Mexican menu to its classic Italian offerings.
