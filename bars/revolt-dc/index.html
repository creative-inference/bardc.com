Category: Nightclub, LGBTQ+ Bars, Karaoke Bars

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Revolt DC is an energetic, multi-level dance destination in the heart of the 14th and U corridor, boasting cutting-edge lighting, high-quality sound, and a vibrant, inclusive atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Late-night dancing, LGBTQ+ outings, high-energy karaoke, and celebrity-hosted pop-up events.

🔥 Highlights

  • Cultural Hotspot: Recently served as the primary venue for Wale’s “Gifted Week” and his eighth album launch, establishing itself as a premier destination for major D.C. creative activations.
  • Immersive Tech: Features a standout DJ booth and a professional-grade light and sound system designed to keep the dance floor moving all night.
  • Diverse Entertainment: Offers a wide range of themed nights and interactive karaoke sessions that cater to a diverse and welcoming crowd.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of their highly-rated happy hour for great deals on drinks before the late-night rush.
  • The venue is a multi-level space, offering plenty of room to explore different vibes and dance floors throughout the night.
