Red Bear Brewing Co.

Category: Brewery, LGBTQ+ Bar, Game Bar

Price: $$

Address: 209 M St NE, Washington, DC 20002

Located in NoMa, Red Bear Brewing Co. is D.C.'s first 100% gay-owned brewery. The massive, industrial-style taproom features a wide variety of house-brewed beers, a dedicated area for board games, and hosts numerous community events, creating a welcoming and inclusive space for all.
