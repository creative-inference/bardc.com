Red Bear Brewing Co.

Red Bear Brewing Co.

Category: Breweries, LGBTQ+ Bars, Game Bars

Neighborhood: NoMa

Price: $$

Address: 209 M St NE, Washington, DC 20002

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A spacious and inclusive 7,000-square-foot, adventure-themed taproom that serves as a vibrant community hub for the NoMa neighborhood.

🍸 Best For

Craft beer enthusiasts, LGBTQ+ nightlife, competitive trivia fans, board game nights, and group hangouts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Extensive House Taps: Enjoy 24 house-brewed lines featuring highly-regarded West Coast IPAs, seasonal releases, and a fantastic house-made root beer.
  • Dynamic Community Events: Experience a high-energy lineup of weekly entertainment, including their legendary Wednesday night trivia, drag bingo, and a dedicated library of board games.
  • Flavorful Pub Kitchen: Dig into a menu of elevated favorites like their top-rated smash burgers, loaded tots, and spicy chicken chipotle sandwiches, all easily ordered via a convenient QR-code system.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • This proud gay- and veteran-owned establishment focuses on inclusivity, offering a welcoming "safe space" environment for all guests.
  • Reservations are recommended for their popular event nights, though the large interior offers plenty of seating for walk-in groups and casual gatherings.
