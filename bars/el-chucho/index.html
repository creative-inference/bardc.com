El Chucho

El Chucho

Category: Taco Bars, Mexican, Rooftop Bars

Neighborhood: Columbia Heights

Price: $$

Address: 3313 11th St NW, Washington, DC 20010

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A vibrant and eclectic neighborhood gem that blends a circus-meets-Mexican-punk aesthetic with a cozy, welcoming atmosphere and a year-round heated rooftop deck.

🍸 Best For

Late-night bites, rooftop cocktails, affordable Happy Hour, casual group dinners, and Mezcal enthusiasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Acclaimed Street Food: Recently featured in the Resy Guide to Mexican Restaurants in D.C., the menu shines with signature items like charred elote with brown-butter aioli and the fan-favorite crispy duck tacos.
  • Inventive Drink Program: Enjoy a robust selection of on-tap margaritas, creative frozen cocktails like the strawberry habanero, and an extensive list of premium tequilas and mezcals.
  • Versatile Dining Spaces: Choose between the lively, high-energy bar downstairs or the more peaceful, enclosed heated rooftop patio for a relaxing night out.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue offers a popular daily Happy Hour featuring discounted taco deals and well-crafted drinks, perfect for a budget-friendly evening.
  • Weekend brunch is a neighborhood staple, served every Saturday and Sunday starting at 11:00 AM.

🔄 Weekly Events

Brunch del Chucho 🍳

Sat, Sun

Traditional brunch served every weekend. Menu includes Mexican breakfast specialties.

Happy Hour

All Night Happy Hour on Mondays with deals on Margaritas and Tacos. Tuesday through Friday Happy Hour runs 4pm-6pm, featuring tiered taco pricing ($1 to $3.50) on select varieties with a beverage purchase.
