Lyman's Tavern

Lyman's Tavern

Category: Dive Bars, Neighborhood Bars, Game Bars

Neighborhood: Petworth

Price: $

Address: 3720 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20010

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A warm, unpretentious neighborhood staple that blends the nostalgic charm of a classic dive bar with a vibrant, inclusive energy and a passion for retro arcade gaming.

🍸 Best For

Pinball enthusiasts, vegan-friendly bar bites, birthday celebrations, budget-friendly beers, and patio relaxing.

🔥 Highlights

  • A Pinball Paradise: Boasting an extensive, rotating collection of retro pinball machines and a dedicated local league, Lyman's is a premier destination for arcade lovers and nostalgic gamers.
  • Inclusive Bar Fare: Celebrated for having some of the city’s best vegan and vegetarian comfort food, including standout veggie chili cheese dogs and $5 hot dog specials on Wednesdays.
  • Legendary Birthday Perks: One of DC’s most generous neighborhood spots, offering a special "drink for free" policy all night on well drinks and select beers for patrons celebrating their big day.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue is family-friendly until 9:00 PM and features a prime corner patio that serves as a perfect Petworth-adjacent hangout until midnight.
  • Service is casual and friendly; head straight to the bar to order from an impressive list of "working class" cans, local craft drafts, and fresh-crushed fruit cocktails.
