✨ Vibe

Marshall's Bar & Grille is a quintessential Foggy Bottom neighborhood dive bar that offers a cozy, "second home" atmosphere where students and locals gather for a low-key, friendly night out.

🍸 Best For

Late-night pool games, affordable drinks, casual GW student hangouts, neighborhood socializing, curated jukebox vibes.

🔥 Highlights

  • Classic Entertainment: Enjoy a quintessential bar experience with well-maintained pool tables and a TouchTunes jukebox to set the soundtrack for your evening.
  • Exceptional Value: Known as a "perfect dive," this spot offers great food and budget-friendly prices that make it a staple for the local community.
  • Unique Partnerships: Located upstairs and connected to "Taste of India," patrons can enjoy a rare blend of traditional bar food and flavorful Indian cuisine.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Look for the entrance shared with Taste of India; the venue is situated on the second floor above the restaurant.
  • The staff is known for being incredibly helpful to travelers, even offering phone charging for those in a pinch.
