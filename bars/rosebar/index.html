✨ Vibe

Rosebar is a sophisticated and high-energy sanctuary in Dupont Circle that seamlessly blends an intimate, upscale lounge atmosphere with a pulse-pounding nightclub experience.

🍸 Best For

VIP bottle service, celebrity sightings, weekend late-night dancing, and heated outdoor social sessions.

🔥 Highlights

  • World-Class Sound: The venue features a cutting-edge DAS Audio E11EVEN Sound series system, delivering a premium, immersive acoustic experience throughout the dance floor and patio.
  • Expansive Heated Deck: Boasting a 2,000-square-foot heated outdoor patio, guests can enjoy a vibrant, open-air party atmosphere regardless of the season.
  • Elite Social Hub: Renowned as a premier destination for high-profile athletes and celebrities, Rosebar is a staple of D.C.’s vibrant entertainment and philanthropic scene.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • VIP table reservations are highly recommended for weekend nights and special events to secure the best views and premium bottle service packages.
  • Located at 1215 Connecticut Ave NW, the venue offers a range of exclusive packages featuring premium selections like Moët Nectar Rosé and Clase Azul.
