✨ Vibe

Recently named one of the best cocktail bars in D.C., this lively pan-Latin destination offers a welcoming, high-energy atmosphere where expert bartenders serve up creative spirits in the heart of the Bryant Street Market.

🍸 Best For

Artisanal agave cocktails, a vibrant happy hour, solo drinks at the bar, and exploring unique Latin American spirits like Mexican rums and piscos.

🔥 Highlights

  • Innovative Cocktails: Sip on fan-favorites like the Dona Margarita with passion fruit espuma or their famous Espresso Martini topped with hand-grated Salvadoran chocolate.
  • Expertly Paired Bites: Operated by the renowned team behind Taqueria Habanero, the bar serves delicious street snacks including shrimp fritters, fresh ceviche, and fried yucca.
  • Local & Organic Selections: The beverage program features a thoughtful mix of organic wines and local favorites, including Streetcar Brewing beers on tap.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located within a food hall setting, it is an ideal spot for a quick drink or a full meal paired with various local food vendors.
  • Take the experience home with their convenient canned cocktail system designed for to-go orders.
