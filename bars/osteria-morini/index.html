✨ Vibe

Osteria Morini is a sophisticated yet high-energy waterfront destination that masterfully blends authentic Northern Italian charm with stunning, sun-drenched views of the Anacostia River.

🍸 Best For

Waterfront date nights, lively after-work happy hours, dog-friendly weekend brunches, and celebratory group dinners with handmade pasta.

🔥 Highlights

  • Exceptional Northern Italian Fare: Renowned for perfectly executed classics like buttery Ricotta Gnudi and Braised Veal Shank Ravioli, the menu has recently expanded to include a creative small bites selection featuring Calabrian chile cauliflower.
  • DC’s Premier Brunch: The weekend menu is a local legend, specifically for its seven diverse pancake varieties ranging from indulgent Tiramisu to savory Smoked Salmon with trout roe.
  • Innovative Riverfront Cocktails: Enjoy the "Aperol Crush"—a seasonal strawberry-infused twist on the classic spritz—while taking in the views from one of the city's most vibrant outdoor patios.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Visit on "Morini Mondays" for special dining deals or stop by for Happy Hour between 5:00 PM and 6:00 PM to enjoy the lively atmosphere as the workday winds down.
  • The spacious outdoor patio is exceptionally pet-friendly, providing water bowls and a welcoming environment for diners visiting with their dogs.
