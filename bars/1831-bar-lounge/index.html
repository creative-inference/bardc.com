✨ Vibe

1831 Bar & Lounge is a high-energy Dupont Circle destination known for its vibrant weeknight parties, spectacular DJs, and a welcoming, attentive staff.

🍸 Best For

Midweek dancing, hookah enthusiasts, strong cocktails, and interactive music experiences.

🔥 Highlights

  • Capitol Thursdays: A signature weekly event featuring high-energy music, great vibes, and a lively crowd perfect for kickstarting the weekend early.
  • Interactive Music: A unique setup that allows guests to request their favorite songs, ensuring the soundtrack always matches the energy of the room.
  • Expert Service: Fast and friendly bartenders known for crafting potent, high-quality drinks that offer great value for a night out on the strip.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Make sure to visit on Wednesdays for exclusive sets from spectacular local DJs that draw a dedicated following.
  • Look out for "Capitol Thursday" drink specials and a variety of hookah options to enhance your lounge experience.
