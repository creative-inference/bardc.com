Green Island Cafe

Category: Dive Bar, Hookah Bar

Price: $

Address: 2327 18th St NW, Washington, DC 20009

An unassuming dive and hookah bar in the heart of Adams Morgan, Green Island Cafe offers a very casual, late-night spot for drinks. It's one of the many eclectic watering holes that define the neighborhood's nightlife.
