Grand Duchess

Grand Duchess

Category: Cocktail Bars, Dive Bars, Late Night

Neighborhood: Adams Morgan

Price:

Address: 2337 18th St NW, Washington, DC 20009, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A welcoming neighborhood haunt that seamlessly blends a 1950s diner aesthetic with a rock 'n' roll spirit, featuring a vinyl-focused soundtrack and a cozy, low-key atmosphere perfect for conversation.

🍸 Best For

Craft cocktail enthusiasts, casual dates, vinyl lovers, and after-work happy hours.

🔥 Highlights

  • Artisanal Beverage Program: Enjoy a creative drink menu where cocktails are often named after classic albums, alongside expertly executed favorites like the French 75 and a deep selection of amaro and rye.
  • Elevated Kitchen: This former cocktail bar has expanded into a full-fledged restaurant featuring a diverse menu that includes everything from Philly cheesesteak empanadas and house-made pastas to their highly-praised Brussels sprouts.
  • Local Art Hub: Beyond the drinks, the venue fosters a connection with the D.C. creative scene by hosting rotating pop-up art exhibits like "144," showcasing unique works from local artists.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Visit during their daily happy hour from 5 to 7 PM for $8 Old Fashioneds, $8 wines by the glass, and $5 bottled or canned beers.
  • The bar features a classic jukebox and a deep appreciation for rock 'n' roll vinyl, making it a top spot for music fans in Adams Morgan.
