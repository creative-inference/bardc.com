Dew Drop Inn

Dew Drop Inn

Category: Neighborhood Bars, LGBTQ+ Bars, Dive Bars

Neighborhood: Brookland

Price: $$

Address: 2801 8th St NE, Washington, DC 20017

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A funky and unpretentious neighborhood gem housed in a historic railroad building, offering an award-winning, inclusive atmosphere where high-energy social gatherings meet classic dive bar charm.

🍸 Best For

Craft beer enthusiasts, LGBTQ+ community events, high-energy social gatherings, casual neighborhood hangouts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Atmosphere: Recently honored with the prestigious 2025 RAMMY for "Best Gathering Place," recognizing its role as a premier social hub in the D.C. community.
  • Eclectic Event Lineup: Host to legendary monthly events including the high-energy "Dyke Party" featuring local vendors and DJs, as well as mystical "Witches Night" astrology readings.
  • Unique Rail-Side Setting: Boasts a spacious outdoor deck and patio situated right along the railroad tracks, providing a one-of-a-kind backdrop for enjoying drinks and well-presented pub fare.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Don't miss out on their signature "Crush" drinks and surprisingly high-quality pub food, which locals praise for its great presentation.
  • As a proud LGBTQ+ friendly space, the venue is known for its welcoming, rowdy, and inclusive spirit—perfect for a lively night out.
