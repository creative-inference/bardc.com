Dew Drop Inn

Dew Drop Inn

Category: Bar, Pub, American Restaurant

Price: $$

Address: 2801 8th St NE, Washington, DC 20017

A neighborhood bar in Brookland, the Dew Drop Inn is known for its spacious interior, extensive beer list, and a menu of hearty pub food, including sausages and sandwiches. It features a large patio and is a popular spot for locals to relax and watch a game.
