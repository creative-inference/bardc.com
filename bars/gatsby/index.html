✨ Vibe

Gatsby is a glamorous, two-story Art Deco-inspired diner that masterfully blends 1920s Jazz Age elegance with a high-energy, modern atmosphere featuring live DJ sets and stunning views of Nationals Park.

🍸 Best For

Bottomless brunch, pre-game cocktails, group celebrations, decadent desserts, and lively weekend vibes.

🔥 Highlights

  • Expansive Bottomless Brunch: Beyond classic mimosas, guests enjoy a diverse selection of flowing cocktails, including the Prickly Pear-infused Tequila Sunset and the refreshing Greta’s Punch.
  • Elevated Comfort Food: The menu features sophisticated twists on American classics, such as shrimp and grits, sweet potato waffles with dark meat chicken, and world-class desserts from the renowned Mah-Ze-Dahr bakery.
  • Prime Ballpark Location: Situated directly across from Nationals Park, this stylish venue offers the perfect upscale backdrop for Navy Yard locals and baseball fans alike.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • For the best experience during high-energy weekend brunch, reservations are recommended, and parties are encouraged to arrive together to ensure prompt seating.
  • Look out for their daily "Blue Plate" specials and signature hot sauce to add an extra kick to their popular chicken sandwiches.
