The Dubliner

The Dubliner

Category: Irish Pub, Live Music Venue

Price: $$

Address: 4 F St NW, Washington, DC 20001

Established in 1974 and located near Union Station on Capitol Hill, The Dubliner is one of the country's most famous Irish pubs. It offers authentic Irish food, the exclusive U.S. source for Auld Dubliner Amber Ale, and live Irish music nightly.
