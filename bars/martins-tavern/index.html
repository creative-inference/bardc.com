Martin's Tavern

Category: Historic Bar, American Restaurant, Pub

Price: $$$

Address: 1264 Wisconsin Ave NW, Washington, DC 20007

A Georgetown institution since 1933, Martin's Tavern has served every president from Truman to George W. Bush. This family-owned tavern is steeped in political history, offering classic American cuisine in cozy, wood-paneled booths, including the one where JFK proposed to Jackie.
