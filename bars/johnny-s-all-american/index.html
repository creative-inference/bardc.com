Johnny's All American

Johnny's All American

Category: Sports Bars, Neighborhood Bars, American

Neighborhood: Columbia Heights

Price:

Address: 3226 11th St NW, Washington, DC 20010, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A "fancy dive bar" with a cozy, preppy-vintage aesthetic that blends warm Midwestern hospitality with high-energy Columbia Heights neighborhood charm.

🍸 Best For

Watching the game, Ohio expats, solo bar-hopping, casual group hangs, smash burger cravings, and late-night drinks.

🔥 Highlights

  • Ohio-Inspired Charm: Founded as a tribute to the owner’s roots, the bar features curated vintage memorabilia and a welcoming "everyone is family" spirit that makes locals and visitors feel right at home.
  • Versatile Day-to-Night Concept: A unique neighborhood anchor that transitions seamlessly from serving coffee and doughnuts in the morning to a bustling tavern with "beer-and-shot" combos by night.
  • Entertainment & Games: Beyond the game-day energy of its many TVs, the venue is a go-to for fun, featuring a stacked menu of bar games and board games perfect for social groups.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • With doors opening as early as 9:30 AM daily, it’s the perfect spot for both early birds and those looking for a late-night haunt.
  • Be sure to visit during the holiday season to experience their renowned festive transformations and charming seasonal decor.
