✨ Vibe

Boundary Stone is a warm and energetic neighborhood staple that seamlessly blends the cozy charm of a whiskey-focused pub with the high-octane spirit of a premier sports destination.

🍸 Best For

Philadelphia Eagles and Phillies fans, whiskey enthusiasts, bottomless weekend brunch, trivia nights, and soccer fans.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Ultimate Philly Connection: Recently recognized as one of DC’s best pubs, this is the city's undisputed home for Philadelphia sports, offering an electric atmosphere and "the best cheesesteak in the city by a mile."
  • Award-Winning Pub Fare: From their famous habanero chicken sandwiches to "the best chicken wings in DC," the kitchen serves up elevated comfort food that consistently earns 5-star praise.
  • Legendary Weekend Brunch: A local favorite for day-drinking, featuring the iconic Fried Chicken Benny and what fans call the absolute best savory Bloody Mary in Washington.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Catch "Bloomingdale’s Best Damn Happy Hour" Monday through Thursday from 5pm to 7pm for great deals on drafts and sandwiches.
  • The pub now opens at noon Monday through Friday for lunch, occasionally offering "free beer" promotions for daytime diners.

Time Out Worldwide

Boundary Stone

Boundary Stone is an Irish-leaning pub and whiskey bar in Bloomingdale that has remained a neighborhood staple since opening in 2011. The venue features a front patio, cozy interior booths, and a menu specializing in elevated late-night pub grub and picklebacks.
Arlington Historical

Benjamin Bannaker and the Boundary Stones

Boundary Stones are the nation's oldest federal monuments, marking the original 100-square-mile boundary of the District of Columbia established in 1791. These historical markers, positioned with the help of astronomer Benjamin Banneker, remain visitable sites at various locations throughout Washington D.C., Arlington, and Alexandria.
WJLA

Get ready to go bald for a cause as St. Baldrick’s hits Boundary Stone this Sunday

Boundary Stone is hosting its annual St. Baldrick’s Foundation event this Sunday to raise funds for childhood cancer research. The Bloomingdale pub invites participants to shave their heads or donate to the cause during this neighborhood fundraiser.
