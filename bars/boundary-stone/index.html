Category: Neighborhood Bar, American, Whiskey Bar

A welcoming neighborhood pub in Bloomingdale, Boundary Stone is known for its extensive whiskey list, craft beers, and elevated pub fare, including their popular chicken wings. The bar hosts weekly events like trivia and live music and is a go-to spot for Philadelphia sports fans.
