✨ Vibe

Breadsoda is a welcoming, subterranean Glover Park staple that perfectly blends a chill community atmosphere with the high-energy fun of a classic neighborhood game room.

🍸 Best For

Group game nights, casual dates, craft beer enthusiasts, late-night deli cravings, neighborhood happy hours

🔥 Highlights

  • Premier Gaming Experience: Recognized as one of D.C.’s top pool destinations, the venue features well-spaced tables, ping pong, shuffleboard, darts, and popular trivia nights.
  • Exceptional Deli-Inspired Fare: The kitchen serves up "ridiculously good" Reuben sandwiches, fresh guacamole, and a variety of clearly labeled vegan and vegetarian options.
  • Impressive Beverage Program: An extensive craft beer list is complemented by inventive seasonal cocktails and a thoughtful selection of non-alcoholic "spirit-free" beverages.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Pool tables are highly sought after on weekends; arrive before 8:00 PM on Saturdays to avoid a waitlist.
  • Enjoy rare weekend deals with their Saturday happy hour, and note that the full kitchen remains open until 11:00 PM.
