✨ Vibe

Nestled in the heart of historic Georgetown, Chez Billy Sud is a romantic and charming French bistro that transports guests to Southern France with its elegantly refreshed celadon-painted dining room, cozy art-filled interiors, and a sprawling sun-drenched patio.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, upscale weekend brunches, intimate private celebrations, and sophisticated wine pairings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Expertly Crafted French Classics: Under the direction of Executive Chef Brendan L’Etoile, the kitchen serves perfection ranging from a signature three-day prepared duck confit to "the real deal" crème brûlée and decadent croque madames.
  • The Enchanting Bar à Vin: Located right next door, this cozy sister bar offers an extensive curated wine list of French varietals and handcrafted cocktails like Espresso Martinis, perfect for a pre-dinner drink or a casual evening out.
  • Refined & Renovated Spaces: Following an extensive recent restoration, the venue features a refreshed dining room and a new, elegant upstairs private dining suite that offers a personalized and unforgettable experience for special events.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Indoor reservations are available on a rolling 30-day basis via OpenTable, while the beautiful outdoor patio and Bar à Vin seating are hosted on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • The dress code is casual yet polished, and guests can expect exceptional hospitality with a standard 20% gratuity included to support the attentive service team.
