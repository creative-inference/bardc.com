✨ Vibe

Power Nightclub is a high-energy Ivy City hotspot known for its "lit" atmosphere, pulsing dance floor, and a vibrant crowd that keeps the energy at peak levels until the early morning hours.

🍸 Best For

Late-night dancing, weekend parties, bottle service, high-energy group celebrations, urban nightlife fans.

🔥 Highlights

  • High-octane atmosphere featuring dynamic DJ sets and live music that creates an immersive, high-spirited clubbing experience.
  • A central destination in the growing Ivy City nightlife scene, perfect for those looking for a late-night weekend escape.
  • Exclusive VIP bottle service options available for groups looking to celebrate in the heart of the action with a dedicated host.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The club operates on a late-night schedule, keeping the doors open until 3:00 AM every Friday and Saturday.
  • Reservations for VIP tables and bottle service can be made in advance by contacting 202.445.1050 to ensure your group has a prime spot.
