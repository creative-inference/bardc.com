The Wells

The Wells

Category: Gin Bar, Speakeasy, Cocktail Bar

Price: $$$

Address: 727 C St SE, Washington, DC 20003

A sophisticated, speakeasy-style gin bar tucked away on Capitol Hill, The Wells offers an intimate and elegant experience. With a focus on a wide variety of gins from around the world, it serves expertly crafted classic and contemporary cocktails in a cozy, subterranean setting.
