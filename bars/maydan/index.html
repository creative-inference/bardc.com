Maydan

Maydan

Category: Middle Eastern, North African, Restaurant

Price: $$$

Address: 1346 Florida Ave NW, Washington, DC 20009

A Michelin-starred restaurant tucked away in a back alley, Maydan centers around a massive, live-fire hearth. The dramatic, two-story space offers a transportive dining experience with a menu inspired by the cuisines of the Middle East and North Africa, designed for sharing and dipping with fresh-baked bread.
