Maydan

Maydan

Category: Mediterranean, Groups, Romantic

Neighborhood: U Street

Price: $$$

Address: 1346 Florida Ave NW, Washington, DC 20009

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Maydan offers an immersive, high-energy gastronomical adventure centered around a roaring open-fire hearth that fills the intricately decorated, multi-level space with the warmth and aroma of authentic live-fire Middle Eastern cooking.

🍸 Best For

Communal feasts with friends, romantic celebratory dinners, Michelin-starred culinary tours, and lovers of bold Mediterranean flavors.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Tawle Experience: A spectacular family-style feast inspired by culinary travels through Morocco, Turkey, and Lebanon, offering an incredible variety of flavorful spreads, salads, and hearth-grilled mains.
  • Signature Hearth Dishes: The menu features must-order items like the legendary roasted cauliflower and freshly baked flatbread pulled straight from the fire, embodying the restaurant's "heart is the hearth" philosophy.
  • Expert Mixology: Complement your meal with a signature "Belly Dancer" cocktail, a fan-favorite drink known for its perfectly balanced and exotic flavor profile.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are essential and open via Resy exactly 21 days in advance at 10:00 AM; booking early is highly recommended to secure an indoor table near the action of the open fire.
  • For the most immersive experience, request indoor seating to fully enjoy the immaculate ambiance and the energy of the communal dining room.
