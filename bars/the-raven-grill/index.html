The Raven Grill

The Raven Grill

Category: Dive Bars, Neighborhood Bars, Casual

Neighborhood: Columbia Heights

Price: $

Address: 3125 Mount Pleasant St NW, Washington, DC 20010

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

The Raven Grill is a quintessential neighborhood haunt that offers a warm, dimly lit atmosphere reminiscent of a comfortable basement hangout, blending historical authenticity with a cozy, welcoming spirit.

🍸 Best For

Budget-friendly beers, casual late-night yapping, board game enthusiasts, and low-key group hangouts.

🔥 Highlights

  • An iconic DC dive bar experience featuring an incredible jukebox and curated bartender playlists that set the perfect musical backdrop.
  • A relaxed, social environment where guests are encouraged to bring in their favorite local takeout to enjoy at the booths.
  • Exceptional value with some of the most competitive drink prices in Columbia Heights, served by friendly and knowledgeable staff.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • This is a cash-only establishment, though an ATM is conveniently located inside for all your needs.
  • The venue offers a great selection of board games and light snacks behind the bar to keep the fun going.
