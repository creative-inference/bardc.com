Astro Beer Hall

Astro Beer Hall

Category: Beer Gardens, Gastropubs, Burger Bars

Neighborhood: Downtown

Price:

Address: 1306 G St NW, Washington, DC 20005, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A lively, retro-inspired space in the heart of downtown DC that combines nostalgic charm with a high-energy, modern beer hall atmosphere.

🍸 Best For

Craft beer enthusiasts, casual group hangouts, pre-museum lunches, and late-night sweet treats.

🔥 Highlights

  • Signature Galactic Eats: Indulge in crowd favorites like the crispy Asteroid Chicken Sandwich, juicy smash burgers, and perfectly seasoned tater tots.
  • Craft Brews & Donuts: Enjoy a stellar rotation of regional and national craft beers complemented by fresh, creative treats from the attached artisan donut shop.
  • Modern Tech & Retro Fun: Experience a seamless visit with convenient digital ordering in a vibrant, space-themed environment located just steps from the Metro.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Conveniently located near the Smithsonian museums, making it the perfect refueling station for tourists and locals alike.
  • Offers easy online reservations and dedicated space for booking private group events.
