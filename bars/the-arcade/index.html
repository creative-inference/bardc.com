Category: Nightclub, Arcade Bars, Upscale

Price:

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

The Arcade offers a sleek, upscale fusion of retro-themed decor and high-end global nightlife, channeling the high-energy atmosphere of premier clubs in Miami and Ibiza.

🍸 Best For

High-energy dancing, premium bottle service, luxury nights out, and catching world-class DJ sets.

🔥 Highlights

  • Electric DJ Sets: The venue features standout DJs who curate a high-energy soundtrack reminiscent of the world’s most famous party capitals.
  • Sophisticated Decor: A unique "arcade-themed" aesthetic with an elevated, trendy twist that sets it apart from any other spot in the city.
  • Lively Social Scene: A destination for an upscale, energetic crowd looking for a vibrant and exclusive party atmosphere in downtown DC.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Table reservations are highly encouraged for those seeking the full VIP bottle service experience.
  • Dress to impress to match the venue's sleek, trendy, and elevated nightclub environment.
