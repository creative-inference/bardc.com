Players Club

Address: 1400 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20005, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A stylishly nostalgic "grown-up playground" that perfectly blends 1970s basement charm with sleek retro-modern aesthetics and high-energy gaming energy.

🍸 Best For

Retro date nights, group celebrations, after-work drinks, gaming enthusiasts, weekend shenanigans.

🔥 Highlights

  • Ultimate Retro Arcade: An expansive collection of classic video games, pool tables, and pinball machines—including the fan-favorite John Wick table—all complemented by a vintage-style photo booth for capturing memories.
  • Skybox Rooftop: A chic upstairs escape offering a chill ambience, great music, and scenic views of Logan Circle for those looking to enjoy specialty cocktails in the open air.
  • Burgers & Gaming: A unique partnership with the adjacent Shake Shack allows guests to pair craft beers and cocktails with iconic burgers and shakes for the ultimate "leveled-up" nostalgia trip.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Recently expanded to include The Muni, a brand-new midcentury-themed sister bar on the second floor featuring state-of-the-art golf simulators and interactive sports games.
  • The venue is strictly 21+ and stays open late, making it a prime destination for weekend nightlife with hours extending until 2:45 AM on Fridays and Saturdays.
