Bar Sirenis

Bar Sirenis

Category:

Neighborhood: Ivy City

Price: $$

Address: 1907 Fairview Ave NE, Washington, DC 20002, USA

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A stunning, Mediterranean-inspired oasis in Ivy City that blends industrial distillery elements with a stylish, aquamarine "siren" theme reminiscent of the Amalfi Coast.

🍸 Best For

Amaro tastings, craft spritzes, educational dates, distillery tours, and aperitivo enthusiasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Handcrafted Liqueurs: Enjoy exclusive access to the full lineup of Don Ciccio & Figli’s artisanal Italian amari and cordials, all produced in small batches right on the premises.
  • Award-Winning Aesthetics: Recognized as one of D.C.’s most beautiful spaces, the bar features a striking blue color palette, mythical mermaid accents, and vintage-style glassware.
  • Educational Tastings: Go beyond the glass with immersive flights and the chance to learn about the history and chemistry of herbal liqueurs directly from the expert makers.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located directly inside the Don Ciccio & Figli distillery; guests can often pair their cocktail experience with a tour of the production facility.
  • Look out for specialized cocktail demonstrations and the "Design Your Own Spritz" options for a personalized drink experience.

Happy Hour

Pick ups are available Tue - Fri 11am-330pm and Saturdays 2pm-7pm.
