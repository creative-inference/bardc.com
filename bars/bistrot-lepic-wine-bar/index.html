✨ Vibe

An authentic, "Parisian neighborhood" institution in Upper Georgetown that perfectly blends old-school French charm with a cozy, intimate atmosphere across its first-floor dining room and relaxed second-story wine lounge.

🍸 Best For

Romantic dates, wine connoisseurs, live jazz enthusiasts, authentic French comfort food, sophisticated weeknight drinks.

🔥 Highlights

  • Live Jazz Nights: Every Wednesday from 6 PM to 9 PM, the Wine Bar hosts "sans cover" live jazz sessions, creating a sophisticated and rhythmic backdrop for your evening.
  • Award-Winning French Cuisine: Recognized by Bon Appétit as one of the best restaurants in the country, the menu features masterful classics like the signature potato-crusted salmon, duck confit, and the legendary "Ile Flotante" (floating island) dessert.
  • Extensive Wine Program: A true destination for oenophiles, offering a vast selection of French vintages and expert pairings, recently highlighted as a premier spot for DC’s Winter Restaurant Week 2026.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy half-price wines by the glass during Happy Hour at the Wine Bar, Tuesday through Thursday and Sunday from 5 PM to 7 PM.
  • While the first floor offers a traditional bistro dining experience, the upstairs Wine Bar provides a more casual lounge setting with comfortable sofas and chairs.

Bistrot Lepic & Wine Bar is an unapologetically French establishment in Georgetown featuring a second-story wine bar with an extensive bottle list and a menu of grand cuisine classics like cassoulet and salmon in a potato crust.
