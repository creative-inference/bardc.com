✨ Vibe

Ciel - Downtown is a sophisticated penthouse sanctuary that blends celestial opulence with panoramic city views, creating a high-energy, "main-character" atmosphere perfect for stylish socializing above the skyline.

🍸 Best For

Sunset cocktails, Instagram-worthy moments, upscale happy hours, romantic dates, and live jazz enthusiasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • Breathtaking Views: Offers nearly 360-degree panoramic vistas of the U.S. Capitol and the Washington Monument from its luxurious rooftop perch atop the AC Hotel.
  • Immersive Pop-Ups: Experience the current "Once Upon a Time in Ciel" theme, a fairytale-inspired escape featuring cascading book pages, lush floral installations, and whimsical cocktails like "The Fairest of Them All."
  • Rooftop Jazz Nights: Every Tuesday, guests can enjoy the smooth sounds of a live jazz trio paired with a complimentary glass of champagne to set an elegant mid-week mood.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue enforces a strict "business casual or better" dress code—leave the hoodies and activewear at home to match the upscale, celebratory environment.
  • Reservations are highly recommended, especially for the popular weekday happy hour featuring discounted cocktails and specials on Veuve Clicquot.
