Culture

Culture

Category: Nightclub, Dance Clubs, Late Night

Neighborhood: Ivy City

Price: $$$

Address: 2006 Fenwick St NE Washington, DC 20002

Website
View on Google Maps →

Culture is an innovative, modular warehouse venue in Ivy City that serves as the premier home for Nü Androids' experimental dance music events, blending industrial grit with high-end production.

✨ Vibe

A cutting-edge, immersive warehouse space that prioritizes world-class sound and visual art for a dedicated community of dance music enthusiasts.

🍸 Best For

Experimental dance music, 360° DJ sets, immersive audiovisual experiences, high-fidelity sound, and late-night house and techno.

🔥 Highlights

  • Equipped with a state-of-the-art Danley Sound Labs audio system designed for crystal-clear, high-impact club acoustics.
  • Features advanced projection mapping technology that transforms the modular walls into a dynamic, ever-changing visual landscape.
  • Boasts a versatile 360-degree layout that fosters an intimate connection between the crowd and world-renowned touring artists.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Events are typically ticketed and curated by Nü Androids; check the calendar for international headliners and underground favorites.
  • Bottle service is available for those seeking a premium, elevated experience within the high-energy warehouse atmosphere.
