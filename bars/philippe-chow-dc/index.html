Philippe Chow DC

Philippe Chow DC

Category: Supper Clubs, Upscale, Asian Fusion

Neighborhood: The Wharf

Price:

Address: 635 Wharf St SW, Washington, DC 20024, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

This glitzy Wharf destination pairs high-energy New York flash with a sophisticated, white-tablecloth atmosphere, offering guests a "red carpet" experience defined by world-class service and a vibrant social scene.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, milestone birthday celebrations, upscale girls' nights out, and high-profile philanthropic events.

🔥 Highlights

  • The legendary two-day dry-aged Peking Duck, prepared with meticulous detail and served tableside with house-made steamed pancakes.
  • Exceptional hospitality from a white-jacketed service team dedicated to creating a warm, personalized dining experience for every guest.
  • The ultra-indulgent "Crown Tasting Experience," an elite 14-course culinary journey featuring hand-pulled noodles and artisanal dim sum.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • A top choice for "splurge-worthy" group dining at the District Wharf; advance reservations are highly recommended for weekend evenings.
  • Look out for special multi-course menus and seasonal highlights like the show-stopping Cotton Candy Baked Alaska.

Philippe Chow Partners with Arena Stage Theater

Philippe Chow DC has announced a strategic partnership with the nearby Arena Stage theater to enhance the pre-show dining experience at the Wharf. This collaboration offers theater patrons exclusive dining options and highlights the venue's role as a key culinary destination within Washington D.C.’s vibrant arts district.
