The Hamilton

The Hamilton

Category: Live Music Venues, Upscale, Late Night

Neighborhood: Downtown

Price: $$$

Address: 600 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20005

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Sophisticated yet high-energy, The Hamilton seamlessly blends a polished, upscale dining atmosphere with a world-class, tiered live music venue in the heart of downtown DC.

🍸 Best For

Live music lovers, late-night dining, out-of-town guests, upscale brunch, craft cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • World-Class Concert Venue: The Hamilton Live offers an intimate and fancy concert experience where guests can enjoy premium table service and dine directly in front of the stage while watching national touring acts.
  • Exceptional Late-Night Dining: A rare downtown gem that provides a high-end "bussdown" dining experience after hours, featuring a full menu of sushi, Maryland-style crab cakes, and top-shelf mixology.
  • Diverse Culinary Mastery: The kitchen excels at a wide range of flavors, from tender grilled octopus and lobster-stuffed rolls to artisanal pastas and house-made desserts like their smooth people's cheesecake.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Advance reservations are highly recommended for both dinner and live performances to secure prime seating near the stage.
  • Take advantage of their daily Sushi Happy Hour and extensive late-night menu for a more casual way to experience this downtown staple.

🔄 Weekly Events

Late Night Dining 🌙

Daily

Available during late-night hours, featuring a selection of starters and snacks including Hoisin Glazed Pork Belly, Angus Cheeseburger Sliders with bone marrow onion jam, and District Wings.

Weekend Brunch 🥂

Sat, Sun

The Hamilton offers a dedicated brunch service featuring elevated items like Chesapeake Benedict with crab cakes, Peanut Butter Pancakes with salted caramel, and Shrimp & Grits.

Late Night at The Hamilton 🌙

Daily

After the dining room closes, the bar offers a full late-night menu. Last call for food is at 12:45 AM and drinks at 1:30 AM.
