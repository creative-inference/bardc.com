✨ Vibe

A high-energy, futuristic rooftop escape featuring vibrant neon LED lighting, retractable glass walls, and a "big game" atmosphere with panoramic city views.

🍸 Best For

Game-day viewing, budget-friendly happy hours, rooftop cocktails, retail sports betting, group gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Immersive Sports Experience: Recently recognized as one of D.C.’s authorized retail sports betting destinations, the venue features on-site kiosks, live ticket writers, and wall-to-wall TVs for a premium wagering environment.
  • Versatile All-Season Design: The rooftop's unique retractable roof and floor-to-ceiling glass walls allow for a breezy outdoor feel in the summer and cozy, climate-controlled skyline views in the winter.
  • Unbeatable Daily Value: Standout specials like the phenomenal pork belly tacos and buffalo wings are paired with daily drink deals that offer some of the most competitive prices in the district.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of half-off Happy Hour deals on weekdays and a dedicated $7 Martini night every Thursday.
  • Ideally located in the Mt. Vernon Triangle neighborhood, it is a prime spot for pre- or post-game drinks less than a mile from Capital One Arena.
