Ivy and Coney

Ivy and Coney

Category: Sports Bars, Dive Bars, Pizza Bars

Neighborhood: Shaw

Price: $

Address: 1537 7th St NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A high-energy, no-frills Midwest dive bar that brings the authentic grit and heart of Chicago and Detroit to the Shaw neighborhood.

🍸 Best For

Midwest sports fans, Detroit-style pizza enthusiasts, budget-friendly beer and shot combos, and festive holiday pop-ups.

🔥 Highlights

  • Midwest Culinary Classics: Feast on an authentic menu featuring 10/10 burgers, Chicago-style dogs, and thick Detroit "za" alongside their signature curly fries and zesty cheese sauce.
  • The "Chai-vy & Cohen-y" Pop-Up: Every December, the bar transforms into a legendary Hanukkah destination featuring a 16-foot "shotnorah," nightly menorah lightings, and traditional family-recipe latkes.
  • The Ultimate Sports Den: With plenty of TVs and a spirited "outdoorish" deck space, it is the city’s premier spot to catch a Michigan or Detroit game while sipping an Old Style or braving a shot of Malort.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Find the entrance right above the iconic Old Style sign on 7th Street; if you're visiting in late December, look out for their signature Chinese food and movie night on Christmas Day.
