Ivy and Coney

Category: Dive Bar, Sports Bar

Price: $

Address: 1537 7th St NW, Washington, DC 20001

Tucked away above a different storefront in Shaw, Ivy and Coney is a no-frills bar that pays homage to Chicago and Detroit. Known for serving hot dogs, cheap beer, and Malört, it's a cash-only haven for Midwestern sports fans and anyone looking for a laid-back, unpretentious experience.
