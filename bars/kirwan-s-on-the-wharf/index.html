Kirwan’s on the Wharf

Kirwan’s on the Wharf

Category: Irish Pubs, Waterfront, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: The Wharf

Price:

Address: 749 Wharf St SW, Washington, DC 20024, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A beautifully authentic and laid-back Irish retreat that blends traditional "home away from home" warmth with stunning waterfront views of the Potomac River.

🍸 Best For

Waterfront dining, authentic Irish comfort food, late-night drinks, live weekend music, and scenic river views.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Fish & Chips: Renowned for serving some of the best fish and chips in the city, the dish features a traditional Galway-inspired recipe with North Atlantic cod and double-fried Yukon Gold potatoes.
  • Lively Weekend Entertainment: A perennially busy staple of Wharf nightlife that hosts energetic live cover bands on weekends and stays open later than many neighboring establishments.
  • Authentic Irish Flavors: The menu boasts premium imported Irish Nature beef, signature Beef Wellington, and perfectly poured, frothy pints of Guinness.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • For those seeking a late-night bite, a dedicated late-night menu is served from 10:00 PM to 12:00 AM.
  • Outdoor seating is highly recommended for prime views of the boats and river, and reservations can be made for both lunch and dinner.
