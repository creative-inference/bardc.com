✨ Vibe

Hamilton’s Bar & Grill is a warm and welcoming neighborhood staple that brings a slice of Midwestern hospitality to Capitol Hill, serving as the high-energy "Lambeau on the Hill" for local sports fans.

🍸 Best For

Green Bay Packers game days, authentic Wisconsin cheese curds, casual group lunches, and live local comedy.

🔥 Highlights

  • The official home of the Green Bay Packers fan club in DC, offering an electric atmosphere and game-day specials for every snap.
  • A standout menu of "gratifying pub grub" featuring their famous tater tots, rotating craft beer drafts, and genuine Wisconsin-style cheese curds.
  • Weekly entertainment in the upstairs performance space, including the "Spaghetti on the Beach" comedy standup and open mic every Tuesday night.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy a dynamite happy hour every weekday from 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM featuring $5 rail drinks and $5 Miller Lite bottles.
  • While the venue typically follows standard weekday hours, they open specifically on Sundays and Mondays to accommodate Packers fans during the season.

Comedy Standup at Hamilton’s on Capitol Hill

Hamilton’s Bar & Grill hosts 'Spaghetti on the Beach,' a weekly comedy show and open mic event held every Tuesday at 7 p.m. in its upstairs performance space. The program, run by Capitol Hill Comedy, offers a welcoming environment for both novice and veteran comedians to test new material in front of a live audience.
