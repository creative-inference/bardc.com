Problem Child

Problem Child

Category: Sports Bars, Burger Bars, Dog Friendly

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price:

Address: 1201 Half St SE Unit 115 - Street Level, Washington, DC 20003, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Problem Child is a high-energy, neighborhood dive bar in Navy Yard that blends a casual "come as you are" atmosphere with nostalgic D.C. sports memorabilia and vintage decor.

🍸 Best For

Game days, Buffalo Bills fans, affordable rounds, dog-friendly hangouts, seasonal pop-ups, casual weekend brunch.

🔥 Highlights

  • Budget-Friendly Fun: As one of Navy Yard’s most accessible spots, guests can enjoy $4 select beers, shooters, and no-frills cocktails alongside free popcorn.
  • Creative Comfort Food: The menu features elevated pub fare like honey-bourbon baby back ribs, signature smash burgers, and the unique peanut butter and jelly sandwich with bacon.
  • Immersive Pop-Ups: The venue frequently transforms into themed experiences, such as the "Stranger Things"-inspired holiday bar and exclusive Chicago-style hot dog takeovers.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • This venue is a dedicated Buffalo Bills affiliate bar and features a 20% automatic service charge to directly support its hardworking staff.
  • Weekend brunch is served from 11am to 4pm, and the spacious layout includes both indoor and outdoor seating options.
