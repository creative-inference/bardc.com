The Lobby Lounge | Cocktail & Wine Bar

The Lobby Lounge | Cocktail & Wine Bar

Category: Cocktail Bars, Speakeasies, Lounges

Neighborhood: Georgetown

Price:

Address: 1660 33rd St NW, Washington, DC 20007, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An intimate and luxurious hidden gem tucked away in a Georgetown townhouse, blending the mystery of a secret speakeasy with a chic, picturesque "oasis" patio.

🍸 Best For

Girls' nights out, intimate dates, bespoke cocktails, and aesthetic photo ops.

🔥 Highlights

  • Bespoke Mixology: Highly skilled bartenders specialize in "off-menu" creations tailored to your specific palate, alongside signature favorites like the refreshing blackberry cocktail.
  • Craveable Small Plates: A curated menu of fresh, shareable bites featuring a "must-try" silky hummus and savory salmon bites.
  • The Hidden Oasis: A stunning outdoor patio lined with arched mirrors offers a glamorous backdrop for selfies, while the interior provides a cozy, "secret hideout" feel.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue is located inside a classic Georgetown townhouse; look for the unassuming exterior to find this true hidden speakeasy.
  • The bar is exceptionally mocktail-friendly, with staff able to craft sophisticated non-alcoholic versions of any drink on the menu.
