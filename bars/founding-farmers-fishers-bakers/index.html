Founding Farmers Fishers & Bakers

Founding Farmers Fishers & Bakers

Category: Waterfront, American, Brunch Spots

✨ Vibe

A vibrant, farm-to-table destination on the Georgetown waterfront that blends a laid-back, classy atmosphere with the energetic buzz of a bustling open kitchen.

🍸 Best For

Indulgent weekend brunches, waterfront dining, casual celebrations, farm-to-table comfort food, and family gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Over-the-Top Brunch Buffet: Recently recognized as one of DC’s premier brunch spots, featuring a made-from-scratch spread with table-side service for skillet cinnamon rolls and fried shrimp.
  • Scratch-Made American Classics: A versatile menu highlighting fresh, locally sourced ingredients, from the fan-favorite Chimichurri skirt steak to the double smashed burger on homemade potato bread.
  • In-House Treats & Spirits: Enjoy a unique beverage experience with spirits from their own distillery and a dedicated counter for house-made classic candies and artisanal pies.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended, especially for the popular weekend brunch buffet, as the venue fills up quickly.
  • The restaurant offers a full-service breakfast during the week, making it a perfect spot for early morning meetings or casual waterfront starts.
