Lulu's Winegarden

Lulu's Winegarden

Category: Wine Bars, Happy Hour Spots, Patios

Neighborhood: U Street

Price: $$

Address: 1940 11th St NW, Washington, DC 20001

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A lush, Southwestern-inspired oasis in the heart of U Street that blends a relaxed backyard party atmosphere with a chic, plant-filled "indoor garden" aesthetic.

🍸 Best For

Group gatherings, casual dates, golden hour drinks, and weekend brunch.

🔥 Highlights

  • Curated Wine Program: Features an approachable selection of flat-rate bottles and a "Fancy Stuff" reserve list, with communal tables uniquely equipped with built-in ice buckets to keep your selections perfectly chilled.
  • Southwestern-Influenced Bites: A delicious menu of elevated comfort food, including their signature white queso, black bean hummus, and crispy chicken sandwiches smothered in fermented chile.
  • Year-Round Outdoor Comfort: The charming patios are designed for every season, functioning as a "Wine(ter)garden" with cozy fire pits and heaters to keep the inviting atmosphere going all year long.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Experience "Golden Hour" happy hour specials Tuesday–Thursday (5-7pm), Friday (4-6pm), and an extended session on Sunday (3-8pm).
  • Reservations are recommended for the indoor garden and patio tables, though communal seating is perfect for walk-ins and larger groups.
