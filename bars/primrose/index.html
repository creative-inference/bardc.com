Primrose

Primrose

Category: French, Brunch Spots, Romantic

Neighborhood: Brookland

Price:

Address: 3000 12th St NE, Washington, DC 20017, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A whimsical and sophisticated French-Euro bistro featuring stunning ostrich-feather chandeliers, weathered teal walls, and a beautifully relaxed greenhouse patio.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, celebratory dinners, natural wine lovers, stylish weekend brunch.

🔥 Highlights

  • Recently refreshed with a modern culinary upgrade under Executive Chef Patrick Benton, featuring an inventive $55 "Taste of Primrose" three-course menu alongside a five-course prix fixe option.
  • A world-class natural wine program curated by sommelier Sebastian Zutant, paired with sophisticated seasonal cocktails like the "All Green Everything #2."
  • Exceptional hospitality and a "European-inspired" atmosphere that has earned it a top spot in the Resy Guide to Where to Drink in D.C.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended to secure a table in the popular outdoor solarium or the main dining room.
  • Wine enthusiasts can join the "Stardust Bottle Club" for exclusive access to their eclectic, low-intervention wine selections and special offers.
