✨ Vibe

Quill is a sophisticated, literary-inspired sanctuary within the historic Jefferson hotel, featuring a warm library-like ambiance, polished brass accents, and a stunning amber-toned lighted glass bar.

🍸 Best For

Upscale cocktails, intimate conversations, whiskey enthusiasts, romantic dates, historic atmosphere

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Mixology: Recently celebrated for its luxury positioning with the $100 "Centennial Cocktail" featuring 24-karat gold-encapsulated ice, the bar is also famous for world-class whiskey flights and seasonal craft creations.
  • Historic Madeira Collection: Guests can enjoy a truly rare experience with an extensive collection of historic Madeira dating back to the 18th century.
  • Chef-Driven Menu: Under the direction of Executive Chef Fabio Salvatore, the lounge serves refined Italian-influenced fare, including a celebrated crab soufflé and premium charcuterie platters with caviar-topped deviled eggs.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue offers a full dinner service alongside its signature cocktail menu, making it an excellent choice for a complete evening of fine dining and drinks.
  • Located just blocks from the White House, it serves as a premier destination for those seeking a professional and polished environment for business or leisure.
