✨ Vibe

A chic and intimate neighborhood gem where glamorous interiors meet a soulful, music-focused atmosphere perfect for deep conversation and sophisticated relaxation.

🍸 Best For

Romantic dates, pre-theater drinks for the Lincoln Theatre, Real Housewives of Potomac fans, happy hour catch-ups, and private milestone celebrations.

🔥 Highlights

  • Star-Studded Setting: Recently featured on season nine of The Real Housewives of Potomac as a glamorous destination for speed dating, this spot offers a truly "TV-ready" aesthetic.
  • Expert Curation: Known for an impeccable wine list and "nice and strong" cocktails, the knowledgeable staff provides phenomenal wine recommendations and pairs them with a "divine" food menu.
  • Impeccable Ambiance: From the "impeccable" music playlists to the beautiful decor—so pretty it has served as a proposal backdrop—the atmosphere is designed for making memories.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Ideally located right off the U Street Metro, it is a convenient "must-visit" for anyone attending a show at the nearby Lincoln Theatre.
  • The venue is highly recommended for private event planning, with staff praised for being exceptionally accommodating for groups of up to 24 guests.

Washington.org

Wine Bars & Wineries to Get Your Vino Fix in Washington, DC

Barkada Wine Bar is a natural wine bar in Washington, DC, dedicated to spotlighting small, under-represented winemakers who produce complex yet affordable vintages. The venue provides an intimate space designed for social connection, reflecting its name, which is a Filipino word meaning a 'group of friends.'
Rappler

Like it, hate it? Filipinos react to American-owned US restaurant ‘Barkada’

Barkada Wine and Cocktail Bar sparked significant public debate and controversy regarding cultural appropriation due to its non-Filipino ownership using a Filipino name. The venue's branding prompted a wide range of reactions from the Filipino community and raised questions about cultural sensitivity in the hospitality industry.
