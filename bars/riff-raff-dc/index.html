Riff Raff DC

Riff Raff DC

Category: Cocktail Bars, Speakeasies, Neighborhood Bars

Neighborhood: Adams Morgan

Price:

Address: 2333 18th St NW, Washington, DC 20009, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An intimate, unpretentious speakeasy that blends eco-conscious innovation with a warm, community-driven atmosphere tucked away in the heart of Adams Morgan.

🍸 Best For

Sustainable cocktails, intimate date nights, creative mixology, solo adventures at the bar, small group gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Zero-Waste Mixology: Led by award-winning beverage expert Jon Schott, the bar features a groundbreaking "mindful drinking" program with over 40 cocktails utilizing upcycled ingredients like banana peel syrup and citrus skin "super juices."
  • A True Hidden Gem: Located discreetly inside Johnny Pistolas, this 22-seat sanctuary offers a sophisticated escape from the neighborhood bustle without any of the typical speakeasy pretension.
  • Elevated Small Plates: The drink menu is perfectly paired with stellar Latin-inspired bites, including modern tapas, tacos, and nachos.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Find this secret spot by entering Johnny Pistolas and heading to the back of the ground floor.
  • The lounge currently operates Thursday through Saturday, making it an exclusive destination for weekend cocktail enthusiasts.
