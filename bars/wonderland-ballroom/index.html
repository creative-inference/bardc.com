✨ Vibe

A legendary Columbia Heights neighborhood staple, Wonderland Ballroom offers a perfect mix of cozy dive-bar charm downstairs and a high-energy, pulsing dance floor upstairs.

🍸 Best For

Late-night dancing, weekend brunch, neighborhood drinks, daily happy hour, live music

🔥 Highlights

  • Experience the ultimate weekend transformation as the second floor turns into a high-energy dance party from 11:30 PM until close every Friday and Saturday night.
  • Enjoy savory comfort food favorites, including their popular breakfast burritos and signature chicken sandwiches served with perfectly crunchy tater tots.
  • Celebrate DC's unique local culture with famous annual events like the Sundress Fest, which highlights the venue’s fun-loving community spirit.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Happy Hour runs daily from 4:00 PM to 6:30 PM, making it a great spot for an early evening drink.
  • The venue is transit-friendly, located just three blocks from the Green Line Metro with a Capital Bikeshare station directly across the street.
