A quirky and beloved multi-level dive bar in Columbia Heights, The Wonderland Ballroom features a lively main bar, a more relaxed lounge, and a spacious, dog-friendly outdoor patio. It's a quintessential neighborhood hangout known for its weekly trivia, comedy shows, and unpretentious vibe.

Happy Hour

Happy Hour is every day from 4:00 pm to 6:30 pm. On the first Sunday of June, there is an all-day happy hour for anyone wearing a sundress.
