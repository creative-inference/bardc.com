✨ Vibe

A stylish and chill neighborhood gem that blends a cozy, Victorian-inspired lounge atmosphere with a lively and welcoming energy perfect for any occasion.

🍸 Best For

Authentic Neapolitan pizza, craft cocktail dates, lively happy hours, and weekend dinner gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Neapolitan Pizza: Celebrated as some of the best in the city, the pizza features a perfect balance of charred, chewy crust and authentic, vibrant tomato sauce that brings the taste of Naples to D.C.
  • Exquisite Small Plates & Drinks: Beyond the pizza, guests rave about the flavorful nduja croquettes and a sophisticated cocktail program designed to deliver "liquid fantasies."
  • Exciting Brand Expansion: Building on its success near U Street, the brand recently launched "Taste of Lucy," a new sibling location in Union Market featuring curated cocktails and weekly pasta specials.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reserving a table in advance is recommended for peak hours, though the bar area is an excellent spot for walk-ins looking for attentive service.
  • The venue offers versatile seating options, including a beautiful outdoor area and a cozy indoor layout ideal for dinner dates.
