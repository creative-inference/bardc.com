No Goodbyes

No Goodbyes

Category: Hotel Bars, Cocktail Bars, Brunch Spots

Neighborhood: Adams Morgan

Price: $$$

Address: 1770 Euclid St NW, Washington, DC 20009

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Housed in a breathtakingly converted 110-year-old church, No Goodbyes blends historic architectural grandeur with a warm, communal atmosphere that serves as Adams Morgan’s premier all-day social hub.

🍸 Best For

Sophisticated weekend brunch, pet-friendly dining, artisanal coffee dates, and elevated hotel bar cocktails.

🔥 Highlights

  • Award-Winning Pet Hospitality: Recently honored as the #6 best pet-friendly restaurant in the U.S. by TripAdvisor’s 2024 Travelers' Choice Awards, this venue welcomes furry companions both in its spacious interior and on the alfresco patio.
  • Hearth-Centric Mid-Atlantic Cuisine: The menu celebrates the Chesapeake Bay region with wood-fired flavors, featuring fan favorites like the Phil’s Burger with rosemary shoestring fries, savory rigatoni bolognese, and decadent challah bread French toast.
  • Expertly Crafted Beverage Program: From creamy morning cardamom lattes at the coffee shop to creative "New Trick" cocktails designed by veteran D.C. bartender Lukas B. Smith, the drink menu is a curated journey through local and seasonal flavors.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Enjoy a robust Happy Hour Monday through Saturday from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM, featuring "formerly fancy" $9 cocktails and a variety of small plates like fresh oysters and chicken sliders.
  • The venue is a "choose your own adventure" space with distinct hours for the coffee shop, full-service restaurant, and bar, making it as perfect for daytime coworking as it is for a formal three-course holiday dinner.

🔄 Weekly Events

🍳 Weekend Brunch

Sat, Sun

Enjoy an eclectic mix of feel-good brunch dishes including The Benedict, Hangover Hash, Lox & Bagels, and Challah Bread French Toast.

Happy Hour

Enjoy $2 raw oysters, $9 classic cocktails (Martinis, Manhattans, Old Fashioneds), $12 contemporary cocktails, $8 wines, and $6 beers. Food specials include $8 chips and dip and $14 fried chicken sliders.
