✨ Vibe

Licht Cafe is a warm, European-inspired boutique lounge featuring gold-and-wood decor, edgy sensual artwork, and an intimate "Cheers" atmosphere that serves as a cozy hideaway from the bustle of U Street.

🍸 Best For

Date nights, LGBTQ+ gatherings, craft cocktail enthusiasts, intimate conversations, espresso martini aficionados.

🔥 Highlights

  • Expertly crafted cocktails with a focus on classics, including a fan-favorite espresso martini and a signature aviation described by guests as "tops."
  • A unique, art-filled interior modeled after a Mannheim coffee house, offering a bright and inclusive 600-square-foot space with a warm, welcoming glow.
  • A sophisticated menu of boutique snacks, including traditional German flammkuchen (flatbreads), artisanal cheese boards, and premium nuts and chips.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located on the second floor directly above Big Planet Comics, this hidden gem offers a relaxed, upscale alternative to the neighborhood’s high-energy dance clubs.
  • Enjoy exceptional value on Sundays and Mondays, when the venue is known for offering particularly great prices on its high-quality drink menu.
