Suzie Q's

Suzie Q's

Category: Neighborhood Bars, Dive Bars, Dog Friendly

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price:

Address: 71 Potomac Ave SE, Washington, DC 20003, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Suzie Q’s is a soulful, retro-inspired neighborhood bar that blends a 1970s rock ‘n’ roll aesthetic with a laid-back waterfront atmosphere, offering a refreshing dose of dive-bar personality to the Navy Yard.

🍸 Best For

Affordable drinks, BBQ enthusiasts, dog owners, late-night cravings, and pre-game social hangs near Nats Park.

🔥 Highlights

  • Top-Tier Texas BBQ: Recently opened under the culinary expertise of a Hill Country alum, the menu features standout Texas-style favorites like chili-rubbed ribs, smoked wings, and a signature Big Brisket Sandwich.
  • Budget-Friendly Beverages: A welcome addition to the neighborhood's nightlife, offering highly affordable drink options including $5-7 beers, creative beer-and-shot combos, and a must-try "Ranch Water" that locals rave about.
  • Nostalgic Interior: The space features a cozy, retro feel decorated with vintage jerseys and band tees, providing a unique character that feels both welcoming and unpretentious.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • The venue is exceptionally dog-friendly and perfectly situated right by the local dog park, making it the ideal pitstop for pup owners.
  • Enjoy extended late-night hours most days of the week, perfect for post-game celebrations or a midnight snack.
