✨ Vibe

Live-K offers a high-octane, futuristic atmosphere at The Wharf, blending a club-like energy with state-of-the-art private karaoke suites that feel like your own personal concert hall.

🍸 Best For

Birthday celebrations, large group outings, late-night entertainment, and "rock star" fantasies.

🔥 Highlights

  • Cutting-Edge Technology: Sing your heart out with professional-grade audio systems and ultra-clear LED projection screens, featuring an extensive song library and YouTube accessibility.
  • Immersive Design: The venue boasts a breathtaking "Grand Scene" aesthetic, anchored by a massive 22-square meter LED mega-screen lobby and 15 stylishly themed private rooms.
  • Excellent Service & Dining: Enjoy highly-rated, attentive service alongside a premium food and beverage program curated by renowned restaurateur Chris Zhu.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended for private rooms, as this popular Wharf destination is known for its "jumping" atmosphere and quick-moving lines.
  • The venue welcomes birthday touches, allowing guests to bring in cakes and gifts for private party bookings.
