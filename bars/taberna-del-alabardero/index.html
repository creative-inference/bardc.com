Taberna del Alabardero

Taberna del Alabardero

Category: Spanish, Restaurant, Historic

Price: $$$

Address: 1776 I St NW, Washington, DC 20006

A D.C. institution for over 30 years, this elegant restaurant near the White House offers a taste of old-world Spain. Known for its traditional Spanish cuisine, including paella and tapas, it provides a formal and classic dining experience with beautiful patios and stately dining rooms.

Visit Website

Happy Hour

Monday-Friday, 3:00 pm - 7:00 pm. Three different Happy Hour menus are available for private parties of 10 or more guests, with prices ranging from $28 to $44 per person (tax and gratuity not included). Menus include options like Spanish olives, gazpacho shots, Serrano ham, Manchego cheese, and various tapas. Complimentary parking is available Monday-Friday after 4:00 pm and all day Saturday & Sunday.
