Taberna del Alabardero

Taberna del Alabardero

Category: Spanish, Live Music Venues, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: Downtown

Price: $$$

Address: 1776 I St NW, Washington, DC 20006

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A sophisticated and regal slice of Madrid in the heart of DC, featuring elegant red silk wallpaper and a classic, old-school atmosphere that transports guests straight to Spain.

🍸 Best For

Special occasions, corporate dinners, live flamenco performances, authentic Spanish tapas, and high-end happy hours.

🔥 Highlights

  • World-Class Entertainment: Vibrant live flamenco performances on Friday and Saturday nights (8–10 p.m.) bring the passion of Spanish culture directly to your table, often featuring interactive moments for celebratory guests.
  • Award-Winning Legacy: As a 2019 Honorary RAMMY Award winner and recognized as one of the "9 Classic Old-School Restaurants That Define DC," this historic establishment has been a premier destination for diplomats and connoisseurs since 1989.
  • Authentic Spanish Gastronomy: The kitchen excels at traditional staples, from the signature "igloo" salt-cooked bream and luscious paellas to their famous mushroom croquettes and extensive list of world-renowned Spanish wines.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Take advantage of complimentary parking at the adjacent Penn Parking garage after 4:00 p.m. on weekdays and all day on weekends.
  • Reservations are highly recommended for the weekend flamenco shows, and the daily Happy Hour runs from 3:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m. Monday through Friday.
