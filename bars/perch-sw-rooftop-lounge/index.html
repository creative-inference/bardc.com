Perch SW Rooftop Lounge

Perch SW Rooftop Lounge

Category: Rooftop Bars, Lounges, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price:

Address: 69 Q St SW, Washington, DC 20024, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A swanky and sophisticated rooftop destination that blends sleek, modern decor with breathtaking panoramic views of the Washington DC skyline.

🍸 Best For

Pre-game cocktails for Nationals Park or Audi Field, upscale happy hours, festive group parties, and high-energy weekend DJ sets.

🔥 Highlights

  • Elevated Culinary Experience: Under the direction of Executive Chef Angela Bethea, the menu features phenomenal dishes like signature shrimp and grits and spicy jerk chicken skewers.
  • Prime Skyline Views: The gorgeous indoor-outdoor layout offers some of the best vistas in the city, making it a highly desirable spot for sunset drinks and photography.
  • Vibrant Atmosphere: With live DJs on the weekends and a newly added satellite bar on the outdoor patio, the lounge maintains a lively and upscale energy.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located atop the Cambria Hotel Washington, D.C. Capitol Riverfront, this is a top-tier choice for sports fans looking for a refined experience near the stadium district.
  • The venue frequently hosts high-profile community events and themed celebrations, including WNBA fundraisers and seasonal rooftop parties.
