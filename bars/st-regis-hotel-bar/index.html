St. Regis Hotel Bar

St. Regis Hotel Bar

Category: Hotel Bars, Cocktail Bars, Piano Bars

Neighborhood: Downtown

Price: $$$$

Address: 923 16th St NW, Washington, DC 20006

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

This grand, fairytale-like lounge seamlessly blends historic elegance with an intimate atmosphere, featuring plush red and gold velvet seating, gilded fixtures, and a warm modern fireplace just steps from the White House.

🍸 Best For

Sophisticated pre-dinner drinks, romantic dates, festive holiday outings, high-end business meetings, and signature cocktail enthusiasts.

🔥 Highlights

  • World-Class Mixology: Under the expertise of Head Bartender Jane T., the bar serves exceptional cocktails including a legendary "swimming pool" sized Old Fashioned and the "Capital Mary," a unique DC-inspired take on the brand’s iconic Bloody Mary.
  • Michelin-Caliber Culinary Program: Recently appointed Executive Chef Jason Galbao brings Michelin-starred experience to the menu, elevating the bar’s offerings with bold Spanish-inspired flavors and modern techniques.
  • Timeless Traditions: Experience the nightly champagne sabrage ritual to celebrate the evening’s start, complemented by the elegant ambiance of a live pianist and seasonal features like the interactive Cherry Blossom Dessert Wheel.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Be sure to arrive by early evening to witness the signature nightly champagne sabrage, a storied St. Regis tradition.
  • The venue offers a rotating calendar of seasonal experiences, including themed afternoon tea and Japanese-inspired cocktail menus during the spring.
