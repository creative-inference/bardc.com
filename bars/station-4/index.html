✨ Vibe

Station 4 is a chic and welcoming New American bistro that blends modern, "niced up" decor with a relaxed, neighborhood feel perfect for any occasion.

🍸 Best For

Bottomless brunch, pre-theater dining, spirited happy hours, and elevated comfort food cravings.

🔥 Highlights

  • Gourmet Comfort Classics: The menu shines with standout items like the Smoked Gouda Mac n Cheese topped with an excellent crab cake and the fan-favorite, decadent Crème Brûlée.
  • Top-Tier Bottomless Brunch: Renowned for a lively weekend scene where attentive servers ensure your mimosas, bellinis, and margaritas are never empty alongside dishes like Shrimp Shakshuka and Steak & Eggs.
  • Unbeatable Location: Conveniently situated directly across from the Waterfront Metro station and just steps from Arena Stage, making it the premier destination for theater-goers and Southwest explorers.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are recommended for their popular $45 bottomless brunch deal, and the venue is fully wheelchair accessible for all guests.
  • A 20% service fee is typically included as gratuity, ensuring seamless and professional service throughout your visit.
