Tom's Watch Bar

Category: Sports Bars, American, Groups

Neighborhood: Navy Yard

Price:

Address: 1250 Half St SE, Washington, DC 20003, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An electric, high-octane sports sanctuary in the heart of Navy Yard featuring a 360-degree viewing experience that makes every guest feel like they are sitting courtside or on the 50-yard line.

🍸 Best For

Game-day watch parties, pre-stadium drinks, lively group celebrations, family birthdays, and catching multiple games simultaneously.

🔥 Highlights

  • Immersive Viewing: Massive, oversized stadium screens and a 360° layout ensure you never miss a moment of the action from any seat in the house.
  • Elevated Game-Day Fare: A chef-driven menu featuring crowd favorites like crispy cheese curds, Nashville hot tenders, prime rib dip sandwiches, and zesty carne asada tacos.
  • Exceptional Hospitality: A friendly and energetic staff—frequently praised for their attentive service—creates a welcoming environment for die-hard fans and families alike.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Join "Tom’s Fan Club" online before your visit to claim a $1 pint of beer or a signature cocktail.
  • The venue features versatile indoor-outdoor spaces with garage-style doors and a dedicated outdoor viewing area perfect for soaking up the Navy Yard atmosphere.

🔄 Weekly Events

🎙️ Reds Hot Stove League Live Broadcasts

A weekly offseason radio program featuring the Cincinnati Reds and 700WLW, taped live at the BetMGM Sportsbook (Cincinnati location).

⛏️ Denver Nuggets Watch Parties

Daily

The ultimate spot for Nuggets Nation to gather for every slam dunk and clutch performance with dedicated sound zones.

🏀 NBA Game Watch Parties

Daily

Watch every NBA game, home and away, streaming live with sound on 360° viewing screens and a stadium-sized screen.
