The Next Whisky Bar

The Next Whisky Bar

Category: Whiskey Bars, Cocktail Bars, Lounges

Neighborhood: Foggy Bottom

Price:

Address: 2650 Virginia Ave NW, Washington, DC 20037, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An opulent and visually stunning retreat within the iconic Watergate Hotel, blending mid-century modern glamour with a warm, golden glow from thousands of illuminated whiskey bottles.

🍸 Best For

High-end whiskey flights, Instagram-worthy date nights, sophisticated pre-theater cocktails, and cozy winter gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • The breathtaking "bottle wall," featuring over 2,500 custom-illuminated whiskey bottles that create a unique, high-design atmosphere.
  • World-class service from expert bartenders who offer educational tastings, guiding guests through a curated selection of premier spirits and unique cocktail creations.
  • The seasonal outdoor terrace, which transforms into a winter wonderland featuring private, heated igloos for a cozy and exclusive social experience.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located in the heart of Foggy Bottom, it is a perfect destination for a drink before or after a performance at the nearby Kennedy Center.
  • Advanced reservations are highly encouraged if you wish to secure one of the popular private igloos during the colder months.
