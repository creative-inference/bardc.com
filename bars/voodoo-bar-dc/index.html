✨ Vibe

Voodoo Bar DC delivers a high-energy yet relaxed New Orleans-inspired atmosphere where "no-skips" playlists and warm, family-style hospitality make everyone feel like a U Street local.

🍸 Best For

Post-work happy hours, interactive group hangouts, craft cocktail enthusiasts, and vibrant weekend warm-ups.

🔥 Highlights

  • Exceptional Hospitality: Led by standout bartender Isaiah, the staff is renowned for providing personalized service, expert drink recommendations, and a welcoming environment that treats every guest like family.
  • Signature Tiki Cocktails: Sip on creative, island-inspired drinks like the fan-favorite "Mardi-Gras-arita" or the adventurous "Gator Bait."
  • Interactive Entertainment: The energy stays high with curated music sets that keep the crowd "lit" and a selection of fun games to play while you drink and socialize.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Frequently cited as a top-tier happy hour destination on U Street for those looking to wind down with great vibes and conversation.
  • Expect a personal touch, as the owner is often present to interact with guests and ensure a memorable experience.
