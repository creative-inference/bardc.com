Capitol City Brewing Company

Category: Brewpub, Sports Bar, American Restaurant

Price: $$

Address: 1100 New York Ave NW, Washington, DC 20005

★★★★★ 4 (3271 reviews)

Opened in 1992 as the first brewpub in the nation's capital since Prohibition, Capitol City Brewing Company serves a variety of award-winning, hand-crafted beers alongside American comfort food. It's a popular downtown destination for sports fans, featuring large-screen TVs and a spacious private event room.
