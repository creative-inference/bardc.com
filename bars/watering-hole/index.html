Watering Hole

Watering Hole

Category: Neighborhood Bars, Waterfront, Happy Hour Spots

Neighborhood: The Wharf

Price:

Address: 850 Wharf St SW, Washington, DC 20024, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

A breezy, open-air waterfront retreat that offers a front-row seat to the scenic beauty and vibrant energy of the District Wharf.

🍸 Best For

Waterfront cocktails, golden hour sunsets, people-watching, festive parade viewing

🔥 Highlights

  • Unbeatable panoramic views of the Potomac River from its prime location along the boardwalk.
  • Seasonal drink specialties, such as cozy mulled wine, perfect for enjoying the waterfront year-round.
  • The ideal pit-stop for a refreshing beverage while exploring the shops and attractions of the Wharf.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • This is an outdoor-focused venue, making it the ultimate destination for a drink on a beautiful sunny day.
  • Positioned perfectly for front-row access to the Wharf’s frequent parades and community celebrations.
