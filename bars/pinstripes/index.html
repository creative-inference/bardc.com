✨ Vibe

A sophisticated yet high-energy social hub in the heart of Georgetown that seamlessly blends upscale Italian-American dining with the nostalgic fun of bowling and bocce.

🍸 Best For

Group outings, corporate happy hours, weekend family brunch, date nights, and private celebrations.

🔥 Highlights

  • Elite Gaming & Entertainment: Enjoy a modern spin on classic games with full-service bowling lanes and bocce courts designed for stylish social competition.
  • Gourmet From-Scratch Menu: Indulge in elevated Italian-American fare, including legendary tenderloin sliders, artisan wood-fired pizzas, and an expansive Sunday brunch buffet featuring green chilaquiles and a chocolate fondue station.
  • Scenic Outdoor Oasis: Relax on the beautiful outdoor patio, which offers pretty views and a peaceful atmosphere tucked away from the Georgetown bustle.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • This premier Georgetown location remains a flagship destination, fully operational and welcoming guests for daily dining, gaming, and private events.
  • Take advantage of family-friendly perks on Sundays, where children under 12 can enjoy the famous brunch buffet for free.
