JINYA Ramen Bar

JINYA Ramen Bar

Category: Mocktails, Casual

Neighborhood: The Wharf

Price:

Address: 675 Wharf St SW, Washington, DC 20024, United States

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Recently debuted at The Wharf, this lively ramen destination pairs a modern, high-energy atmosphere with a stunning waterfront patio perfect for scenic Southwest D.C. dining.

🍸 Best For

Waterfront dining, authentic ramen, creative mocktails, casual group dinners.

🔥 Highlights

  • 20-Hour Signature Broths: Experience incredible depth of flavor with authentic broths slow-simmered for up to 20 hours using premium Fuji water and paired with handcrafted noodles.
  • Expertly Crafted Mocktails: The bar program shines with balanced and refreshing alcohol-free options, including the standout Matcha Lychee Lemonade and the J.Girl Strawberry Mocktail.
  • Premier Waterfront Patio: Occupying a prime space in the Southwest development, the restaurant features a spacious outdoor seating area ideal for soaking in the river views.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Be sure to try the Beef Birria Ramen, a fan-favorite signature dish known for its rich and comforting flavor profile.
  • This location is a fresh addition to the area, having officially opened its doors in August 2025 in the former Lucky Buns space.
