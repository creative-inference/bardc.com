The Sovereign

The Sovereign

Category: Belgian, Beer Bar, Restaurant

Price: $$$

Address: 1206 Wisconsin Ave NW, Washington, DC 20007

Tucked away in a Georgetown alley, The Sovereign is a transportive Belgian beer bar and bistro. It offers an encyclopedic list of Belgian brews, with dozens on tap and hundreds in bottles, complemented by a menu of traditional Belgian cuisine like mussels and frites in a rustic, two-story space.
