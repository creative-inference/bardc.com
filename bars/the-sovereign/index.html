The Sovereign

The Sovereign

Category: Gastropubs, Belgian, Beer Gardens

Neighborhood: Georgetown

Price: $$$

Address: 1206 Wisconsin Ave NW, Washington, DC 20007

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

Tucked away in a charming Georgetown alley, The Sovereign offers a rustic and lively atmosphere that beautifully celebrates the fabled brewing culture and refined bistro traditions of Belgium.

🍸 Best For

Craft beer connoisseurs, sophisticated dates, group celebrations, and upscale weekend brunches.

🔥 Highlights

  • World-Class Beer Selection: A masterfully curated beverage program by Greg Engert featuring 50 drafts and over 350 bottles, specializing in rare Lambics and authentic Belgian-style ales.
  • Authentic Belgian Cuisine: An ingredient-driven menu showcasing classic regional dishes such as Dutch-style mussels, crispy flammekuchen, and the signature Lapin a la Kriek (rabbit braised in cherry beer).
  • Enchanting Ambiance: A rustic-chic interior with a warm, lantern-lit approach that creates a memorable and vibrant setting for dinner or drinks.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are recommended for dinner and their popular weekend brunch, which is served Saturdays and Sundays from 11am to 3pm.
  • Located on Wisconsin Avenue, the venue is a short distance from Georgetown’s premier shopping and offers nearby parking options off Prospect Street.
