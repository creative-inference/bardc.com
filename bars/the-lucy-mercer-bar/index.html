The Lucy Mercer Bar

The Lucy Mercer Bar

Category: Speakeasies, Cocktail Bars, Romantic

Neighborhood: NoMa

Price:

Address: 1287 4th St NE, Washington, DC 20002, United States

View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

An immaculate, prohibition-era speakeasy tucked away above Minetta Tavern, offering a historical and transportive atmosphere that feels both exclusive and intimate.

🍸 Best For

Romantic date nights, craft cocktail enthusiasts, intimate conversations, "see and be seen" outings

🔥 Highlights

  • Thoughtfully crafted signature drinks, including the rye-based Nijinsky and a variety of standout, expertly prepared martinis.
  • A sophisticated, distraction-free environment curated by a strict "no phone" policy that preserves the venue's mysterious allure.
  • A recently opened addition to the NoMA nightlife scene that perfectly blends old-world charm with modern Union Market energy.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Reservations are highly recommended to guarantee seating in this intimate upstairs lounge.
  • Be sure to grab their signature matchbooks at the front on your way out for a unique memento of the evening.
