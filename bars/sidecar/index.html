✨ Vibe

Sidecar offers a sophisticated and intimate retreat featuring a classic dark-wood interior and a creative, upscale lounge atmosphere perfect for discerning guests.

🍸 Best For

Creative cocktails, private dates, professional networking, and upscale bar dining.

🔥 Highlights

  • Expertly Crafted Cocktails: The menu features a refined selection of signature drinks, including the creative "Queen’s Gambit" and their namesake "Bourbon Sidecar," crafted with premium spirits.
  • Gourmet Bar Fare: Beyond the drinks, the kitchen serves high-end classics ranging from fresh East Coast oysters and Maine peekytoe crab dip to the legendary "Cadillac" burger.
  • An Exclusive Atmosphere: Known for its "no-camera" policy and private club feel, the venue provides a secluded and personalized environment that ensures privacy and comfort for all patrons.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Conveniently located within the Washington Hilton, making it an ideal destination for both locals and travelers in the Dupont Circle area.
  • With its emphasis on privacy and attentive service, it is a premier choice for those looking for an elevated and quiet alternative to the standard city bar.
