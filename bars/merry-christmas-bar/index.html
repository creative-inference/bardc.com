✨ Vibe

This sprawling three-level holiday destination transforms the heart of U Street into an immersive winter wonderland bursting with festive energy and over-the-top seasonal decor.

🍸 Best For

Themed holiday dates, festive group outings, Instagrammable cocktail sessions, late-night dancing, rooftop views

🔥 Highlights

  • Explore three distinct levels of "Holiday Magic," each offering a unique immersive atmosphere and stunning, floor-to-ceiling festive decorations.
  • Experience high-energy DJ sets and a vibrant dance floor that keeps the holiday spirit alive late into the night.
  • Enjoy a premier rooftop bar experience that combines seasonal charm with the bustling energy of the iconic U Street corridor.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Located in the historic former Patty Boom Boom space at 1359 U Street NW.
  • This highly anticipated seasonal destination officially opened on November 28, 2025, for the peak holiday season.
