The Bottom Line

The Bottom Line

Category: Dive Bars, Sports Bars, Neighborhood Bars

Neighborhood: Downtown

Price: $

Address: 1716 I St NW, Washington, DC 20006

Website
View on Google Maps →

✨ Vibe

With a 45-year legacy as a downtown fixture, this unpretentious basement hideaway offers a nostalgic, "where everyone knows your name" atmosphere that feels like a warm, familiar escape from the city bustle.

🍸 Best For

Cincinnati Bengals fans, budget-friendly happy hours, high-energy sports watch parties, and casual group gatherings.

🔥 Highlights

  • The Ultimate Bengals Hideout: Known as the premier DC headquarters for Bengals fans, this spot features an electric atmosphere on game days with big screens and dedicated sound for every chant.
  • Signature Sips & Specials: Beyond their famous $5 house margaritas, the bar boasts an impressive selection of house-specialty mules and budget-friendly "hump day" deals on Coronas and Rudy’s Nachos.
  • Award-Winning Hospitality: Voted for having the city's most popular bartenders, the staff provides friendly, efficient service that welcomes everyone from suit-and-tie professionals to sports fans in full gear.

ℹ️ Need to Know

  • Don't miss the legendary Happy Hour running Monday through Friday from 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM for some of the best value deals in downtown DC.
  • Look for the entrance just below street level to find this hidden gem, where the kitchen stays open late to serve up their must-try wings and comforting bar favorites.
